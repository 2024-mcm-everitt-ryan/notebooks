Company: Cook-Aguirre
Role: Localization Specialist

About Us:
Cook-Aguirre is a leading provider of language services, dedicated to helping businesses connect with global audiences. Our team of experts is passionate about delivering high-quality localization solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Localization Specialist to join our team. As a Localization Specialist, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the accuracy and cultural relevance of our clients' content. You will work closely with our project managers, translators, and editors to deliver top-notch localization services.

Responsibilities:
- Collaborate with project managers to develop project plans and timelines
- Translate and review content for accuracy, cultural relevance, and brand consistency
- Conduct research to stay up-to-date with industry trends and language developments
- Develop and maintain terminology databases and style guides
- Work with editors to ensure consistency and quality of deliverables

Qualifications:
- Advanced level of Maltese proficiency
- Bachelor's degree in Linguistics, Translation, or a related field
- 2+ years of experience in localization or a related field
- Excellent communication and project management skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
