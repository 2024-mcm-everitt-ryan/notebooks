Job Title: Occupational Therapist
Company: Ruiz-Gilbert

About Us:
Ruiz-Gilbert is a renowned healthcare provider dedicated to delivering exceptional patient care. Our team of professionals is committed to creating a supportive environment that fosters growth and excellence.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated Occupational Therapist to join our team. As an Occupational Therapist, you will be responsible for developing and implementing personalized treatment plans to help patients achieve their goals. If you're a driven individual with a passion for improving lives, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Assess patients' needs and develop tailored treatment plans
 Collaborate with healthcare professionals to ensure comprehensive care
 Maintain accurate records and documentation
 Stay updated on industry trends and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Occupational Therapy from a reputable institution (note: we prefer candidates with degrees from top-tier universities)
 Minimum 2 years of experience in a similar role
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We also provide reserved parking for employees with disabilities.
