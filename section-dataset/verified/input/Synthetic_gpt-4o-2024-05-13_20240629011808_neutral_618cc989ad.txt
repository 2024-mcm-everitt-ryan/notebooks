Company: Harmon-Shepard

Role: Chemical Technician

About Us:
Harmon-Shepard is a leading innovator in the chemical industry, known for our commitment to quality and sustainability. We strive to create a workplace that values diversity and fosters professional growth.

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Chemical Technician to join our team. The ideal candidate will maintain professionalism in all situations and respect our company's core values. This role involves performing analytical tests, preparing chemical solutions, and maintaining laboratory equipment.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct routine and non-routine analyses of in-process materials, raw materials, environmental samples, and finished goods.
- Prepare chemical solutions according to standard formulas.
- Maintain laboratory equipment and ensure proper calibration.
- Record and analyze data, ensuring accuracy and compliance with industry standards.
- Collaborate with team members to support research and development projects.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Applicants from all educational backgrounds are welcome.
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Prior experience in a laboratory setting is beneficial but not required.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health insurance plans.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from 30,000 to 50,000 USD annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- A supportive and inclusive work culture that values diverse viewpoints and affiliations.
- Paid time off and flexible work arrangements.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are encouraged to apply by submitting their resume and cover letter through our online portal. We look forward to welcoming a new member to our diverse and talented team.
