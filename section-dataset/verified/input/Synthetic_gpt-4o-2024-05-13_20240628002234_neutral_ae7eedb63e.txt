Company: Wilson Inc

Role: Parks Enforcement Patrol

Company Background: Wilson Inc is a leader in providing comprehensive public safety services, ensuring the security and well-being of community spaces. We are dedicated to creating a supportive, inclusive, and diverse work environment.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description: We are seeking a diligent and dedicated individual to join our team as a Parks Enforcement Patrol Officer. This role involves ensuring the safety and security of public parks, enforcing local laws and regulations, and providing exceptional customer service to park visitors.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct regular patrols of designated park areas.
- Enforce park regulations and local laws.
- Assist in emergency situations and provide first aid when necessary.
- Document incidents and complete reports accurately.
- Engage with the community to promote park safety and enjoyment.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent; additional education in criminal justice or related field is a plus.
- Previous experience in law enforcement or security is preferred.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Must be able to pass a background check and physical fitness test.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $40,000 to $55,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous parental leave designed to support our diverse workforce.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Inclusive and welcoming workplace culture.
- Paid time off, including vacation, sick leave, and holidays.

How to Apply: Interested candidates should submit a resume and cover letter detailing their qualifications and experience. Wilson Inc is an equal opportunity employer. We welcome individuals from all backgrounds and experiences and encourage applications from candidates who identify as LGBTQ.
