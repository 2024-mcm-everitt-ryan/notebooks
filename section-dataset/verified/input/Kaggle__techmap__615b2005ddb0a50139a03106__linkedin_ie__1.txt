What we need

We are looking for Graduate Cyber Security Engineer.

Job Purpose and Primary Objectives

TCS Letterkenny is offering a Graduate Programme for cyber-savvy graduates who are enthusiastic about pursuing a career in information security. Recent graduates and post-graduates with a minimum 2:1 degree in Forensics, IT, computer science or an alternative cyber tech-related subject are encouraged to apply.

Prerequisite requirements for selection include:
- A deep interest and passion for cyber technology and an ability to speak knowledgeably about the subject.
- An enquiring and innovative mindset and willingness to raise and explore new ideas.
- Curiosity and a desire to learn and meet new challenges.
- A technical aptitude for Cyber Security.

Key responsibilities
- Learning about the role's responsibilities and functions within a Threat Center or Security Operations Center.
- Supporting the implementation, management, monitoring and upgrading of security measures for the protection of the organization's data, systems and networks.
- Involvement in troubleshooting security and network problems.
- Participating in the change management process.
- Testing and identifying network and system vulnerabilities.
- Daily administrative tasks including reporting and communication.

Technical Skills
- Fundamental Computer Forensics Skills.
- An Understanding of Hacking.
- A certification in CompTIA Network or Security is desirable but not essential.

Other Skills
- Resourcefulness.
- Analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Ability to work as part of a team.
- Attention to detail.
- Strong communication skills.

Potential start date: October 2021.
