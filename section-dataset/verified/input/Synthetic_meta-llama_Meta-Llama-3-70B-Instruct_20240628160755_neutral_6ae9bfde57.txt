Company: Wong-Harris

Job Title: Human Resources Manager

Job Type: Full-time

About Us:
Wong-Harris is a diverse and inclusive company that values the contributions of all employees. We believe that our employees' unique perspectives and experiences are essential to our success.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Human Resources Manager to join our team. As an HR Manager, you will be responsible for developing and implementing HR strategies, managing recruitment and talent acquisition, and ensuring compliance with labor laws and regulations.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement HR strategies to support business objectives
 Manage recruitment and talent acquisition processes
 Ensure compliance with labor laws and regulations
 Develop and implement diversity, equity, and inclusion initiatives
 Provide training and support to employees and management
 Analyze HR data and metrics to inform business decisions

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field
 5+ years of experience in HR management
 Proven experience in recruitment, talent acquisition, and compliance
 Strong knowledge of labor laws and regulations
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
