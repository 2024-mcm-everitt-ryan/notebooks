Company Background:

Allen-Solis is a renowned dance company known for its innovation and dedication to the art of dance. We pride ourselves on our heritage and achievements, creating an inclusive and dynamic environment where collaboration and respect are key.

Job Title: Dancer

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:

We seek a dedicated and resolute professional to join our team as a dancer. The candidate will perform in various productions, participate in rehearsals, and collaborate with choreographers and fellow dancers. The ideal candidate is passionate about dance, adept at problem-solving, and committed to continuous improvement.

Responsibilities:

- Perform in company productions and events.
- Participate actively in rehearsals and practice sessions.
- Collaborate with choreographers to develop and refine dance routines.
- Maintain peak physical condition and technical skills.
- Engage with the community through outreach and educational programs.

Qualifications and Experience:

- Proven experience as a professional dancer.
- Strong technical skills in at least one dance style; versatility is a plus.
- Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and adaptability.
- Commitment to maintaining physical fitness and conditioning.

Perks and Benefits:

We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £25,000 to £40,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. Additional benefits include:

- Comprehensive health insurance.
- Opportunities for professional development and continuous education.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Access to company fitness facilities and training sessions.

Allen-Solis encourages all qualified candidates to apply, regardless of gender. We foster an environment where everyone can thrive and contribute to our shared success.
