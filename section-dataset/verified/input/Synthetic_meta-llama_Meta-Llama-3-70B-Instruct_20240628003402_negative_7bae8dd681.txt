Company: Johnston-Ramirez

Job Title: Executive Project Auditor

About Us:
Johnston-Ramirez is a leading consulting firm dedicated to delivering exceptional project management services to our clients. We pride ourselves on our traditional values and commitment to excellence.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Executive Project Auditor to join our team. As an Executive Project Auditor, you will be responsible for conducting audits, identifying areas of improvement, and implementing process enhancements to ensure project efficiency. You will work closely with our project managers to ensure compliance with company standards and best practices.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct project audits to identify areas of improvement
 Develop and implement process enhancements to increase project efficiency
 Collaborate with project managers to ensure compliance with company standards
 Provide training and guidance to project team members
 Develop and maintain audit reports and dashboards

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in project auditing or a related field
 Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Business Administration, or a related field
 Professional certification in auditing or project management (e.g. CPA, PMP)
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
