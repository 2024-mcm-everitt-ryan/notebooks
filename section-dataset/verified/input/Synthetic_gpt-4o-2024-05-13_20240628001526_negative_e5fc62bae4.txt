Company Background:
Lewis-Gibson is a forward-thinking firm dedicated to ensuring compliance and regulatory excellence in every facet of operation. We pride ourselves on fostering innovation and integrity within the industry.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dynamic and energetic Compliance Manager to join our team. This role is ideal for new professionals with a fresh outlook who are ready to take on the challenges of ensuring our company adheres to all relevant regulations and standards. This position is perfect for recent graduates or up-and-coming professionals eager to make a significant impact in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:
- Develop, implement, and oversee compliance programs and policies.
- Conduct regular audits and risk assessments.
- Provide training and support to staff on compliance-related matters.
- Stay updated with changes in regulatory requirements and ensure company adherence.
- Prepare comprehensive reports for senior management.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Law, Business Administration, or related field.
- Fresh out of university preferred.
- Excellent analytical and communication skills.
- Basic understanding of compliance regulations and industry standards.
- Demonstrated ability to work in high-energy, fast-paced settings.
