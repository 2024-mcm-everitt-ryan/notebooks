Company: Brown-Johnston
Role: Interviewer

At Brown-Johnston, we're dedicated to fostering a culture of diversity, equity, and inclusion. As an equal opportunities employer, we welcome applications from individuals of all backgrounds and identities.

Job Description:
We're seeking an experienced Interviewer to join our team. As an Interviewer, you'll be responsible for conducting fair and unbiased interviews, ensuring that all candidates have an equal opportunity to showcase their skills and experience. You'll work closely with our hiring managers to develop interview questions, assess candidate fit, and provide feedback.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct in-person and virtual interviews with candidates
 Develop and implement fair and inclusive interview practices
 Collaborate with hiring managers to identify ideal candidate profiles
 Provide constructive feedback to candidates
 Maintain accurate records of candidate interactions

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a similar role
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion in the hiring process

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $55,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our benefits package includes comprehensive health insurance, flexible working hours, and a generous paid time-off policy. We also provide Nursing Rooms equipped for breastfeeding and respect and support all forms of gender expression.
