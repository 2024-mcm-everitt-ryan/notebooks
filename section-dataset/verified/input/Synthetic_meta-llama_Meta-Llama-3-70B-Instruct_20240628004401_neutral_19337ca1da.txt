Company: Suarez-Garcia

Role: Lifeguard

Job Description:
Suarez-Garcia is seeking a highly skilled and dedicated Lifeguard to join our team. As a Lifeguard, you will be responsible for ensuring the safety and well-being of our guests and patrons. This is a unique opportunity to work in a dynamic and fast-paced environment, while making a meaningful impact on the lives of others.

Responsibilities:

 Monitor pool areas to prevent accidents and respond to emergencies as needed
 Provide emergency care and first aid as necessary
 Enforce pool rules and regulations to maintain a safe environment
 Collaborate with other staff members to maintain a clean and organized pool area
 Develop and maintain a positive relationship with guests and patrons

Qualifications and Experience:

 Must be at least 15 years old (in accordance with local regulations)
 Current lifeguard certification (American Red Cross or equivalent)
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and respond to emergencies quickly and effectively
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work varying shifts, including weekends and holidays

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $50,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional development and advancement
 Comprehensive training program to ensure your success
 Flexible scheduling to accommodate your needs
 A supportive and inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities
