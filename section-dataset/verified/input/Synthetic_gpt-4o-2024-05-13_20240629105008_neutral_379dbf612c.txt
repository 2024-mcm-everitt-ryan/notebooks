Company: Young, Hensley and Carter  
Role: Compliance Manager  

Company Background:  
Young, Hensley and Carter is a global leader in financial services, committed to offering innovative solutions while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and compliance. We value a diverse and inclusive workforce that brings a wide range of perspectives and skills.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description and Responsibilities:  
We are seeking a Compliance Manager to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for:

- Ensuring all company operations comply with relevant regulations and internal policies.
- Developing and implementing compliance programs and monitoring their effectiveness.
- Conducting regular audits and assessments to identify and rectify compliance issues.
- Collaborating with various departments to ensure understanding and adherence to compliance standards.
- Staying current with changes in laws and regulations that may affect the company.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:

- Bachelor's degree in Business, Law, or related field.
- Minimum of 5 years' experience in a compliance role.
- Strong understanding of regulatory frameworks and compliance requirements.
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Effective communication and interpersonal skills.

- Comprehensive health insurance and wellness programs.
- Professional development opportunities and career growth.
- Generous paid time off and flexible working arrangements.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
