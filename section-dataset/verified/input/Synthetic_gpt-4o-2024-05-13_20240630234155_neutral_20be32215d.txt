Company Background:
Riley Inc is a leading healthcare provider dedicated to improving patient outcomes through innovative medical solutions and exceptional care. Our team of professionals is committed to excellence and compassion in service.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a motivated and dynamic Respiratory Therapist to join our dedicated team. The ideal candidate will provide high-quality respiratory care to patients, ensuring their comfort and well-being. This role requires a professional who is knowledgeable and committed to patient care.

  Responsibilities:
    - Assess and treat patients with respiratory and cardiopulmonary disorders.
    - Develop and implement patient care plans based on individual needs.
    - Perform diagnostic tests and analyze results to determine appropriate treatments.
    - Educate patients and families on managing respiratory conditions.
    - Collaborate with healthcare professionals to provide comprehensive care.
    - Maintain accurate and detailed patient records.


  Qualifications:
    - Bachelor's degree in Respiratory Therapy or related field.
    - Valid Respiratory Therapist license.
    - Minimum of 2 years of experience in a clinical setting.
    - Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
    - Ability to work independently as well as part of a team.
    - Proficiency in using respiratory care equipment and technology.
