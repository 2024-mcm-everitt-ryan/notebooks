Company: Jones-Yates

Job Title: Senior Accountant

About Us:
Jones-Yates is a leading accounting firm dedicated to providing exceptional financial services to our clients. We believe in fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment that encourages collaboration and creativity.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Accountant to join our team. As a Senior Accountant, you will be responsible for preparing and reviewing financial statements, conducting audits, and providing expert financial guidance to our clients.

Responsibilities:
- Prepare and review financial statements, including balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements
- Conduct audits and ensure compliance with accounting standards and regulatory requirements
- Provide financial guidance and support to clients
- Develop and maintain relationships with clients and internal stakeholders
- Stay up-to-date with changes in accounting standards and regulatory requirements

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Accounting or related field
- 5+ years of experience in accounting, preferably in a public accounting firm
- CPA certification or eligibility to obtain
- Excellent analytical, communication, and interpersonal skills
- Fluent in English

Benefits:
- Salary range: $80,000 - $100,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment

We welcome applications from candidates of all backgrounds and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
