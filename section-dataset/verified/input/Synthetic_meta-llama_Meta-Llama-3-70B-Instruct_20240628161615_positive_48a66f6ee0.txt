Company Background:
Marquez-Perez is a progressive law enforcement agency dedicated to serving and protecting our diverse community. We believe that our workforce should reflect the community we serve, and we are committed to fostering an inclusive and equitable work environment.

Job Type:
Police Administrative Aide

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Police Administrative Aide to join our team. As a Police Administrative Aide, you will provide administrative support to our law enforcement team, ensuring seamless operations and exceptional service to our community.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to law enforcement personnel, including preparing reports, maintaining records, and performing data entry tasks.
 Assist with scheduling, coordination, and logistics for police operations and events.
 Develop and maintain effective filing systems, both physical and digital.
 Provide exceptional customer service to the public, responding to inquiries and resolving issues in a timely and professional manner.

Qualifications and Experience:
We welcome applications from candidates with disabilities who possess:

 A high school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree in a related field preferred.
 2+ years of experience in an administrative role, preferably in a law enforcement setting.
 Excellent organizational, communication, and interpersonal skills.
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with attention to detail and accuracy.

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a 403(b) retirement plan with a 5% employer match. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and offer opportunities for professional growth and development.
