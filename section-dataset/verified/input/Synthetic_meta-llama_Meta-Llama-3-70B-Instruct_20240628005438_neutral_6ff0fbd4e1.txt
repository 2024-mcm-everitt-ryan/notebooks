Company: Case, Barron and Estrada
Role: Help Desk Technician

About Us:
Case, Barron and Estrada is a leading IT solutions provider, dedicated to delivering exceptional customer experiences. We are committed to creating an inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities for all.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Help Desk Technician to join our team. As a Help Desk Technician, you will be responsible for providing technical support to our customers, troubleshooting hardware and software issues, and resolving problems in a timely and efficient manner.

Responsibilities:

 Provide technical support to customers via phone, email, and chat
 Troubleshoot and resolve hardware and software issues
 Document customer interactions and resolutions in our database
 Collaborate with other teams to resolve complex issues
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in a help desk or technical support role
 Strong technical skills in hardware, software, and networking
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from USD 45,000 to USD 60,000, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We also provide a supportive and inclusive work environment, with opportunities for professional growth and development.
