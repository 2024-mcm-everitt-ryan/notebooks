Full-Time

Job Description:
Johnson Inc is looking for a detail-oriented Webform Team Member to join our dynamic team. This role entails creating, managing, and optimizing webforms to boost user engagement and data collection. The ideal candidate will be adept at working in a collaborative environment and possess strong problem-solving skills.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain webforms for various projects and clients.
- Collaborate with design and development teams to ensure seamless integration of webforms.
- Optimize webform performance and user experience.
- Conduct regular testing and debugging to ensure webforms are functioning correctly.
- Provide support and troubleshooting for webform issues.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- Experience with webform tools and platforms.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
