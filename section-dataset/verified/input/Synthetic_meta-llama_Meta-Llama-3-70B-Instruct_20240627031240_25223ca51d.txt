Company: Cooke Group

Job Title: Division Director, Senior Staffing Manager

About Us:
Cooke Group is a leading provider of staffing solutions, dedicated to building diverse and inclusive teams that reflect the communities we serve. We are committed to fostering a culture of belonging, where everyone feels valued, respected, and empowered to succeed.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced and results-driven Division Director, Senior Staffing Manager to lead our team in delivering exceptional staffing services to our clients. As a senior leader, you will be responsible for developing and implementing strategic plans to drive business growth, manage high-performing teams, and build long-lasting client relationships.

Responsibilities:

- Develop and execute business strategies to achieve sales targets and expand our client base
- Lead and manage a team of staffing professionals, providing coaching, guidance, and support to ensure exceptional performance
- Foster strong relationships with clients, understanding their needs, and delivering tailored staffing solutions
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive business growth and improve operational efficiency
- Analyze market trends, identifying opportunities to enhance our service offerings and stay ahead of the competition

Qualifications:

- 5+ years of experience in staffing, recruitment, or a related field
- Proven track record of achieving sales targets and leading high-performing teams
- Excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment, adapting to changing priorities and deadlines

What We Offer:

- Salary range: $90,000 - $120,000 per year, plus performance-based bonuses
- Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage
- Ongoing training and development opportunities to enhance your skills and knowledge
- Collaborative and inclusive work environment, focused on diversity, equity, and inclusion

We Welcome Diversity:
Cooke Group is committed to creating a workforce that reflects the diversity of our communities. We actively encourage applications from individuals of African, Latinx, Asian, Indigenous, and Pacific Islander heritage, as well as other underrepresented groups. We believe that a diverse and inclusive workforce is essential to delivering exceptional staffing services and driving business success.
