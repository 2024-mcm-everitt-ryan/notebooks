Computer Service Technician

Company Background:
Nielsen, Sharp and Davis is a leading technology firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions to our clients. We take pride in our heritage and achievements, and we're looking for talented individuals to join our team.

Job Description:
We're seeking a skilled Computer Service Technician to join our team. As a craftsman with attention to detail, you'll be responsible for installing, configuring, and maintaining computer systems and networks. You'll work closely with our clients to understand their needs and provide a gentleman's approach to customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Install, configure, and maintain computer systems and networks
 Troubleshoot and repair technical issues
 Provide exceptional customer service
 Work effectively in a fast-paced environment

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in computer service and repair
 Strong technical skills and knowledge of computer systems and networks
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds (note: reasonable accommodations will be made for candidates with disabilities)

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $55,000 to $75,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You'll also receive comprehensive health benefits, paid time off, and opportunities for professional growth and development.
