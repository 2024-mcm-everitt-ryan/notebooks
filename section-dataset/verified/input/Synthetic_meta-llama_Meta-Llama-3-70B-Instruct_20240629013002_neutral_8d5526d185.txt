Company: Kelly LLC
Role: Licensed Vocational Nurse

Job Description:
Kelly LLC is seeking a compassionate and skilled Licensed Vocational Nurse (LVN) to join our dedicated team. As an LVN, you will provide high-quality patient care, maintain accurate records, and collaborate with our interdisciplinary team to ensure exceptional healthcare outcomes.

Responsibilities:

 Provide direct patient care, including administering medications, performing treatments, and monitoring vital signs
 Develop and implement individualized care plans, in collaboration with the healthcare team
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date patient records, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements
 Foster a culture of respect, empathy, and kindness among patients, families, and team members

Qualifications and Experience:
 Current LVN licensure in good standing
 Minimum 2 years of experience in a healthcare setting
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
 Continuous professional experience is valued

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 55,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as paid time off and opportunities for professional growth and development.
