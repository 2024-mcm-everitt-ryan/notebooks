Company Background:
Mcclain, Hawkins and Harmon is a leading industrial engineering firm specializing in the design, fabrication, and maintenance of complex boiler systems. We are committed to delivering innovative solutions and high-quality services to our clients within various industries.

Job Title:
Boilermaker

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and detail-oriented Boilermaker to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for assembling, installing, and repairing boilers, tanks, and closed vats. Applicants should be comfortable working in various environments and adhering to safety protocols.

Key Responsibilities:
- Read blueprints and schematics to determine project requirements.
- Assemble and install boilers, tanks, and vats using relevant tools and techniques.
- Inspect and repair boilers and other related equipment to ensure efficient operation.
- Use a variety of welding and cutting equipment to shape and join metal parts.
- Conduct routine maintenance and safety checks.
- Collaborate with other team members to complete projects on time and within budget.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience as a Boilermaker or in a similar role.
- Proficiency in reading blueprints, schematics, and technical manuals.
- Strong understanding of welding and metal fabrication techniques.
- Ability to work in confined spaces and at heights.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Physical stamina to work in temperatures of 25 degrees Celsius and the ability to lift heavy materials with mechanical assistance.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance plans.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- Collaborative and supportive work environment.
- Paid time off and holidays.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are invited to submit their resume and a brief cover letter outlining their relevant experience and qualifications.
