Permanent part-time opportunity based at Sydney Airport.

At Qantas, we represent Australia to the world. Our diverse country is known for our spirit, mateship, and a can-do attitude. We’re resilient and strong, and we work hard to keep things moving. We firmly believe that the sky is the limit. Demonstrate the right skill and attitude and we’ll place an incredible amount of trust in you to move on to bigger and better things.

Qantas Ground Services (QGS) is a wholly owned subsidiary of Qantas Airways and we’re responsible for providing ground handling services to the Qantas Freight, Australia’s largest independent air freight services business. You will work in all areas of the Freight department including Domestic and International, you will load and unload freight, operate forklifts, tugs and equipment on a day to day basis within OH&S guidelines. You’ll also operate communication aids and computer equipment as required, including the freight warehousing system.

You’ll have:
- A current and valid forklift license (essential requirement)
- Previous experience using a forklift ideally in a warehouse environment
- Recent experience in a manual handling or physical role, within a fast paced operational environment
- A passion for safety and working within OH&S guidelines at all times
- Ability to work effectively within a team
- A hard working nature and a positive ‘can do’ attitude
- The ability to repetitively lift items up to 32 kgs in weight and successfully pass a pre-employment medical
- The ability to work all shifts in our dynamic 24 hour, 7 day a week, rotational shift roster (meaning shifts will vary from week to week)

Please note: applications will only be considered for candidates who have the right to work in Australia/New Zealand without restrictions or sponsorship.
