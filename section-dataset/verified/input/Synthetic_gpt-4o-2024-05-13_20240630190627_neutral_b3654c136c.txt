Company: Jackson, Welch and Lara

Role: Safe Event Coordinator

Company Background:
Jackson, Welch and Lara is a leading event management company known for organizing memorable and safe events. We are committed to fostering a culture of inclusivity and are dedicated to promoting diversity and equity.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a meticulous and experienced Safe Event Coordinator to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for planning and executing events, ensuring all safety measures are in place, and coordinating with various stakeholders to deliver seamless experiences.

Responsibilities:
- Plan, organize, and execute events with a focus on safety and security.
- Collaborate with clients, vendors, and team members to ensure event success.
- Conduct risk assessments and develop emergency response plans.
- Monitor event activities to ensure compliance with safety protocols.
- Manage on-site logistics and troubleshoot any issues that arise.
- Provide excellent customer service and support to clients.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in Event Management or a related field.
- Proven experience in event coordination, with a strong focus on safety.
- Excellent negotiation and communication skills.
- Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
- Proficiency in event management software is a plus.

- Competitive salary ranging from $50,000 to $70,000 per year, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
