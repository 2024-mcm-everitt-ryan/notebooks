Company: Leonard PLC
Role: Senior Human Resources Specialist

About Us:
Leonard PLC is a leading global consultancy firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions to businesses worldwide. We pride ourselves on our diverse and inclusive work environment, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and grow.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced and talented Senior Human Resources Specialist to join our team. As a Senior Human Resources Specialist, you will be responsible for developing and implementing HR strategies that align with our business objectives. You will work closely with our management team to identify and address HR-related issues, develop policies and procedures, and ensure compliance with employment laws and regulations.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement HR strategies to support business objectives
 Collaborate with management to identify and address HR-related issues
 Develop and maintain policies and procedures to ensure compliance with employment laws and regulations
 Manage employee relations, including conflict resolution and performance management
 Coordinate training and development programs to enhance employee skills and knowledge
 Analyze HR data to identify trends and areas for improvement

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field
 At least 5 years of experience in HR, with a focus on strategy development and implementation
 Proven track record of success in managing HR projects and initiatives
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £50,000 to £70,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings plan, and paid time off. Our offices are located in a prime area, with easy access to public transportation and amenities.
