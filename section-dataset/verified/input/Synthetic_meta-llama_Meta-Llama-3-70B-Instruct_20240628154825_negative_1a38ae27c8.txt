Job Title: Water Use Inspector

Company Background:
Whitehead LLC is a leading environmental consulting firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions for water resource management. We pride ourselves on our expertise in water conservation and our commitment to sustainable practices.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Water Use Inspector to join our team. As a Water Use Inspector, you will be responsible for conducting on-site inspections to monitor and report on water usage patterns. Your attention to detail and analytical skills will be essential in identifying opportunities for water conservation and implementing effective solutions.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct on-site inspections of commercial and residential properties to monitor water usage patterns
 Collect and analyze data to identify areas of inefficiency and opportunities for conservation
 Develop and implement water conservation plans in collaboration with clients and internal teams
 Provide technical support and training to clients on water-saving practices and technologies
 Maintain accurate records and prepare detailed reports on inspection findings and recommendations

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Engineering, or related field
 Minimum 2 years of experience in water resource management or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Must have a valid driver's license and own transportation
 Must be able to climb stairs regularly throughout the day

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
