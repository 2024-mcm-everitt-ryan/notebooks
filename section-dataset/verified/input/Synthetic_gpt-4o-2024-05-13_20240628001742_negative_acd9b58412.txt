Company: Lawrence and Sons

Role: Mailroom Control Clerk

Company Background:
Lawrence and Sons is a leading logistics company with over 50 years of experience in delivering high-quality services. We are dedicated to creating a dynamic, forward-thinking, and innovative environment for our team.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
Lawrence and Sons is seeking an energetic and media-savvy Mailroom Control Clerk to join our team. This role is ideal for up-and-coming professionals who are ready to contribute to the fast-paced and modern work environment we cultivate. The successful candidate will be responsible for managing mailroom operations and ensuring efficient processing and distribution of mail and packages within the organization.

Responsibilities:
- Sort and distribute incoming mail and packages.
- Manage outgoing mail and packages, ensuring timely dispatch.
- Maintain accurate records of mailroom activities.
- Operate and maintain mailroom equipment.
- Collaborate with other departments to improve mailroom processes.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Recent college graduates preferred.
- Athletically inclined candidates are encouraged to apply.
- Strong organizational and multitasking skills.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
- Basic computer skills and familiarity with modern office equipment.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health insurance.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Paid vacation and sick leave.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £25,000 to £30,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Team-building activities and company events.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and cover letter to hr@lawrenceandsons.com. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis until the position is filled.
