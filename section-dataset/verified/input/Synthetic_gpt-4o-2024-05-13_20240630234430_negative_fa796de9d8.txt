Full-Time

Job Description:
We are looking for a police surgeon to join our esteemed team. This role is critical in providing medical expertise and support to police personnel. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in surgery and emergency medicine.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct medical examinations for police personnel.
- Provide immediate medical care in emergency situations.
- Support law enforcement operations with medical expertise.
- Maintain accurate medical records and documentation.
- Participate in training and educational sessions for police staff.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Medical degree with specialization in surgery.
- Valid medical license to practice.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in a similar role.
- Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to assert control in high-pressure situations.
- Previous experience working with law enforcement agencies is highly valued.

- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holiday leave.
- Opportunities for professional development and continuing education.
