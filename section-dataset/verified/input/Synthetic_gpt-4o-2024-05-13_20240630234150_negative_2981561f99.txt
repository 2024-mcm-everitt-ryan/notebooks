Company Background:
Cook, Kelley and Leach is a leading provider of quality child care services, committed to creating nurturing and safe environments for children to grow and thrive. Our organization prides itself on delivering exceptional care and educational programs tailored to the needs of each child.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Quality Child Care Manager to join our team. The ideal candidate will possess exceptional leadership skills and demonstrate the ability to manage multiple child care centers effectively. He should have a proven track record in overseeing daily operations, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards, and maintaining a high level of care and education.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee the daily operations of multiple child care centers.
- Ensure all centers comply with local, state, and federal regulations.
- Develop and implement educational programs that promote child development.
- Lead a team of child care professionals, providing guidance and support.
- Maintain strong relationships with parents and address their concerns promptly.
- Handle budgeting and financial management of the centers.
- Conduct regular performance evaluations of child care staff.
- Ensure the safety and security of all children in the centers.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Early Childhood Education, Child Development, or related field.
- Minimum of 5 years' experience in managing child care centers.
- Strong decision-making and problem-solving skills are essential.
- Ability to work under pressure and handle multiple tasks simultaneously.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- He should be physically fit and energetic, as the role may require active supervision of children.
