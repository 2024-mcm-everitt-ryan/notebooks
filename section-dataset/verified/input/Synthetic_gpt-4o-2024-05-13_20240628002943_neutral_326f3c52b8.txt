Company: Turner-Baker

Role: Senior Investment Analyst

Company Background:
Turner-Baker is a leading global financial services firm providing a wide range of investment banking, securities, wealth management, and investment management services. Our mission is to deliver exceptional financial solutions to our diverse client base, including individuals, corporations, and governments.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
Turner-Baker is seeking a Senior Investment Analyst to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will possess strong analytical skills, deep knowledge of financial markets, and the ability to provide insightful investment advice. This role is critical for driving our investment strategies and ensuring that our clients receive the best possible financial guidance.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct comprehensive financial analysis and research on potential investment opportunities.
- Develop financial models to support investment decisions.
- Monitor and assess the performance of investment portfolios.
- Prepare detailed investment reports and presentations for clients.
- Collaborate with other team members to develop and implement investment strategies.
- Stay abreast of market trends and economic developments.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Economics, or related field; Master’s degree preferred.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in investment analysis or a related field.
- Strong proficiency in financial modeling and analysis tools.
- Excellent communication and presentation skills.
- Ability to work collaboratively in a team-oriented environment.
- Professional certification (e.g., CFA) is a plus.
