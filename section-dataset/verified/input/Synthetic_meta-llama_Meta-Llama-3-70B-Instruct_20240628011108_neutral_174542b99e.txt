Company: Jordan PLC

Job Title: Computer Specialist

About Us:
Jordan PLC is a leading technology firm that values diversity, equity, and inclusion in the workplace. Our team is committed to upholding inclusive practices, and we welcome individuals from all backgrounds to join us.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Computer Specialist to join our IT department. As a Computer Specialist, you will be responsible for installing, configuring, and maintaining computer systems and networks. You will also provide technical support to our employees, troubleshoot issues, and develop solutions to improve our systems.

Responsibilities:

 Install, configure, and maintain computer systems and networks
 Provide technical support to employees
 Troubleshoot issues and develop solutions to improve systems
 Collaborate with the IT team to implement new technologies
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
 2+ years of experience in IT or a related field
 Strong knowledge of computer systems, networks, and software applications
 Excellent problem-solving and communication skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off. Our company benefits extend to all spouses and partners.

Our team is committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere, and we encourage candidates from all backgrounds to apply.
