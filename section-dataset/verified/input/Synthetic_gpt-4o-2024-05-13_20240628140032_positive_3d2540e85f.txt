Job Title:
Computer Hardware Engineer

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented Computer Hardware Engineer to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will work on the design, development, and testing of computer hardware systems to meet the company's high standards of excellence.

Responsibilities:
- Design and develop computer hardware components.
- Test and debug hardware systems to ensure optimal performance.
- Collaborate with software engineers to integrate hardware and software functionality.
- Stay updated with the latest technological advancements in computer hardware.
- Prepare detailed documentation and reports on project status and hardware performance.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
- Proven experience in hardware design and development.
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
- Commitment to fostering an inclusive work environment.
