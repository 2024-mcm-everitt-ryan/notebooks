Company: Burns-Spencer
Role: Employee Benefits Coordinator

Job Type: Full-time

About Us:
Burns-Spencer is a leading provider of employee benefits solutions, dedicated to helping companies create a positive work environment. We are a fast-paced and dynamic team seeking a highly motivated Employee Benefits Coordinator to join our team.

Job Description:
We are looking for an experienced and detail-oriented individual to assist in the administration of employee benefits programs. The successful candidate will have excellent organizational skills, strong analytical skills, and the ability to work independently.

Responsibilities:

 Assist in the administration of employee benefits programs, including health, dental, and vision plans
 Coordinate employee communications and ensure compliance with company policies
 Maintain accurate and timely records of employee benefits data
 Provide exceptional customer service to employees and management
 Perform other related duties as required

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience in employee benefits administration or a related field
 Strong analytical skills and attention to detail
 Ability to work independently and meet tight deadlines
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Post-secondary education in business administration, human resources, or a related field

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 55,000 to USD 65,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision plans, as well as a retirement matching program.

Note: We require applicants to be able to work in a fast-paced environment and meet tight deadlines.
