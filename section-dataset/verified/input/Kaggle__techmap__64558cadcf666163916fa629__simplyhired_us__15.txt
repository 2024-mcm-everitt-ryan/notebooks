Join the claims team of a successful A-rated company. Service the Wisconsin counties of Waupaca, Winnebago, Outagamie, Shawano, and Menomonie. Work out of your home. You will be provided with a company vehicle.

Compensation:  
$60,000-$80,000/yr based on experience plus company-provided vehicle.

Responsibilities:  
- Verify coverage on newly assigned claims.  
- Establish and monitor reserves throughout the life of the claim.  
- Investigate claims.  
- Evaluate damage.  
- Ensure that proper documentation exists for the recovery process (subrogation, salvage, etc).  
- Settle claims through negotiation.  
- Provide prompt claims service to claimants.  
- Provide support to CAT teams (when necessary).  
- Perform other duties as assigned.

Qualifications:  
- Three to five years of multi-line property and casualty claims experience.  
- Bachelor’s degree in business administration, risk management & insurance or related discipline or equivalent work experience required.  
- Property claims experience is a plus.  
- Outstanding oral and written communication skills.  
- Ability to operate a personal computer.  
- Knowledge of auto physical damage, medical terminology, and the ability to evaluate injuries is helpful.  
- Must possess a valid driver’s license.  
- Ability to perform the physical requirements of the job such as climbing ladders, walking roofs, working inside collapsed/burnt structures, walking uneven terrain, etc.  
- Bilingual abilities (Spanish, Hmong, etc.) are a plus.
