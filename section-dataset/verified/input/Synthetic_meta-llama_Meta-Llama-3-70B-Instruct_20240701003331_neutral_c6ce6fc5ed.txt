Standards Specialist at Sullivan Group

Company Background: 
Sullivan Group is a leading consulting firm that provides expertise in quality management and process improvement. We are committed to delivering exceptional services that meet the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficiency.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Standards Specialist to join our team. As a Standards Specialist, you will be responsible for developing, implementing, and maintaining quality standards and procedures to ensure compliance with industry regulations and best practices.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and maintain quality standards, procedures, and guidelines to ensure compliance with industry regulations and best practices
 Conduct audits and assessments to identify areas for improvement and implement corrective actions
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure alignment and consistency in quality standards and procedures
 Provide training and guidance to employees on quality standards and procedures
 Analyze data and metrics to identify trends and areas for improvement

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g., engineering, quality management)
 At least 3 years of experience in quality management or a related field
 Proven track record of developing and implementing quality standards and procedures
 Excellent analytical, communication, and problem-solving skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
