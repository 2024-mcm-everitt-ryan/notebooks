Company Name: Roach and Sons
Job Type: Full-Time
Role: Supervising Housing Groundskeeper
Description:
Roach and Sons is looking for a dedicated Supervising Housing Groundskeeper to join our team. We are committed to maintaining a high standard for our properties and ensuring a safe, welcoming environment for all residents. This role involves overseeing the upkeep of housing grounds, managing a team of groundskeepers, and collaborating with other departments to ensure a well-maintained property.
Responsibilities:
- Supervise and coordinate activities of groundskeeping staff.
- Ensure all grounds are maintained to a high standard.
- Conduct regular inspections of the grounds and address any issues.
- Collaborate with other departments to ensure the property is safe and welcoming.
- Handle administrative tasks such as scheduling and budgeting for the groundskeeping department.
Qualifications:
- Proven experience in groundskeeping or a related field.
- Strong leadership and organizational skills.
- Ability to work collaboratively with diverse teams.
- Creativity and problem-solving skills.
- A background in public relations is a plus.
Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $40,000 to $55,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.
