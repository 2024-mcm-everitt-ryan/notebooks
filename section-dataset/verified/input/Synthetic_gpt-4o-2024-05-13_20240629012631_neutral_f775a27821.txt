Company: Edwards-Russo  
Role: Senior Cook  

Company Background:  
Edwards-Russo is a renowned hospitality group committed to providing exceptional dining experiences through unique culinary creations. We pride ourselves on our inclusive and diverse workplace, encouraging all team members to grow and excel.

Job Type:  
Full-time

Job Description and Responsibilities:  
We are seeking a proactive and self-motivated Senior Cook to join our culinary team. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience in kitchen management and food preparation. Responsibilities include:

- Overseeing kitchen operations and ensuring high culinary standards.
- Training and mentoring junior kitchen staff.
- Collaborating with the head chef on menu planning and development.
- Ensuring compliance with health and safety regulations.
- Managing inventory and ordering supplies efficiently.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Proven experience in a senior cook or similar role.
- Culinary degree or equivalent work experience in skilled trades.
- Ability to remain calm under pressure.
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Excellent communication and organizational abilities.
- A positive and professional attitude.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive pay: The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and flexible working hours.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Employee discount on dining experiences.

Edwards-Russo is committed to fair and equitable pay practices. This role offers a dynamic and supportive environment for individuals passionate about culinary arts.

How to Apply:  
Interested candidates should submit their resume and a cover letter detailing their relevant experience and why they are a great fit for this role.
