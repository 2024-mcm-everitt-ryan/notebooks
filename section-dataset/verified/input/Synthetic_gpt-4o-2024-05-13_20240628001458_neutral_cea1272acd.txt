Company: Hall, May and Blanchard

Role: Juvenile Implementation Manager

Company Background:
Hall, May and Blanchard is a leading consultancy firm specializing in juvenile justice reform. We partner with governments, NGOs, and community organizations to develop and implement effective juvenile justice policies and programs.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a Juvenile Implementation Manager to oversee the implementation of juvenile justice programs. The successful candidate will work closely with stakeholders to ensure programs are executed efficiently and meet the intended outcomes. This role requires strong project management skills, an understanding of juvenile justice, and the ability to engage with diverse community groups.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and manage detailed implementation plans for juvenile justice programs.
- Coordinate with stakeholders including government agencies, NGOs, and community organizations.
- Monitor and report on program progress, making adjustments as necessary.
- Provide training and support to program staff.
- Ensure compliance with all relevant regulations and standards.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Must have a relevant qualification in juvenile justice, social work, or a related field.
- Proven experience in project management and program implementation.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Familiarity with juvenile justice policies and practices is preferred.
- All qualified individuals welcome.

Perks and Benefits:
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday leave.
- Professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Retirement savings plan with employer match.

Interested candidates are encouraged to apply by submitting their resume and a cover letter outlining their qualifications and experience.
