Position Description

The mission of our Pregnant Girls Services (PGS) is to provide a safe place where pregnant girls in crisis are embraced, equipped, and empowered for a hopeful future. PGS provides pregnant teens with the tools and comprehensive programs to break the generational cycle of abuse and neglect. We know from our history that the more time we are able to spend with the girls ahead of the birth of their child, the more successful they are in transitioning back to the community.

The Manager, PGS, is responsible for creating, innovating, and managing the newly developed service framework. He/she will lead, build, and develop a core team of PGS professionals. The Manager will partner with other service teams, Community Education, and Volunteer Engagement in developing strategies, operational plans, and metrics to advance the programs in a more effective and sustainable way.

Key Responsibilities

- Provide strategic leadership in implementing the enhanced PGS framework to guide the high-quality crisis service.
- Collaborate with academic partners to develop evidence-based tools and an evaluation system to upgrade the standard and effectiveness of the programs as well as measuring the impact.
- Together with the Child Protection Manager, develop an advocacy-based approach to case management for the youth clients.
- Model and demonstrate the best practices in case management in youth clients in order to influence our peers and partners such as Social Welfare Department and other NGOs.
- Lead and oversee the implementation and evaluation of the case management system.
- With support from Community Education, ensure the whole PGS team are equipped and empowered with sexuality education and child protection training to uphold the standards and values of Mothers Choice.
- Lead, coach, develop, and retain a high-performing team.
- Support the development of pilot programs (e.g. Safe Families) which support PGS clients.
- Support cross-services relationship mapping for community partners.
- Support Leadership and Development in developing Professional Competency Training.
- Oversee the annual operations plan and budget.
