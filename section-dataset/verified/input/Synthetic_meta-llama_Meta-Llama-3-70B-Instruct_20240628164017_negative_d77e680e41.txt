Company: Mcguire, Escobar and Williams
Role: Administrative Assistant

Job Description:

We are seeking an Administrative Assistant to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be highly organized, efficient, and able to work effectively in a fast-paced environment. As an Administrative Assistant, you will be responsible for providing administrative support to our executives, preparing meeting materials, and maintaining accurate records.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to executives, including preparing correspondence, reports, and presentations
 Coordinate travel arrangements and schedule meetings
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records and files
 Prepare meeting materials, including agendas, minutes, and handouts
 Perform other administrative tasks as needed

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as an Administrative Assistant
 High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree preferred
 Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite
 Excellent organizational, communication, and time management skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize tasks effectively
 Must be able to lift up to 25 pounds and stand for long periods of time
 Must be able to attend meetings in person and participate in video conferences without accommodations

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Retirement plan with company match
 Paid time off and holidays
