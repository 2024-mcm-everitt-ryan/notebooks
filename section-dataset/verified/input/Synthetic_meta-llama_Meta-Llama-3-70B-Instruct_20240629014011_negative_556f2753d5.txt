Company: Powell Group

Job Title: Press Officer

About Us:
The Powell Group is a leading communications firm that specializes in crisis management and media relations. We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Press Officer to join our team.

Job Description:
We are looking for a Press Officer who can effectively manage our client's media presence and craft compelling press releases that grab attention. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of the media landscape and be able to think strategically.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement media strategies to secure coverage for our clients
 Write and distribute press releases, media alerts, and other materials to the press
 Build and maintain relationships with key media contacts
 Monitor and report on media coverage of our clients
 Collaborate with the account team to develop messaging and pitching strategies

Qualifications and Experience:
 3+ years of experience in media relations or a related field
 Bachelor's degree in Communications, Journalism, or a related field
 Proven track record of securing media coverage for clients
 Excellent writing and communication skills
 Ability to work well under pressure and meet deadlines
 Must be able to work a standard 9-to-5 schedule with minimal flexibility

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $75,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
