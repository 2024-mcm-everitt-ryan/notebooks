Company: Norman-Mendez

Role: Agricultural Technician

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and dedicated Agricultural Technician to join our team at Norman-Mendez. As an Agricultural Technician, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient operation of our agricultural facilities and contributing to the success of our crops.

Responsibilities:

 Assist in the daily maintenance of agricultural equipment and facilities
 Monitor and record crop health, growth, and development
 Collaborate with our team of experts to develop and implement effective crop management strategies
 Participate in quality control and assurance activities to ensure high-quality crop production

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in agricultural production or a related field
 Strong knowledge of crop management practices and agricultural equipment operation
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance and retirement benefits
 Opportunities for professional growth and development within our company
