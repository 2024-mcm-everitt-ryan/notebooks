Company: Fields-Simmons

Role: Junior Public Health Nurse

About Us:
Fields-Simmons is a leading healthcare organization dedicated to promoting health and wellness in our communities. We believe in fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment that values the unique perspectives and talents of our employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and compassionate Junior Public Health Nurse to join our team. As a Junior Public Health Nurse, you will work closely with our healthcare professionals to provide exceptional patient care and education. Your responsibilities will include conducting health assessments, developing treatment plans, and collaborating with community partners to promote health initiatives.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct health assessments and develop treatment plans for patients
 Collaborate with healthcare professionals to provide exceptional patient care
 Develop and implement health education programs for the community
 Work with community partners to promote health initiatives and services

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Nursing or related field
 Current registered nurse license
 1-2 years of experience in public health nursing
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan. We also offer a generous paid time-off policy and opportunities for professional development and growth.

Our Team:
Our team is made up of individuals from a variety of backgrounds and identities. We believe that diversity and inclusion are essential to providing exceptional patient care and promoting health equity. We encourage applications from candidates who share our values and are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive work environment.
