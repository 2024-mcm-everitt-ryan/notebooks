Internship

Job Description:
We are seeking a motivated Intergovernmental Affairs Intern to join our dynamic team. This internship offers an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in public policy, governmental relations, and strategic communications. The ideal candidate will have a strong interest in government affairs and policy development.

Responsibilities:
- Assisting in the research and analysis of policy issues.
- Supporting the preparation of reports, briefs, and presentations.
- Attending and summarizing key meetings and events.
- Helping coordinate intergovernmental initiatives and outreach programs.
- Assisting with project management and administrative tasks as necessary.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in political science, public policy, or a related field.
- Excellent research, writing, and analytical skills.
- Strong organizational and time management abilities.
- Effective communication skills, both written and verbal.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
