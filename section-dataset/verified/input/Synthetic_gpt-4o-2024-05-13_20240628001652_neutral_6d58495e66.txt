Job Description:
We are looking for a Permit Records Assistant to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for maintaining permit records, ensuring data accuracy, and assisting with compliance documentation. This role requires meticulous attention to detail and a high level of organizational skills.

Responsibilities:
- Maintain and update permit records in compliance with company and regulatory standards.
- Ensure data accuracy and integrity within the records management system.
- Assist with preparing and filing compliance documentation.
- Coordinate with various departments to gather and verify necessary information.
- Respond to internal and external inquiries regarding permit records.

Qualifications:
- Proven proficiency in record-keeping and data management.
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
- Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Familiarity with compliance standards and regulatory requirements is a plus.
