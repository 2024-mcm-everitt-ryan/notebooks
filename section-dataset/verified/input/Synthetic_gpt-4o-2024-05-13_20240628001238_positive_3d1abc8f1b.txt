Company Background:
Hodge-Hardy is a renowned name in the jewelry industry, celebrated for its exquisite craftsmanship and innovative designs. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and dynamic workplace where creativity thrives, and diverse perspectives are valued.

Job Type:
Full-Time Jeweler

Job Description:
Hodge-Hardy is seeking a talented and passionate jeweler to join our team. The ideal candidate will have exceptional skills in jewelry design, repair, and customer service, contributing to our legacy of excellence.

Responsibilities:
- Design and create unique jewelry pieces.
- Repair and restore jewelry items.
- Provide exceptional customer service, assisting clients with their needs.
- Maintain a clean and organized workspace.
- Collaborate with team members to bring innovative ideas to life.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a jeweler or similar role.
- Proficiency in jewelry-making tools and techniques.
- Strong attention to detail and creativity.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000 based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Inclusive workplace fostering respect and collaboration.
- We actively seek to recruit LGBTQ+ individuals to enhance our diversity efforts.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their resume, cover letter, and portfolio of previous work. At Hodge-Hardy, we specifically value the rich experiences of LGBTQ+ individuals and actively welcome their applications to enhance our team.
