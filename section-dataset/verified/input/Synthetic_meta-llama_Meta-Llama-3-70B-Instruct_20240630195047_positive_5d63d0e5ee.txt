Job Title: Maintenance Worker
Company: Hines, Kirk and Soto

About Us:
Hines, Kirk and Soto is a leading facilities management company dedicated to providing exceptional services to our clients. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Maintenance Worker to join our team. As a Maintenance Worker, you will be responsible for performing routine maintenance tasks, repairs, and renovations to ensure our facilities are safe, efficient, and well-maintained.

Responsibilities:

 Perform routine maintenance tasks, including plumbing, electrical, and carpentry work
 Conduct repairs and renovations as needed
 Collaborate with other team members to ensure timely completion of projects
 Maintain accurate records of maintenance activities and repairs

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in maintenance or a related field
 Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Valid driver's license and willingness to travel to different locations

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.

Why Join Our Team:
Our company is dedicated to supporting women's careers and increasing female representation in the industry. We believe that a diverse and inclusive workplace is essential to our success, and we are committed to creating opportunities for women to grow and develop in their careers.
