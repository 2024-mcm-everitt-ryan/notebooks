Description

Reporting directly to the Head of Regulatory Affairs, the Licensing Specialist serves as primary contact, coordinator and oversees licensing applications, maintenance/updating, and renewals for Travel Insurance (TI) throughout the US for all of the US affiliate companies. This person will serve as a strategic internal business partner as a subject matter resource for applicable regulations and requirements.

Principal Duties and Responsibilities:
- Manages the P&C licensing process for Travel Insurance (includes renewals, reports, needed information, and enrolling, tracking, maintaining classes and continuing education for employees).
- Maintains an updated database of licenses.
- Provides reporting support regarding licensing related data.
- Establish and maintain accurate and organized files, records and documentation.
- Deal discreetly with confidential information.
- Maintains culture of regulatory compliance.
- Other duties as assigned.

Required / Desired Knowledge, Experiences and Skills:
- 3-5 years of experience with P&C licensing.
- Ability to handle different and unexpected issues in a calm, decisive manner.
- Ability to develop and implement new solutions in handling unusual issues.
- Ability to analyze data, prepare and draft reports and related correspondence.
- Ability to successfully execute duties with general instruction and minimal guidance.
- Able to communicate effectively both in verbal and written communications.
- Self-starter with attention to detail.
- Superior customer service skills.
- Able to multi-task with changing priorities.
- Experience in an office or insurance setting is helpful.
- Experience with Microsoft Office Suite is a plus.
