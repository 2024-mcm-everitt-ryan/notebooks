Company: Montes, Banks and Foster
Role: Loan Counselor

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Loan Counselor to join our team. As a Loan Counselor, you will be responsible for working closely with clients to understand their financial needs and provide personalized loan solutions.

Responsibilities:
- Meet with clients to discuss loan options and financial goals
- Analyze client financial data to determine loan eligibility
- Develop and present personalized loan plans to clients
- Maintain accurate records of client interactions and loan applications
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless client experience

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Business, or related field
- 2+ years of experience in loan counseling or related field
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
- Familiarity with local slang and idioms is required

Benefits:
- Salary range: $50,000 - $70,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
