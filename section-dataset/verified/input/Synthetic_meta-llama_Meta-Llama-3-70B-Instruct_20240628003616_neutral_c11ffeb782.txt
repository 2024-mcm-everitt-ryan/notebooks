Company: Miles-Johnson
Role: Butcher

At Miles-Johnson, we're committed to providing our customers with the highest quality meats and exceptional customer service. As a Butcher, you will play a crucial role in our team by ensuring that our products are cut, prepared, and presented to the highest standards.

Job Description:

 Cut, trim, and prepare meat products for display and sale
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace, adhering to safety and sanitation standards
 Provide exceptional customer service, answering questions and making recommendations
 Collaborate with the team to achieve sales goals and promote products

Responsibilities and Qualifications:

 2+ years of experience in a butcher shop or meat department
 Strong knowledge of meat cuts, preparation, and presentation
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and stand for long periods

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental insurance
 401(k) matching program
 Paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 We support all gender identities and orientations, and provide a welcoming and inclusive work environment.
