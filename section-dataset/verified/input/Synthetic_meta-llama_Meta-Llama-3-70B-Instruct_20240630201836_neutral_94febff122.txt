Company: Lawson LLC

Job Title: Senior Project Analyst

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Senior Project Analyst to join our team. As a Senior Project Analyst, you will be responsible for leading cross-functional teams, developing project plans, and ensuring timely project delivery. You will work closely with stakeholders to identify business needs and develop solutions to drive business growth.

Responsibilities:

 Lead project teams to deliver high-quality results
 Develop and manage project plans, timelines, and budgets
 Collaborate with stakeholders to identify business needs and develop solutions
 Analyze project data to identify trends and areas for improvement
 Develop and mentor team members to improve project delivery

Qualifications and Experience:
 5+ years of experience in project management or a related field
 Proven track record of delivering high-quality projects on time and within budget
 Strong leadership and team management skills
 Excellent communication and analytical skills
 Experience in training and development is preferred
 Candidates should have experience in team leadership

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 80,000 to USD 110,000, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off. We also offer opportunities for professional growth and development, and a dynamic work environment that values diversity and inclusion.
