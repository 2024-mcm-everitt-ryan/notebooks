Description

Brandt is currently seeking a Loader for our Regina, SK location. This position will require mainly working outdoors.

Duties & Responsibilities:
- Working in teams to load trucks and sea containers with finished goods.
- Inspecting crates and bundles for damage during the loading process.
- Assist in counting of inventory as required.
- Keeping area clean and in order at all times.
- Other duties as assigned.

Must have the ability to perform work-related duties outside in a safe and competent manner in adverse conditions, extreme heat/cold/slippery conditions. Must be capable of working under minimum supervision. Must be capable of operating all yard equipment in a safe and efficient manner. Must be physically fit to manually handle all goods, steel, and products as required. Must have good positive communication skills and good attendance.
