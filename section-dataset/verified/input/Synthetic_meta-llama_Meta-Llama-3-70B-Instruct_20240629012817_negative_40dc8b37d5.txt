Company Background:
Green-Duncan is a leading manufacturer of steel products, dedicated to delivering high-quality solutions to our clients.

Job Type:
Welder

Job Description:
We're seeking a skilled Welder to join our team. As a Welder at Green-Duncan, you will be responsible for welding parts together to create a strong bond, inspecting welds, and maintaining a clean workspace.

Responsibilities:

 Welding parts together using various techniques
 Inspecting welds for defects and imperfections
 Maintaining a clean and organized workspace
 Collaborating with other team members to meet project deadlines

Qualifications and Experience Needed:

 2+ years of welding experience
 High school diploma or equivalent required; vocational training or certification in welding a plus
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
