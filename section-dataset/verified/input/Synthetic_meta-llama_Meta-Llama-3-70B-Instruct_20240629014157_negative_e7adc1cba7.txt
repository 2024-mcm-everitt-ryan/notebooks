Company: Stone-Rodriguez

Job Type: Paramedic

Job Description:
We're seeking a skilled Paramedic to join our team at Stone-Rodriguez. As a Paramedic, you will be responsible for providing emergency medical care to patients in a fast-paced environment. 

Responsibilities:
- Respond to emergency calls and provide medical assistance to patients
- Conduct patient assessments and develop treatment plans
- Collaborate with other healthcare professionals to ensure quality patient care
- Maintain accurate records of patient interactions and treatment plans

Qualifications and Experience:
- 2+ years of experience as a Paramedic
- Valid certification as a Paramedic (EMT-P)
- High school diploma or equivalent required; bachelor's degree in a related field preferred
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and make quick decisions

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $55,000 to $70,000 per year, based on experience and skills. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. Our benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.

Note: Applicants must be willing to adhere to our company's Code of Conduct, which includes upholding a drug-free workplace.
