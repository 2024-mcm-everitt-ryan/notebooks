Company: Patel Inc

Job Title: Senior Sales Staff/Fit Specialist

Patel Inc is a leading retailer of athletic wear and equipment, dedicated to providing high-quality products and exceptional customer service.

- Participate in visual merchandising and store displays

Qualifications:
- 2+ years of experience in retail sales or a related field
- Advanced knowledge of athletic wear and equipment
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
- Skilled in French (an asset)
