Good old kiwi ingenuity, a great team culture, and a relentless commitment to preserve New Zealand's natural beauty and resources, EnviroNZ ensure we produce the best possible outcomes for both our customers and the environment. We are looking for a customer-focussed candidate to join our North Shore Transfer station team as Points Person/Traffic Controller. This is a full-time, customer-facing role, with 40 hours and includes rostered weekends.

What you'll be doing:
- Working as part of a successful & close-knit team, you will be responsible for:
  - Communicating clearly with all customers and directing all traffic on-site to ease traffic flow.
  - Being mindful of hazards, and ensuring all employees & visitors are safe.
  - Assisting customers on where to offload safely.
  - General site maintenance which includes tidying up site & common areas.
  - Growing your own career within a thriving organisation.

Above all, our aim is to create a safe site for all visitors & customers, and continue to provide great customer service.

You will have:
- 2 years face-to-face customer service experience.
- Previous traffic control experience is preferred, however full training will be provided.
- A can-do attitude, physically fit and enjoy working outside.
- Have working knowledge and understanding of Health & Safety, and the importance of.
- Be Supportive and Respectful.
