Company: Benton Group
Role: Employee Benefits Coordinator

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and nurturing individual to join our team as an Employee Benefits Coordinator. As a key member of our HR department, she will be responsible for developing and maintaining warm relationships with our clients, ensuring seamless benefits administration, and providing exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and maintain client relationships through regular communication and benefits education
 Coordinate benefits enrollment, changes, and terminations
 Analyze and resolve benefits-related issues
 Collaborate with internal teams to ensure compliance with benefits policies and procedures

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in benefits administration or a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities
 Proficiency in benefits software and systems

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.

If you are a caring and detail-oriented individual looking for a new challenge, we encourage you to apply!
