Company: Wilson Group

Job Type: Service Desk Manager

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced and talented Service Desk Manager to lead our team in providing exceptional customer service and technical support to our clients. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in IT service management, excellent communication skills, and the ability to motivate and guide a team of service desk analysts.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and lead a team of service desk analysts to provide timely and effective technical support to clients
 Develop and implement service desk processes and procedures to ensure high-quality service delivery
 Collaborate with other teams to resolve complex technical issues and improve overall customer experience
 Analyze service desk metrics and develop strategies to improve efficiency and reduce resolution time
 Ensure compliance with IT service management frameworks and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in IT service management, with a focus on service desk operations
 Strong knowledge of ITIL framework and service management best practices
 Excellent leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field
