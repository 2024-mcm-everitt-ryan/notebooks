Company: Allen, Bryant and Dyer

Job Title: Registered Nurse

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and dedicated Registered Nurse to join our team. As a Registered Nurse at Allen, Bryant and Dyer, you will be responsible for providing high-quality patient care and working collaboratively with our healthcare team. If you have a strong work ethic and are able to work effectively in a fast-paced environment, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Provide direct patient care and develop individualized care plans
 Collaborate with healthcare team to achieve patient goals
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date patient records
 Participate in quality improvement initiatives

Qualifications and Experience:

 Current registration with the relevant nursing authority
 2+ years of experience in a healthcare setting
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment
 Reliable transportation to and from work (note: we do not provide transportation reimbursement)

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 60,000 to USD 80,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
