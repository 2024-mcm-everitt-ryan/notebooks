About the company:
For over 35 years, Simplicity Funerals has been giving Australian families a simple, practical, affordable yet respectful alternative to the services offered by other funeral providers. The first Simplicity funeral home opened in 1979 with the vision of giving families real choice in funeral services through simple funeral services made easy, and at affordable prices. To this day, we remain true to this vision.
