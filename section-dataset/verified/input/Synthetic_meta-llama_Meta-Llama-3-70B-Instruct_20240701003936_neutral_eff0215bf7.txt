Company: Mason Group

Role: Social Worker

Job Description:
The Mason Group is seeking a compassionate and skilled Social Worker to join our team. As a Social Worker, you will work closely with our clients to provide emotional support, connect them with resources, and develop personalized plans to improve their well-being. Your empathetic nature and ability to build strong relationships will be essential in helping our clients overcome challenges and achieve their goals.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct initial assessments and develop individualized plans for clients
 Provide counseling and support to clients, either individually or in groups
 Connect clients with community resources, services, and programs
 Collaborate with other healthcare professionals to ensure comprehensive care
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records of client interactions

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Social Work or a related field
 2+ years of experience in social work or a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
 Familiarity with case management software and electronic records

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program.
