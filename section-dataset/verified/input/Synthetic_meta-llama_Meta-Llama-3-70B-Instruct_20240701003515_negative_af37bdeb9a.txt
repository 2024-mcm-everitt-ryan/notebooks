Company: West Inc

Job Title: Roofer

Job Description:
We're seeking a skilled Roofer to join our team at West Inc. As a Roofer, you will be responsible for installing, repairing, and replacing roofs on commercial and residential buildings. If you're a driven individual who can work at heights and handle physically demanding tasks, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities:

 Install, repair, and replace roofs on commercial and residential buildings
 Work safely at heights, using appropriate equipment and safety protocols
 Manage your time effectively to meet project deadlines
 Collaborate with other team members to achieve project goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Roofer or in a related trade
 Ability to lift 50 pounds (unassisted) and work at heights
 Valid driver's license and a clean driving record

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary, ranging from $55,000 to $75,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. You'll also receive comprehensive health insurance, paid time off, and opportunities for professional development and advancement.
