Company: Christensen-Christensen

Job Title: Maintenance Worker

About Us:
Christensen-Christensen is a leading provider of innovative solutions, dedicated to creating a safe and inclusive work environment. We are committed to fostering a culture of respect, empathy, and understanding.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Maintenance Worker to join our team. As a Maintenance Worker, you will be responsible for performing routine maintenance tasks, repairs, and upkeep of our facilities. Your attention to detail, problem-solving skills, and ability to work independently will make you a valuable asset to our team.

Responsibilities:

 Perform routine maintenance tasks, including plumbing, electrical, and carpentry work
 Conduct repairs and upkeep of facilities, including HVAC and mechanical systems
 Collaborate with other departments to ensure efficient and effective maintenance operations
 Maintain accurate records of maintenance activities and reports

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience in maintenance or a related field
 Strong knowledge of plumbing, electrical, and carpentry principles
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and work at heights
 High school diploma or equivalent required

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program. We also offer a generous paid time-off policy, including vacation, sick leave, and holidays.

Our office includes Nursing Rooms for all nursing needs, and we support and celebrate all forms of family structures. We are dedicated to creating an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive.
