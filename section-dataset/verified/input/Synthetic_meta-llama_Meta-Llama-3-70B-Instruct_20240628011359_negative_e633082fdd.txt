Company: May, Palmer and Green

Job Title: Outreach Intern

Job Type: Internship

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and enthusiastic Outreach Intern to join our team at May, Palmer and Green. As an Outreach Intern, you will be responsible for building and maintaining relationships with our community partners, assisting in event planning, and supporting our marketing efforts.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement outreach strategies to engage with community partners
 Assist in planning and executing events, including logistics and promotion
 Support the marketing team in creating promotional materials and social media content
 Build and maintain relationships with community partners and stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 Currently enrolled in a degree program in a related field (e.g. communications, marketing, public relations)
 1-2 years of experience in outreach, event planning, or marketing
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $40,000 to $50,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our benefits package includes health insurance for you and your spouse (husband or wife), paid time off, and opportunities for professional development. We also provide
mother-friendly spaces.
