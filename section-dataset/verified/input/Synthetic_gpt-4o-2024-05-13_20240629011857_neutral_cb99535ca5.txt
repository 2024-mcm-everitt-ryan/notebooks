Company: Hines Inc.

Role: Ship Engineer

Company Background:
Hines Inc. is a leading maritime solutions provider, dedicated to ensuring the highest standards in nautical engineering and vessel maintenance. We pride ourselves on our commitment to innovation, safety, and inclusivity within our workforce.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and skilled Ship Engineer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for maintaining and operating the mechanical systems on our fleet of vessels. This role requires a strong understanding of marine engineering principles and the ability to work effectively under pressure.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee the operation and maintenance of ship machinery and systems.
- Perform routine inspections and preventative maintenance.
- Troubleshoot mechanical issues and execute repairs in a timely manner.
- Collaborate with other crew members to ensure the vessel's safe and efficient operation.
- Maintain accurate logs and records of all activities.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Marine Engineering or a related field.
- Prior experience in hands-on roles in maritime or mechanical engineering.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
- Meet all legal requirements for employment.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $70,000 to $100,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance plans.
- Paid time off and flexible working hours.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
