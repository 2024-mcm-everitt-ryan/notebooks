Company: Brown-Jackson

Job Title: Stonemason

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Stonemason to join our team at Brown-Jackson. As a Stonemason, you will be responsible for constructing, repairing, and maintaining structures and monuments using stone, brick, and other masonry materials. Your attention to detail and ability to work safely will ensure high-quality results and a safe working environment.

Responsibilities:

 Construct and repair structures using stone, brick, and other masonry materials
 Interpret architectural drawings and specifications to plan and execute projects
 Ensure compliance with safety protocols and regulations
 Collaborate with other construction professionals to achieve project goals
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Stonemason or in a related field
 Strong understanding of masonry techniques and materials
 Ability to lift 50 pounds (with reasonable accommodations made) and work at heights
 Valid driver's license (optional)

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
