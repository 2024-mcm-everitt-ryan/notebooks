Data Research Analyst

Company: Montoya, Reyes and Sanchez

Job Type: Full-time

About Us: Montoya, Reyes and Sanchez is a leading market research firm dedicated to providing accurate and reliable data insights to our clients. We are committed to fostering diversity at all levels and promoting a culture of inclusivity and respect.

Job Description:

As a Data Research Analyst, you will play a critical role in conducting market research, analyzing data, and providing actionable insights to our clients. Your strong analytical skills, attention to detail, and excellent communication skills will enable you to excel in this role.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct market research to identify trends, opportunities, and challenges
 Analyze and interpret large datasets to extract meaningful insights
 Develop and present reports to clients and internal stakeholders
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate insights into business strategies
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging methodologies

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field (e.g., mathematics, statistics, computer science)
 2+ years of experience in data analysis or market research
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and presentation skills
 Experience with data visualization tools and statistical software (e.g., R, Python, Tableau)
