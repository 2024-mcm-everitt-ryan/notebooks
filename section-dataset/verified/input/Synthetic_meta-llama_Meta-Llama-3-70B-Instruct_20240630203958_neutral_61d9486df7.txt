Company: Leon-Chapman
Role: Auto Machinist

About Us:
Leon-Chapman is a leading manufacturer of automotive parts, dedicated to delivering high-quality products and exceptional customer service. We believe that a diverse and inclusive workforce is key to driving innovation and creativity.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Auto Machinist to join our team. As an Auto Machinist, you will be responsible for operating and maintaining machinery, fabricating parts, and ensuring quality control. If you have a strong analytical mind, excellent problem-solving skills, and a passion for precision, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities:

 Operate and maintain machinery, including lathes, milling machines, and grinders
 Fabricate parts according to blueprints and specifications
 Conduct quality control checks to ensure precision and accuracy
 Collaborate with team members to improve processes and efficiency
 Develop and implement new techniques to improve productivity

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a machining or manufacturing environment
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment
 Experience in customer relations is preferred

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 45,000 to USD 60,000, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.

At Leon-Chapman, we foster an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive. We are proud to be an equal opportunity employer and welcome applications from diverse candidates.
