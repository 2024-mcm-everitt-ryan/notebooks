Deputy Executive Director

Company Background:
Nguyen and Sons is a leading construction company that has been in operation for over 20 years. We pride ourselves on our commitment to delivering high-quality projects on time and within budget.

Job Description:
We are seeking a dynamic and results-driven Deputy Executive Director to join our senior leadership team. As a key member of our executive team, you will be responsible for overseeing the strategic direction of our company and ensuring that our operations are running smoothly.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement business strategies to drive growth and profitability
 Lead cross-functional teams to achieve company goals and objectives
 Build and maintain relationships with key stakeholders, including clients, partners, and suppliers
 Oversee the management of company operations, including finance, HR, and marketing

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Construction Management, or a related field
 At least 5 years of senior leadership experience in the construction industry
 Proven track record of driving business growth and improving operational efficiency
 Strong leadership and communication skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $120,000 to $150,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you will be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, which includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.

About Our Culture:
We are a team-oriented company that values collaboration and innovation. Our fast-paced and dynamic environment is perfect for individuals who are looking to make a real impact.
