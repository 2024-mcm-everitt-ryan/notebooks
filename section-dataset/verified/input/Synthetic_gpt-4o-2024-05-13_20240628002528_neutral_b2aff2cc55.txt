Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Power Plant Operator to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for operating and maintaining our state-of-the-art power plant facilities, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency.

Responsibilities:
- Operate and monitor power plant equipment and systems.
- Perform routine maintenance and troubleshooting.
- Ensure compliance with safety protocols and environmental regulations.
- Collaborate with team members to achieve operational goals.
- Prepare reports and document operational activities.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent; technical certification preferred.
- Proven experience in power plant operations or a similar field.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Ability to work effectively in a team-oriented environment.
- Excellent communication skills.
