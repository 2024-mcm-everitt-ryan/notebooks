Company Background:
Blake Ltd is a leading provider of professional services, dedicated to delivering high-quality administrative support to businesses across various industries. We pride ourselves on our inclusive and diverse workplace culture.

Job Type:
Full-time Administrative Office Assistant

Job Description:
Blake Ltd is seeking a dedicated Administrative Office Assistant to join our dynamic team. The candidate will be responsible for managing day-to-day office tasks, ensuring efficient operations, and providing support to our team. This position requires excellent organizational and communication skills.

Responsibilities:
- Manage and organize office operations and procedures.
- Handle correspondence, phone calls, and emails promptly.
- Assist in scheduling meetings and appointments.
- Maintain office supplies and inventory.
- Prepare and edit documents, reports, and presentations.
- Support team members with administrative tasks as needed.
- Ensure the office environment is clean, organized, and welcoming.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as an administrative assistant or in a similar role.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
- Strong organizational and time-management skills.
- Excellent verbal and written communication abilities.
- Ability to multi-task and prioritize effectively.
- High school diploma; additional qualifications in office administration are a plus.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $30,000 to $45,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- A supportive and inclusive workplace culture.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and cover letter detailing their experience and why they are a great fit for this position.
