Company Background:
Smith, Richards, and Rogers is a distinguished dental services provider committed to delivering exceptional oral health care. We are dedicated to fostering an inclusive and diverse workplace that values each individual's unique contributions.

Job Type:
Full-time Dental Assistant

Job Description and Responsibilities:
We are seeking a dedicated Dental Assistant to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will assist dentists during procedures, manage patient records, and ensure the cleanliness and sterilization of dental instruments. The role requires excellent communication skills and a commitment to patient care.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Certification or diploma in Dental Assisting
- Previous experience in a dental office is an asset
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
- Ability to work well in a team environment
- Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $30,000 to $40,000 annually, based on experience and skills
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance
- Opportunities for professional development and continuous learning
- Supportive and inclusive work environment
