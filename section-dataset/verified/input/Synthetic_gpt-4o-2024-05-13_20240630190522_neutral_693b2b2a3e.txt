Company Background:
Smith-Cobb is a leading provider of veterinary services, dedicated to the health and well-being of animals. We prioritize inclusivity and diversity in our workplace, recognizing the immense value that different perspectives bring to our team.

Job Title:
Veterinarian

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and compassionate veterinarian to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for diagnosing and treating illnesses in animals, performing surgeries, and advising pet owners on proper care. The role involves analyzing performance metrics to improve patient outcomes.

Responsibilities:
- Diagnose and treat medical conditions in animals.
- Perform surgical procedures and post-operative care.
- Conduct routine check-ups and vaccinations.
- Advise pet owners on nutrition, preventive care, and general health.
- Maintain accurate patient records and case histories.
- Collaborate with other veterinary professionals to provide comprehensive care.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- DVM or equivalent degree from an accredited veterinary school.
- Valid veterinary license.
- Strong diagnostic and surgical skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
- Proficiency in project management and ability to analyze performance metrics.
- Commitment to continuing education and professional development.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $70,000 to $100,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Continuing education opportunities.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.
- Relocation assistance, if necessary.

Smith-Cobb is committed to providing equal opportunities for all candidates. We believe that diversity enriches our workplace and drives our success. We are dedicated to promoting diversity and equity and strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive.
