Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are looking for a skilled Photogrammetrist to join our dynamic team. This role involves capturing, processing, and analyzing spatial data to create detailed maps and models. The candidate will work closely with our GIS specialists and cartographers to support various projects.

Responsibilities:
- Capture and process aerial, satellite, and drone imagery.
- Analyze spatial data to create accurate and detailed maps.
- Collaborate with GIS specialists and cartographers on project deliverables.
- Ensure data accuracy and integrity.
- Stay updated with the latest photogrammetry technologies and techniques.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Geomatics, Geography, or a related field.
- Proficiency in photogrammetry software (e.g., Pix4D, Agisoft Metashape).
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Excellent communication skills.
- Previous experience in photogrammetry is preferred.
