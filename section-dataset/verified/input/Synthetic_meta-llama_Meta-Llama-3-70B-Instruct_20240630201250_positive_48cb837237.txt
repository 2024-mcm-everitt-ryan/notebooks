HR Specialist at Garcia, Bryan and Smith

Company Background: Garcia, Bryan and Smith is a leading consulting firm dedicated to helping businesses thrive in a rapidly changing environment. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description: We are seeking an experienced HR Specialist to join our team. As an HR Specialist, you will play a critical role in developing and implementing HR initiatives that promote a positive and inclusive work environment. You will work closely with our management team to design and deliver programs that foster employee engagement, diversity, and growth.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement diversity and inclusion initiatives that promote gender parity in the workplace
 Design and deliver training programs that focus on unconscious bias, cultural competence, and inclusive leadership
 Collaborate with hiring managers to develop job descriptions and interview questions that attract a diverse pool of candidates
 Analyze and report on HR metrics, including diversity and inclusion metrics, to inform business decisions
 Provide guidance and support to employees on HR-related matters

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in HR, with a focus on diversity and inclusion
 Strong knowledge of employment laws and regulations
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize multiple tasks

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance, including medical, dental, and vision
 Generous paid time off, including vacation days, sick leave, and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
