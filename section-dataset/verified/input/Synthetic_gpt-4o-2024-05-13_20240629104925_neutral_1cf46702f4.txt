Company: Cox PLC  
Role: Press Officer  
Location: London, UK

Cox PLC is a leading company in the telecommunications industry, known for our commitment to innovation and excellence. We are seeking a diligent and skilled Press Officer to join our team and help us manage our public relations efforts effectively.

Job Description:

As a Press Officer at Cox PLC, you will be responsible for managing the company's external communications. You will work closely with various departments to draft and distribute press releases, handle media inquiries, and ensure our messaging aligns with our corporate values and goals.

Responsibilities:

- Draft and disseminate press releases.
- Handle media inquiries and coordinate interviews.
- Develop and maintain relationships with journalists and media outlets.
- Monitor media coverage and report on the company’s public image.
- Collaborate with internal teams to ensure consistent messaging.
- Organize and manage press events and conferences.

Qualifications and Experience:

- Bachelor’s degree in Communications, Journalism, Public Relations, or a related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in a similar role.
- Exceptional written and verbal communication skills.
- Strong organizational and multitasking abilities.
- Ability to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines.

Perks and Benefits:

- Competitive salary: $45,000 - $60,000 annually, with opportunities for performance-based bonuses.
- Comprehensive healthcare coverage.
- Professional development and training programs.
- Generous vacation policy.
- Flexible working arrangements.
