Company: Mason-Bowen
Role: Social Service Specialist

About Us:
Mason-Bowen is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality social services to underserved communities. We are committed to creating an inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities for all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and compassionate Social Service Specialist to join our team. As a Social Service Specialist, you will be responsible for providing support services to our clients, including case management, counseling, and advocacy. You will work closely with our team to develop and implement individualized service plans that address the unique needs of our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Provide support services to clients, including case management, counseling, and advocacy
 Develop and implement individualized service plans that address the unique needs of clients
 Collaborate with team members to ensure comprehensive service delivery
 Maintain accurate records and reports
 Participate in training and professional development opportunities

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Social Work, Psychology, or a related field
 At least 2 years of experience in social services or a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment
 Knowledge of community resources and services

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $55,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on experience and skills. We also provide a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan. In addition, we offer a generous paid time off policy and opportunities for professional development and growth.
