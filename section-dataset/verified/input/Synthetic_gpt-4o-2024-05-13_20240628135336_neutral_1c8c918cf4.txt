Company: Buchanan, Shaw and Garcia  
Role: Facility Operations Custodial Assistant  

Company Background:  
Buchanan, Shaw and Garcia is a leading firm in providing comprehensive facility management services. We pride ourselves on maintaining a diverse and inclusive workplace where everyone feels valued and respected.

Job Type: Full-Time  

Job Description:  
We are seeking a dedicated Facility Operations Custodial Assistant to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for maintaining cleanliness and orderliness in all areas of the facility. This role is essential to ensuring a safe and sanitary environment for employees and visitors.

Responsibilities:  
- Perform general cleaning tasks such as sweeping, mopping, dusting, and waste disposal.  
- Ensure restrooms are cleaned and stocked regularly.  
- Monitor and report any maintenance or repair needs.  
- Assist with setting up and breaking down for company events.  
- Follow health and safety guidelines at all times.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- High school diploma or equivalent.  
- Prior custodial or janitorial experience is preferred but not required.  
- Ability to follow instructions and work independently.  
- Strong attention to detail.  
- Good physical stamina to perform cleaning tasks.

Perks and Benefits:  
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €20,000 to €24,000 per year, based on your experience and skills.  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Paid time off and holidays.  
- Retirement savings plan with company match.  
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
