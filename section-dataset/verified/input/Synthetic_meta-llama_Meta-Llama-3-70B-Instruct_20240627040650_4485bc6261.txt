Company: Shields LLC

Job Title: Order Filler

About Us:
Shields LLC is a fast-paced and dynamic logistics company dedicated to providing exceptional customer service. We strive to create an inclusive work environment where all employees feel valued and respected.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Order Filler to join our team. As an Order Filler, you will be responsible for accurately and efficiently filling customer orders in a timely manner.

Responsibilities:
- Fill customer orders in accordance with company procedures
- Conduct quality control checks to ensure accuracy and quality of orders
- Maintain a clean and organized workspace
- Collaborate with coworkers to achieve team goals
- Develop and maintain a strong understanding of our products and services

Qualifications:
- 1-2 years of experience in a warehouse or logistics environment
- Skilled in French
- Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and stand for long periods
- Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Benefits:
- Hourly rate of $15-$18
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Comprehensive benefits package including health, dental, and vision insurance
- Paid time off and holidays
