Company Name: Solis-Day  
Role: Fiscal Coordinator  

Company Background:  
Solis-Day is a forward-thinking financial services company dedicated to providing innovative solutions and outstanding client experiences. We are seeking a Fiscal Coordinator to join our dynamic team.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:  
The Fiscal Coordinator will play a pivotal role in managing financial transactions, maintaining accurate records, and ensuring financial compliance. She will lead financial reporting and budgeting processes, working closely with team members to create a harmonious workspace. Her ability to connect intimately with clients is essential to this role.

Responsibilities:  
- Lead the financial reporting and budgeting processes.
- Maintain accurate financial records.
- Ensure compliance with financial regulations.
- Collaborate with team members to ensure smooth financial operations.
- Establish strong relationships with clients, ensuring their financial needs are met.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field.
- Proven experience in financial coordination or a similar role, preferably in a customer-focused environment.
- Strong analytical skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication skills, with the ability to connect intimately with clients.
- Proficiency in financial software and tools.
