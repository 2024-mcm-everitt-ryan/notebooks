Company Background:
Holmes, Berry and Adkins is a renowned legal and consulting firm with a strong commitment to diversity and inclusion. We believe that diverse perspectives drive innovation and excellence.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented Librarian to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for managing our extensive collection of legal and business resources. This role supports our attorneys and consultants by providing comprehensive research and information retrieval services.

Responsibilities:
- Maintain and organize the library's collection of legal and business resources.
- Assist team members with research and information retrieval.
- Develop and manage electronic resources and databases.
- Conduct training sessions for staff on effective use of library resources.
- Stay updated on new resources, technologies, and best practices in library management.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- A Master's degree in Library Science or Information Science is required.
- Experience in a law firm or corporate library is preferred.
- Strong organizational, research, and interpersonal skills.
- Proficiency with electronic databases and library management systems.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a diverse team.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €45,000 to €55,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and continuing education.
- A collaborative and inclusive work environment that values diverse perspectives.

Holmes, Berry and Adkins is an equal opportunity employer. We welcome applicants with diverse experiences and perspectives.
