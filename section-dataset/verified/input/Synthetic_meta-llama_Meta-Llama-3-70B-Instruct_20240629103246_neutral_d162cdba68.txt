Company: Wiley-Stevenson

Job Title: Machinist

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Machinist to join our team at Wiley-Stevenson. As a Machinist, you will be responsible for operating and maintaining machinery to produce high-quality parts and tools. If you have a strong attention to detail and enjoy working with precision instruments, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Operate and maintain machinery, including lathes, milling machines, and grinders
 Read and interpret blueprints and schematics to ensure accurate production
 Measure and inspect parts to ensure quality standards are met
 Collaborate with team members to optimize production processes
 Maintain a safe and clean work environment

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Machinist or in a related field
 Strong understanding of mechanical principles and machining techniques
 Ability to work accurately and efficiently in a fast-paced environment
 High school diploma or equivalent required; post-secondary education in a related field an asset

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 45,000 to USD 65,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program.
