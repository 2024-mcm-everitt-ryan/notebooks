Company Background:
Hernandez and Sons is a leading construction and architectural firm with over 35 years of experience. We specialize in innovative and sustainable design solutions, delivering excellence through every project.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are looking for a Program Manager and CADD Coordinator who can seamlessly integrate into our dynamic team. This role is pivotal to our operations, requiring a detail-oriented individual who can handle multiple responsibilities and ensure project timelines are met.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee and manage multiple programs and projects.
- Coordinate CADD operations and ensure all designs meet company standards.
- Maintain clear and open communication with team members and clients.
- Ensure project timelines are adhered to without deviation.
- Provide regular status updates to senior management.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Must have a degree in Engineering or Architecture from a prestigious institution.
- Minimum of 7 years of experience in program management and CADD coordination.
- Must be a perfectionist with an eye for detail.
- We prefer candidates who have a spotless credit history.
- Experience in a highly conservative corporate environment is a plus.

- Competitive salary, ranging from $70,000 to $90,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance and wellness programs.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Generous vacation policy and paid time off.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
