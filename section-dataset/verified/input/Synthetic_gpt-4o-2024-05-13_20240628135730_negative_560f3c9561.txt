Company: Wood and Sons  
Role: Service Desk Agent

Company Background:
Wood and Sons is a leading provider of IT solutions, offering high-quality services to our clients. Our commitment to excellence has established us as a trusted partner in the industry.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
As a Service Desk Agent, you will be the first point of contact for our clients, providing expert support and ensuring their issues are resolved efficiently. This role requires a high level of professionalism and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:
- Provide first-level support to clients via phone, email, and chat.
- Maintain a rapid work pace to ensure timely resolution of issues.
- Document and track all customer interactions in our ticketing system.
- Escalate complex issues to higher-level support teams when necessary.
- Participate in on-call rotations and be available for frequent travel without restrictions.
- Perform in environments with variable temperatures and maintain the ability to lift up to 50 pounds regularly.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience in a customer support or help desk role.
- Strong communication skills and a professional demeanor.
- Ability to stand for long periods during training sessions and presentations.
- Technical proficiency in common IT software and hardware.
- High attention to detail and strong problem-solving skills.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $35,000 to $45,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.

How to Apply:
If you are a highly motivated individual with a passion for customer service and meet the above requirements, please submit your resume and cover letter to careers@woodandsons.com.

Wood and Sons is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
