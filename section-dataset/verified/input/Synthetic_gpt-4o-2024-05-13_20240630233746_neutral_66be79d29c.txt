Company Background:
Lucas, Brown, and Mann is a leading global firm specializing in innovative data solutions. We pride ourselves on fostering a collaborative environment where talent can thrive and contribute to our growing success.

Job Title: DBA Engineer

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled DBA Engineer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in database management and be responsible for ensuring the integrity, performance, and security of our databases.

Responsibilities:
- Design, implement, and maintain database systems.
- Ensure database performance, availability, and security.
- Conduct performance tuning and optimization.
- Develop and implement database backup, recovery, and disaster recovery strategies.
- Collaborate with development teams to integrate database solutions.
- Monitor database performance and troubleshoot issues as they arise.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- Proven experience as a DBA Engineer or similar role.
- Proficiency in database management systems such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, or Oracle.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

- Comprehensive health insurance.
- Opportunity for career growth and professional development.
- Flexible working hours and remote work options.
- Generous vacation policy and paid time off.
- Employee wellness programs and support.
