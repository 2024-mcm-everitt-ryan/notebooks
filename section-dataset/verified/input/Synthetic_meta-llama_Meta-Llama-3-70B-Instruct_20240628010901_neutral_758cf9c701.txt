Company: Trevino, Campos and Edwards

Role: Trapper

About Us:
Trevino, Campos and Edwards is a leading wildlife management company dedicated to preserving ecosystems and promoting biodiversity. We are committed to building a workforce that reflects the diversity of our community.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Trapper to join our team. As a Trapper, you will be responsible for managing team projects, ensuring the safe and humane capture of wildlife, and maintaining accurate records of trapping activities. You will work closely with our biologists and conservationists to develop and implement effective trapping strategies.

Responsibilities:

 Manage team projects to achieve trapping goals and objectives
 Develop and implement effective trapping strategies in collaboration with biologists and conservationists
 Ensure the safe and humane capture of wildlife
 Maintain accurate records of trapping activities
 Participate in training and development programs to stay up-to-date with industry best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in wildlife trapping or a related field
 Strong project management and leadership skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment
 Knowledge of wildlife management principles and practices
 Valid trapping license
