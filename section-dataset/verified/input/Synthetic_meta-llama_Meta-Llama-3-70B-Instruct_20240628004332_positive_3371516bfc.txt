Company: Mullins-Green
Role: Medical and Health Services Manager

About Us:
Mullins-Green is a leading healthcare organization committed to delivering exceptional medical services to our diverse patient population. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for our patients, employees, and partners.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Medical and Health Services Manager to lead our team of healthcare professionals. As a Medical and Health Services Manager, you will oversee the delivery of high-quality patient care, develop and implement healthcare programs, and ensure compliance with industry regulations.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement healthcare programs to improve patient outcomes
 Lead and manage a team of healthcare professionals
 Ensure compliance with industry regulations and standards
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to achieve organizational goals
 Analyze data to inform healthcare program development and improvement

Qualifications and Experience:

 Master's degree in Healthcare Administration, Public Health, or related field
 5+ years of experience in healthcare management
 Proven track record of developing and implementing successful healthcare programs
 Excellent leadership, communication, and analytical skills

We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
