Pipefitter Position at Richardson-Norman

Company Background:
Richardson-Norman is a leading provider of piping solutions, committed to delivering high-quality services to our clients. We pride ourselves on our diverse and inclusive workplace, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Pipefitter to join our team. As a Pipefitter, you will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing pipes and fittings. You will work collaboratively with our team to ensure the efficient operation of our piping systems.

Responsibilities:

 Install, maintain, and repair pipes and fittings
 Collaborate with team members to ensure efficient operation of piping systems
 Troubleshoot issues and develop solutions
 Ensure compliance with safety regulations and industry standards

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in pipefitting or a related field
 Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 Ability to work collaboratively and communicate effectively
 Valid certification in pipefitting (or equivalent)

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $55,000 to $75,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. Additionally, you will be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
