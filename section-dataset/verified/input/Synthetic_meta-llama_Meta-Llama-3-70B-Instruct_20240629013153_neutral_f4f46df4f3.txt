Field Operations Training Coordinator

Company Background
Ruiz-James is a leading provider of innovative field operations solutions, dedicated to delivering exceptional customer experiences. Our team is comprised of diverse and talented individuals who share a passion for excellence and a commitment to making a positive impact.

Job Description
We are seeking a highly skilled Field Operations Training Coordinator to join our team. As a Field Operations Training Coordinator, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and delivering training programs that enhance the skills and knowledge of our field operations team. You will work closely with our operations team to identify training needs, develop effective training materials, and evaluate program effectiveness.

Responsibilities

 Design and develop comprehensive training programs that meet the needs of our field operations team
 Deliver training sessions to field operations personnel, ensuring engaging and interactive learning experiences
 Collaborate with operations team to identify training needs and develop effective training materials
 Evaluate program effectiveness and make recommendations for improvement
 Maintain accurate records of training activities and program evaluations

Qualifications and Experience

 3+ years of experience in training and development, preferably in a field operations environment
 Proven track record of designing and delivering effective training programs
 Excellent communication, facilitation, and presentation skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
 Experience with adult learning principles and instructional design models

Perks and Benefits

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits package
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
