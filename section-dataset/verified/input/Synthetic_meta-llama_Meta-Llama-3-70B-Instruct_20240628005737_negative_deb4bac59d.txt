Job Title: Junior Accountant
Company: Nguyen Group

About Us:
The Nguyen Group is a dynamic and growing company in the financial services industry. We are seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented Junior Accountant to join our team.

Job Description:
As a Junior Accountant, you will be responsible for preparing financial statements, reconciling accounts, and performing various accounting functions. You will work closely with our senior accountants to ensure accurate and timely financial reporting.

Responsibilities:

 Prepare and review financial statements, invoices, and other accounting documents
 Reconcile accounts and perform general ledger maintenance
 Assist with budgeting, forecasting, and financial analysis
 Collaborate with senior accountants to ensure accurate and timely financial reporting

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Accounting or related field
 1-2 years of accounting experience
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Proficient in accounting software and Microsoft Office

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 45,000 to USD 60,000, based on your experience and skills. Our benefits package is tailored to support employees with traditional family structures, including facilities designed for those with spouses and children.
