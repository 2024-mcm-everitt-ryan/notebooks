Company: Pearson-Montoya

Job Title: Avionics Technician

About Us:
Pearson-Montoya is a leading innovator in the aerospace industry, committed to delivering exceptional solutions to our clients. We are seeking a highly skilled Avionics Technician to join our team of experts.

Job Description:
As an Avionics Technician, you will be responsible for installing, testing, and repairing electrical systems on aircraft. You will work closely with our team of engineers to ensure the highest standards of quality and safety.

Responsibilities:

 Install, test, and repair electrical systems on aircraft
 Collaborate with engineers to identify and resolve technical issues
 Conduct routine maintenance and inspections to ensure compliance with safety regulations
 Develop and implement troubleshooting procedures to improve efficiency

Qualifications and Experience:
 3+ years of experience in avionics or a related field
 Strong understanding of electrical systems and safety protocols
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
 Excellent problem-solving skills

Perks and Benefits:
 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
