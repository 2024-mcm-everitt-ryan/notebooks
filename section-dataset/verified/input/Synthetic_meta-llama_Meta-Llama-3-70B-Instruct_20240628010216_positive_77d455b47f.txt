Training Specialist at Odom Ltd

Company Background:
Odom Ltd is a leading provider of training and development solutions, dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations to reach their full potential. We believe in fostering a culture of inclusivity, respect, and empathy, where everyone can thrive and grow.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented Training Specialist to join our team. As a Training Specialist, you will be responsible for designing, delivering, and evaluating training programs that meet the needs of our diverse client base. You will work closely with our team to identify training gaps, develop engaging content, and facilitate workshops that drive results.

Responsibilities:

 Design and deliver training programs that meet the needs of our clients
 Develop engaging content and materials for training sessions
 Facilitate workshops and training sessions, both in-person and virtually
 Evaluate the effectiveness of training programs and make recommendations for improvement
 Collaborate with the team to identify training gaps and develop solutions

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Education, Training and Development, or a related field
 3+ years of experience in training and development
 Proven track record of designing and delivering effective training programs
 Excellent communication and facilitation skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize multiple projects

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. In addition, you will be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
