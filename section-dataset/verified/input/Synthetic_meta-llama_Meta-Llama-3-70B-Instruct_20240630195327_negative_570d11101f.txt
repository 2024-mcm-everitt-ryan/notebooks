Criminal Investigator

Company: Grant, Anderson and Norris

Job Summary:

We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Criminal Investigator to join our team. As a Criminal Investigator, you will be responsible for conducting investigations, gathering evidence, and analyzing data to solve crimes. If you are a meticulous and analytical individual with excellent interpersonal skills, we encourage you to apply.

Job Description:

The successful candidate will have a keen eye for detail and be able to work effectively in a fast-paced environment. She will have excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, and be able to foster a collaborative team environment. The ideal candidate will have experience in managing multiple cases simultaneously and be able to prioritize tasks efficiently.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct investigations and gather evidence
 Analyze data and develop theories on cases
 Collaborate with law enforcement agencies and other stakeholders
 Develop and maintain case files and reports
 Communicate effectively with clients and stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Criminal Justice or related field
 3+ years of experience in criminal investigation
 Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $90,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 401(k) matching program
 Ongoing training and professional development opportunities
