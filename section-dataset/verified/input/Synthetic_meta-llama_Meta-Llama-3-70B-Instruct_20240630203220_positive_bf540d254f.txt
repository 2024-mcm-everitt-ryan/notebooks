Implementation Coordinator at Gibson PLC

Job Description:
We're seeking a highly skilled Implementation Coordinator to join our team. As an Implementation Coordinator, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the successful rollout of our solutions, working closely with our clients and internal teams to deliver exceptional results.

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in project coordination or a related field
 Excellent communication, organizational, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, prioritizing multiple tasks and deadlines
 Strong analytical skills, with experience in data analysis and process improvement
