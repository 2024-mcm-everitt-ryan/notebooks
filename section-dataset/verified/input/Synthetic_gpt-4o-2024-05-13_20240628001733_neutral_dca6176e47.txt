Company: Burch, Gordon and Leblanc

Role: Marine Oiler, Mate

Company Background:
Burch, Gordon and Leblanc is a prestigious maritime services firm known for its commitment to excellence in the marine industry. Our team of professionals is dedicated to providing top-notch services and support to our diverse clientele.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Marine Oiler, Mate to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring the smooth operation and maintenance of our vessels. This role requires a strong understanding of marine operations and a commitment to safety and efficiency.

Responsibilities:
- Perform regular maintenance and inspections on vessel machinery.
- Operate and maintain engines, pumps, and other machinery.
- Ensure compliance with safety regulations and procedures.
- Monitor and report any mechanical issues or irregularities.
- Assist in docking and undocking operations.
- Keep detailed records of maintenance and repairs.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Must demonstrate required skills in marine engineering and operations.
- Must meet experience requirements; a minimum of 3 years in a similar role is preferred.
- Qualified candidates with relevant certifications (e.g., STCW, AB Seaman) are welcomed.
- Required industry knowledge of vessel operations and safety standards.
- Experienced professionals encouraged to apply.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £40,000 to £60,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are invited to submit their resume and cover letter detailing their experience and qualifications. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis.
