Company: Colon and Sons

Job Title: Aircraft Mechanic

About Us:
Colon and Sons is a leading aircraft maintenance company, dedicated to providing top-notch services to our clients. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and commitment to safety.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Aircraft Mechanic to join our team. As an Aircraft Mechanic, you will be responsible for performing routine maintenance, repairs, and inspections on aircraft to ensure they are airworthy and meet safety standards.

Responsibilities:

 Perform routine maintenance, repairs, and inspections on aircraft
 Troubleshoot and diagnose issues with aircraft systems
 Collaborate with other team members to ensure timely and efficient completion of tasks
 Maintain accurate records of maintenance and repairs
 Stay up-to-date with industry standards and regulations

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in aircraft maintenance
 Hold a valid FAA certification (or equivalent)
 Must have a high school diploma or equivalent
 Applicants with a background in manual labor or trade schools will be prioritized
 Must have a valid driver's license

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
