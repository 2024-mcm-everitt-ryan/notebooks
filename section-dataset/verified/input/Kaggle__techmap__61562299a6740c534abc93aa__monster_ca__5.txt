Job Description

How about flexible hours that let you do your thing, whatever your thing is. The opportunity to grow, professionally, personally, and financially. You are trustworthy and have trust in others. You find joy in the details and believe good quality is what keeps guests coming back. You remain cool, calm, and collected under pressure. You understand there is no “I” in team and thrive in social situations. You get that service isn’t a transaction. It’s about connection. That a smile can make all the difference in someone’s day. It’s genuine, and it’s who you are. If that sounds good to you, then we’d love to hear from you.

Good customer service skills are integral to the way we do things at McDonald's. A crew member will be expected to provide customers with a quick and accurate service and show sensitivity to their individual needs, both from behind the till as well as in the dining areas.

Qualifications

- A wonderful smile and great attitude working with a team (mix of full-time and part-time staff).
- Energetic, strong work ethic, flexible, and the ability to learn and thrive with new skills and opportunities.
- Uses proper procedures to ensure the accuracy of every order for every guest.
- Prepares and finishes products to ensure quality standards are met.
- Helps support speed of service targets by working quickly and efficiently to fill orders and meet guest’s needs.
