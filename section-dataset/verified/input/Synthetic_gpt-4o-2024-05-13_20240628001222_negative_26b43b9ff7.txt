Company Background:
Shea, Fuller, and Garrett is a leading logistics company providing comprehensive transportation solutions for a diverse range of clients. We pride ourselves on our efficiency, reliability, and committed workforce.

Role: Fleet Administrator

Job Description:
We are looking for an organized and detail-oriented Fleet Administrator to join our team. The Fleet Administrator will be responsible for managing our fleet of vehicles, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements, and coordinating maintenance schedules.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee the daily operations of the company's fleet.
- Ensure all vehicles are properly maintained and comply with safety standards.
- Maintain accurate records of vehicle inspections, maintenance schedules, and repairs.
- Coordinate with drivers to ensure timely deliveries and pickups.
- Manage fleet-related budgets and expenses.
- Assist in the development and implementation of fleet policies and procedures.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration or related field.
- At least 3 years of experience in fleet management.
- Strong organizational and multitasking skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Proficiency in fleet management software.
- Valid driver’s license.
