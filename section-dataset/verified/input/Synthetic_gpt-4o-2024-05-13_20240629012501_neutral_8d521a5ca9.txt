Company Background:
Medina LLC is a leading provider of comprehensive grounds maintenance services. We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality service to our clients through a dedicated and skilled workforce.

Job Type: 
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a proactive and self-motivated Grounds Maintenance Worker to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will demonstrate attention to detail, a high level of accuracy and precision, and a strong work ethic and commitment to excellence. This role involves maintaining the grounds of various properties, ensuring they are aesthetically pleasing and safe for all users.

Responsibilities:
- Perform routine maintenance tasks such as mowing, trimming, weeding, and leaf removal.
- Operate and maintain grounds maintenance equipment.
- Assist in planting, pruning, and general landscaping duties.
- Ensure the cleanliness and safety of outdoor areas.
- Perform minor repairs and maintenance on equipment and facilities as needed.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Previous experience in grounds maintenance or skilled trades is an advantage.
- Ability to perform physical tasks in various weather conditions.
- Strong attention to detail and the ability to work independently.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and a proactive approach to tasks.
- Reliable and punctual with a commitment to maintaining high standards.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €25,000 to €30,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday leave.
- Opportunities for professional development and advancement within the company.

Medina LLC is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
