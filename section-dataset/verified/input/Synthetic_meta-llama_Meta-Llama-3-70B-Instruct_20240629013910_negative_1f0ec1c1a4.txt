Company Background:
Phillips, Allen and Mora is a leading food technology company dedicated to providing innovative solutions to the food industry.

Job Type: Food Scientist

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Food Scientist to join our team. As a Food Scientist, you will be responsible for developing and implementing new food products, ensuring compliance with food safety regulations, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to drive business growth.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement new food products, focusing on taste, texture, and nutritional value
 Conduct sensory evaluations and gather feedback from consumers and stakeholders
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including R&D, marketing, and sales to drive business growth
 Ensure compliance with food safety regulations and industry standards

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Food Science, Nutrition, or related field
 3+ years of experience in food product development
 Strong understanding of food safety regulations and industry standards
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills

We offer a competitive salary, ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we provide comprehensive health insurance, retirement plan matching, and opportunities for professional growth and development.
