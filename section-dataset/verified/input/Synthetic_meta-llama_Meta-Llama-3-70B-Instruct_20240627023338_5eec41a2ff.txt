Company: Morris, Leonard and Jones

Job Title: Tutor / Language Support Worker

About Us:
Morris, Leonard and Jones is a leading education provider dedicated to creating a supportive learning environment for students from diverse backgrounds. We are committed to fostering a culture of inclusivity and respect, where every individual can thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly qualified and experienced Tutor / Language Support Worker to join our team. As a Tutor / Language Support Worker, you will be responsible for providing language support to students, developing individualized learning plans, and creating a conducive learning environment.

Responsibilities:
- Provide language support to students to help them overcome language barriers
- Develop and implement individualized learning plans tailored to students' needs
- Create a supportive and inclusive learning environment
- Collaborate with teachers to develop language-rich lesson plans
- Support students from diverse linguistic and cultural backgrounds

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Education, Linguistics, or a related field
- Experience working with students from diverse linguistic and cultural backgrounds
- Strong knowledge of language acquisition and pedagogy
- Ability to communicate effectively with students, teachers, and parents

- Flexible working hours
