Company: Wright-Martin

Job Title: Database Administrator

Join our dynamic team at Wright-Martin as a Database Administrator, where you will play a critical role in ensuring the integrity and efficiency of our database systems. As a committed equal opportunities employer, we value diversity and inclusion in the workplace.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Database Administrator to manage, maintain, and optimize our database systems. The ideal candidate will have strong technical expertise, excellent problem-solving skills, and the ability to work collaboratively with our development teams.

Responsibilities:

 Design, implement, and maintain database architectures to meet business needs
 Ensure database performance, scalability, and security
 Collaborate with development teams to optimize database queries and troubleshoot issues
 Develop and maintain database documentation and standards

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
 3+ years of experience as a Database Administrator
 Strong knowledge of database management systems, including MySQL and Oracle
 Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail

 Comprehensive health insurance and retirement benefits
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Flexible working hours and remote work options
