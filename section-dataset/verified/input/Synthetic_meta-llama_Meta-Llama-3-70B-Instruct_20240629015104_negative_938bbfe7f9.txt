Company: Wilson-Rodriguez

Lifeguard Position

Job Type: Part-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled lifeguard to join our team at Wilson-Rodriguez. As a lifeguard, you will be responsible for ensuring the safety of our pool patrons. If you have a strong work ethic and a willingness to learn, we encourage you to apply. Please note that only candidates with a degree from a reputable institution will be considered.

Responsibilities:

 Monitor pool area to prevent accidents and ensure patron safety
 Respond to emergencies and provide first aid as needed
 Maintain a clean and organized pool area
 Collaborate with other lifeguards to ensure efficient operations

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required; college degree preferred
 Current lifeguard certification
 Ability to work varying shifts, including weekends and holidays
 Strong communication and teamwork skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $18 to $22 per hour, based on your experience and skills. We also offer opportunities for professional development and a supportive work environment.
