Company: Schwartz, Gallagher and Jackson

Radio Room Operator

About Us:
Schwartz, Gallagher and Jackson is a leading broadcasting company dedicated to providing exceptional audio content to our listeners. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace where every team member feels valued and respected.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Radio Room Operator to join our dynamic team. As a Radio Room Operator, you will be responsible for coordinating the flow of programming, ensuring seamless transitions between shows, and providing technical support to our on-air talent. If you have excellent communication skills, are comfortable working in a fast-paced environment, and have a passion for radio broadcasting, we want to hear from you!

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate the scheduling of programs and commercials
 Operate radio broadcasting equipment, including consoles and software
 Provide technical support to on-air talent and production staff
 Monitor and adjust audio levels and signal quality
 Collaborate with production teams to ensure timely and efficient broadcast delivery

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in radio broadcasting or a related field
 Strong technical skills and knowledge of broadcasting software and equipment
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from USD 45,000 to USD 60,000, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you will be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and provide opportunities for professional growth and development.
