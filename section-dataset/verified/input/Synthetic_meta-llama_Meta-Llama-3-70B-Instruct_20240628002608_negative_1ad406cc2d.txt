Company: Montes and Sons
Role: Court Representative

About Us:
Montes and Sons is a reputable law firm specializing in family law and divorce cases. We are committed to providing exceptional legal services to our clients.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and ambitious Court Representative to join our team. As a Court Representative, you will be responsible for preparing and filing legal documents, attending court hearings, and providing administrative support to our attorneys.

Responsibilities:

 Prepare and file legal documents in a timely and accurate manner
 Attend court hearings and take notes as required
 Provide administrative support to our attorneys, including scheduling appointments and managing case files
 Maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Law, Paralegal Studies, or a related field
 1-2 years of experience in a law firm or legal environment
 Recent graduates with a strong academic record are encouraged to apply
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and legal software

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary range of $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our benefits package includes health insurance, retirement savings plan, and paid time off.
