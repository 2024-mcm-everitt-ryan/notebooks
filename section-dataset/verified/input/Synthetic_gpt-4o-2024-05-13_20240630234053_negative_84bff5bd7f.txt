Company Background:
Taylor, Wiggins and Monroe is a leading provider in industrial automation and control systems. With over 30 years of experience, we deliver innovative solutions to our clients across various sectors including manufacturing, energy, and pharmaceuticals.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled industrial control technician to join our dynamic team. His past experience in similar roles is valuable and he must possess strong organisational skills. The ideal candidate will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and troubleshooting industrial control systems.

Responsibilities:
- Install and maintain control systems and components.
- Troubleshoot and repair system malfunctions.
- Collaborate with engineering teams to improve system performance.
- Ensure compliance with safety regulations and standards.
- Document all work performed and maintain accurate records.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience as an industrial control technician.
- Excellent problem-solving skills.
- Strong understanding of control systems and electrical components.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Relevant certification or degree in electrical engineering or a related field is preferred.
