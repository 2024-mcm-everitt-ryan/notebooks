Job title: Graduate Sales Manager  
Location: Rotherham  
Salary: £22k (bonus and incentives)  
REF: J11621:LEE  
Sector: Manufacturing  

Starting in 1983 from a small garage in Rotherham, our client has grown exponentially from its humble beginnings to become one of the largest stockists of bearings, power transmission products, couplings & drives, linear systems, and maintenance equipment in Europe. With over six million stocked items and branches throughout the UK, our client strives to offer prompt and reliable delivery, technical support, and solutions via their dedicated product specialists. They are looking for passionate individuals ready to start a career in sales and business development. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication and interpersonal skills and be very target-driven.  

Key responsibilities:  
- Achieve targets by presenting the range of products to clients and progressing projects through the supply chain  
- Working with different departments to ensure to maximize sales  
- Lead generation  
- Building and maintaining relationships with new and existing clients  
- Carrying out site surveys  
- Management and administration through company CRM  

With near immediate client interaction, this role offers a recent graduate the opportunity to gain real-life experience from very early on, all with the support of a nurturing office environment.  

Benefits:  
- £22,000 basic salary  
- Bonus and incentives  
- Childcare vouchers  
- Pension contributions  
- Team nights out/social events  
- On-going training and support  

Candidate requirements:  
- Educated to degree level  
- Excellent communication skills  
- Written, verbal, and IT literate  
- Passion for customer service  
- Comfortable working in a target-driven environment
