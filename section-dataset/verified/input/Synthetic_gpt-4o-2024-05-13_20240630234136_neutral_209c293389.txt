Company: Allen, Powell and Cummings
Role: Storage Engineer
  
Company Background:
Allen, Powell and Cummings is a leading IT consulting firm specializing in data storage solutions. We pride ourselves on delivering innovative and efficient storage solutions to our diverse clientele.
  
Job Type: Full-Time
  
Job Description:
We are looking for a dedicated Storage Engineer to join our team. This role involves designing, implementing, and managing storage solutions that support our clients' data needs. The ideal candidate will be proactive, detail-oriented, and capable of working independently.
  
  Responsibilities:
    - Design, implement, and manage storage solutions.
    - Monitor and optimize storage performance.
    - Ensure data security and compliance with industry standards.
    - Collaborate with other IT teams to integrate storage solutions with broader infrastructure.
    - Troubleshoot and resolve storage-related issues.

  Qualifications:
    - Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
    - 3+ years of experience in storage engineering or related roles.
    - Proficiency with storage technologies such as SAN, NAS, and cloud storage solutions.
    - Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
    - Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
