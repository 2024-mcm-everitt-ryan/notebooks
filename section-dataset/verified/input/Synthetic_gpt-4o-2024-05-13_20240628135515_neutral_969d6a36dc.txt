Company: Floyd-Garcia

Role: Stormwater Program Coordinator

Company Background: Floyd-Garcia is a leading environmental consultancy firm based in London, UK. Our team is dedicated to providing innovative and sustainable solutions to manage water resources effectively. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and supportive work environment.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description: 
Floyd-Garcia is seeking a highly organized and motivated Stormwater Program Coordinator. This role involves developing, implementing, and managing stormwater programs to ensure compliance with environmental regulations. The ideal candidate will work closely with various stakeholders, including government agencies, community groups, and internal teams, to achieve project goals.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement comprehensive stormwater management programs.
- Ensure compliance with local, state, and federal environmental regulations.
- Coordinate with government agencies and community groups.
- Conduct site evaluations and provide technical guidance.
- Prepare detailed reports and documentation.
- Monitor project progress and make necessary adjustments.
- Provide training and support to staff on stormwater management practices.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Environmental Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in stormwater management or related areas.
- Strong understanding of environmental regulations and compliance.
- Excellent project management and organizational skills.
- Proficient in using GIS and other relevant software.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from £40,000 to £50,000, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Flexible working hours and remote work options.
- Employee wellness programs and resources.
