Company: Frederick and Sons
Role: Compliance Auditor

We are seeking a Compliance Auditor to join our team at Frederick and Sons. As a Compliance Auditor, you will be responsible for reviewing and evaluating our company's compliance with regulatory requirements and internal policies.

Job Description:
The Compliance Auditor will conduct regular audits to identify areas of non-compliance and develop corrective action plans to mitigate risks. The ideal candidate will have excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, as well as strong communication and interpersonal skills.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct regular audits to identify areas of non-compliance
 Develop corrective action plans to mitigate risks
 Collaborate with departments to implement corrective actions
 Provide training and guidance on compliance policies and procedures
 Maintain accurate and detailed records of audit findings and corrective actions

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
 2+ years of experience in compliance or auditing
 Professional certification (e.g. CPA, CFA) preferred
 Must have a continuous employment history

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
