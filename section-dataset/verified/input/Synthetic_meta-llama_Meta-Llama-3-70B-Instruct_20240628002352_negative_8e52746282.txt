Job Title: Tort Attorney
Company: Elliott-Miller

About Us:
Elliott-Miller is a dynamic law firm dedicated to providing exceptional legal services to our clients. We are seeking a talented Tort Attorney to join our team.

Job Description:
As a Tort Attorney at Elliott-Miller, you will be responsible for managing a caseload of complex tort cases, conducting legal research, and collaborating with our team of experts to deliver high-quality results. If you are a recent law graduate with a passion for tort law and a keen sense of innovation, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Manage a caseload of complex tort cases
 Conduct legal research and analysis
 Collaborate with our team of experts to deliver high-quality results
 Stay up-to-date with the latest developments in tort law

Qualifications and Experience:

 Recent law degree (J.D. or LL.B.)
 0-2 years of experience in tort law
 Strong research and analytical skills
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
