Company: Wood Ltd

Job Title: Laboratory Associate

Job Description:
Wood Ltd is seeking a highly skilled and physically able Laboratory Associate to join our team. As a key member of our laboratory team, you will be responsible for conducting experiments, collecting and analyzing data, and maintaining a safe and organized laboratory environment.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct experiments and collect data under the supervision of senior laboratory staff
 Maintain accurate and detailed records of experiments and results
 Ensure the laboratory is kept clean and organized, adhering to safety protocols
 Collaborate with team members to achieve project goals
 Participate in training and development programs to stay up-to-date with industry standards

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in a scientific field (e.g. biology, chemistry, physics)
 1-2 years of laboratory experience
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
 Ability to lift up to 25 kg and move around the laboratory regularly

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 40,000 to USD 55,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan.
