Job Description:

 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure efficient operations

 Bachelor's degree in a relevant field (e.g., engineering, physics) from a reputable university
 5+ years of experience in ordnance handling, explosive safety, or a related field
 Proven track record of developing and implementing safety protocols
 Excellent communication and leadership skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision
