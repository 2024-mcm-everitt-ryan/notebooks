Company Background:

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
As an Assistant Commissioner at Holland-Wilson, you will provide leadership and support to the Commissioner, ensuring the effective and efficient operation of the department. You will assist in policy development, strategic planning, and oversight of departmental activities.

Responsibilities:
- Assist in the formulation and implementation of policies and strategic plans.
- Supervise and support department staff to ensure high performance.
- Maintain professional relationships with various stakeholders.
- Conduct legal history checks as part of our consideration process.
- Ensure compliance with ethical standards and practices.
- Manage multiple projects simultaneously, maintaining calm under pressure.

Qualifications and Experience:
- A diploma from any accredited academic institution is preferred.
- Proven experience in a leadership role within public service or related fields.
- Strong analytical and decision-making skills.
- Ability to communicate effectively with diverse groups.
- Demonstrated commitment to ethical standards and transparency.
