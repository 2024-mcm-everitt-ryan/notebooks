Company Background:
Castillo-Jackson is a leading provider of comprehensive animal control services, committed to ensuring public safety and animal welfare. We pride ourselves on a culture of respect, inclusivity, and professionalism. 

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Animal Control Worker to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for responding to calls about stray or dangerous animals, as well as enforcing animal control laws and ordinances. This role requires compassion for animals, a strong sense of responsibility, and the ability to work in various environmental conditions.

Responsibilities:
- Responding to public inquiries and complaints regarding stray animals.
- Capturing and transporting stray or dangerous animals safely.
- Investigating incidents of animal cruelty and neglect.
- Educating the public about animal care and control laws.
- Maintaining records and preparing reports.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Previous experience in animal care or control is preferred.
- Ability to handle animals safely and humanely.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Valid driver's license with a clean driving record.

- Competitive salary ranging from $35,000 to $45,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Our office includes Nursing Rooms for all nursing needs.
- Our company benefits extend to all spouses and partners.
- We ensure that everyone feels welcome.
- Our company culture is built on respect and inclusivity.
