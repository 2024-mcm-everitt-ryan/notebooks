Company: Howard Group
Role: Crime Analyst

At Howard Group, we are committed to providing opportunities for women in the criminal justice industry. As a crime analyst, you will play a crucial role in helping us drive informed decision-making and improve community safety.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated crime analyst to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for analyzing crime trends, identifying patterns, and developing actionable insights to inform police operations. This is an exciting opportunity to make a real difference in the community and contribute to the development of evidence-based policing practices.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze crime data to identify trends, patterns, and correlations
 Develop and maintain databases, reports, and visualization tools to communicate insights to stakeholders
 Collaborate with law enforcement agencies to inform operational decisions
 Provide expert advice on crime analysis and intelligence to internal and external stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Criminology, Statistics, Computer Science, or a related field
 At least 2 years of experience in crime analysis or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 60,000 to USD 80,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings, and generous leave entitlements. Our team is dedicated to supporting women's advancement in the workplace, and we provide opportunities for professional development and growth.
