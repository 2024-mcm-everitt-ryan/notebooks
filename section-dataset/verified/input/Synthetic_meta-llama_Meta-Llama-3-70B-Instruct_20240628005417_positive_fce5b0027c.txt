Company: Morgan Ltd

Job Title: Bailiff

Job Description:
Morgan Ltd is seeking a highly motivated and organized Bailiff to join our team. As a Bailiff, you will be responsible for serving legal documents, executing court orders, and performing other duties as required by law. If you are a detail-oriented individual with excellent communication skills and a passion for upholding the law, we encourage you to apply as we are actively hunting for talent that can bring unique perspectives to our firm.

Responsibilities:

 Serve legal documents, such as summonses and subpoenas, to individuals and businesses
 Execute court orders, including evictions and repossessions
 Maintain accurate records of services performed
 Communicate effectively with clients, attorneys, and other stakeholders
 Perform other duties as required by law

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience in a related field preferred
 Strong communication and organizational skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance package
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
