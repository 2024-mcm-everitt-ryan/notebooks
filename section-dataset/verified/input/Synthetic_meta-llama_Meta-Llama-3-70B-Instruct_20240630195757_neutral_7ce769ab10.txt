Administrative Contract Specialist

Company Background:
Hall LLC is a leading provider of administrative services, dedicated to delivering exceptional support to our clients. We pride ourselves on our culture of inclusion, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and grow.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Administrative Contract Specialist to join our team. As an Administrative Contract Specialist, you will be responsible for managing and coordinating contracts, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements, and providing exceptional administrative support to our team.

Responsibilities:

 Develop, negotiate, and manage contracts with clients and vendors
 Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and company policies
 Provide administrative support to the team, including preparing reports and presentations
 Develop and maintain relationships with clients and vendors
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to achieve business objectives

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Law, or a related field
 3+ years of experience in contract administration or a related field
 Excellent communication, negotiation, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work collaboratively and effectively in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and a generous paid time-off policy. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and provide opportunities for professional growth and development.
