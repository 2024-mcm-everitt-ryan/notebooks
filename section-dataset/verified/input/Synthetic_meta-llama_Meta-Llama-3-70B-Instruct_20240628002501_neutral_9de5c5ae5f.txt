Company: Mitchell, Riley and Jackson
Role: Human Resources Associate

About Us:
Mitchell, Riley and Jackson is a dynamic and diverse organization dedicated to fostering inclusive environments. We believe that our team's unique perspectives and experiences are essential to driving innovation and success.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Human Resources Associate to join our team. As an HR Associate, you will play a vital role in supporting our recruitment processes, onboarding new employees, and ensuring compliance with company policies. You will work closely with our HR team to develop and implement initiatives that promote a positive and inclusive work environment.

Responsibilities:

 Assist in the recruitment process, including scheduling interviews and communicating with candidates
 Coordinate new employee onboarding, ensuring a smooth transition into the team
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date employee records and databases
 Provide administrative support to the HR team as needed
 Develop and implement HR initiatives that promote diversity, equity, and inclusion

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
 1-2 years of experience in an HR role
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
