Company: Johnson Ltd
Role: Human Resources Associate

About Us:
Johnson Ltd is a leading organization dedicated to providing exceptional service and products to our customers. We believe in fostering a culture of inclusivity, respect, and empathy, where everyone can thrive and grow.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Human Resources Associate to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for providing administrative support to our HR department, ensuring seamless day-to-day operations, and contributing to the development and implementation of HR initiatives.

Responsibilities:

 Assist in the recruitment process, including scheduling interviews and maintaining accurate records
 Develop and maintain employee files, ensuring compliance with company policies and regulatory requirements
 Provide support in the development and execution of projects, ensuring timely completion and high-quality outcomes
 Collaborate with the HR team to design and implement training programs, promoting employee growth and development
 Foster a positive and inclusive work environment, ensuring all employees feel valued and respected

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
 1-2 years of experience in an HR role, preferably in a fast-paced environment
 Excellent organizational, communication, and interpersonal skills
 Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion
 Strong knowledge of HR principles, practices, and legislation

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $65,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Generous paid time off, including vacation days, sick leave, and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A dynamic and inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes work-life balance

We welcome individuals from the LGBTQ community to join us and contribute to our mission of creating a workplace where everyone can thrive.
