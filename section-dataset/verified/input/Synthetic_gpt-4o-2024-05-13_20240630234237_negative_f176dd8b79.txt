Company: Butler, Buck and Jimenez

Role: Public Health Epidemiologist

Company Background: Butler, Buck and Jimenez is a leading consulting firm specializing in public health. We pride ourselves on delivering innovative and effective health solutions to our clients.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description: 
We are seeking a dynamic Public Health Epidemiologist to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for designing and conducting epidemiological studies, analyzing health data, and developing strategies to control and prevent disease outbreaks.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement epidemiological studies.
- Analyze and interpret health data to identify trends and patterns.
- Develop strategies and recommendations for disease prevention and control.
- Collaborate with public health officials, healthcare providers, and community organizations.
- Prepare and present reports on findings to stakeholders.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- A Master's degree or higher in Public Health, Epidemiology, or a related field.
- At least 3 years of experience in epidemiological research.
- Strong analytical and statistical skills.
- He should have excellent interpersonal skills and the ability to work in a team environment.
- Experience in field work and data collection is essential.
- Looking for a motivated and dynamic individual who can work under pressure and meet tight deadlines.
