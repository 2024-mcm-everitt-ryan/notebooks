Company: Taylor PLC

Job Title: Project Development Coordinator

About Us:
Taylor PLC is a leading project management firm that delivers innovative solutions to clients worldwide. We are committed to excellence and strive to create a dynamic work environment that fosters growth and development.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Project Development Coordinator to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for coordinating project activities, developing project plans, and ensuring timely delivery of projects. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate project activities, including scheduling meetings and tracking progress
 Develop project plans, including setting goals, objectives, and timelines
 Ensure timely delivery of projects, including monitoring progress and identifying potential roadblocks
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure project goals are met
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date project records and reports

Requirements:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Project Management, or a related field
 At least 2 years of experience in project coordination or a related field
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Ability to stand for long periods of time and work in a noisy environment
 Must be able to clean and organize office spaces as needed

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £40,000 to £55,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings plan, and paid time off.
