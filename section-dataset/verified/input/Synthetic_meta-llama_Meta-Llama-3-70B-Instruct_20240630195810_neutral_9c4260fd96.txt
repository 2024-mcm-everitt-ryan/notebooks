Field Outreach Specialist
Holmes-Moore

Company Background:
Holmes-Moore is a leading organization dedicated to providing innovative solutions to improve community engagement. We value diversity, equity, and inclusion in all aspects of our work.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Field Outreach Specialist to join our team. As a Field Outreach Specialist, you will be responsible for developing and implementing outreach strategies to engage with local communities, fostering partnerships, and promoting our programs.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and execute outreach plans to reach diverse community groups
 Build and maintain relationships with community partners and stakeholders
 Conduct outreach and engagement activities, including presentations, workshops, and events
 Collaborate with internal teams to develop program materials and resources
 Monitor and evaluate outreach efforts, making recommendations for improvement

Qualifications and Experience:

 Experience in community outreach, engagement, or a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Experience in operations management is desirable
 Experience in quality assurance is advantageous

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as opportunities for professional development and growth.
