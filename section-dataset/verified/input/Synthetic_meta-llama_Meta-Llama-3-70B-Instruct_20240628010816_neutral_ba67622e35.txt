Company Background:
Ferguson Group is a leading manufacturer of high-quality steel products, committed to delivering exceptional results and building strong relationships with our customers. We pride ourselves on our inclusive work environment, where all employees are valued and respected.

Job Type:
Steel Worker

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Steel Worker to join our production team. As a Steel Worker, you will be responsible for operating and maintaining steel production machinery, ensuring the highest quality of our products. If you have a strong work ethic, attention to detail, and a passion for working with steel, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Operate and maintain steel production machinery
 Monitor and control steel production processes
 Ensure compliance with safety protocols and quality control measures
 Collaborate with team members to achieve production targets
 Participate in training and development programs to improve skills and knowledge

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in steel production or a related field
 Strong mechanical aptitude and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet production deadlines
 Valid certification in steel production or a related field (an asset)

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Opportunities for career advancement and professional development
