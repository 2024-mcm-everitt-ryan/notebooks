Company Background:
Contreras, Roberson and Murphy is a leading construction firm specializing in high-quality residential and commercial projects. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has earned us a stellar reputation in the industry.

Job Type: Full-time

Role: Taper

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Taper to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have experience in preparing drywall for painting, ensuring a seamless and smooth finish. This role is essential to delivering the high standards our clients expect.

Responsibilities:
- Apply tape to seams between drywall panels.
- Smooth out excess compound.
- Ensure that all surfaces are prepped for painting.
- Work closely with other team members to meet project deadlines.
- Adhere to safety regulations and company policies.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a Taper.
- Familiarity with different types of drywall and finishing techniques.
- Attention to detail and commitment to quality.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Strong communication skills.
