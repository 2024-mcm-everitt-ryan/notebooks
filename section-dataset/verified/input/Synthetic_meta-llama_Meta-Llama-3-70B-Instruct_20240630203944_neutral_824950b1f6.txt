Job Title: Lead Design Engineer
Company: Miller LLC

Company Background: Miller LLC is a leading design and engineering firm committed to creating innovative solutions that transform industries. Our team of talented professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional results, and we're looking for a Lead Design Engineer to join our team.

Job Description: As a Lead Design Engineer, you will be responsible for leading the design and development of new products and features. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify design requirements, develop prototypes, and test solutions. Your expertise in design engineering will help us drive innovation and growth.

Responsibilities:

 Lead the design and development of new products and features
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify design requirements and develop prototypes
 Conduct testing and analysis to ensure design solutions meet requirements
 Develop and maintain design documentation and standards
 Mentor junior design engineers and provide guidance on design principles and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, Industrial Design, or related field
 Minimum 5 years of experience in design engineering, with a focus on product development
 Proven track record of leading design teams and delivering successful projects
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills
 Experience with CAD software and design tools

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $90,000 to $120,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance and retirement benefits
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Flexible work arrangements, including remote work options
 Access to cutting-edge design tools and technologies
