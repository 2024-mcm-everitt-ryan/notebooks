Company: Gould, Schneider and Barker

Role: Facility Operations Custodial Assistant

Company Background:  
Gould, Schneider and Barker is a leading provider of comprehensive facility services for a variety of industries. We pride ourselves on maintaining high standards of cleanliness, efficiency, and safety in all the facilities we manage.

Job Type:  
Full-Time

Job Description:  
We are seeking a dynamic and digitally proficient Facility Operations Custodial Assistant to join our team. The ideal candidate will bring fresh insights and a modern approach to custodial tasks, ensuring the cleanliness and proper maintenance of our facilities.

Responsibilities:  
- Perform routine cleaning and maintenance tasks in accordance with company standards.
- Utilize digital tools and software to track and report on equipment maintenance and inventory.
- Assist in the development and implementation of custodial schedules and procedures.
- Communicate effectively with team members and supervisors to ensure efficient operations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Recent education in facility management or a related field preferred.
- Demonstrated ability to bring fresh ideas to the table.
- Proficiency in using digital tools and software for facility management.
- Strong communication skills and a collaborative mindset.
- Ability to perform physical tasks related to custodial duties.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive salary ranging from 30,000 to 45,000 USD per year, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Opportunities for career advancement within the company.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.

How to Apply:  
Interested candidates should submit their resume and a cover letter detailing their relevant experience and fresh educational background.
