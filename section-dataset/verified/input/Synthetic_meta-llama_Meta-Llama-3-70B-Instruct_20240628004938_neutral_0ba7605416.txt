Newman LLC is seeking an experienced Earth Driller to join our team. As an Earth Driller, you will be responsible for operating drilling equipment, maintaining safety standards, and collaborating with our team to achieve project goals.

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as an Earth Driller
 Valid certification in drilling operations and safety protocols
 Strong communication and teamwork skills
