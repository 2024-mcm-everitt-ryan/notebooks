Highly motivated with ability to work effectively with multidisciplinary teams or independently. Strong team player. Good proactive approach & manner.

Education Required:
- Bachelors Degree in Engineering, Pharmaceutical Sciences, or related Science discipline.

Experience Required:

  - Cycle time and yield improvement initiatives.
  - Continuous improvement projects.
