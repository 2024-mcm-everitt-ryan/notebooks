Company: Chambers, Henry and Mitchell

Job Title: Retail Buyer

About Us:
Chambers, Henry and Mitchell is a global retail company dedicated to providing high-quality products to our customers. We believe in fostering an inclusive and respectful work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities for all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a Retail Buyer to join our dynamic team. As a Retail Buyer, you will be responsible for sourcing and purchasing products that meet our customers' needs. You will work closely with our suppliers, logistics team, and store managers to ensure timely and efficient delivery of products.

Responsibilities:

 Source and purchase products that meet customer demand and company standards
 Develop and maintain relationships with suppliers
 Negotiate prices and terms with suppliers
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure product availability and quality
 Analyze sales data and market trends to inform purchasing decisions

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in retail buying or a related field
 Strong analytical and negotiation skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Bachelor's degree in Business, Retail, or a related field

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
