Company: Mcknight Group
Role: Insulation Worker

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Insulation Worker to join our team at Mcknight Group. As an Insulation Worker, you will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing insulation systems in various settings. This is an excellent opportunity to work with a dynamic team and develop your skills in the insulation industry.

Responsibilities:

 Install, maintain, and repair insulation systems in accordance with safety regulations and industry standards
 Collaborate with coworkers to complete tasks efficiently and effectively
 Conduct regular inspections to identify and address potential issues
 Develop and implement solutions to improve insulation system performance
 Maintain a clean and organized work environment

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in insulation work or a related field
 Ability to work at heights for long periods
 Strong problem-solving and communication skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our team embraces a culture of encouragement and unity, and we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
