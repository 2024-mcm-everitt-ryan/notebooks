Company Background:

Sanchez-Gomez is a leading non-profit organization dedicated to providing comprehensive case management services to underserved communities. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and supportive work environment where diverse perspectives are valued and respected.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:

As a Case Monitor Supervisor, you will oversee a team of case monitors, ensuring that all case management activities are conducted effectively and in compliance with organizational policies. You will play a critical role in supporting our mission by providing guidance, mentorship, and supervision to your team.

Responsibilities:

- Supervise and support a team of case monitors.
- Ensure compliance with all relevant regulations and organizational policies.
- Provide training and development opportunities for team members.
- Conduct regular performance evaluations and provide constructive feedback.
- Coordinate with other departments to ensure seamless service delivery.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:

- Bachelor's degree in Social Work, Psychology, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in case management, with at least 2 years in a supervisory role.
- Strong leadership and communication skills.
- Ability to work effectively with diverse populations.
- Commitment to creating an inclusive and supportive work environment.

Perks and Benefits:

- Competitive salary, ranging from $50,000 to $70,000 based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Retirement plan with employer matching.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.

Company Values:

At Sanchez-Gomez, we value the diverse experiences that our employees bring. We support diverse family structures within our team, respect and support all forms of gender expression, and are committed to creating a supportive environment for the LGBTQ2+ community. We provide a respectful environment for all people.
