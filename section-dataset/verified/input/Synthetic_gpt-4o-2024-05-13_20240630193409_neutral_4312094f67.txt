Company Background:
Trujillo, Phelps and Michael is a leading engineering firm specializing in the energy sector. With a robust portfolio spanning oil and gas, renewable energy, and environmentally sustainable projects, we pride ourselves on innovation, quality, and a commitment to excellence.

Role: Petroleum Engineer

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Petroleum Engineer to join our growing team. The ideal candidate will have experience in the exploration, extraction, and production of oil and gas. They will be responsible for designing and implementing effective methods to improve oil and gas well production. 

Responsibilities:
- Analyze geological data to determine the best drilling methods.
- Design and develop extraction plans that maximize efficiency and safety.
- Monitor and evaluate wells to ensure optimal performance.
- Collaborate with geologists, geophysicists, and environmental scientists to minimize environmental impact.
- Prepare detailed reports and presentations on project status and outcomes.
- Ensure compliance with all regulatory requirements.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Petroleum Engineering or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in the oil and gas industry.
- Strong analytical skills are a must for this position.
- Proficiency in data analysis and computer modeling software.
- Experience in managing budgets is required.
- Candidates should have a solid understanding of regulatory requirements.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Flexible work environment.
