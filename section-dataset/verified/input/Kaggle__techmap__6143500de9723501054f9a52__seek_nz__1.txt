Electrix Limited is a high profile, long term leader in the Australasian electrical and gas contracting markets. Electrix Limited is also one of New Zealand's foremost electrical construction and maintenance companies and provides a wide range of services to the power, electricity, gas, industrial and commercial sectors.

To keep up with the demand for Line Mechanic's in New Zealand, Electrix offers Trainee Line Mechanic apprenticeship based in Auckland (Albany and Silverdale). No experience is required as training will be provided. The two-year apprenticeship is a full-time position where you will get on the job training working alongside our experienced Line Mechanics within various departments within the Perimeter. Through your apprenticeship, you will attend block courses working towards a New Zealand Certificate in Electricity Supply (Line Mechanic Distribution, Level 4).

Ideally, you will have:
- A commitment to safety.
- An interest in the electrical industry.
- Enjoy working outdoors.
- Physically fit as the job involves hands-on physical work and climbing.
- A School Certificate of NCEA Level 1 equivalent with passes in Mathematics and English is preferable.
- A current full New Zealand driver's licence.
- Must be a New Zealand Citizen or a Resident.

In return, we offer you a vibrant & challenging team environment, career prospects, and competitive remuneration. In accordance with the Company's Drug & Alcohol Policy, the successful applicants will be required to undergo a pre-employment drug test and medical.
