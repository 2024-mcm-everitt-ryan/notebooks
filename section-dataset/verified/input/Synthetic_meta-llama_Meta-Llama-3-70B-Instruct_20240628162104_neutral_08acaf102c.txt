Company: Payne Ltd
Role: Furniture Finisher

We are seeking a skilled and detail-oriented Furniture Finisher to join our team at Payne Ltd. As a Furniture Finisher, you will be responsible for applying finishes to furniture pieces, ensuring high-quality results that meet our company's standards.

Responsibilities:

 Apply various types of finishes to furniture, including stains, paints, and lacquers
 Operate finishing equipment, such as sprayers and sanders
 Inspect finished pieces for quality and accuracy
 Collaborate with other team members to meet production deadlines

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in furniture finishing or a related field
 Strong attention to detail and ability to work accurately
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and stand for long periods
 High school diploma or equivalent required
