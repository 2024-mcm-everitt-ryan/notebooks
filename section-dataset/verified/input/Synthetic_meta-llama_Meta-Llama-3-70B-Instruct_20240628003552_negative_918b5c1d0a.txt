Company: Johnson, Hall and Watts
Job Title: Data Management Assistant

We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Data Management Assistant to join our team. As a Data Management Assistant, you will play a crucial role in maintaining and improving our data management systems.

Job Description:
The successful candidate will be responsible for managing daily operations, ensuring smooth workflow, and providing administrative support to our data management team. The ideal candidate should have extensive experience in project management and be proficient in data analysis software.

Responsibilities:

 Manage daily operations and ensure smooth workflow
 Provide administrative support to the data management team
 Analyze and interpret data to inform business decisions
 Develop and implement data management procedures
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to achieve business objectives

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
 At least 2 years of experience in data management or a related field
 Proficiency in data analysis software and project management tools
 Excellent organizational and communication skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.

Note: We have a traditional family structure and prioritize supporting our employees' family needs. We believe in the importance of work-life balance, especially for those with traditional family responsibilities. We also support employees undergoing a sex change to ensure they feel comfortable in their new roles.
