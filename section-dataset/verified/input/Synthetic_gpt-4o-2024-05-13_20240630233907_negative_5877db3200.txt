Company Background:
Blanchard-Smith is a reputable organization committed to fostering inclusive and vibrant communities. We pride ourselves on delivering excellent services and support to our clients through community-focused initiatives.

Job Title: Community Assistant

Job Description:
We are seeking a Community Assistant to support our community outreach and engagement efforts. The ideal candidate will have a strong work ethic, excellent communication skills, and a passion for community service.

Responsibilities:
- Assist in planning and executing community events and programs.
- Provide support to community members and address their inquiries.
- Maintain and update community resource materials.
- Collaborate with team members to enhance community engagement.
- Manage administrative tasks related to community projects.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Previous experience in community service or a related field is preferred.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to handle a fast-paced work environment.
- Self-reliant and able to work independently.
- Attention to detail and excellent organizational skills.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $30,000 to $45,000 per year, based on experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.

Application Process:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and cover letter detailing their relevant experience and why they are a good fit for this role.
