Summary:

Catholic Charities Archdiocese of New Orleans (CCANO) will provide assistance to hurricane IDA survivors in the most affected parishes covered by the agency. The Construction Cost Analysts is an integral part of the disaster recovery efforts and will be responsible for developing estimates for damaged homes utilizing Xactimate software. Estimates will be shared with survivors to help them understand the cost of their repairs, support the short term and long term work of disaster case managers, and help the agency plan for the scope of services and cost of the intervention.

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:

This position requires strong organizational, relational, and communication skills plus the ability to manage multiple projects at the same time. The Construction Cost Analyst must be very well versed in the construction field. S/H will be working in a fast-paced environment and must be able to provide excellent customer service under these circumstances. Proficiency in Excel and Word is essential, knowledge of Xactimate is highly preferred and/or ability to learn this software quickly with minimum guidance. Cost estimator is also expected to maintain records of each household served and must be very comfortable using or learning to use our agency data tracking system. S/He must be comfortable working with a diverse team and be able to make presentations to large groups when presentation of cases requires his/her assistance. Applicant should have demonstrated the ability to relate well to people of diverse ethnic and economic backgrounds and have a commitment to CCANO's mission. Reliable transportation and the ability to be mobile is a must since the majority of time will be spent in different parishes.

Minimum Qualifications:

- Minimum of 5 years experience in general construction and experience with hurricane-damaged dwellings preferred.
- Basic knowledge of plumbing, electrical, and HVAC systems and the Universal Standard Building Codes is required.
- Ability to do an itemized estimate using computerized software, preferably Xactimate.

Essential Functions:

- Complete estimates for hurricane-damaged homes utilizing Xactimate software as requested by case management agencies participating in the disaster response.
- Support case managers when using estimates for appeals and case presentation.
- Follow guidelines as set out in the Spirit of Hope (SOH) director and/or case manager supervisor in preparing and submitting estimates.
- Follow local and state building codes in preparing estimates.
- Work with case managers and clients to review and understand estimates.
