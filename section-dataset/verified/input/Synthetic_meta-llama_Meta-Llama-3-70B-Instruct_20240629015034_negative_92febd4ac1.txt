Company: Anthony-Brown

Job Title: Senior Public Health Inspector

About Us:
Anthony-Brown is a leading healthcare organization dedicated to promoting public health and well-being. We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Public Health Inspector to join our team.

Job Description:
The Senior Public Health Inspector will be responsible for conducting inspections and investigations to ensure compliance with public health regulations. The ideal candidate will have excellent analytical and communication skills, with the ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct inspections and investigations to identify public health risks and hazards
 Develop and implement corrective action plans to mitigate risks
 Collaborate with other departments to ensure compliance with public health regulations
 Provide education and training to the public and stakeholders on public health issues

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Environmental Health, Public Health, or a related field
 5+ years of experience in public health inspection or a related field
 Valid certification as a Public Health Inspector (CPHI)

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $70,000 to $90,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement plan.

Note: Please ensure you meet the necessary qualifications and have a strong understanding of public health regulations before applying. We thank all applicants for their interest; however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
