Company: Greer, Ibarra and Henry  
Role: Physical Therapist Assistant  

Company Background:  
Greer, Ibarra and Henry is a leading healthcare provider dedicated to delivering high-quality physical therapy services. Our team is committed to helping patients achieve their health goals through personalized care and expertise.

Job Type:  
Full-time

Job Description:  
We are seeking a dedicated Physical Therapist Assistant to join our team. The ideal candidate will work closely with our licensed physical therapists to provide exceptional care to our patients. This role involves assisting with patient treatments, maintaining patient records, and ensuring a supportive and efficient clinic environment.

Responsibilities:  
- Assist physical therapists in implementing treatment plans for patients
- Document patient progress and responses to treatment
- Maintain a clean and organized treatment area
- Provide patients with instructions on therapeutic exercises
- Collaborate with the healthcare team to optimize patient outcomes

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Associate degree from an accredited Physical Therapist Assistant program
- Current state license or eligibility for licensure as a Physical Therapist Assistant
- CPR and First Aid certification
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Must be willing to participate in family-friendly company events
- Seeking candidates who are comfortable with a traditionally-oriented environment

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive salary ($45,000 - $55,000 per year, based on experience and skills)
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
- Access to mother rooms for privacy and comfort
- Opportunities for professional development and continuing education
- Paid time off and holiday pay
