Company: Peters LLC

Role: Photographer

Peters LLC is a leading creative agency specializing in capturing stunning visuals and providing top-notch photography services. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity and inclusion, and we aim to create an environment where everyone feels valued.

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented and passionate photographer to join our creative team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for capturing high-quality images that align with our clients' expectations and brand standards.

- Retouch and edit photos as needed to ensure high-quality results.
- Collaborate with clients to understand their vision and requirements.
- Manage and organize a portfolio of digital assets.
- Stay updated with the latest photography trends and techniques.
- Assist in marketing and promotional efforts through high-quality visual content.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a photographer, with a strong portfolio.
- Proficiency in photo editing software such as Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom.
- Strong attention to detail and creativity.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Flexibility to work on weekends or evenings as needed.
