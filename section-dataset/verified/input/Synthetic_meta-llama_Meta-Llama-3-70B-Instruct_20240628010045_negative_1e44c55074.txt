Job Title: Meeting and Convention Planner

Company: Werner-Dixon

About Us:
Werner-Dixon is a leading event management company specializing in corporate conferences and meetings. We pride ourselves on our exceptional organizational skills and attention to detail, ensuring that every event is a success.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Meeting and Convention Planner to join our team. As a Meeting and Convention Planner, you will be responsible for planning, coordinating, and executing corporate events, conferences, and meetings. Your expertise in event management will ensure that our clients' expectations are exceeded, and their events are memorable and successful.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate and plan logistics for corporate events, conferences, and meetings
 Develop and manage event budgets
 Liaise with clients to understand their event requirements and preferences
 Negotiate with vendors and suppliers to secure the best deals
 Ensure events are executed according to plan, on time, and within budget

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Hospitality, Event Management, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in event planning and management
 Proven track record of successfully planning and executing events
 Excellent communication, organizational, and project management skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off. Our benefits are designed to support the well-being of all our employees, especially moms and dads who are balancing work and family life.
