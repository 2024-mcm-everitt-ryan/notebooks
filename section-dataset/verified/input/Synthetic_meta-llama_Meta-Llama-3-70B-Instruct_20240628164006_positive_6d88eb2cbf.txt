Company Background:
Salazar, Rios and Reyes is a leading urban design firm dedicated to creating sustainable and inclusive spaces that enrich communities. We are committed to diversity, equity, and inclusion in our hiring practices, ensuring our team reflects the diversity of the communities we serve.

Job Type:
Senior Urban Designer

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Urban Designer to lead our team in designing innovative and accessible public spaces. As a Senior Urban Designer, you will work closely with our clients, stakeholders, and internal teams to develop and implement urban design projects that prioritize equity, sustainability, and community engagement.

Responsibilities:

 Lead urban design projects from concept to completion, ensuring they meet our firm's high standards of quality and accessibility
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement project plans, timelines, and budgets
 Conduct site analyses, develop design concepts, and create detailed design plans and specifications
 Ensure that all designs are inclusive, accessible, and meet the needs of diverse users

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's or Master's degree in Urban Design, Architecture, or a related field
 5+ years of experience in urban design, with a focus on accessible and inclusive design
 Strong portfolio showcasing your urban design skills and experience
 Excellent communication, project management, and collaboration skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes:
 20 days of paid vacation, plus 10 paid holidays
 Medical, dental, and vision insurance
 401(k) matching program
 Professional development opportunities, including training and conference sponsorships
 Flexible work arrangements, including remote work options

Why Join Us:
Our inclusive hiring practices support candidates with disabilities. We believe that diverse perspectives and experiences enrich our team and the communities we serve. Join us in promoting inclusion and creating a more equitable and accessible built environment.
