Clay LLC is a leader in financial consulting services, specializing in risk management and actuarial services. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional value to our clients.

Job Description:
Clay LLC is seeking an experienced actuary to join our dynamic team. - Professional actuarial certifications (e.g., ASA, FSA).
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in an actuarial role.
- Strong proficiency in actuarial software and data analysis tools.
- Excellent communication and presentation skills.
