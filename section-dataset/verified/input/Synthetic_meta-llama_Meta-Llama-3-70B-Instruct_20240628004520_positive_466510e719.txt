Company Background:
Martin-Choi is a leading provider of interior design solutions, committed to creating inclusive and welcoming spaces for our clients. We pride ourselves on our diverse and vibrant team, and we're excited to expand our paperhanging department.

Job Type: Paperhanger

Job Description:
As a Paperhanger at Martin-Choi, you will be responsible for installing and removing wallpaper, borders, and other decorative finishes in residential and commercial settings. You will work closely with our design team to ensure seamless execution of design concepts and provide exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Install and remove wallpaper, borders, and other decorative finishes
 Collaborate with designers to ensure accurate implementation of design concepts
 Provide exceptional customer service and maintain a high level of professionalism
 Maintain a safe and organized work environment

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in paperhanging or a related field
 Strong attention to detail and ability to work independently
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and work at heights

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
