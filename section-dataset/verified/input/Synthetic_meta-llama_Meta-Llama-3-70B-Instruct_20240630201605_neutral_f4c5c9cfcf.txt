Job Title: Roof Bolter
Company: Pugh, Winters and Wade

Job Description:

We are seeking a skilled Roof Bolter to join our team at Pugh, Winters and Wade. As a Roof Bolter, you will be responsible for installing roof bolts to support the roof of underground mines. Your attention to detail and ability to work effectively in a team will be essential in ensuring the safety of our mining operations.

Responsibilities:

 Install roof bolts to support the roof of underground mines
 Conduct safety inspections to identify potential hazards
 Collaborate with team members to achieve operational goals
 Maintain accurate records of work completed

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a similar role
 Strong understanding of mine safety regulations
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance package
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A dynamic and inclusive work environment
