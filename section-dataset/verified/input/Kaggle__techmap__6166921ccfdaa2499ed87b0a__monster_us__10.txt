Description

Arcosa Laurel Aggregates is a leading producer and distributor in the Western and Southwestern U.S. of lightweight and natural aggregates, including expanded shale and clay, crushed stone, sand and gravel, asphalt rock, and other products.

What you'll do:
- Comply with all industry safety rules, procedures, and applicable government regulations.
- Lead the blaster helpers in loading and firing of the shot.
- Maintain an accurate count of all explosive material.
- Operate the blasting equipment in a safe and efficient manner.

What you'll need:
- Prompt, regular, and predictable attendance.
- The ability to stand, stoop, bend, lift, walk, climb stairs, ladders, and catwalks up to 60 feet as required and lift weights up to 60 pounds.
- The ability to work in a varied outdoor environment (cold, hot, dry, dusty, rainy).
- The ability to work in a man basket up to 30 feet.
- The ability to work in an underground limestone mine.
- Must be able to pass an ATF background check.
