Company: Daniel PLC

Job Title: Loan Counselor

About Us:
Daniel PLC is a leading financial institution dedicated to providing exceptional loan services to our clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to create a work environment that is respectful, collaborative, and welcoming to all.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Loan Counselor to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for providing expert guidance to clients on loan options, assessing creditworthiness, and developing personalized financial plans.

Responsibilities:
- Evaluate clients' financial information and credit history to determine loan eligibility
- Develop and present tailored loan solutions to clients
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless loan processing
- Maintain accurate records and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, or related field
- 2+ years of experience in loan counseling or a related field
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
- Competent in Mandarin (an asset)

Benefits:
- Salary range: $50,000 - $70,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Recognition and rewards for outstanding performance
