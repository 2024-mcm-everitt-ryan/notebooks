Job Description:
We're seeking an experienced Senior Windows Administrator to join our IT team. As a Senior Windows Administrator, you will be responsible for managing and maintaining our Windows-based infrastructure, ensuring high availability, security, and performance. You will work closely with our team to design, implement, and support our Windows environment, and provide technical guidance and training to junior team members.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and maintain our Windows-based infrastructure, including servers, workstations, and applications
 Ensure high availability, security, and performance of our Windows environment
 Design, implement, and support new Windows-based solutions
 Provide technical guidance and training to junior team members
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to resolve technical issues and implement new solutions

Qualifications and Experience:
 5+ years of experience in Windows administration
 Strong knowledge of Windows Server, Active Directory, and Windows troubleshooting
 Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize multiple tasks
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
