6.0 Required Skills  
6.1.1 Excellent technical, written, and verbal communication skills.  
6.1.2 Excellent planning and organization skills required for our fast-paced environment.  

7.0 Physical Requirements  
7.1.1 Ability to work in the field for site inspection, data gathering, and on-site observation of work progress.  
7.1.2 Ability to travel outside the local area, depending on local workload and project location.  
7.1.3 Ability to lift and carry materials up to 50 lbs.  
7.1.4 Ability to read construction drawings, diagrams, printed materials, and computer screens.  
7.1.5 Ability to record data clearly and write passages for reports, letters, emails, etc.  
7.1.6 Ability to safely work at heights.  
7.1.7 Ability to safely use and/or operate ladders, scaffolds, lifts, and other access equipment.
