Company: Howe Group
Role: Supervising Health Nurse

About Us:
The Howe Group is a leading healthcare organization dedicated to providing high-quality patient care. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace that values respect, empathy, and professionalism.

Job Description:
We are seeking a compassionate and experienced Supervising Health Nurse to join our team. As a Supervising Health Nurse, you will be responsible for overseeing the delivery of patient care, leading a team of healthcare professionals, and ensuring that our patients receive exceptional care.

Responsibilities:

 Supervise and coordinate the activities of healthcare professionals to ensure high-quality patient care
 Develop and implement patient care plans in collaboration with interdisciplinary teams
 Provide guidance and mentorship to nursing staff
 Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations and standards
 Participate in quality improvement initiatives to improve patient outcomes

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Nursing or related field
 Current registration with the relevant nursing regulatory body
 Minimum 5 years of experience in a healthcare setting, with at least 2 years in a supervisory role
 Strong leadership and communication skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $85,000 to $110,000 CAD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a generous retirement savings plan. Our employees can benefit from our family-friendly workplace policies, and we are dedicated to supporting the LGBTQ2+ community and all its members. Spouses and partners of employees are included in our benefits.
