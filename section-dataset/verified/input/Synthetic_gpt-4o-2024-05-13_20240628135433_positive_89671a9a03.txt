Company Background:
Gutierrez, Butler and Hall is a prestigious legal firm known for its commitment to justice and equality. With decades of experience, we serve a diverse clientele, providing top-notch legal representation and advice.

Job Type:
Full-Time Court Representative

Job Description:
As a Court Representative at Gutierrez, Butler and Hall, you will be responsible for representing clients in court, preparing legal documents, and liaising with clients to ensure their cases are handled efficiently. This role requires attention to detail, excellent communication skills, and the ability to work under pressure.

Responsibilities:
- Represent clients in court proceedings.
- Prepare and file legal documents.
- Communicate with clients to understand their needs and provide legal advice.
- Coordinate with legal teams to develop case strategies.
- Maintain up-to-date knowledge of laws and regulations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Law or related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in a legal setting.
- Strong understanding of court procedures and legal documentation.
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $80,000 based on your experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Retirement savings plan.
- Paid time off and holiday benefits.
- Professional development opportunities.
