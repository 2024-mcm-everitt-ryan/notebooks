Dentist Position at Merritt, Grant and Griffin

Company Background: Merritt, Grant and Griffin is a reputable dental clinic dedicated to providing high-quality oral care to our patients. We pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art facilities and innovative approach to dentistry.

Job Type: Full-time Dentist

Job Description: We are seeking a skilled and experienced Dentist to join our team. As a Dentist at Merritt, Grant and Griffin, you will be responsible for examining patients, diagnosing oral health issues, and developing treatment plans. You will work closely with our team of dental assistants and hygienists to provide exceptional patient care.

Responsibilities:

 Examine patients to diagnose oral health issues and develop treatment plans
 Perform routine dental procedures such as fillings, extractions, and cleanings
 Collaborate with dental assistants and hygienists to provide patient care
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date patient records
 Stay current with industry developments and best practices in dentistry

Qualifications and Experience:

 Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) or Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD) degree from a "prestigious" institution
 3+ years of experience in a clinical setting
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work well under pressure and manage a high-volume patient load

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $120,000 to $150,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 A dynamic and supportive work environment
