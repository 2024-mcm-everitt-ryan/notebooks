Steel Workers at Campbell Group

Company Background:
Campbell Group is a leading steel manufacturing company dedicated to producing high-quality steel products for various industries. We prioritize diversity, equity, and inclusion in our workforce, fostering a culture of respect and opportunities for all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking skilled Steel Workers to join our team. As a Steel Worker, you will be responsible for operating and maintaining steel production equipment, ensuring efficient and safe operations. You will work closely with our production team to meet production targets and maintain excellent product quality.

Responsibilities:

 Operate and maintain steel production equipment, including furnaces, rolling mills, and finishing lines
 Monitor and control production processes to ensure high-quality products
 Collaborate with production team members to achieve production targets
 Perform routine maintenance tasks to prevent equipment downtime
 Follow safety protocols to ensure a safe working environment

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in steel production or a related field
 Strong mechanical aptitude and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced, physically demanding environment
 High school diploma or equivalent required; post-secondary education in a related field an asset

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. You will also receive:

 Comprehensive health insurance
 401(k) matching program
 Paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth

Our Commitment to Inclusion:
We invite candidates with disabilities to apply and contribute to our diverse workforce. Our inclusive hiring practices support candidates with disabilities, and we provide reasonable accommodations to ensure equal opportunities for all.
