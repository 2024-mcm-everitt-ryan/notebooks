Reynolds-Bennett is seeking a qualified Staff Attorney to join our team. As a Staff Attorney, you will be responsible for providing legal counsel and support to our clients. This is an excellent opportunity to work with a dynamic team and contribute to the growth and success of our organization.

Job Description:
The Staff Attorney will provide legal advice and guidance to our clients, conduct legal research, and draft legal documents. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication and analytical skills, with the ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Provide legal counsel and support to clients
 Conduct legal research and analyze complex legal issues
 Draft legal documents, including contracts and agreements
 Collaborate with other attorneys and staff members
 Stay up-to-date with changes in the law and industry trends

Qualifications and Experience:

 Juris Doctor (J.D.) degree from an accredited law school
 Admission to practice law in the relevant jurisdiction
 At least 2 years of experience as an attorney
 Excellent communication and analytical skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $120,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
