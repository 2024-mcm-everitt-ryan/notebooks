Company Background:
Elliott LLC is a leading energy consulting firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions to our clients. 

Job Type: Energy Program Analyst

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and results-driven Energy Program Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in energy management and a passion for driving sustainability initiatives. 

Responsibilities:
The Energy Program Analyst will be responsible for analyzing energy usage patterns, identifying areas of improvement, and developing strategies to optimize energy efficiency. The successful candidate will have excellent analytical skills, be able to work independently, and have strong communication skills to effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
Bachelor's degree in a relevant field (e.g., energy management, environmental science) from a top-tier university. At least 2 years of experience in energy management or a related field. Strong proficiency in energy analysis software and tools.

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary package, ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per annum, based on experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
