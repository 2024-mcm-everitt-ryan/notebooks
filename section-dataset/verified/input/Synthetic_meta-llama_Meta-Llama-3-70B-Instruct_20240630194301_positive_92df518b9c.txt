Company: Aguilar, Delacruz and Jones

Job Title: Medical Technician

About Us:
Aguilar, Delacruz and Jones is a leading healthcare provider dedicated to delivering exceptional patient care. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment that celebrates the unique perspectives and skills of our team members.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Medical Technician to join our team. As a Medical Technician, you will be responsible for conducting laboratory tests, analyzing results, and providing accurate diagnoses to patients. You will work closely with our healthcare professionals to ensure timely and effective patient care.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct laboratory tests and analyze results
 Provide accurate diagnoses and recommendations to healthcare professionals
 Maintain accurate records and reports
 Collaborate with healthcare professionals to ensure timely and effective patient care

Qualifications and Experience:

 Associate's degree in Medical Technology or related field
 Certification as a Medical Laboratory Technician (MLT) or Medical Technologist (MT)
 2+ years of experience in a clinical laboratory setting
 Strong analytical and communication skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.

Women in STEM:
We recognize the importance of diversity in the STEM field and are committed to creating opportunities for women to succeed. We encourage women with relevant expertise to apply and join our team of dedicated healthcare professionals.
