Company: Nguyen-Kim

Job Title: Mixed Signal IC Validation Engineer

About Us:
Nguyen-Kim is a leading semiconductor company that designs, manufactures, and supplies a broad range of analog and mixed-signal integrated circuits. We are committed to delivering high-quality products and services to our customers.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Mixed Signal IC Validation Engineer to join our team. As a Mixed Signal IC Validation Engineer, you will be responsible for validating and characterizing mixed-signal ICs to ensure they meet the required specifications and standards.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and execute validation plans and procedures to ensure the quality and reliability of mixed-signal ICs
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including design, product, and manufacturing engineering to identify and resolve issues
- Analyze and troubleshoot complex mixed-signal IC issues using various debugging tools and techniques
- Develop and maintain technical documentation, including test plans, test reports, and design specifications

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or a related field
- At least 3 years of experience in mixed-signal IC validation or a related field
- Strong knowledge of mixed-signal IC design and validation principles
- Proficiency in programming languages, such as C, Python, or MATLAB
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills

Benefits:
- Salary range: $90,000 - $120,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment

Nguyen-Kim is an equal opportunities employer and welcomes applications from diverse candidates.
