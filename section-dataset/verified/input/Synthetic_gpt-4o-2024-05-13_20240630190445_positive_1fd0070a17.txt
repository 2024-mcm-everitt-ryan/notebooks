Company Background:

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
Robinson-Willis is seeking a dedicated and skilled Radiation Therapist to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for administering radiation therapy to patients as prescribed by a physician, ensuring the highest standards of care and safety.

Responsibilities:
- Administer radiation treatments to patients following the physician's prescription.
- Monitor patients during treatment for any adverse physical reactions.
- Maintain accurate records of treatment administered.
- Collaborate with the medical team to plan and execute treatment strategies.
- Provide emotional support to patients and their families throughout the treatment process.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Radiation Therapy or a related field.
- Valid certification and licensure as a Radiation Therapist.
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in radiation therapy.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Women with relevant expertise are encouraged to apply.

- Employee wellness programs.
