Sanitation Engineer at Nelson-Knapp

Company Background:
Nelson-Knapp is a leading environmental services company dedicated to providing innovative solutions for a cleaner and healthier planet. We strive to create a diverse and inclusive work environment that fosters growth, collaboration, and creativity.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Sanitation Engineer to join our team. As a Sanitation Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining sanitation systems that meet the highest standards of quality and safety. Your expertise will play a critical role in ensuring our operations are efficient, effective, and environmentally sustainable.

Responsibilities:

 Design and develop sanitation systems that meet regulatory requirements and industry standards
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless project execution
 Conduct site visits to assess sanitation needs and provide recommendations for improvement
 Develop and implement maintenance schedules to ensure optimal system performance
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices to continuously improve our sanitation systems

Qualifications and Experience:
We seek individuals with a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Engineering, Civil Engineering, or a related field. A minimum of 3 years of experience in sanitation engineering is required. Strong problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and excellent communication skills are essential for success in this role.

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, a retirement matching program, and paid time off.
