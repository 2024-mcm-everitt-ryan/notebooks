Company: Castaneda-Hodges  
Role: Strategic Planning Associate

Company Background:  
Castaneda-Hodges is a leading consulting firm specializing in strategic planning and business development. With decades of experience, our mission is to provide exceptional insights and strategies that drive growth and sustainability for our clients.

Job Type:  
Full-time

Job Description:  
We are seeking a meticulous and results-oriented Strategic Planning Associate to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for conducting research, analyzing market trends, and collaborating with senior consultants to develop strategic plans for our clients.

Responsibilities:  
- Conduct comprehensive research and analysis on industry trends and market conditions.  
- Develop and present strategic recommendations to clients.  
- Assist in the preparation of reports, proposals, and presentations.  
- Collaborate with team members to ensure the successful implementation of strategic initiatives.  
- Maintain strict confidentiality and adhere to company policies and guidelines.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor’s degree in Business, Economics, or a related field from a reputable institution.  
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in strategic planning or a similar role.  
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.  
- Excellent communication and presentation skills.  
- Must be able to work without taking frequent breaks and show an exceptional level of dedication.  
- Candidates should respect and follow our company’s religious guidelines.  
- Applicants must be childless and willing to work long hours.  
- We don’t accept applications from people with criminal records.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive salary ranging from £40,000 to £55,000 annually, based on experience and skills.  
- Comprehensive health insurance plan.  
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.  
- Flexible working hours.

How to Apply:  
Interested candidates should send their resume along with a cover letter detailing their experience and suitability for the role to careers@castaneda-hodges.com. Please note that only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
