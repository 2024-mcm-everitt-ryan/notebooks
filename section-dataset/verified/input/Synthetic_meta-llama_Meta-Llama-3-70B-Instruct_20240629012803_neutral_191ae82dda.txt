Company: Hutchinson, Young and Wilson

Role: Maintenance Worker

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and proactive maintenance worker to join our team. As a maintenance worker, you will be responsible for performing routine maintenance tasks, repairs, and upkeep of our facilities and equipment. You will work closely with our operations team to ensure a safe and efficient working environment.

Responsibilities:

 Perform routine maintenance tasks, including repairs and upkeep of facilities and equipment
 Collaborate with operations team to identify and address maintenance needs
 Troubleshoot and resolve equipment malfunctions
 Maintain accurate records of maintenance activities and repairs
 Support a culture of open dialogue and respect

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience in maintenance or a related field
 Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 We value individuals with diverse spiritual beliefs and experiences

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program.
