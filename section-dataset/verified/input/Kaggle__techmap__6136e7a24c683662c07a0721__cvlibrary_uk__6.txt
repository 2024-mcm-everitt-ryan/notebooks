Site Secretary

The Company

This Site Secretary’s role is with one of the UK’s leading main contractors. Primarily operating in the residential and hotel sectors, they are known for building and refurbishing some of the capital's most high-profile projects.

The Role

We are looking for a Site Secretary who has previous experience of working on £multi-million construction projects in a site-based/site office-based role. You will be working alongside the senior management team, providing both secretarial and PA services on this 3-year £130m project. Ideally, you will have some experience of document control systems (4P), be reliable, and a team player.

The Plus Points

This is a great opportunity to join a leading contractor that is experiencing a boom in workload, who can offer long-term careers with good prospects for progression. A competitive salary is also on offer.
