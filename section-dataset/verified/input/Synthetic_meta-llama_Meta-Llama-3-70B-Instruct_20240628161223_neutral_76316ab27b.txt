Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a relevant field (e.g., engineering, environmental science)
 Minimum 5 years of experience in project management, with a focus on solar energy or renewable energy projects
 Proven track record of successfully managing large-scale projects
 Strong leadership and communication skills
