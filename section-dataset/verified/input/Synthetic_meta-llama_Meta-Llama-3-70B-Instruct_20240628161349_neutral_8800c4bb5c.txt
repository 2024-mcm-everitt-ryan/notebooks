Dunn, Bishop and Garner is seeking a Parking Enforcement Officer to join our team. As a Parking Enforcement Officer, you will be responsible for patrolling assigned areas to ensure compliance with parking regulations, issuing parking citations, and providing excellent customer service to the public.

We are an equal opportunity employer committed to diversity and inclusion. Our office is fully accessible to all employees, and we provide training on accessibility and inclusion to ensure our workplace is welcoming to everyone.

Responsibilities:

 Patrol assigned areas on foot or by vehicle to identify parking violations
 Issue parking citations and warnings in accordance with municipal regulations
 Provide excellent customer service to the public, answering questions and responding to concerns
 Maintain accurate records of parking citations and warnings
 Collaborate with other departments to achieve parking enforcement goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience in parking enforcement, customer service, or a related field
 Ability to work outdoors in various weather conditions
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to lift up to 25 pounds occasionally

We offer:

 A competitive salary ranging from $40,000 to $55,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A dynamic and inclusive work environment

If you are a motivated and customer-focused individual who is passionate about ensuring public safety, we encourage you to apply.
