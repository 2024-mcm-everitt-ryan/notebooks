Company: Rosales LLC
Role: Senior Design Engineer

About Us:
Rosales LLC is a leading design and engineering firm dedicated to creating innovative solutions for a better tomorrow. We value diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to create a workplace where everyone feels welcome and empowered to succeed.

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented Senior Design Engineer to join our team. As a Senior Design Engineer, you will be responsible for leading design projects from concept to completion, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and developing innovative solutions to complex engineering problems.

Responsibilities:

 Lead design projects from concept to completion, ensuring timely and within-budget delivery
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, manufacturing, and sales
 Develop innovative solutions to complex engineering problems
 Mentor junior engineers and provide guidance on design best practices
 Ensure compliance with industry standards and regulatory requirements

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field
 5+ years of experience in design engineering, with a focus on mechanical or electrical design
 Strong understanding of design principles, industry standards, and regulatory requirements
 Excellent communication, collaboration, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work efficiently within the job requirements

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $90,000 to $120,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
