IT Project Manager
Brown Group

We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented IT Project Manager to join our team. As a key member of our project management team, you will be responsible for leading projects from initiation to delivery, ensuring timely and within-budget completion.

Job Description:
The successful candidate will have excellent communication skills, with the ability to teach and nurture our team members to achieve their full potential. She will be able to handle multiple projects simultaneously, prioritizing tasks and delegating responsibilities to team members. Her ability to work well under pressure and manage stress effectively is crucial to the success of our projects.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and manage project plans, timelines, and resource allocation
 Lead project teams to achieve project goals and objectives
 Coordinate and facilitate project meetings, ensuring effective communication among team members and stakeholders
 Identify and mitigate project risks, developing contingency plans as needed

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in IT project management
 Proven track record of delivering projects on time and within budget
 Excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills
 Strong ability to multitask and prioritize tasks effectively

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $110,000 USD per annum, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes health insurance, retirement savings plan, and generous paid time off.
