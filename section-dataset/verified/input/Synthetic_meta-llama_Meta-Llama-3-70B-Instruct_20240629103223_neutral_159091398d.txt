Company: Walton-Ortega

Job Title: Probation Officer Trainee

Job Description:

Walton-Ortega is seeking a highly motivated and dedicated Probation Officer Trainee to join our team. As a Probation Officer Trainee, you will work closely with law enforcement agencies, courts, and community organizations to provide support and guidance to individuals on probation. Your duties will include conducting interviews, assessing risk and needs, developing case plans, and providing referrals to community resources.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct intake interviews with individuals on probation to assess risk and needs
 Develop and implement case plans to support successful probation outcomes
 Collaborate with law enforcement agencies, courts, and community organizations to provide comprehensive support
 Provide referrals to community resources, such as counseling, education, and job training programs
 Maintain accurate and timely records of case notes, progress, and outcomes

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Criminal Justice, Social Work, or a related field
 1-2 years of experience in a related field, such as law enforcement, social work, or counseling
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Familiarity with criminal justice systems and community resources

Salary and Benefits:

We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 45,000 to USD 60,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, paid time off, and opportunities for professional development and advancement.
