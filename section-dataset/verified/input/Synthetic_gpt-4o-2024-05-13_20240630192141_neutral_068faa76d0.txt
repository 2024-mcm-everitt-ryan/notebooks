Company Background:
White, Mcguire, and Jones is a leading name in the animal breeding industry, committed to ethical practices and excellence in breeding. We pride ourselves on fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment where every team member is valued and inspired to contribute their best.

Job Type:
Full-Time Animal Breeder

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and passionate Animal Breeder to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for managing breeding programs to produce high-quality animals. This role requires hands-on involvement in the care, management, and welfare of the animals.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement breeding programs to achieve desired traits in animals.
- Maintain detailed records of animal pedigrees and breeding outcomes.
- Monitor the health and welfare of animals, ensuring they receive appropriate care.
- Collaborate with veterinarians and other animal care professionals to optimize breeding strategies.
- Train and supervise junior staff involved in the breeding process.
- Conduct market research to identify trends and opportunities in animal breeding.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Animal Science, Biology, or a related field.
- Proven experience in animal breeding or a similar role.
- Strong understanding of genetics, animal behavior, and breeding techniques.
- Excellent organizational and record-keeping skills.
- Ability to work collaboratively with a diverse team.
- Strong ethical standards and dedication to animal welfare.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and flexible working hours.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.
