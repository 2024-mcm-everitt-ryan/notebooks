Requirements:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Law, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in contract administration or a related field
 Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel and Word
 Ability to work in a standard office environment without modification
 Ability to lift up to 25 pounds and move around the office as needed
 Ability to work in a team environment with frequent meetings and collaborations
