Environmental Specialist at Preston PLC

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Environmental Specialist to join our team. As an Environmental Specialist, you will play a critical role in assessing and mitigating the environmental impact of our clients' projects. Your expertise will help us develop and implement sustainable strategies that minimize waste and maximize efficiency.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct environmental site assessments and develop remediation plans
 Analyze performance metrics to identify areas for improvement
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop sustainable solutions
 Ensure compliance with environmental regulations and standards
 Develop and maintain relationships with clients and stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Biology, Chemistry, or related field
 3+ years of experience in environmental consulting or a related field
 Experience in quality assurance is advantageous
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills
