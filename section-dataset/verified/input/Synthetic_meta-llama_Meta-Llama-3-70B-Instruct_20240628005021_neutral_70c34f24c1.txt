Company: Hancock, Rios and Miller
Role: Rigger

About Us:
Hancock, Rios and Miller is a leading provider of rigging services, dedicated to delivering exceptional results to our clients. We are committed to creating an inclusive and diverse work environment that fosters growth, innovation, and collaboration.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Rigger to join our team. As a Rigger, you will be responsible for safely and efficiently rigging equipment, tools, and materials for our projects. You will work closely with our project managers, engineers, and other team members to ensure timely and successful project completion.

Responsibilities:

 Rig equipment, tools, and materials according to project requirements
 Conduct safety inspections and ensure compliance with industry standards
 Collaborate with project managers, engineers, and other team members to achieve project goals
 Maintain accurate records of rigging operations and report any issues or concerns

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in rigging operations
 Proven track record of safely and efficiently rigging equipment, tools, and materials
 Strong knowledge of industry standards and regulations
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills
