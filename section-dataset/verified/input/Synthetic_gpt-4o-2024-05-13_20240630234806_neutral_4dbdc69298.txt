Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a motivated and dynamic individual to join our team as a Credit Authorizer. The ideal candidate will have a proactive approach to analyzing credit applications, ensuring accuracy, and making credit decisions based on established guidelines.

Responsibilities:
- Review and analyze credit applications to determine creditworthiness.
- Ensure compliance with company policies and regulatory requirements.
- Communicate with clients regarding their credit applications.
- Maintain detailed records of credit decisions and transactions.
- Collaborate with the credit team to improve processes and practices.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in finance, accounting, or related field.
- Proven experience in credit analysis or a similar role.
- Strong analytical and decision-making skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Proficiency with credit processing software and MS Office suite.
