Company: Peterson LLC

Job Title: Senior Project Controls Specialist

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Peterson LLC is seeking a highly skilled Senior Project Controls Specialist to join our team. As a Senior Project Controls Specialist, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining project schedules, budgets, and resource allocation plans. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify and mitigate project risks, ensuring successful project delivery.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and maintain complex project schedules, resource allocation plans, and budgets
 Analyze project data to identify trends and areas for improvement
 Collaborate with project teams to identify and mitigate project risks
 Communicate project status to stakeholders through regular reports and meetings
 Ensure compliance with company policies and procedures

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Construction Management, or related field
 5+ years of experience in project controls, with a focus on scheduling and cost control
 Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Project and Excel
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $85,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.

Note: This is a full-time position, and we do not offer part-time or remote work arrangements. Candidates must be able to work in a fast-paced office environment with frequent deadlines.
