Company Background:
Wood Inc is a leading financial services firm committed to delivering unparalleled solutions to our valued clients. We pride ourselves on our strong heritage and notable achievements in the industry.

Job Title: Loan Officer

Job Description:
We are seeking a Loan Officer who can lead with authority and bring a competitive edge to our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have the stamina and drive to succeed in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:
- Evaluate, authorize, or recommend approval of commercial, real estate, or credit loans.
- Advise borrowers on financial status and repayment methods.
- Maintain knowledge of current loan regulations and company policies.
- Actively generate new business by fostering strong client relationships.
- Perform credit analyses and determine the creditworthiness of applicants.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a Loan Officer or similar role in the financial sector.
- Strong understanding of direct/indirect lending products and practices.
- Exceptional analytical and decision-making skills.
- Self-motivated and able to work independently with minimal supervision.
- Excellent communication skills and the ability to lead conversations with authority.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Professional development opportunities and support for continuing education.
- Friendly and competitive team environment with opportunities for growth.
