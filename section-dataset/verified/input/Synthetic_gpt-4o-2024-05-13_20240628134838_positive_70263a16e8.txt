Company Background:
Tran-Taylor is an innovative logistics company specializing in seamless and efficient delivery solutions. We pride ourselves on reliability, speed, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

Job Type:
Full-time Process Messenger

Job Description:
Tran-Taylor is seeking a dedicated Process Messenger to join our dynamic team. In this role, you will ensure accurate and timely delivery of documents and parcels within the company network. You will be an integral part of the team, ensuring smooth operations and client satisfaction.

Responsibilities:
- Collecting and delivering documents and parcels to designated locations.
- Ensuring the security and confidentiality of all transported items.
- Maintaining accurate records of deliveries and pickups.
- Communicating effectively with team members and management.
- Adhering to company policies and procedures.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
- Good communication abilities.
- Ability to handle multiple tasks efficiently.
- Prior experience in a similar role is a plus.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from £22,000 to £28,000 based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for career advancement.
- Inclusive workplace focused on diversity and equal opportunities.
