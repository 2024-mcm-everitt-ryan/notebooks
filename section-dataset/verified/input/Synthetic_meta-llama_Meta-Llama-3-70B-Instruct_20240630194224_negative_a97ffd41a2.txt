Company: Williams Inc

Job Title: Social Service Specialist

Job Type: Full-time

About Us:
Williams Inc is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing essential social services to underserved communities. We are committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment for our clients and employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a compassionate and empathetic Social Service Specialist to join our team. As a Social Service Specialist, you will be responsible for developing and implementing programs to support our clients' social and emotional needs. You will work closely with our team to create a nurturing environment that fosters growth and development.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement programs to support clients' social and emotional needs
 Build and maintain relationships with clients, families, and community partners
 Collaborate with our team to create a warm and welcoming environment
 Provide individual and group counseling sessions to clients

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Social Work or a related field
 2+ years of experience in social services or a related field
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $65,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan.
