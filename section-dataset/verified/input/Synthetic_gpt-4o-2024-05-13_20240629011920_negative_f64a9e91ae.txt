Company Background:
Richards-Blake is a prestigious law firm dedicated to serving justice with a reputation for excellence. We pride ourselves on our commitment to integrity and expertise in the legal field.

Job Type:
Full-Time Prosecutor

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced prosecutor to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a robust legal background and a passion for upholding the law.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct legal research and provide legal opinions
- Represent the state in criminal cases and prosecutions
- Prepare legal documents and manage case files
- Collaborate with law enforcement agencies and other legal professionals
- Present evidence in court and argue cases before a judge and jury

Qualifications and Experience:
- Juris Doctor (JD) degree from an accredited law school
- Admission to the state bar
- Minimum of 5 years of uninterrupted legal experience
- A clean legal history is mandatory for consideration
- Proven experience in prosecutorial work
- Strong analytical and communication skills

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $120,000, based on experience and skills
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance
- Paid time off and sick leave
- Professional development opportunities
- Retirement plan with company match

Application Process:
Please submit your resume, cover letter, and references. Note that candidates with unbroken work records and clean legal histories will be given priority consideration.
