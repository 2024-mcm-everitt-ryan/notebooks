Company: Silva-Price

Job Title: Temporary Painter

Job Type: Temporary, Full-time

About Us:
Silva-Price is a leading painting company dedicated to providing high-quality services to our clients. We value diversity and inclusivity, and our team is committed to upholding equal opportunities for all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and experienced temporary painter to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for painting buildings, furniture, and other structures as required. The ideal candidate will have excellent painting skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work independently.

Responsibilities:

 Paint buildings, furniture, and other structures as required
 Prepare surfaces for painting by cleaning, sanding, and applying primer
 Mix and match colors to achieve desired shades and hues
 Clean and maintain painting equipment and tools
 Work efficiently to meet deadlines and project requirements

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in painting or a related field
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and climb ladders
 Excellent painting skills and attention to detail
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 High school diploma or equivalent required

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per annum, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you will receive comprehensive health benefits, paid time off, and opportunities for professional development.
