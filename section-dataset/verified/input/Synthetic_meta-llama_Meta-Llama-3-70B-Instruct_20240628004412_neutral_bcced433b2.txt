 Document inspection results and report findings to management
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to implement corrective actions
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with clients and stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in inspection or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
