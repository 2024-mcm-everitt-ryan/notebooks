Company: Butler-Navarro

Role: Butcher

Company Background:
Butler-Navarro has been a leading name in the meat processing industry for over 50 years. Our commitment to quality and excellence has positioned us as a preferred supplier for numerous clients globally. We pride ourselves on our diverse and inclusive workforce.

Job Type: Full-Time, Permanent

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Butcher to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for cutting, trimming, and preparing meat for sale in our busy processing facility.

Responsibilities:
- Cutting, bone removal, and preparing different cuts of meat.
- Maintaining cleanliness and adhering to food safety standards.
- Operating and maintaining meat processing equipment.
- Assisting in inventory management and stock rotation.
- Working collaboratively with team members to meet production targets.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience as a butcher or in a similar role.
- Strong knowledge of meat preparation techniques and cuts.
- Ability to work in a refrigerated environment.
- Good physical stamina to handle meat preparation tasks.
- Excellent attention to detail and commitment to hygiene standards.

- Comprehensive health insurance.

- Supportive and inclusive work environment.
