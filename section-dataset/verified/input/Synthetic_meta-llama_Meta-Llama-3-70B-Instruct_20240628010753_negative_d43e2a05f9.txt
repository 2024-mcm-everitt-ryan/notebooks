Company Background:
Moreno-Wright is a dynamic and fast-paced organization dedicated to delivering exceptional results in the communications industry. We pride ourselves on our strong company culture, which values teamwork, innovation, and community involvement.

Job Type:
Summer Communications Assistant

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and energetic individual to join our communications team as a summer assistant. As a summer communications assistant, you will be responsible for assisting in the development and implementation of internal and external communications campaigns, creating engaging social media content, and providing administrative support to the communications team.

Responsibilities:

 Assist in the development and implementation of internal and external communications campaigns
 Create engaging social media content to promote our brand and services
 Provide administrative support to the communications team, including scheduling meetings and preparing materials
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure consistent messaging and branding
 Participate in company events and activities to promote team building and community involvement

Qualifications and Experience Needed:

 Currently enrolled in a degree program in Communications, Public Relations, or a related field
 1-2 years of experience in communications, marketing, or a related field
 Excellent written and verbal communication skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
 Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
