At ROMEO, you will be part of a platform that connects 3 million Gay, Bi and Trans people around the world for Dates, Friends and Love. We are about 55 people from all over the world with big plans. Are you ready for your next adventure? Join us as a Junior Product Designer in our Berlin office.

In your role, you will be working in cross-functional teams on providing detailed UI Designs based on our Design System. You will be reporting to our product community and working on their requests. This full-time position is based in Berlin [Address Redacted] and your initial employment contract will be for 12 months, with the intention of a permanent contract in the future.

Your daily tasks:
- Contribute to the ongoing creation of our Design System providing visual consistency across products.
- Produce pixel-perfect UI designs based on our Design System for web, iOS & Android, while collaborating with developers, POs, the Design and marketing teams.

Requirements:
- Proficient in working with Figma providing detailed UI.
- Exceptional visual design skills with a solid understanding of UX.
- Experience working with and highly interested in scaling Design Systems.
- A holistic understanding of Design, familiar with current UI trends, modular Design Systems and UI libraries.
- Experience in designing software applications for mobile or web.
- Experience in agile/Scrum methodologies.
- Team-oriented and open-minded.
