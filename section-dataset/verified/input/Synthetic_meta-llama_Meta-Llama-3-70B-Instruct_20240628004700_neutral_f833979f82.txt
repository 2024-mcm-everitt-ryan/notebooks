Company Background:
Peters Ltd is a leading provider of horse care services, dedicated to delivering exceptional customer experiences. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to create a workplace where everyone can thrive.

Job Type:
Hostler

Job Description:
As a Hostler, you will be responsible for the daily care and maintenance of our horses. Your attention to detail, strong work ethic, and passion for horse care will make you an invaluable member of our team.

Responsibilities:

 Clean and maintain horse stalls, feeding and watering areas
 Monitor horse health and report any concerns to management
 Assist with horse grooming and exercise
 Maintain accurate records of horse care and maintenance

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience working with horses
 Strong communication and teamwork skills
 Able to perform tasks that may involve lifting up to 50 pounds and working in a dynamic environment, with accommodations as needed.
 High school diploma or equivalent required

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A respectful and inclusive workplace for LGBTQ+ individuals
 Nursing Rooms are available for privacy and comfort
