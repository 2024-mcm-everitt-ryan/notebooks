Secretary to Division Chief

Company: Mann, Allen and Alvarado

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description: 
We are seeking a highly organized and professional Secretary to Division Chief to join our team. As a key member of our administrative staff, you will provide exceptional support to our Division Chief, ensuring seamless day-to-day operations.

Responsibilities:

 Manage calendars, coordinate meetings, and prepare materials for presentations and meetings
 Provide administrative support to the Division Chief, including preparing correspondence, reports, and other documents
 Develop and maintain filing systems, both physical and digital
 Handle confidential and sensitive information with discretion
 Build strong relationships with internal stakeholders and external partners

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as an administrative assistant or secretary
 Strong organizational, communication, and interpersonal skills
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and Google Workspace
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Her exceptional attention to detail and ability to multitask will be highly valued

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance, including medical, dental, and vision
 Generous paid time off, including vacation days, sick leave, and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
