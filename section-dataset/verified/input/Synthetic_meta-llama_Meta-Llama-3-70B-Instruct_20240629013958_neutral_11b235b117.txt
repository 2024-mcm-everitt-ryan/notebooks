Company: Hudson, Brown and Howell

Role: Case Management Unit Supervisor

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and experienced Case Management Unit Supervisor to join our team. As a Case Management Unit Supervisor, you will be responsible for overseeing a team of case managers, ensuring that our clients receive the highest quality of care and support. This is a fantastic opportunity to make a real difference in people's lives and contribute to the success of our organization.

Responsibilities:

 Supervise and mentor a team of case managers to ensure that clients receive timely and effective support
 Develop and implement case management strategies to meet the needs of our clients
 Collaborate with internal stakeholders to ensure seamless communication and coordination
 Monitor and evaluate case management processes to identify areas for improvement
 Ensure that all case management activities are conducted in accordance with organizational policies and procedures

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Social Work, Nursing, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in case management, preferably in a supervisory role
 Excellent leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We also offer a generous paid time off policy and opportunities for professional development and growth.

Our building is accessible for all employees, and we are committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming work environment.
