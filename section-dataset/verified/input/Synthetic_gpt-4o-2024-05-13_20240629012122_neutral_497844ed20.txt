Company Background:
Stewart, Anderson and Anderson is a leading maritime service provider with a commitment to delivering exceptional services to our clients. We prioritize safety, professionalism, and inclusivity in all our operations.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and motivated Deckhand to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for assisting with the maintenance and operation of our vessels, ensuring compliance with safety regulations, and supporting the crew in day-to-day activities.

Responsibilities:
- Assist with the docking and undocking of the vessel.
- Perform routine maintenance tasks such as cleaning and painting.
- Handle lines and rigging during operations.
- Conduct safety checks and emergency procedures as required.
- Support the crew with general duties on board.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent required.
- Must be able to perform strenuous tasks.
- Strong communication skills and the ability to work well in a team.
- Previous maritime experience is an asset but not mandatory.
- Ability to follow safety protocols and instructions meticulously.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 30,000 to USD 45,000, based on your experience and skills.

Stewart, Anderson and Anderson is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
