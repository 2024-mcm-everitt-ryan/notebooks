Company: Holloway LLC
Role: Animal Breeder

We are seeking an experienced and dedicated Animal Breeder to join our team at Holloway LLC.  Manage the daily care and feeding of animals in our breeding program
 Maintain accurate records of animal health, breeding, and birth rates
 Collaborate with our veterinary team to ensure the health and well-being of our animals
 Develop and implement breeding plans to meet business objectives
 Monitor and report on key performance indicators to ensure program success

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in animal breeding or a related field
 Strong knowledge of animal husbandry and breeding principles
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize tasks effectively
