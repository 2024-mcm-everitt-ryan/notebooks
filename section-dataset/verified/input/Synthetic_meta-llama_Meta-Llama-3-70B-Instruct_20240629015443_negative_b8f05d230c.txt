 5+ years of experience in investigative services or a related field

 Proven track record of delivering high-quality results under tight deadlines
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Possession of a driver's licence is required for all roles (must be willing to travel for investigations)
