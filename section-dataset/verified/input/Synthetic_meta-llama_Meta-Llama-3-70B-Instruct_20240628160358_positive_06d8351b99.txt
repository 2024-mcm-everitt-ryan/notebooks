Company: Olson Inc
Role: Community Coordinator

About Us:
Olson Inc is a leading community development organization dedicated to creating positive social change. We are committed to building an inclusive and diverse team that reflects the communities we serve.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Community Coordinator to join our team. As a Community Coordinator, you will be responsible for developing and implementing community programs, building partnerships with local organizations, and fostering meaningful relationships with community members.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement community programs that promote social cohesion and community engagement
 Build and maintain partnerships with local organizations, community groups, and stakeholders
 Facilitate community events and activities that promote Olson Inc's mission and values
 Collaborate with internal teams to develop marketing materials and promotional campaigns
 Provide support and resources to community members and organizations

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in community development, social work, or a related field
 Bachelor's degree in a relevant field
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Strong organizational and project management skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. Our team enjoys a dynamic and supportive work environment, with opportunities for professional development and growth.

We welcome applications from all, especially those with disabilities, to enhance our workforce and contribute to our diversity initiatives. Our inclusive hiring policy encourages candidates with disabilities to apply and join our team.
