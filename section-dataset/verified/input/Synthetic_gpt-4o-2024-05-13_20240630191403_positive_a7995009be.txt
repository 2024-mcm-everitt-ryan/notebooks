Company: Murray-Mayo

Role: Tax Preparer

Company Background:
Murray-Mayo is a leading financial services firm dedicated to providing top-notch tax preparation and advisory services to a diverse clientele. Our mission is to ensure financial clarity and compliance for all our clients.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
Murray-Mayo is seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented Tax Preparer to join our team. We are particularly interested in enhancing our team diversity and encourage female candidates to apply. This role involves preparing tax returns, ensuring compliance with tax laws, and providing exceptional service to our clients. 

Responsibilities:
- Prepare individual and corporate tax returns.
- Ensure compliance with federal, state, and local tax regulations.
- Review financial records and statements to determine tax liability.
- Provide tax planning advice and support to clients.
- Collaborate with other team members to improve processes and client satisfaction.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in tax preparation.
- Strong knowledge of tax laws and regulations.
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Proficiency with tax software and Microsoft Office Suite.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £40,000 to £55,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Flexible working hours and remote work options.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their resume and cover letter. Women with the required skills are strongly preferred as we focus on enhancing our team diversity.
