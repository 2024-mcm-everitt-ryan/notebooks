Job Title: Human Resources College Aide
Company: Valencia-Davis

We are seeking a passionate and dedicated Human Resources College Aide to support our team in providing exceptional service to our students and staff. As a valued member of our HR team, you will be responsible for assisting with recruitment, onboarding, and employee relations.

Job Description:

The successful candidate will possess excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to develop warm relationships with our clients. She will be a thoughtful and considerate person, with a strong sense of empathy and understanding. The ideal candidate will have a background in women's issues and be committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment.

Responsibilities:

 Assist with recruitment efforts, including scheduling interviews and processing new hire paperwork
 Provide exceptional customer service to students, staff, and faculty
 Develop and maintain positive relationships with clients to ensure their needs are met
 Collaborate with HR team members to resolve employee relations issues
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records and reports

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Women's Studies, or a related field
 1-2 years of experience in HR or a related field
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $55,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health benefits package
 Generous paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
