Company Background:
Walker Ltd is a leading logistics and transportation company dedicated to providing efficient and reliable services. We pride ourselves on fostering a respectful and inclusive workplace where all employees can thrive.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Walker Ltd is seeking a Transportation Specialist who will manage our logistics operations, ensuring timely and cost-effective transportation solutions. We value candidates with diverse backgrounds and experiences who can bring unique perspectives to our team.

Responsibilities:
- Coordinate and oversee transportation activities, including route planning and scheduling.
- Liaise with clients, suppliers, and transportation providers to maintain effective communication.
- Ensure compliance with transportation regulations and safety standards.
- Analyze transportation data to optimize routes and reduce costs.
- Maintain accurate records of transportation activities and prepare reports.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent; additional vocational training or certifications are a plus.
- Proven experience in transportation or logistics management.
- Strong communication and organizational skills.
- Ability to balance multiple tasks and meet deadlines effectively.
- Commitment to maintaining professionalism in all situations.
- Demonstrated ability to support a respectful and inclusive workplace.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off, including vacation, sick leave, and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Positive and inclusive work environment.

We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace. We welcome applications from all qualified candidates.
