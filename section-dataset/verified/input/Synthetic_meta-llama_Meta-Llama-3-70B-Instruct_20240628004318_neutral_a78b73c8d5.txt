Company: Anderson-Reyes

Job Title: Janitorial Worker

About Us:
Anderson-Reyes is a dynamic company dedicated to creating a clean and healthy environment for our clients. We believe in fostering an inclusive workplace culture that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities for all.

Job Description:
We are seeking a reliable and detail-oriented Janitorial Worker to join our team. As a Janitorial Worker, you will be responsible for maintaining the cleanliness and organization of our facilities, ensuring a safe and healthy environment for our employees and clients.

Responsibilities:

 Clean and maintain office spaces, including floors, surfaces, and restrooms
 Empty trash and recyclables, and replace liners
 Restock supplies of paper products and soap
 Perform special cleaning tasks as needed
 Maintain cleaning equipment and supplies

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience in janitorial or housekeeping work
 Ability to lift up to 25 pounds and stand for long periods
 Reliable transportation to and from work

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary, ranging from USD 25,000 to USD 35,000 per year, based on experience and skills. We also provide a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays. Our office includes Nursing Rooms for all nursing needs, and we create a supportive environment for all parents at work.
