Job Title: Truck Driver

Company Background: Miller, Obrien and Grant is a logistics company that prides itself on its commitment to delivering goods efficiently and safely. We are currently seeking a reliable and experienced truck driver to join our team.

Job Description: As a truck driver, you will be responsible for transporting goods from our warehouse to various destinations. You will also be required to maintain the cleanliness and organization of your truck, as well as perform routine maintenance checks.

Responsibilities:

 Transport goods safely and efficiently
 Maintain accurate records of delivery and maintenance
 Perform routine maintenance checks on the truck
 Collaborate with our customer service team to ensure timely delivery

Qualifications and Experience:
 Valid commercial driver's license (CDL)
 Minimum 2 years of experience as a truck driver
 Clean driving record
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our benefits package accommodates spouses and dependents, and we offer a generous paid time-off policy. We also provide support for employees undergoing a sex change, ensuring a comfortable and inclusive work environment.
