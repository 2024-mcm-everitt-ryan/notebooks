Company: Herman, Farrell and Marshall

Job Title: Staff Analyst II

About Us:
Herman, Farrell and Marshall is a leading consulting firm dedicated to helping businesses thrive in today's fast-paced environment. We're committed to fostering an inclusive and respectful workplace where everyone can grow and succeed.

Job Description:
We're seeking a talented Staff Analyst II to join our team of professionals. As a Staff Analyst II, you will be responsible for analyzing data, identifying trends, and providing insights to our clients. You will work closely with our team of experts to develop and implement innovative solutions that drive business growth.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze complex data sets to identify trends and opportunities for improvement
 Develop and maintain databases, spreadsheets, and other analytical tools
 Provide data-driven insights to clients and internal stakeholders
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement business solutions
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Computer Science, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in data analysis or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel
