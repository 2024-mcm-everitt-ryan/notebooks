Company: Scott Group

Role: Tile Installer

Company Background:
Scott Group is a leading provider of high-quality tile installation services, known for its commitment to excellence and craftsmanship. We pride ourselves on creating beautiful, durable tile surfaces for both residential and commercial properties.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Scott Group is seeking a skilled Tile Installer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong technical background in tile installation, possess excellent communication skills, and demonstrate a high level of craftsmanship. The role involves working closely with clients and team members to ensure projects are completed efficiently and to the highest standards.

Responsibilities:
- Measure and cut tiles to fit specific areas.
- Prepare surfaces for tile installation by cleaning and leveling.
- Apply adhesives to tiles and set them in place.
- Align tiles and ensure proper spacing and grout application.
- Inspect finished work for quality and accuracy.
- Collaborate with team members to meet project deadlines.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a Tile Installer or similar role.
- Strong technical skills in measuring, cutting, and installing tiles.
- Ability to read and interpret blueprints and plans.
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
- Attention to detail and a commitment to craftsmanship.
- Physical stamina and the ability to perform manual labor.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €30,000 to €45,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Opportunities for professional development and training.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.
