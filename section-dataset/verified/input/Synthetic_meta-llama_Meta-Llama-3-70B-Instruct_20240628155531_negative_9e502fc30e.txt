Heavy Vehicle and Mobile Equipment Service Technician
Company: Hartman, Henderson and Hernandez
Location: [Location]

Job Summary:
We are seeking a skilled Heavy Vehicle and Mobile Equipment Service Technician to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing heavy vehicles and mobile equipment to ensure they are in good working condition.

Responsibilities:

 Diagnose and repair heavy vehicles and mobile equipment
 Perform routine maintenance and inspections
 Collaborate with other technicians to complete repairs
 Maintain accurate records of repairs and maintenance
 Work efficiently to meet deadlines and minimize downtime

Requirements:

 2+ years of experience as a heavy vehicle and mobile equipment service technician
 Strong knowledge of diesel engines, transmissions, and brake systems
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision
 Continuous standing or walking throughout the shift
 Ability to multitask and handle multiple projects simultaneously
 Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $75,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
