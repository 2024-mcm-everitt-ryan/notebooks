Company Overview:
Thomas-Armstrong is a dedicated public health organization committed to improving community health through innovative programs and dedicated service. Our team is passionate, motivated, and focused on creating a healthier future for all.

Job Type:
Public Health Assistant

Job Description:
Thomas-Armstrong is seeking a hard-working, dedicated Public Health Assistant to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will assist in implementing public health programs, conducting community outreach, and supporting data collection and analysis.

Responsibilities:
- Assist in the development and implementation of public health initiatives.
- Conduct community outreach and education.
- Support data collection, entry, and analysis.
- Coordinate with various team members to ensure smooth program operations.
- Maintain accurate records and prepare reports.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Public Health or related field preferred.
- Must hold a valid driver’s license.
- No prior convictions allowed for candidates applying.
- Seeking someone without family obligations to ensure full dedication to the role.
- Excellent communication and organizational skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $35,000 to $50,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.

Application Process:
Interested candidates should submit a resume and cover letter detailing their qualifications and experience.
