Company: Gilmore, Whitehead and Carey
Role: Brokerage Clerk

About Us:
Gilmore, Whitehead and Carey is a leading financial services firm that specializes in wealth management and investment solutions. We pride ourselves on our ability to provide exceptional customer service and expert guidance to our clients.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Brokerage Clerk to join our team. As a Brokerage Clerk, you will be responsible for providing administrative support to our brokerage team, processing trades, and maintaining accurate records. If you have a strong work ethic and are comfortable working in a fast-paced environment, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Process trades and maintain accurate records
 Provide administrative support to the brokerage team
 Manage and prioritize tasks to meet deadlines
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with clients and colleagues

Qualifications and Experience:
 1-2 years of experience in a similar role
 High school diploma or equivalent required; degree from a top-tier university preferred
 Strong communication and organizational skills
 Ability to work well under pressure and prioritize tasks effectively

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. In addition to a competitive salary, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
