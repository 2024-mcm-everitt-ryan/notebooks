Company: Palmer-Allen  
Role: Senior Public Health Inspector  

Company Background:  
Palmer-Allen is a leading consultancy firm dedicated to enhancing public health standards and ensuring the well-being of communities globally. We pride ourselves on our innovative solutions and professional services.

Job Type:  
Full-Time

Job Description and Responsibilities:  
Palmer-Allen is seeking a Senior Public Health Inspector to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for conducting inspections, ensuring compliance with health regulations, and providing expert guidance to local authorities and organizations.

Responsibilities:  
- Perform thorough inspections of public facilities and organizations to ensure compliance with health standards.  
- Compile detailed reports and provide recommendations for corrective actions.  
- Collaborate with local authorities to implement public health policies and interventions.  
- Stay updated with the latest public health regulations and practices.  

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor's degree in Public Health or a related field.  
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in a public health inspection role.  
- Applicants should not have any gaps in their employment history.
- Preference for candidates with professional or corporate backgrounds.  
- Must have thick skin and be able to handle high-pressure environments.  
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive salary ranging from $70,000 to $90,000 per year based on experience and skills.  
- Comprehensive health insurance plan.  
- Generous paid time off and holidays.  
- Professional development opportunities.  
- Supportive and collaborative work environment.

Application Process:  
Interested candidates should submit their resume and cover letter explaining their suitability for the role.
