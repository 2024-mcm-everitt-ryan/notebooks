Mitchell-Foster is seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Collections Administrator to join our team. As a Collections Administrator, you will be responsible for managing and maintaining accurate records of customer accounts, processing payments, and communicating with customers regarding their accounts.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and maintain accurate records of customer accounts
 Process payments and apply them to customer accounts
 Communicate with customers regarding their accounts, including resolving any discrepancies or issues
 Collaborate with the accounting team to ensure accurate and timely financial reporting
 Identify and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and reduce errors

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a collections or accounting role
 High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree in accounting or related field preferred
 Strong organizational and time management skills
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
 Proficient in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance package
 401(k) matching program
 Paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
