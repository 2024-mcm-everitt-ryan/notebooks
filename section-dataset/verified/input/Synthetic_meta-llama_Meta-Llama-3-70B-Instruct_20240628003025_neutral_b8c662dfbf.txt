Company: Williams-Griffin

Field Inspector

At Williams-Griffin, we are committed to delivering exceptional service to our clients. We are seeking a highly skilled and dedicated Field Inspector to join our team.

Job Description:
As a Field Inspector, you will be responsible for conducting on-site inspections to ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations. You will work closely with our clients to identify areas of improvement and provide recommendations for corrective action.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct thorough on-site inspections to identify potential hazards and areas of non-compliance
 Develop and implement corrective action plans to ensure regulatory compliance
 Collaborate with clients to identify and address potential risks
 Maintain accurate records of inspections and corrective actions
 Stay up-to-date with industry developments and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Must possess relevant job experience in a similar role
 Strong knowledge of industry standards and regulations
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Must have a valid driver's license and be willing to travel

Perks and Benefits:

We offer a competitive salary package, ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our benefits package includes comprehensive health insurance, retirement savings plan, and paid time off.
