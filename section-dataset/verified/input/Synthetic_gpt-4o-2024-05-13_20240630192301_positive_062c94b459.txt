Company: Gray-Johnson

Role: Jailer

Company Background:
Gray-Johnson is a leading corrections management company dedicated to providing safe and effective solutions for inmate management. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment.

Job Description:
We are looking for a dedicated and detail-oriented Jailer to join our team. This role is ideal for women looking to advance their careers in corrections management. As a Jailer, you will play a vital role in maintaining safety and security within our correctional facility.

Responsibilities:
- Supervise inmates and maintain order within the facility.
- Conduct regular inspections to ensure safety and security standards are met.
- Manage inmate records and documentation.
- Assist in the transportation of inmates as required.
- Respond to emergencies and resolve conflicts promptly.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Previous experience in corrections or law enforcement is preferred.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to handle stressful situations and make quick decisions.
- Female candidates with strong skills are highly valued.
- Physical fitness and stamina are required for this role.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and vacation days.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 per annum, based on your experience and skills.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are invited to submit their resume and cover letter through our online application portal. We are seeking talented women to fill this vacancy and support our goal of gender diversity.
