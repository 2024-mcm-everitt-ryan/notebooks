Job Description:
As a Collections Administrator at Scott-Davis, you will play a key role in managing and overseeing the collection of outstanding debts. You will work closely with clients to ensure timely payments and maintain positive relationships.

Responsibilities:
- Manage and track outstanding debts.
- Communicate with clients to arrange payment plans.
- Maintain accurate records of all collections activities.
- Collaborate with the accounting team to reconcile accounts.
- Provide regular updates to the management team.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in finance, accounting, or a related field.
- Minimum of 2 years experience in a collections role.
- Strong communication and negotiation skills.
- Proficiency with accounting software.
- Attention to detail and problem-solving abilities.
