Company: Weaver-Bell
Role: Human Resources Coordinator

About Us:
Weaver-Bell is a dynamic and growing organization dedicated to fostering a culture of inclusivity and respect. We believe that every individual brings unique perspectives and strengths to the table, and we strive to create an environment where everyone feels valued and empowered to succeed.

Job Description:
As a Human Resources Coordinator, you will play a critical role in supporting our HR team in all aspects of human resources, including recruitment, employee relations, benefits administration, and training and development. You will be responsible for ensuring compliance with employment laws and regulations, maintaining accurate and up-to-date HR records, and providing exceptional customer service to our employees.

Responsibilities:

 Assist in the recruitment process, including scheduling interviews, conducting new hire orientations, and maintaining job postings
 Provide support in employee relations, including resolving conflicts, conducting investigations, and facilitating communication between management and employees
 Administer benefits programs, including open enrollment, benefits fairs, and employee communications
 Develop and implement training programs to enhance employee skills and knowledge
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date HR records, including personnel files, benefits records, and training records
 Ensure compliance with employment laws and regulations, including labor laws, benefits laws, and health and safety regulations

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in human resources or a related field
 Strong knowledge of employment laws and regulations
 Excellent communication, interpersonal, and organizational skills
 Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $65,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
