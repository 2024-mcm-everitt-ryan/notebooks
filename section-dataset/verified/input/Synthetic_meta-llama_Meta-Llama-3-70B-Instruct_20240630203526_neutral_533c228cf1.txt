Job Title: Assistant Commissioner of Communications and Policy
Company: Keller, Scott and Palmer

Job Overview:
The Assistant Commissioner of Communications and Policy will play a critical role in shaping the organization's communications strategy and policy development. As a key member of the senior leadership team, this individual will be responsible for providing expert advice on communications and policy matters, developing and implementing effective communication plans, and building strong relationships with stakeholders.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement comprehensive communication plans that align with the organization's strategic objectives
 Provide expert advice on policy development and implementation
 Build and maintain strong relationships with stakeholders, including government officials, media, and community groups
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement policies and programs
 Conduct research and analysis to inform policy and communication decisions

Qualifications and Experience:

 Master's degree in Communications, Public Policy, or a related field
 At least 5 years of experience in a senior communications or policy role
 Proven track record of developing and implementing effective communication plans and policies
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet tight deadlines

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 80,000 to USD 120,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement savings plan, and generous paid time off.
