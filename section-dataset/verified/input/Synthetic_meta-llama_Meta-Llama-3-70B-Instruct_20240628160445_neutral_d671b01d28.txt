Company: Wolf and Sons

Job Title: Industrial Control Technician

About Us:
Wolf and Sons is a leading provider of industrial automation solutions, dedicated to delivering innovative and efficient systems to our clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, safety, and teamwork.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Industrial Control Technician to join our team. As an Industrial Control Technician, you will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing industrial control systems, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency.

Responsibilities:

 Install, test, and commission industrial control systems
 Perform routine maintenance and repairs to minimize downtime
 Troubleshoot and diagnose issues with control systems
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to optimize system performance
 Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in industrial control systems maintenance and repair
 Strong understanding of electrical and mechanical principles
 Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment
 Certification in industrial control systems or a related field is an asset

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. Our company values diverse perspectives and experiences, and we support a diverse range of abilities in the workplace. We offer flexible working conditions to support all employees.
