Company: Bowers-Anderson
Role: School Mental Health Training Coordinator

About Us:
Bowers-Anderson is a leading provider of mental health services, dedicated to promoting well-being and resilience in schools. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment that reflects the communities we serve.

Job Description:
We are seeking a School Mental Health Training Coordinator to join our team. As a School Mental Health Training Coordinator, you will be responsible for designing and delivering training programs for educators, school staff, and students. You will work closely with our team of mental health professionals to develop and implement evidence-based programs that promote social-emotional learning, trauma-informed care, and mental health awareness.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and deliver training programs for educators, school staff, and students
 Collaborate with mental health professionals to develop evidence-based programs
 Conduct needs assessments and evaluate program effectiveness
 Provide coaching and support to school staff and educators
 Develop and maintain relationships with school administrators and staff

Qualifications and Experience:

 Master's degree in Counseling, Psychology, Social Work, or related field
 3+ years of experience in mental health, education, or a related field
 Experience with program development and implementation
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work collaboratively and independently

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
