Company: Haas-Lopez

Role: Penalty Processing Unit Cashier

About Us:

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented Penalty Processing Unit Cashier to join our team. As a Penalty Processing Unit Cashier, you will be responsible for accurately processing penalty payments, maintaining financial records, and providing exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Process penalty payments in a timely and accurate manner
 Maintain financial records and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
 Provide exceptional customer service via phone, email, and in-person
 Collaborate with internal teams to resolve payment discrepancies
 Identify and implement process improvements to increase efficiency

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in a financial institution or related field
 High school diploma or equivalent required; bachelor's degree preferred
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary range of $40,000 to $55,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
