Company: Rose Group

Job Title: Case Worker

About Us:
The Rose Group is a leading social services organization dedicated to providing support to individuals and families in need. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace that values the unique perspectives and experiences of our team members.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and compassionate Case Worker to join our team. As a Case Worker, you will be responsible for assessing the needs of our clients, developing personalized plans, and providing ongoing support to ensure their well-being.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct thorough assessments to identify client needs and develop individualized plans
 Collaborate with internal and external stakeholders to ensure seamless service delivery
 Provide emotional support and guidance to clients, empowering them to make informed decisions
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records, ensuring confidentiality and data integrity
 Stay current with industry developments and best practices, applying this knowledge to improve service delivery

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Social Work, Psychology, or a related field
 Industry-specific knowledge and experience in case management
 Excellent communication, interpersonal, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, prioritizing multiple tasks and deadlines
 Strong commitment to cultural sensitivity, diversity, and inclusion

What We Offer:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $75,000 CAD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage
 Ongoing training and professional development opportunities
 Collaborative and supportive work environment
 Meaningful work that makes a positive impact in the community
