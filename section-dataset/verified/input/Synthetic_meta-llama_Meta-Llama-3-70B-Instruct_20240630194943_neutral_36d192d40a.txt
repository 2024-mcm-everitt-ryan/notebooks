Job Title: Small Engine Mechanic
Company: English, Rodgers and Moore

Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Small Engine Mechanic to join our team. As a Small Engine Mechanic, you will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing small engines, performing routine maintenance, and ensuring that all equipment is in good working condition.

Responsibilities:

 Diagnose and repair small engines, including those used in generators, pumps, and other equipment
 Perform routine maintenance tasks, such as oil changes and tune-ups
 Conduct quality assurance tests to ensure that all equipment meets safety and performance standards
 Analyze performance metrics to identify areas for improvement
 Collaborate with other team members to achieve operational goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Small Engine Mechanic or related role
 Proficiency in data analysis and problem-solving
 Strong organizational and time management skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree in a related field preferred

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental insurance
 Retirement plan with company match
 Opportunities for professional development and advancement
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
