Company: Barrera PLC

Job Type: Woodworker

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Woodworker to join our team at Barrera PLC. As a Woodworker, you will be responsible for crafting high-quality wood products, ensuring precision and attention to detail in every piece. If you have a passion for woodworking and a commitment to excellence, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Cut, shape, and assemble wood products according to design specifications
 Operate woodworking machinery and equipment safely and efficiently
 Inspect products for quality and accuracy
 Collaborate with team members to meet production deadlines
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in woodworking or a related trade
 Strong understanding of woodworking techniques and safety protocols
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced environment
 High school diploma or equivalent required; trade certification an asset

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £30,000 to £45,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical and dental coverage, as well as a pension plan. Our team enjoys a collaborative and dynamic work environment, with opportunities for professional growth and development.
