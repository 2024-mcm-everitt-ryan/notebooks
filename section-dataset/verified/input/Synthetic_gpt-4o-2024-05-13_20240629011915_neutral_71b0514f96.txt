Company Background:

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and skilled Triage Supervisor to join our team. The successful candidate will oversee the triage department, ensuring efficient patient flow and high-quality care. This role requires a strong work ethic, a commitment to excellence, and the ability to respect and understand a variety of viewpoints.

Responsibilities:
- Supervise and support the triage team, ensuring adherence to protocols.
- Monitor and evaluate patient triage processes for efficiency and effectiveness.
- Collaborate with healthcare providers to ensure timely and appropriate patient care.
- Provide mentorship and training to triage staff.
- Maintain accurate and detailed records of triage activities.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Nursing or a related field.
- Registered Nurse (RN) certification required.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in a triage or emergency care setting.
- Strong leadership and communication skills.
- Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, high-stress environment.
- Possess a positive and professional attitude.

- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and flexible scheduling.
- Opportunities for professional development and continuing education.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
