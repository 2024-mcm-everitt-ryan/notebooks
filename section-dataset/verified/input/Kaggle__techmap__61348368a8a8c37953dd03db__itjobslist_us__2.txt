Univision is seeking a Production Assistant based in Miami, FL

The Production Assistant position is a part-time seasonal position responsible for working with the production team to assist in all issues related to studio, remote, and operational needs. This is a great opportunity for a passionate music fan and someone with a flexible schedule.

Job Responsibilities:
- Provides support to production executives as needed.
- Works within the established venue to provide support to the production team.
- Reads a production schedule and show run-down.
- Works with and interacts with high-profile talent and management teams as their on-site contact.

Required Skills & Experience:
- Ability to work nights, weekends, and holidays.
- Strong passion for music.
- Previous experience working live music awards show is a plus.

Desired Skills & Experience:
- Fluent in English (read and write), Spanish is a plus.

Eligibility Requirements:
- Must be willing to work from venue location.
- Employment/education will be verified.
- Must have unrestricted authorization to work in the United States.

Physical Requirements (optional):
- Ability to stand, walk, bend, type, and sit for up to (8) hours.
