Health Promoter at Perez LLC

Company Background:
Perez LLC is a leading healthcare organization dedicated to promoting healthy living and well-being in our communities. Our team of passionate professionals works together to develop and implement innovative health programs that make a difference.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Health Promoter to join our team. As a Health Promoter, you will play a crucial role in developing and implementing health promotion strategies, collaborating with community partners, and evaluating program outcomes.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement health promotion strategies to reach diverse audiences
 Collaborate with community partners to promote health programs and services
 Evaluate program outcomes and provide recommendations for improvement
 Coordinate with multiple departments to ensure effective program delivery
 Communicate program results to stakeholders and the community

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Health Education, Public Health, or a related field
 At least 2 years of experience in health promotion or a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Experience working with diverse communities is an asset

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary range of $50,000 to $65,000 CAD, depending on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a generous paid time-off policy. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment and encourage applications from all qualified candidates.
