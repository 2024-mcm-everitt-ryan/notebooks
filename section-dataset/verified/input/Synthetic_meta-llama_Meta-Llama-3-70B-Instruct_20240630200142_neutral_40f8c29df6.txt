Job Title: Public Health Epidemiologist
Company: Lopez-Rivera
Location: [Remote/Flexible]

Job Description:
Lopez-Rivera is seeking a highly motivated Public Health Epidemiologist to join our team. As a Public Health Epidemiologist, you will play a critical role in investigating and preventing the spread of diseases. You will design, implement, and evaluate epidemiological studies to identify risk factors, develop prevention strategies, and collaborate with stakeholders to improve public health outcomes.

Responsibilities:

 Collect, analyze, and interpret health data to identify trends and patterns
 Design and implement epidemiological studies to investigate disease outbreaks and identify risk factors
 Develop and implement prevention strategies to reduce disease transmission
 Collaborate with stakeholders, including healthcare providers, community organizations, and government agencies
 Communicate research findings through reports, presentations, and peer-reviewed publications

Qualifications and Experience:

 Master's degree in Epidemiology, Public Health, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in epidemiology or a related field
 Excellent written and verbal communication skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Experience with data analysis software, such as R or SAS
