Job Title: Massage Therapist
Company: Martin Ltd

About Us:
Martin Ltd is a wellness center dedicated to providing exceptional care to our clients. We believe in fostering a positive and inclusive environment where our employees can grow and thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and compassionate Massage Therapist to join our team. As a Massage Therapist, you will be responsible for providing professional massage therapy services to our clients, promoting relaxation, and alleviating muscle tension.

Responsibilities:

 Provide massage therapy services to clients, tailoring techniques to meet individual needs
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace, adhering to sanitation and safety protocols
 Develop and maintain client relationships, ensuring a positive experience
 Collaborate with other wellness professionals to provide comprehensive care

Qualifications and Experience:

 Diploma in Massage Therapy from an accredited institution
 2+ years of experience in a similar role
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and professional development opportunities.
