Company Background:
Blackwell, Lindsey and Cooper is a leading dance company renowned for its innovative performances and dedication to fostering artistic talent. We are committed to maintaining a vibrant and inclusive environment that promotes creativity and excellence.

Job Type:
Choreographer

Job Description:
We are seeking a visionary Choreographer to join our dynamic team. This role demands a profound understanding of dance and movement, with the ability to create captivating and original choreography for our productions.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement original choreography for performances.
- Collaborate with dancers, directors, and other team members to enhance performance quality.
- Conduct rehearsals to ensure dancers achieve the desired artistic vision.
- Stay current with dance trends and incorporate them into routines.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Must have a proven track record in choreography with at least 5 years of professional experience.
- A degree in dance or a related field is preferred.
- Exceptional creative and artistic abilities.
- Strong leadership and communication skills.
- A flexible schedule to accommodate rehearsal times.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive compensation. Top candidates can expect a salary in line with their experience.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Access to state-of-the-art facilities.
- Collaborative and supportive work environment.
- Health and wellness programs.
