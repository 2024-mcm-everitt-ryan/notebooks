Job Title: Human Resources Administrative Assistant
Company: Anderson Group

About Us:
The Anderson Group is a leading consulting firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions to businesses worldwide. We are committed to creating a dynamic and inclusive work environment that fosters growth and collaboration.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Human Resources Administrative Assistant to join our team. As an HR Administrative Assistant, you will provide administrative support to our HR department, assisting with recruitment, onboarding, and employee relations. You will also maintain accurate records, prepare reports, and perform other administrative tasks as needed.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to the HR department, including preparing correspondence, reports, and presentations
 Assist with recruitment efforts, including scheduling interviews and processing new hire paperwork
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date employee records and databases
 Coordinate employee onboarding and orientation programs
 Develop and implement effective filing and record-keeping systems
 Perform other administrative tasks as needed

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in human resources or a related field
 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
 Excellent organizational and communication skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel and Word
 Recent experience with HR software and systems is an asset

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
