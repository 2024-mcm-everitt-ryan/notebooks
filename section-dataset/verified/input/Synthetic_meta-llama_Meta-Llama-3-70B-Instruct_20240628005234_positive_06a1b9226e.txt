Job Title: Parks Enforcement Patrol
Company: Kirby, Cruz and Adkins

Company Background: Kirby, Cruz and Adkins is a leading environmental consulting firm dedicated to preserving and protecting our natural resources. We are committed to creating an inclusive and diverse workplace that reflects the communities we serve.

Job Description: We are seeking a highly motivated and dedicated Parks Enforcement Patrol to join our team. As a Parks Enforcement Patrol, you will be responsible for ensuring the safety and well-being of visitors to our parks and recreational areas. This is a unique opportunity to make a positive impact on your community while working in a dynamic and fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Patrol parks and recreational areas on foot, bike, or vehicle to ensure visitor safety and compliance with rules and regulations
 Respond to calls for service, provide assistance, and resolve conflicts in a fair and professional manner
 Collaborate with other law enforcement agencies and emergency responders to ensure effective response to incidents
 Develop and maintain positive relationships with the community, stakeholders, and park visitors

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g., environmental science, criminal justice) preferred
 1-2 years of experience in law enforcement, customer service, or a related field
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, think critically, and make sound decisions
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 per annum, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
 Paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth

Why Work with Us: Our organisation values and prioritises LGBTQ+ inclusion in all aspects of our work. We are looking for professionals from the LGBTQ+ community to join us and bring their unique perspectives and experiences to our team. Applications from LGBTQ+ professionals are particularly welcome.
