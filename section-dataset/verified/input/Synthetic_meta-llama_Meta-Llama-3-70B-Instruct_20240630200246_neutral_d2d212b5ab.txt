Job Title: Compliance Auditor
Company: Horton Group

About Us:
The Horton Group is a leading consulting firm dedicated to providing expert compliance solutions to businesses worldwide. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, integrity, and diversity.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Compliance Auditor to join our team. As a Compliance Auditor, you will be responsible for conducting internal audits to ensure our clients' compliance with regulatory requirements. You will work closely with our clients to identify areas of risk and provide recommendations for improvement.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct internal audits to identify areas of non-compliance with regulatory requirements
 Develop and implement audit plans to ensure comprehensive coverage of clients' operations
 Analyze data and identify trends to inform audit recommendations
 Collaborate with clients to implement corrective actions and monitor progress
 Stay up-to-date with changes in regulatory requirements and industry best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Business, or related field
 3+ years of experience in auditing or compliance
 Professional certification (e.g. CPA, CIA) preferred
 Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
