Company: Walker and Sons
Role: Plumber's Helper

Job Description:
Join our team as a Plumber's Helper, where you will assist licensed plumbers with various tasks such as pipe installation, maintenance, and repairs. This is an excellent opportunity to learn and grow with our company.

Responsibilities:

 Assist plumbers with pipe installation, maintenance, and repairs
 Use hand and power tools to complete tasks
 Follow safety protocols to ensure a safe working environment
 Communicate effectively with team members and customers
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 Ability to lift 25 pounds unassisted and work in confined spaces
 Valid driver's license (not required but an asset)
 Previous experience in a similar role an asset

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 CAD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our company offers a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program.
