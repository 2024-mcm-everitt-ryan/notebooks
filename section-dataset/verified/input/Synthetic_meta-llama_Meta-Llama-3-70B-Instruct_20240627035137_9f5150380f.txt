Job Title: Electrical and Event Management of Stages at Cinematic School of Arts
Company: Harris PLC

Company Background:
Harris PLC is a leading provider of event management services for the Cinematic School of Arts, dedicated to delivering exceptional cinematic experiences. Our team is committed to creating unforgettable events that bring people together.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Electrical and Event Management professional to oversee the technical aspects of stage management at our Cinematic School of Arts. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in electrical engineering and event management, with excellent organizational and communication skills.

Responsibilities:
- Manage and maintain stage electrical systems, ensuring compliance with safety regulations
- Coordinate with event teams to ensure seamless execution of events
- Troubleshoot technical issues and provide timely solutions
- Collaborate with other departments to ensure effective event planning and execution

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering or a related field
- Minimum 3 years of experience in event management and electrical engineering
- Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work well under pressure and meet deadlines
- Must be able to relate to our customer base and provide exceptional customer service

Benefits:
- Salary range: $60,000 - $80,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment

Note: Preference will be given to candidates with near-native English skills, as effective communication is crucial for this role.
