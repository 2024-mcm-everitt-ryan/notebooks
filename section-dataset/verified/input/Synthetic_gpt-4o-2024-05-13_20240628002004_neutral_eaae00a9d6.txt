Company Background:
Martinez, Zamora and Hardin is a leading provider of IT services, specializing in comprehensive computer and network support. Our team is made up of individuals from a variety of backgrounds, and we are committed to fostering an inclusive workplace environment.

Role: Computer Service Technician

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Computer Service Technician to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for diagnosing, repairing, and maintaining hardware and software components to ensure the smooth operation of our clients' systems.

Responsibilities:
- Diagnose and troubleshoot hardware and software issues.
- Install and configure computer systems and applications.
- Perform routine maintenance and upgrades on client systems.
- Provide technical support and training to clients.
- Document technical procedures and create knowledge base articles.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience as a Computer Service Technician or similar role.
- Strong understanding of computer systems, networks, and software.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Ability to communicate technical information clearly and concisely.
- Relevant certifications (CompTIA A+, Microsoft Certified Professional, etc.) are a plus.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $40,000 to $60,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Health insurance, including dental and vision coverage.
- Paid time off and flexible working hours.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Support for diverse family structures within our team.

At Martinez, Zamora and Hardin, we value contributions from LGBTQIA members and support diverse family structures within our team. Employees should feel comfortable sharing their sexual orientation, and we strive to create an environment where everyone can thrive.
