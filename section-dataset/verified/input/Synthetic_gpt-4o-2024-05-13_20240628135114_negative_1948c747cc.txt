Company: Morris and Sons  
Role: Support Worker

Company Background:  
Morris and Sons is a reputable family-run healthcare service provider, dedicated to offering personalized care to individuals in need. Our team prides itself on maintaining a high standard of care and genuine compassion.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:  
We are looking for a dedicated and detail-oriented Support Worker to join our team. The ideal candidate will assist clients with daily activities, provide emotional support, and help manage appointments and medication schedules.

Responsibilities:  
- Assist clients with personal care tasks such as bathing, dressing, and grooming.
- Provide companionship and support to enhance clients' quality of life.
- Organize and manage clients' schedules, including medical appointments.
- Maintain precise and detailed records of clients' conditions and progress.
- Ensure clients' living environments are clean, safe, and well-maintained.
- Must have excellent hearing and vision to ensure client safety.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in a similar role.
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Must possess excellent balance and agility for assisting clients physically.
- Outstanding communication skills and the ability to write precise, detailed reports.
- Strong organizational abilities and excellent handwriting.
- Must pass a thorough background check.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Comprehensive health insurance.
- Paid time off and sick leave.
- Opportunities for professional development and training.

Salary:  
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from 30,000 to 45,000 USD annually, based on your experience and skills.
