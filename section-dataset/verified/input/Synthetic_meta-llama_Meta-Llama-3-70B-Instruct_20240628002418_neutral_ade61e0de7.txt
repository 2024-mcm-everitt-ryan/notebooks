Company: Sharp-Riggs
Role: Mailroom Section Supervisor

About Us:
Sharp-Riggs is a leading provider of logistics and supply chain management solutions. We are committed to delivering exceptional service to our customers and fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced and skilled Mailroom Section Supervisor to join our team. As a Mailroom Section Supervisor, you will be responsible for overseeing the daily operations of our mailroom, ensuring timely and efficient processing of mail and packages. You will also supervise a team of mailroom staff, providing guidance and support to ensure high-quality service.

Responsibilities:

 Manage the daily operations of the mailroom, including sorting, processing, and distributing mail and packages
 Supervise and train a team of mailroom staff, providing guidance and support to ensure high-quality service
 Develop and implement processes to improve efficiency and productivity
 Collaborate with other departments to ensure seamless delivery of services
 Maintain accurate records and reports, and perform other administrative tasks as required

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in a mailroom or similar environment
 Strong leadership and supervisory skills
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities
 High school diploma or equivalent required; post-secondary education in a related field an asset

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement savings plan.
