Company: Gillespie-Young

Job Title: City Assessor

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented City Assessor to join our team at Gillespie-Young. As a City Assessor, you will be responsible for accurately assessing the value of properties, conducting on-site inspections, and communicating with property owners. This role requires strong analytical and communication skills, as well as the ability to work independently and as part of a team.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct on-site inspections of properties to determine their value
 Analyze data and market trends to accurately assess property values
 Communicate with property owners and respond to inquiries and concerns
 Work closely with other departments to ensure accurate and timely assessments
 Meet strict deadlines and work in a fast-paced environment

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g. real estate, finance)
 2+ years of experience in property assessment or a related field
 Strong analytical and communication skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Must be able to lift up to 25 pounds and stand for long periods of time
 Must be comfortable working in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Generous paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
