Company Background:
Cook PLC is a leading food manufacturing company dedicated to providing high-quality products to our customers. We are seeking a highly skilled Human Resources Analyst to join our team.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
The Human Resources Analyst will be responsible for providing analytical support to the HR department, including data analysis, reporting, and project management. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills, be highly organized, and have the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze HR data to identify trends and areas for improvement
 Develop and maintain reports to track key HR metrics
 Manage HR projects from start to finish, ensuring timely completion and high-quality results
 Collaborate with HR team members to develop and implement process improvements
 Perform other HR-related duties as assigned

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in an HR role, preferably in a manufacturing environment
 Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
