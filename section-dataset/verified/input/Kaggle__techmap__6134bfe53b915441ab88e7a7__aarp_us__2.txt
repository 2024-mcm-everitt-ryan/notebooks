Future Leaders Program

Amica is searching for talented individuals for our Associate Adjuster position in our countrywide branch offices as part of our Future Leaders Program. An Associate Adjuster class is scheduled for October 2021. Our Future Leaders Program begins with paid hands-on training at our headquarters in Rhode Island.

- Personal mentorships
- Professional growth opportunities
- Relocation throughout the U.S.

Your Role

Be there to help customers in their times of need and restore peace of mind.

Your Responsibilities

- Conduct thorough examinations of claims through investigations, which includes assessing loss sites, performing research, and consulting experts.
- Balance investigations of claims with file reporting in your branch office.
- Analyze information and make decisions on claim files.
- Educate customers, vendors, and other involved parties throughout the claims process.
- Negotiate with attorneys and other parties to ensure fair and equitable claims settlements.
- Develop positive relationships with customers and other industry professionals.
- Acquire and apply knowledge of legal concepts, medical terminology, and home construction.
- Deliver extraordinary customer service at every touchpoint throughout the claims process.

Your Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree, any major.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Demonstrated customer service experience.
- Willingness and ability to relocate across the U.S.
- Strong interpersonal and leadership skills.
- Ability to identify, analyze, and solve problems.

Physical Requirements

- The ability to bend/lift/carry/move up to 15 pounds and adjust and move objects and debris.
- Move about to accomplish tasks.
- Ascend and descend stairs, ramps, and ladders.
- Position yourself in different positions to accomplish tasks in various environments including tight and confined spaces and uneven spaces.
- Inspect and fully appraise all physical aspects of the property and building.
- Be able to drive up to 4 hours per day and possess a valid driver's license.
- Must be able to work under all environmental conditions including low and high temperatures, outdoor elements such as precipitation and wind, noisy environments, hazardous conditions, poor ventilation, and small and/or enclosed spaces.

About Amica

Amica Mutual Insurance Company is America's oldest mutual insurer of automobiles. A direct national writer, Amica also offers home, marine, and umbrella insurance. Amica Life Insurance Company, a wholly owned subsidiary, provides life insurance and retirement solutions.
