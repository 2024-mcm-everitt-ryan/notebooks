We have two exciting opportunities for enthusiastic and motivated graduates to join our team of passionate designers in Tāmaki Makaurau/Auckland. We are looking for a graduate to work in all stages of the design process, as well as a graduate who is wanting to specialise in the Te Ao Māori/cultural design space.

As a Landscape Architect, you’ll work within our multi-disciplinary team to deliver a wide range of industry-leading, transformational projects throughout New Zealand. We will help you grow in confidence, refine the skills you need, and build your networks to help you accelerate your personal and professional development. From the outset, you’ll be actively contributing ideas to projects that help shape our communities and adding to our unique and fun Auckland Team culture.

The successful candidates will be recent graduates or have up to two years’ professional experience, along with the following skills and attributes:
- A Bachelor’s Degree (Hons) or Master’s Degree in Landscape Architecture.
- Excellent graphic skills with experience in the use of a range of digital tools and techniques, including Adobe Creative Cloud, AutoCAD, and SketchUp, as well as hand drawing.
- An eye for detail and an ability to think both spatially and laterally.
- A passion for design and forging stronger connections between people and environments.
- Experience in the use of AutoCAD, SketchUp, Adobe Creative Suite, and draughting and detailing to develop documentation of landscape projects.
- Experience and good understanding of how to take a design from concept through to construction documentation.
- Strong problem-solving skills and an ability to work collaboratively.
- Pro-active approach to workload management with the ability to meet deadlines.
- An enquiring mind, be hungry to learn, and willing to put in the hard yards when things get busy.
- Clear and well-articulated written and pro-active communication skills.
- Are a graduate member of the NZ Institute of Landscape Architects and have an interest in achieving NZILA registration.

Boffa Miskell is a leading New Zealand professional services consultancy, which is focused on better outcomes for our environment. We are entirely employee-owned and growing, with an increasingly diverse portfolio of clients and projects. We offer a supportive and collaborative environment, and our mahi kōkēi initiative enables our team to have greater flexibility around working remotely. We value having an inclusive and diverse workplace and offer ongoing opportunities for professional and personal development to ensure our people can excel and grow.
