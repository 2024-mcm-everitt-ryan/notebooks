Company: Murphy and Sons

Job Title: Grounds Maintenance Worker

Job Description:
Murphy and Sons is seeking a reliable and physically capable individual to join our grounds maintenance team. As a grounds maintenance worker, you will be responsible for ensuring our outdoor spaces are well-manicured and maintained.

Responsibilities:

 Maintain lawns, gardens, and outdoor facilities
 Perform routine maintenance tasks, such as pruning, weeding, and watering
 Operate machinery, including lawn mowers and trimmers
 Lift and move heavy equipment and materials
 Work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision

Requirements:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience in grounds maintenance or a related field
 Ability to lift 50 pounds regularly and work in a physically demanding environment
 Must be able to work in noisy environments and withstand extreme weather conditions
 Ability to pass a physical fitness test

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you will receive comprehensive health benefits, paid time off, and opportunities for professional development.
