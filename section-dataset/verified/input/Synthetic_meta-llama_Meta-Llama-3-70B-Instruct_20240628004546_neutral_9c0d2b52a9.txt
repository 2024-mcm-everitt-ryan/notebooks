Company: Best, Rivera and Murray
Role: Senior Cook

About Us:
Best, Rivera and Murray is a renowned restaurant group dedicated to providing exceptional dining experiences. We value diversity, equity, and inclusion in our workforce and strive to create a welcoming environment for all employees and customers.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Senior Cook to join our culinary team. As a Senior Cook, you will be responsible for managing kitchen operations, leading a team of cooks, and ensuring the highest quality dishes are served to our customers.

Responsibilities:

 Manage kitchen operations, including meal preparation, inventory management, and kitchen maintenance
 Lead and mentor a team of cooks, providing guidance and support to ensure exceptional culinary skills
 Develop and implement new recipes, menus, and cooking techniques
 Maintain a clean and safe kitchen environment, adhering to health and safety standards
 Collaborate with other departments to ensure seamless service delivery

Requirements:

 3+ years of experience as a cook or chef in a high-volume kitchen
 Strong culinary skills, with the ability to lead by example
 Excellent leadership and communication skills
 Ability to multitask, prioritize tasks, and work under pressure
 Food Safety Certification an asset

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 55,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and opportunities for professional development.
