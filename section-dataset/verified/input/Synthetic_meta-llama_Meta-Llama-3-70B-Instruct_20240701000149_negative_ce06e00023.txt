Job Title: Kiln Operator
Company: Jacobson, Moore and Miller

About Us:
Jacobson, Moore and Miller is a leading manufacturer of high-quality ceramics, dedicated to delivering exceptional products and services to our customers.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Kiln Operator to join our team. As a Kiln Operator, you will be responsible for operating and maintaining our kilns, ensuring the production of high-quality ceramics. This is a hands-on role that requires attention to detail, physical strength, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Operate and maintain kilns, ensuring optimal performance and minimal downtime
 Monitor and control temperature, pressure, and other process variables to achieve desired results
 Load and unload kilns, ensuring safe and efficient handling of materials
 Perform routine maintenance tasks, including cleaning and repairing kilns
 Collaborate with production teams to optimize ceramic production

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a manufacturing environment, preferably with kiln operation experience
 Strong mechanical aptitude and ability to work with heavy equipment
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and work in a physically demanding environment
 High school diploma or equivalent required; degree in a related field preferred

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
