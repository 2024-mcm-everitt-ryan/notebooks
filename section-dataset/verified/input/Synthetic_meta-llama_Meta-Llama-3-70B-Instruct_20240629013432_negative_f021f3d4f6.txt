Company Background:
Goodwin Group is a leading logging company dedicated to providing high-quality timber products to customers worldwide.

Job Type:
Logging Worker

Job Description:
We are seeking a motivated and experienced Logging Worker to join our team. As a Logging Worker, you will be responsible for operating heavy machinery, such as harvesters and skidders, to fell and process trees. You will also be required to maintain equipment, follow safety protocols, and work efficiently to meet production targets.

Responsibilities:

 Operate heavy machinery, including harvesters and skidders, to fell and process trees
 Maintain equipment to ensure optimal performance and safety
 Follow safety protocols to ensure a safe working environment
 Work efficiently to meet production targets
 Collaborate with team members to achieve common goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a logging or related industry
 Valid driver's license (required for all jobs)
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced environment
 Preference for candidates with experience in blue-collar jobs

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary range of $40,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we provide a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
