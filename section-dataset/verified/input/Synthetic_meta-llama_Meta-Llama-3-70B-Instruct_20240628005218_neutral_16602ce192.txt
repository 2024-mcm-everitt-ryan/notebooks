Company: Edwards, Gross and Hunt

Job Title: City Medical Examiner

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced City Medical Examiner to join our team at Edwards, Gross and Hunt. As a City Medical Examiner, you will be responsible for performing autopsies, examining bodies, and determining the cause and manner of death. You will also be responsible for testifying in court as an expert witness and collaborating with law enforcement agencies.

Our ideal candidate will have a strong background in forensic pathology, excellent communication skills, and the ability to work well under pressure. If you are a dedicated and compassionate professional looking to make a difference in your community, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Perform autopsies and examinations to determine the cause and manner of death
 Testify in court as an expert witness
 Collaborate with law enforcement agencies to investigate deaths
 Develop and maintain accurate and detailed reports
 Stay up-to-date with the latest developments in forensic pathology

Qualifications and Experience:

 Doctor of Medicine or Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine degree
 Completion of residency program in forensic pathology
 Board certification in forensic pathology
 5+ years of experience in forensic pathology
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
