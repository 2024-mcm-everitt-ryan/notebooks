Company: Donaldson Inc  
Role: Claims Processor  

Company Background:  
Donaldson Inc is a leading provider of insurance services, dedicated to delivering top-notch customer satisfaction and innovative solutions. Our team thrives on a dynamic and fast-paced work environment, aiming to set industry standards.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:  
As a Claims Processor, you will be responsible for managing and processing insurance claims with precision and efficiency. Your role will involve detailed documentation, liaising with clients, and ensuring accurate claims assessments. This position requires a high level of attention to detail and the ability to function in a fast-paced, often chaotic environment.

Responsibilities:  
- Accurately process and manage insurance claims  
- Perform manual tasks such as gathering and sorting physical documents  
- Regularly handle heavy or bulky items as part of claims verification  
- Maintain excellent hand-eye coordination for data entry and verification  
- Function effectively in a chaotic and dynamic work environment  
- Liaise with clients and provide updates on claim status  
- Ensure all claims are documented and processed in compliance with company policies  

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- High School Diploma or equivalent required; Bachelor's degree preferred  
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in claims processing or related field  
- Excellent hand-eye coordination required  
- Ability to handle heavy or bulky items regularly  
- Must be able to perform manual tasks such as assembling office furniture  
- Strong attention to detail and problem-solving skills
