Company: Duncan Inc

Job Title: Structural Engineer

Job Description:
As a Structural Engineer at Duncan Inc, you will be responsible for designing and developing innovative structures that meet our clients' needs. You will work closely with our team of architects, engineers, and project managers to ensure that our projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of quality.

Responsibilities:

 Design and develop structural systems for buildings, bridges, and other infrastructure projects
 Conduct site visits to inspect construction progress and ensure compliance with design specifications
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to resolve design and construction issues
 Develop and implement quality control procedures to ensure accuracy and precision in structural designs
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends, best practices, and regulatory requirements

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's or Master's degree in Civil Engineering or related field
 5+ years of experience in structural engineering, with a focus on building design and construction
 Proficiency in Autodesk Revit, AutoCAD, and other relevant software
 Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills
 Experience in working with diverse teams and stakeholders

Perks and Benefits:
 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $120,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental insurance
 401(k) matching program
 Paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
