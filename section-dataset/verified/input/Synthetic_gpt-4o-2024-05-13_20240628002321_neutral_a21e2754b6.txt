Company: Jenkins, Baldwin and Perkins

Role: Claims Specialist

Company Background:
Jenkins, Baldwin and Perkins is a leading law firm specializing in insurance claims and litigation. With over 50 years of experience, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional service and creating a diverse and inclusive work environment.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Claims Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for reviewing, evaluating, and processing insurance claims efficiently and accurately.

Responsibilities:
- Review and analyze insurance claims to ensure compliance with insurance policies and regulations.
- Communicate with clients, insurance companies, and legal representatives to gather information and resolve claims.
- Maintain accurate records and documentation of claims.
- Provide exceptional customer service and support to clients throughout the claims process.
- Collaborate with team members to improve processes and ensure timely resolution of claims.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Business, Finance, or related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in insurance claims or related field.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and claims management software.

- Family-friendly workplace policies to support work-life balance.
- Inclusive environment that encourages employees to share their unique experiences.
- Access to Nursing Rooms equipped for breastfeeding.
- Professional development opportunities and continuous learning programs.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
