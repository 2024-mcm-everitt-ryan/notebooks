Company: Williams-Taylor

Job Title: Purchasing Agent - Buyer

Job Description:
Williams-Taylor is a leading global supply chain management company dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service and driving business growth. We are committed to fostering an inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities for all.

As a Purchasing Agent - Buyer, you will be responsible for sourcing and procuring goods, materials, and services to support our operations. You will work closely with our cross-functional teams to identify and mitigate risks, negotiate contracts, and ensure timely delivery of high-quality products.

Responsibilities:

 Source and procure goods, materials, and services from suppliers
 Negotiate contracts and prices with vendors
 Collaborate with internal stakeholders to identify and mitigate risks
 Ensure timely delivery of high-quality products
 Develop and maintain relationships with suppliers

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Supply Chain Management, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in procurement or purchasing
 Strong negotiation and communication skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and procurement software

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. You will also receive comprehensive health insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off. Our inclusive culture values hiring candidates with disabilities, and we are committed to providing reasonable accommodations to support your success.
