Company: Mata-Baker

Job Title: Junior Public Health Nurse

About Us:
Mata-Baker is a leading healthcare organization dedicated to providing quality services to our communities. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion.

Job Description:
We are seeking a Junior Public Health Nurse to join our team. As a Junior Public Health Nurse, you will play a crucial role in promoting and protecting the health of our community. Your responsibilities will include:

 Developing and implementing health education programs
 Conducting health assessments and providing guidance to patients
 Collaborating with healthcare professionals to develop care plans
 Maintaining accurate records and reports

Responsibilities and Qualifications:
 Bachelor's degree in Nursing or a related field
 Current registration with the relevant nursing council
 1-2 years of experience in public health nursing
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan.

We value the contributions of all employees and provide an inclusive work environment for everyone. We ensure equal opportunities for all job applicants and support a work environment that accommodates all.
