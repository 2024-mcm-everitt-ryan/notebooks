Company: Williams, Schmidt and Hayes

Role: Rancher

Company Background:
Williams, Schmidt and Hayes is a leading agricultural firm specializing in sustainable practices and innovative farming techniques. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment and actively seek to diversify our workforce.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking skilled professionals to join our team as a Rancher. The ideal candidate will possess job-related expertise essential for maintaining and managing our extensive ranch operations. This role involves overseeing livestock, managing grazing schedules, and ensuring the health and well-being of the animals under our care.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee daily ranch operations.
- Manage livestock health and care.
- Develop and implement grazing schedules.
- Ensure adherence to sustainable farming practices.
- Maintain accurate records of animal health and ranch activities.
- Collaborate with team members to optimize ranch productivity.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Relevant qualifications in agriculture, animal science, or a related field.
- Proven experience in ranch management or livestock care.
- Strong understanding of sustainable farming practices.
- Excellent organizational and communication skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Open to all qualified applicants.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $60,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Health insurance and retirement benefits.
- Opportunities for professional development and training.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.
