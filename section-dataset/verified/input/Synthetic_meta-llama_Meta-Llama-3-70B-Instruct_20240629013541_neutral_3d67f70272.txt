Company: Brown-Ruiz

Role: Bookbinder

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Bookbinder to join our team at Brown-Ruiz. As a Bookbinder, you will be responsible for binding books, repairing damaged books, and creating handmade book covers. You will work closely with our team to ensure that all books are bound to the highest standard.

Responsibilities:

 Bind books using various techniques and materials
 Repair damaged books to their original condition
 Create handmade book covers using leather, fabric, and other materials
 Collaborate with the team to meet production deadlines
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in bookbinding or a related field
 Strong attention to detail and manual dexterity
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Relevant health accommodations will be provided

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. Our team enjoys a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays. We value diverse backgrounds and experiences, and we welcome applicants from all walks of life.
