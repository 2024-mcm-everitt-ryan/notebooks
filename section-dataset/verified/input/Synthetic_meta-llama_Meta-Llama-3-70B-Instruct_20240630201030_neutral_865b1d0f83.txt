Company: Hernandez, Mcgrath and Miller

Job Title: Compliance Auditor

About Us:
Hernandez, Mcgrath and Miller is a leading financial services firm dedicated to providing exceptional auditing services to our clients. We are committed to creating a diverse and equitable workplace where everyone can thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Compliance Auditor to join our team. As a Compliance Auditor, you will be responsible for conducting audits to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and company policies. You will work closely with our operations team to identify areas of improvement and implement corrective actions.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct audits to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and company policies
 Identify areas of improvement and implement corrective actions
 Collaborate with the operations team to develop and implement process improvements
 Analyze data to identify trends and areas of risk
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with internal stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
 3+ years of experience in compliance auditing or a related field
 Strong analytical skills and attention to detail
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
