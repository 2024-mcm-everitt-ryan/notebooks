Ceiling Installer

We have a position available on our team for hard-working, motivated ceiling installers/hammer hands.

- No experience is needed. You’ll learn everything on the job.
- Work is based in Wellington CBD, Hutt Valley, and Porirua, so it would be useful to have your own transport.
- Must be physically fit, have a clean criminal record, and be able to work independently.
- Immediate start, pay negotiable based on experience.

You will be joining a small team of young guys who work hard and have a laugh.

The job will involve:
- Installing 2-way ceilings and plasterboard ceilings.
- Plasterboard fixing.
- Acoustic insulation.

For more information, please send us your CV and a little bit about yourself and we’ll get back to you.
