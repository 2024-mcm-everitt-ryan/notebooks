Company: Baker, Case and Evans
Role: Reinforcing Iron Worker

At Baker, Case and Evans, we're committed to building a diverse and inclusive workforce that reflects the communities we serve. As a Reinforcing Iron Worker, you'll play a crucial role in shaping the infrastructure of our cities and towns.

Job Description:
We're seeking a highly skilled Reinforcing Iron Worker to join our team. As a Reinforcing Iron Worker, you'll be responsible for installing, positioning, and securing iron reinforcing materials to support the construction of buildings, bridges, and other structures.

Responsibilities:

 Install and position iron reinforcing materials according to blueprints and specifications
 Secure iron reinforcing materials using wire, clips, or other fastening devices
 Cut and bend iron reinforcing materials to fit around obstructions
 Collaborate with construction teams to ensure timely and efficient project completion

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Reinforcing Iron Worker or similar role
 Strong understanding of construction safety protocols and procedures
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work at heights
 High school diploma or equivalent required; vocational training or certifications a plus

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $55,000 to $75,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
