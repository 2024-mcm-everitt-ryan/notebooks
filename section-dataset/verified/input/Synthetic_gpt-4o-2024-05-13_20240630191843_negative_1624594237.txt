Company Background:
Bailey and Sons is a family-owned and operated restaurant chain celebrated for its homestyle cooking and warm, inviting atmosphere. We pride ourselves on creating a dining experience that feels like home. 

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
Bailey and Sons is seeking an experienced Head Cook to join our team. The ideal candidate will be passionate about cooking and have a nurturing approach to team management. As the Head Cook, she will be responsible for overseeing the kitchen operations and ensuring the quality of the food meets our high standards.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee daily kitchen operations and manage kitchen staff.
- Develop and execute menu items while maintaining consistency and quality.
- Train and mentor kitchen staff with a supportive and caring approach.
- Ensure a clean and safe kitchen environment in compliance with health regulations.
- Manage inventory and coordinate with suppliers for timely deliveries.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience as a head cook or executive chef in a similar establishment.
- Superior culinary skills with a focus on homestyle cooking.
- Strong leadership skills with a nurturing approach.
- Excellent organizational and multitasking abilities.
- Ability to work well under pressure in a fast-paced environment.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $50,000 to $65,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance coverage.
- Paid time off and vacation days.
- Employee discounts on meals.
- Opportunities for professional development and growth within the company.
