Senior Service Desk Agent
Company: Powers Ltd

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We're seeking a results-driven individual to join our Service Desk team as a Senior Service Desk Agent. As a key member of our technical support team, you will be responsible for resolving complex technical issues, providing exceptional customer service, and ensuring seamless communication with our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues in a timely and efficient manner
 Provide top-notch customer service, ensuring client satisfaction and loyalty
 Collaborate with internal teams to resolve issues and improve processes
 Analyze and identify trends, making recommendations for process improvements
 Lead and mentor junior team members to enhance their skills and knowledge

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in a technical support role, with a strong track record of achieving results
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to work independently
 Excellent communication and leadership skills, with experience in mentoring junior team members
 Proficiency in IT service management tools and technologies

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
