Company: Sherman and Sons

Job Title: Auto Mechanic

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and experienced Auto Mechanic to join our team at Sherman and Sons. As an Auto Mechanic, you will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing vehicles, as well as providing excellent customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Diagnose and repair vehicles in a timely and efficient manner
 Provide accurate estimates and explanations to customers
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace
 Stay up-to-date with industry developments and new technologies

Qualifications and Experience:
We are open to all capable applicants who possess a strong understanding of vehicle mechanics and repair. Experience in required tasks is valued, and we offer training and development opportunities to help you grow in your role.

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, paid time off, and a retirement matching program.
