Company Background:
Rowe-Jacobs is a leading organization in the non-profit sector, dedicated to supporting various community initiatives through grants and funding. Our team is made up of individuals from a variety of backgrounds, and we strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued.

Job Type:
Full-time, Grant Manager

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Grant Manager to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for overseeing the grant application process, managing awarded funds, and ensuring compliance with grant regulations. This role is critical in helping us achieve our mission to support community initiatives effectively.

Responsibilities:
- Coordinate and manage all aspects of the grant lifecycle, from application to reporting.
- Develop and maintain relationships with grantors and stakeholders.
- Ensure compliance with all grant requirements and regulations.
- Prepare and submit grant proposals and reports.
- Monitor grant-funded programs to ensure financial and operational goals are met.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Public Administration, or a related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in grant management or related field.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Strong organizational and project management skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Familiarity with grant management software is a plus.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from 40,000 USD to 60,000 USD based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Opportunities for professional development and growth.
- A supportive and inclusive workplace culture that respects and values the diversity of the LGBTQ community.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are encouraged to apply regardless of their background by submitting a resume and cover letter to careers@rowejacobs.com. Our workplace is free from discrimination and harassment of any kind, and people who are committed to excellence will thrive here.
