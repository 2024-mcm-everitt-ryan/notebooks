Company Background:
Frazier LLC is a leading shipbuilding and marine engineering company dedicated to designing and constructing innovative vessels that shape the future of the maritime industry.

Job Type:
Naval Architect

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Naval Architect to join our team of experts in designing and developing state-of-the-art ships. As a Naval Architect at Frazier LLC, you will be responsible for creating detailed designs, plans, and specifications for new ship builds and modifications. You will work closely with our cross-functional teams to ensure that our vessels meet the highest standards of safety, efficiency, and performance.

Responsibilities:

 Design and develop ship concepts, including hull forms, propulsion systems, and onboard systems
 Create detailed 2D and 3D designs, models, and simulations
 Collaborate with engineering teams to ensure design feasibility and compliance with regulatory requirements
 Conduct stability and performance analyses to optimize ship design
 Develop and maintain design documentation, including technical specifications and reports

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's or Master's degree in Naval Architecture, Marine Engineering, or a related field
 Minimum 5 years of experience in naval architecture or a related field
 Proficiency in CAD software, such as AutoCAD or Rhino
 Strong understanding of ship design principles, naval architecture, and marine engineering
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills
