Company: Blankenship-Edwards

Job Title: Case Management Nurse

About Us:
Blankenship-Edwards is a leading healthcare provider dedicated to delivering high-quality patient care. We believe in the power of diversity to drive innovation and strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking a compassionate and experienced Case Management Nurse to join our team. As a Case Management Nurse, you will work closely with patients, families, and healthcare providers to develop and implement personalized care plans. Your expertise will help us improve patient outcomes and enhance the overall patient experience.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct comprehensive patient assessments to identify health needs and develop individualized care plans
 Collaborate with interdisciplinary teams to coordinate patient care and services
 Provide education and support to patients, families, and caregivers
 Monitor patient progress and adjust care plans as needed
 Maintain accurate and timely records

Qualifications and Experience:

 Current RN license in good standing
 2+ years of experience in case management or a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $85,000 to $110,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes:
 Medical, dental, and vision insurance
 Generous paid time off, including parental leave
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A supportive and inclusive work environment
