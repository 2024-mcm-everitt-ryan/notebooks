Company: Martinez-Melton

Job Title: Grant Coordinator

About Us:
Martinez-Melton is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering women and girls in underserved communities. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace that reflects the communities we serve.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Grant Coordinator to join our team. As a Grant Coordinator, you will be responsible for researching and identifying potential funding opportunities, preparing grant proposals, and managing grant reports. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to work effectively with our program teams and external partners.

Responsibilities:

 Research and identify potential funding opportunities that align with our organizational goals
 Prepare and submit grant proposals to foundations, corporations, and government agencies
 Manage grant reports, ensuring timely and accurate submission
 Collaborate with program teams to develop project budgets and proposals
 Build and maintain relationships with external partners and stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in grant writing and management
 Bachelor's degree in Non-Profit Management, Public Administration, or related field
 Proven track record of securing funding from foundations, corporations, and government agencies
 Excellent written and verbal communication skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
