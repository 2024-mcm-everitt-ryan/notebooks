Company: Crawford-Leonard
Role: Unit Clerk

We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Unit Clerk to join our team. As the first point of contact for our patients, you will be responsible for providing exceptional customer service, maintaining accurate records, and ensuring the smooth operation of our unit.

Responsibilities:

 Greet patients and families, addressing their concerns and providing general information
 Manage patient flow, coordinating with healthcare professionals to ensure efficient care
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records, both electronic and physical
 Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to ensure comprehensive care
 Perform various administrative tasks, such as scheduling and data entry

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required; post-secondary education in a related field an asset
 1-2 years of experience in a healthcare setting, preferably as a unit clerk or medical administrative assistant
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
 Proficiency in electronic medical records and Microsoft Office

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 40,000 to USD 55,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment

We welcome candidates of all backgrounds and perspectives, and are committed to creating an inclusive and equitable workplace.
