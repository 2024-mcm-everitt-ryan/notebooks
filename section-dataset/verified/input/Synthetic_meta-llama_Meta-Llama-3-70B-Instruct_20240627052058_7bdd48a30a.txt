Company: Johnson-Moran

Associate & Venture Fellow

About Us:
Johnson-Moran is a dynamic venture capital firm dedicated to empowering entrepreneurs and driving innovation. We believe that diverse perspectives and experiences are essential to achieving success in the fast-paced world of startups.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and ambitious Associate & Venture Fellow to join our team. As an Associate & Venture Fellow, you will play a critical role in identifying and evaluating investment opportunities, working closely with our portfolio companies, and contributing to the growth and development of our firm.

- Conduct market research and analysis to identify potential investment opportunities
- Collaborate with our investment team to evaluate deals and develop investment theses
- Build and maintain relationships with entrepreneurs, investors, and other stakeholders
- Contribute to the development of our firm's strategy and operations

Qualifications:
- 2+ years of experience in venture capital, private equity, or a related field
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Passion for entrepreneurship and innovation

What We Offer:
- Salary range: $80,000 - $120,000 per year, depending on experience
- Bonus structure based on performance
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Flexible work arrangements and remote work options
