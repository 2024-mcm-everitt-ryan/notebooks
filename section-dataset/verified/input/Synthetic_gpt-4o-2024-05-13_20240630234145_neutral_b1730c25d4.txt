Company: Nichols Group

Role: Postmaster

Company Background:
Nichols Group is a leading logistics company specializing in mail and package delivery services. We are committed to providing exceptional service and reliability to our customers.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and proactive Postmaster to oversee our postal operations. The ideal candidate will have experience in managing postal services and ensuring efficient and timely delivery of mail and packages.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee day-to-day postal operations, ensuring efficiency and accuracy.
- Manage a team of postal workers, providing guidance and support.
- Ensure compliance with postal regulations and company policies.
- Implement strategies to improve service delivery and customer satisfaction.
- Handle customer inquiries and resolve any issues promptly.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience in postal service management or a related field.
- Strong organizational and leadership skills.
- Ability to manage a team effectively and ensure high performance.
- Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
- Proficiency in basic engineering construction and equipment management is required.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills.
