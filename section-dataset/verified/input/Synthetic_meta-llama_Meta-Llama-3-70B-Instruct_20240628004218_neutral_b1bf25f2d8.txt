Job Description:

 Provide technical support to clients via phone, email, and in-person
 Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues related to hardware and software

 Collaborate with other teams to resolve complex technical issues
 Document technical issues and their resolutions

Qualifications and Experience Needed:

 Associate's degree in Computer Science or related field
 2+ years of experience in technical support or a related field
 Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
 Strong knowledge of Windows and macOS operating systems
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
