Job Description

Role & Responsibility

- You will become a member of the APAC Treasury team in all aspects of Treasury.
- Daily cash positioning and rolling cash flow forecasting.
- In-house banking.
- FX and settlement.
- Bank account management, etc.
- Partnering with other treasury/finance areas to support closing cycles.
- Support SAP treasury module implementation.
- Ensure relevant compliance with local statutory (such as FX control) requirements and 3M global financial standards.
- Leveraging knowledge of treasury technology & systems (such as SAP, SWIFT, Alteryx, Power BI, etc.) to automate and improve current processes.
- Participating in global change management projects across cash and banking.
- Partnering with business functions to provide proactive finance guidance to influence business thinking related to cash management, financial instruments, and banking strategies.

Qualification

To set you up for success in this role from day one, 3M requires (at a minimum) the following qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree or higher (completed and verified prior to start) from an accredited institution.
- 5 years finance experience and at least 2 years treasury experience in an international enterprise (Max. 15 years).

Additional qualifications that could help you succeed even further in this role include:
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Fluent English both in written and verbal.
- Korean language skill.
- Familiar with general FX regulations and able to plan the response according to policy updates.
- Strong numeracy skills, experienced in using Microsoft Excel.
- SAP knowledge & experience would be an advantage.
- Capacity on technical tools (Power BI, Alteryx).
- Can problem solve and work through issues independently.
- Self-starter with the ability to prioritize projects and drive change.
- Cross-cultural project management experience, good team player and able to motivate self and others.
