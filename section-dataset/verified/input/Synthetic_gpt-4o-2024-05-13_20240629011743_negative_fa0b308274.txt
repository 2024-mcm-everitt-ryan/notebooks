Company Background:
Gray, Pratt and Hooper is a renowned company in the logistics and transportation sector. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional services and maintaining the highest standards in the industry.

Role: Hostler

Job Description:
We are looking for a hostler to join our team. This role is crucial to maintaining the flow of our operations. The hostler will be responsible for moving trailers and containers within our yard, ensuring they are in the correct locations efficiently and without errors. This job demands attention to detail and a high level of precision.

Responsibilities:
- Move trailers and containers within the yard.
- Inspect equipment for defects.
- Communicate effectively with the yard and warehouse teams.
- Maintain a clean and organized work environment.
- Adhere to all safety protocols and regulations.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent required.
- Proven experience in a similar role.
- Continuous employment history required.
- Must execute tasks flawlessly.
- Strong physical stamina and ability to work in various weather conditions.
- No room for dumb mistakes in this role.
- We need someone with a great sense of humor who can lighten the mood.

Perks and Benefits:
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $30,000 to $45,000 per year, based on your experience and skills.
