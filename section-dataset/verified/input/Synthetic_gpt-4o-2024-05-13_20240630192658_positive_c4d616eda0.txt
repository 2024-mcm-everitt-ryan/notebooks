Company: Hubbard-Jones  
Role: Assistant Civil Engineer  

Company Background:  
Hubbard-Jones is a leading engineering consulting firm committed to delivering sustainable infrastructure solutions. We pride ourselves on fostering innovation, excellence, and an inclusive work environment.

Job Type: Full-time  

Job Description:  
Hubbard-Jones is seeking a motivated Assistant Civil Engineer to join our dynamic team. This position is part of our effort to promote women in the industry. You will work closely with senior engineers on various infrastructure projects, contributing to our mission of creating resilient communities through innovative design and engineering solutions.

Responsibilities:  
- Assist in the planning, design, and execution of civil engineering projects.  
- Conduct field surveys and prepare reports.  
- Coordinate with stakeholders, ensuring project specifications and deadlines are met.  
- Support senior engineers in preparing technical documents and drawings.  
- Participate in project meetings and provide technical insights.

Qualifications and Experience:  
- Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering or related field.  
- 1-3 years of experience in civil engineering or related roles.  
- Proficiency in AutoCAD, Civil 3D, and other engineering software.  
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.  
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Professional development opportunities.  
- Flexible work hours and remote work options.  
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000, based on your experience and skills.  
- Paid time off and holidays.  
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
