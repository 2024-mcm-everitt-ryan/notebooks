Company Background:
Campbell, Higgins and Smith is a renowned institution committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive community. We uphold values of integrity, compassion, and service.

Role: Clergy

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and empathetic clergy member to join our diverse team. The ideal candidate will possess strong leadership qualities, a deep sense of compassion, and the ability to support our congregation through spiritual guidance and community service.

Responsibilities:
- Provide spiritual guidance and support to the congregation.
- Lead religious services, ceremonies, and community events.
- Offer counseling and support to individuals and families.
- Collaborate with other clergy members and community leaders.
- Organize and participate in outreach programs to serve the broader community.

Qualifications and Experience:
- A degree in Theology, Divinity, or a related field from an accredited institution.
- Previous experience in a clergy role is preferred, but not mandatory.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Demonstrated dedication to fostering a welcoming and inclusive community.
- Commitment to maintaining a balanced and inclusive perspective in all interactions.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €50,000 to €70,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits.
- Opportunities for professional development and continuing education.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.
- Paid time off and family leave policies.

Application Process:
To apply, please submit your resume, cover letter, and references. Campbell, Higgins and Smith is an equal opportunity employer. We value diverse perspectives and encourage candidates from all backgrounds to apply.
