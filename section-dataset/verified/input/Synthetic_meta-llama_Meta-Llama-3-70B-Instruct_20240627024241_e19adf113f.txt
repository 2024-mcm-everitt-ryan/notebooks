Company: Hays Inc

Job Title: Field Systems Engineer (FSE III)

About Us:
Hays Inc is a leading technology solutions provider dedicated to delivering innovative and effective systems to our clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, teamwork, and customer satisfaction.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Field Systems Engineer (FSE III) to join our team. As an FSE III, you will be responsible for designing, installing, and maintaining complex systems for our clients. You will work closely with our sales team to identify client needs and provide technical solutions to meet those needs.

Responsibilities:

- Design and implement complex systems for clients
- Collaborate with the sales team to identify client needs and provide technical solutions
- Conduct site surveys to determine system requirements
- Install and configure systems according to client specifications
- Troubleshoot and repair system issues as needed
- Provide exceptional customer service and support

Qualifications:

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field
- Minimum 5 years of experience as a Field Systems Engineer
- Strong knowledge of system design and implementation
- Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
