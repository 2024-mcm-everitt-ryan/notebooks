Company: Palmer, Mack and Warner
Job Title: Loan Counselor

About Us:
Palmer, Mack and Warner is a leading financial services provider dedicated to empowering individuals and communities to achieve their financial goals. We believe that a diverse and inclusive workforce is essential to delivering exceptional service and driving business success.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Loan Counselor to join our team. As a Loan Counselor, you will work closely with clients to understand their financial needs and provide personalized loan solutions. Your expertise and guidance will help our clients make informed decisions and achieve financial stability.

Responsibilities:

- Conduct thorough financial assessments to determine clients' loan eligibility
- Develop and present customized loan options to clients
- Build strong relationships with clients to ensure long-term financial success
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless loan processing
- Stay up-to-date with industry trends and regulatory changes to provide expert advice

Qualifications:

- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, or related field
- 3+ years of experience in loan counseling or a related field
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

Benefits:

- Salary range: $60,000 - $80,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Recognition and reward for outstanding performance

We welcome applications from professionals from diverse cultural backgrounds, particularly underrepresented groups. Our goal is to diversify our team with more African American talent. By joining our team, you will be part of a inclusive and diverse work environment that values and celebrates individual differences.
