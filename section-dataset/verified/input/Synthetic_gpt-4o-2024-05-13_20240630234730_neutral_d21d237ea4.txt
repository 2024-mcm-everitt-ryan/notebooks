Company Background:

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking an Investigative Manager to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for overseeing investigative operations and ensuring high-quality outcomes for our clients. This role demands a strong leader with excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to manage multiple projects effectively.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and manage a team of investigators.
- Develop and implement investigative strategies.
- Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory standards.
- Communicate findings to clients and stakeholders.
- Maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Criminal Justice, Law, or related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in investigative roles.
- Proven leadership and team management skills.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
