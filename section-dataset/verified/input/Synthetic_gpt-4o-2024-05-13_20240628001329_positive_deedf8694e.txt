Company: Perry-Morris

Role: Insurance Underwriter

Company Background:
Perry-Morris has been a leader in the insurance industry for over 50 years. We pride ourselves on our stability, innovative approach, and dedication to customer service. We are expanding our team and are looking for experienced professionals to join us in maintaining our high standards and commitment to excellence.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description and Responsibilities:
Perry-Morris is seeking an Insurance Underwriter with a deep understanding of the industry. The successful candidate will evaluate risk, determine policy terms, and set premium prices. Responsibilities include:
- Assessing applications for insurance to determine risk levels.
- Evaluating financial documents and other relevant information.
- Ensuring compliance with company policies and industry regulations.
- Collaborating with agents and brokers to address clients' needs.
- Maintaining thorough and accurate records of decisions made.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Must have significant industry tenure.
- Established career of 15+ years essential.
- Must have a minimum of 14 years in the industry.
- Proven track record over decades.
- Senior-level experience of 20+ years preferred.
- Advanced degree in finance, economics, or related field.
- Strong analytical skills and attention to detail.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $120,000 per year, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Generous paid time off and holidays.
