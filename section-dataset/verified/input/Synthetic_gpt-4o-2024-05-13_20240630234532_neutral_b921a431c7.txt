Company Background:
Gonzalez Inc is a leading construction company specializing in high-rise buildings, bridges, and other complex steel structures. With decades of experience, we take pride in our safe, efficient, and high-quality work.

Job Type:
Full-Time Iron Worker

Job Description:
Gonzalez Inc is looking for a dedicated and skilled Iron Worker to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for installing and reinforcing steel structures, ensuring that all work meets our high standards for safety and quality.

Responsibilities:
- Install steel components according to blueprints and specifications.
- Operate equipment such as welding machines and cutting torches.
- Inspect and maintain tools and equipment.
- Collaborate with other team members to complete projects efficiently.
- Adhere to all safety protocols and guidelines.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Previous experience as an Iron Worker or in a similar role.
- Proficiency in reading blueprints and technical drawings.
- Ability to lift heavy objects up to 50kg in weight unassisted and work at heights.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Certification in welding or relevant skills is a plus.
- Must be able to work in various weather conditions.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $60,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and cover letter to careers@gonzalezinc.com.
