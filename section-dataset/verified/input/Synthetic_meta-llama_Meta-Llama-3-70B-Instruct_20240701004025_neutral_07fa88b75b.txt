Job Title: Sampler
Company: Roberts Ltd

We are seeking a highly skilled Sampler to join our team at Roberts Ltd. As a Sampler, you will be responsible for collecting and preparing samples for laboratory testing, ensuring accuracy and precision in our quality control process.

Job Description:

The successful candidate will be adept at problem-solving, with a strong technical background in sampling and laboratory procedures. They will possess excellent communication skills, with the ability to effectively collaborate with our laboratory team. The Sampler will be responsible for:

 Collecting and preparing samples for laboratory testing
 Maintaining accurate records of sampling procedures and results
 Collaborating with laboratory staff to ensure efficient testing procedures
 Participating in quality control and quality assurance activities

Requirements and Qualifications:

 A strong technical background in sampling and laboratory procedures
 Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a temperature controlled environment and fast-paced environment

What We Offer:

 A competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per annum, based on experience and skills
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage
 Opportunities for professional development and growth within the company
 A dynamic and supportive work environment
