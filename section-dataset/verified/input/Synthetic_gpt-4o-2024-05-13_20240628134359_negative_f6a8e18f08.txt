Company Background:
Cunningham, Good, and Thomas is a leading healthcare provider committed to delivering top-notch medical services. We prioritize excellence in all our endeavors and aim to create a supportive environment for our staff.

Job Type:
Full-Time School Health Nurse Practitioner

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled School Health Nurse Practitioner to join our team. The successful candidate will provide exceptional healthcare services to students, ensuring their well-being and addressing any medical concerns promptly.

Responsibilities:
- Provide comprehensive healthcare services to students, including assessments, diagnoses, and treatments.
- Develop and implement health plans for students with chronic illnesses or special healthcare needs.
- Collaborate with school staff, parents, and external healthcare providers to ensure a coordinated approach to student health.
- Conduct health education sessions for students, staff, and parents.
- Maintain accurate and thorough medical records for all students.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Requires a high level of emotional intelligence and interpersonal skills.
- Requires excellent time management skills and the ability to meet tight deadlines.
- Must be able to stand for long periods of time.
- Requires excellent hand-eye coordination.
- Requires frequent bending and stooping.
- Master's degree in Nursing or a related field.
- Valid Nurse Practitioner license.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in a similar role.

- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $85,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday leave.
- Professional development opportunities and support for continuing education.
- A supportive and collaborative work environment.
