Company: Duncan-Scott

Job Type: Cost Estimator

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Cost Estimator to join our team at Duncan-Scott. As a Cost Estimator, you will be responsible for preparing and managing cost estimates for our projects, ensuring accurate and timely delivery of projects within budget. You will work closely with our project management team to identify and mitigate risks, and develop cost-saving strategies.

Responsibilities:

 Prepare and manage cost estimates for projects
 Develop and maintain cost models and databases
 Analyze project data to identify trends and areas for cost improvement
 Collaborate with project management team to identify and mitigate risks
 Develop cost-saving strategies and implement process improvements

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Engineering, or related field
 3+ years of experience in cost estimating or related field
 Proficiency in cost estimating software and Microsoft Office
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $90,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and parental time off for all new parents. Our company values the contributions of a diverse workforce and is committed to creating an inclusive and respectful environment for all employees.
