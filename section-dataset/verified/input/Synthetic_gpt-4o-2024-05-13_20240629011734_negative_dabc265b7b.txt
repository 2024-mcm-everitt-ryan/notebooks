Company: Ramirez-Adams

Role: Human Resources Specialist

Company Background:
Ramirez-Adams is a dynamic company committed to fostering growth and excellence across various industries. We pride ourselves on creating a professional environment that values hard work, dedication, and the commitment to improving employee wellbeing.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Human Resources Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in HR practices and a proven track record in managing diverse talent pools. This role is crucial in maintaining our high standards of employee satisfaction and operational efficiency.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement HR strategies and initiatives aligned with the overall business strategy.
- Bridge management and employee relations by addressing demands, grievances, or other issues.
- Manage the recruitment and selection process to ensure the right talent is onboarded.
- Support current and future business needs through the development, engagement, motivation, and preservation of human capital.
- Oversee and manage a performance appraisal system that drives high performance.
- Maintain pay plan and benefits program.

Qualifications and Experience:
- A minimum of a Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field.
- At least 5 years of proven experience in a corporate HR role; prior employment in hands-on roles is preferred.
- No gaps in employment history.
- Strong understanding of labor legislation and HR best practices.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- A strong preference for candidates coming from professional or corporate backgrounds.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance plans.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and growth.
