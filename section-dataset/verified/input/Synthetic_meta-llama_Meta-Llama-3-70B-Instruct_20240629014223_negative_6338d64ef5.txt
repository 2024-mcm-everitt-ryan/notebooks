Company: Castillo and Sons

Job Title: Radio Repair Mechanic

About Us:
Castillo and Sons is a well-established electronics repair company dedicated to providing top-notch services to our clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and our ability to adapt to the ever-changing electronics landscape.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Radio Repair Mechanic to join our team. As a Radio Repair Mechanic, you will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing a wide range of radio equipment, from consumer electronics to industrial-grade devices. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in electronics repair and a proven track record of delivering high-quality work.

Responsibilities:

 Diagnose and repair radio equipment to ensure optimal performance
 Troubleshoot complex electrical issues and develop effective solutions
 Collaborate with colleagues to share knowledge and best practices
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace, adhering to safety protocols

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in electronics repair, preferably with radio equipment
 High school diploma or equivalent required; vocational training or degree in electronics preferred
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision
 Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. In addition, we provide a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.

Note: We are an equal opportunities employer, but we cannot accommodate candidates with gaps in employment history. Additionally, we maintain a professional work environment, which means visible piercings are not permitted.
