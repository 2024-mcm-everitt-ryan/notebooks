Company: Robinson and Sons
Role: Staff Counsel

We are seeking a talented and compassionate Staff Counsel to join our legal team. As a key member of our department, you will provide expert legal guidance and support to our organization.

As a Staff Counsel, you will be responsible for providing legal counsel to our management team, reviewing contracts and agreements, and ensuring compliance with relevant laws and regulations. Your ability to create a collaborative and harmonious work environment will be essential in this role.

Responsibilities:

 Provide legal guidance and support to management and staff
 Review and negotiate contracts and agreements
 Ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to achieve business objectives
 Develop and maintain relationships with external partners and stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 Law degree from an accredited institution
 3+ years of experience as a lawyer, preferably in a corporate setting
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
