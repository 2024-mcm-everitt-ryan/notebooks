Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a Motor Vehicle Operator to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for the safe and efficient operation of company vehicles, ensuring timely delivery of goods. This position requires adherence to all safety and traffic regulations, as well as company policies.

Responsibilities:
- Operate company vehicles to transport goods to various locations.
- Ensure timely delivery and maintain communication with dispatch.
- Conduct pre-trip and post-trip inspections of vehicles.
- Adhere to all safety and traffic regulations.
- Maintain accurate records of deliveries and vehicle maintenance.
- Assist with loading and unloading of goods as needed.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Valid driver's license with a clean driving record.
- Proven experience as a motor vehicle operator or in a similar role.
- Strong understanding of safety and traffic regulations.
- Ability to conduct vehicle inspections and perform minor maintenance.
- Effective communication skills.
- Ability to lift and move heavy objects as required.
