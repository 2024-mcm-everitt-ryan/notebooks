Job Type: HRO Application Support Manager

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced and highly motivated HRO Application Support Manager to join our team. As an HRO Application Support Manager, you will be responsible for providing technical support and maintenance for our HR systems, ensuring seamless user experience and optimizing system performance.

Responsibilities:

 Provide expert-level technical support for HR systems, including troubleshooting and resolving issues
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize system enhancements
 Develop and maintain comprehensive system documentation
 Conduct training sessions for end-users to ensure optimal system adoption
 Monitor system performance and implement improvements to ensure high availability and scalability

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field
 Minimum 5 years of experience in HR system support, with a focus on application management
 Strong technical skills in HR systems, including experience with HRO applications
 Excellent communication, problem-solving, and analytical skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
