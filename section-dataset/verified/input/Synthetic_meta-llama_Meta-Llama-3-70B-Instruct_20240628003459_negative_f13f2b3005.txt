At Hopkins, Clark and Prince, we are seeking an apprentice inspector to join our team. As a family-oriented company, we prioritize a strong work-life balance and a supportive environment for our employees who share our traditional values.

Job Description:
The apprentice inspector will assist in conducting site visits, inspecting equipment, and ensuring compliance with industry regulations. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work independently.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct site visits to inspect equipment and ensure compliance with industry regulations
 Assist in identifying and reporting defects or issues
 Develop and maintain relationships with clients and stakeholders
 Collaborate with the inspection team to ensure efficient and effective operations

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience in a related field preferred
 Strong communication and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
