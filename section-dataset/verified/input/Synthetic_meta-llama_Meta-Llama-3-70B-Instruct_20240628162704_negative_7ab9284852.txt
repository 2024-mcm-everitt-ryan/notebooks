Company Background:
Davis Group is a leading correctional services provider dedicated to maintaining a safe and secure environment for our staff and inmates.

Job Type:
Jailer

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and physically capable Jailer to join our team. As a Jailer, you will be responsible for maintaining order and ensuring the safety of inmates, staff, and visitors within our correctional facility.

Responsibilities:

 Supervise and monitor inmate activities, including meals, recreation, and housing assignments
 Conduct security checks and respond to emergencies as needed
 Escort inmates to and from cells, showers, and recreational areas
 Maintain accurate records of inmate behavior and incidents
 Collaborate with other staff members to ensure a safe and secure environment

Qualifications and Experience Needed:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 2+ years of experience in a correctional setting or related field
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and climb stairs regularly throughout the day
 Must be able to drive a company vehicle as needed
 Ability to attend frequent meetings and participate actively

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
