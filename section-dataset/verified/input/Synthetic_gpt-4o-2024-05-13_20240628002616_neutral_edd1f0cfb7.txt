Company Background:
Waller-Goodman is a leading financial services company, dedicated to providing top-tier loan solutions to a diverse clientele. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive work environment where all employees feel valued and respected.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Loan Officer to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for evaluating, authorizing, and recommending loan applications for approval. The role requires excellent communication skills, a strong understanding of loan processes, and the ability to manage client relationships effectively.

Responsibilities:
- Evaluate loan applications and documentation within established guidelines.
- Authorize or recommend loan approvals based on creditworthiness.
- Develop and maintain relationships with clients to ensure customer satisfaction.
- Stay updated on financial services trends and regulations.
- Collaborate with team members to achieve departmental goals.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Business, or related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in a loan officer role or similar.
- Strong analytical and decision-making skills.
- Excellent verbal and written communication abilities.
- Proficiency with loan processing software.
