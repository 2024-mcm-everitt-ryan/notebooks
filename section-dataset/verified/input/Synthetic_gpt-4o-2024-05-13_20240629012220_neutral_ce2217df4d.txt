Company: Reese-Patton

Role: Senior Network Engineer

About Us:
Reese-Patton is a renowned technology firm dedicated to providing cutting-edge network solutions. We emphasize a collaborative and inclusive work environment where every team member is valued and respected.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Network Engineer to join our team. This role involves designing, implementing, and maintaining our network infrastructure to ensure optimal performance and security. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in network engineering and a commitment to fostering a culture of open dialogue and respect.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement network solutions to meet business needs.
- Monitor and maintain network performance and security.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support network-related projects.
- Troubleshoot and resolve network issues in a timely manner.
- Document network configurations and processes.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in network engineering.
- Proficiency in network hardware and software, including routers, switches, and firewalls.
- Strong problem-solving and communication skills.
- Ability to work efficiently in a team-oriented environment.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from 60,000 EUR to 90,000 EUR, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Support a culture of open dialogue and respect.
- Reserved parking available for employees.
- Flexible work hours and remote work options.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Respect all religious practices and principles.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their resume and cover letter detailing their qualifications and experience. Reese-Patton is an equal opportunity employer, and we welcome applicants from all backgrounds to apply.
