Job Title: Community & Industry Relations Associate

Company: Noble-Scott

About Us:
Noble-Scott is a dynamic and innovative company dedicated to building strong relationships with our community and industry partners. We value diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to create a workplace culture that is welcoming and inclusive to all.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Community & Industry Relations Associate to join our team. As a key member of our external relations department, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining strong relationships with community groups, industry partners, and stakeholders. Your exceptional communication and interpersonal skills will enable you to effectively collaborate with internal teams and external partners to achieve our goals.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement community engagement strategies to promote Noble-Scott's brand and values
 Build and maintain relationships with community groups, industry partners, and stakeholders
 Collaborate with internal teams to identify opportunities for partnership and collaboration
 Possess strong attention to detail and ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment
 Experience in skilled trades is an advantage
 Align with our company's ethical standards and practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g. communications, public relations, community development)
 Minimum 2 years of experience in community relations, public affairs, or a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 A legal history check is part of our consideration process for all candidates

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A dynamic and inclusive work environment
