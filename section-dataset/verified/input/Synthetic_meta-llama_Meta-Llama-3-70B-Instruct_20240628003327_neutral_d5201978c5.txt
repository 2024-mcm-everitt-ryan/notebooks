Company: Roth, Riley and Yu

Job Title: Massage Therapist

About Us:
Roth, Riley and Yu is a wellness center dedicated to providing exceptional care to our clients. We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for our staff and clients alike.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and compassionate Massage Therapist to join our team. As a Massage Therapist, you will be responsible for providing professional massage therapy services to our clients, designing customized treatment plans, and maintaining a clean and organized workspace.

Responsibilities:

 Provide exceptional massage therapy services to clients
 Design and implement customized treatment plans
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace
 Communicate effectively with clients and staff

Qualifications and Experience:

 Must possess a valid massage therapy license
 Must have at least 2 years of experience in massage therapy
 Must demonstrate strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Must be able to work in a fast-paced environment

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and opportunities for professional development.
