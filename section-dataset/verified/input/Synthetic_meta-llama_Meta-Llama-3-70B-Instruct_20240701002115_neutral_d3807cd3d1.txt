Company: Lambert, Lawrence and Carpenter
Role: Usher

Job Description:
Lambert, Lawrence and Carpenter is seeking a highly skilled Usher to join our team. As an Usher, you will be responsible for providing exceptional customer service, ensuring the safety and comfort of our patrons, and maintaining a clean and organized environment.

Responsibilities:

 Greet patrons and provide directions to their seats
 Ensure the theatre is clean and tidy before, during, and after performances
 Monitor the audience and respond to any concerns or issues
 Collaborate with other staff members to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for our patrons
 Make important decisions quickly and efficiently in high-pressure situations

Qualifications and Experience:

 Excellent interpersonal skills are required
 Ability to work well under pressure and make confident decisions
 Previous experience in customer-facing roles is an asset
 Ability to stand for long periods and lift up to 25 pounds

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our team enjoys a dynamic work environment, opportunities for professional growth, and a positive and cohesive team dynamic.
