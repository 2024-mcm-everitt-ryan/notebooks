Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a diligent and organized Postal Service Mail Sorter to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for sorting and processing mail efficiently and accurately.

Responsibilities:
- Sort and process incoming and outgoing mail.
- Ensure timely delivery and dispatch of mail.
- Maintain accurate records of mail activities.
- Work collaboratively with other team members to achieve daily targets.
- Adhere to safety and operational guidelines.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Attention to detail and organizational skills.
- Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines.
- Previous experience in a similar role is advantageous but not mandatory.
- Basic computer skills.
