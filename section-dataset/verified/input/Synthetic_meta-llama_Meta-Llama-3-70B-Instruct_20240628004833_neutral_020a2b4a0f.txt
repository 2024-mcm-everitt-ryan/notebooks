Company: Davies-Garrett
Role: Taxi Driver

At Davies-Garrett, we are committed to providing a safe and reliable transportation experience to our customers. We are seeking a qualified and experienced Taxi Driver to join our team.

Job Description:
As a Taxi Driver at Davies-Garrett, you will be responsible for transporting passengers safely and efficiently. You will be expected to maintain a clean and organized vehicle, provide excellent customer service, and follow all traffic laws and regulations.

Responsibilities:

 Transport passengers safely and efficiently
 Maintain a clean and organized vehicle
 Provide excellent customer service
 Follow all traffic laws and regulations
 Communicate effectively with dispatch and passengers

Qualifications and Experience:

 Valid driver's license
 At least 2 years of driving experience
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds
 Excellent communication and customer service skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays. We foster an environment where everyone feels valued, and our policies ensure inclusivity for all.
