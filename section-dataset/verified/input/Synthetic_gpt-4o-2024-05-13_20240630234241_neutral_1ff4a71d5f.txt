Company Background:
Ingram Inc is a leading provider of security services, specializing in transit and railroad environments. We are dedicated to ensuring the safety and security of commuters and freight operations across the nation.

Job Type:
Full-Time, Transit and Railroad Police

Job Description:
Ingram Inc is seeking a highly motivated and skilled problem solver to join our team as a Transit and Railroad Police Officer. The successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring the safety and security of passengers, employees, and infrastructure in transit and railroad environments.

Responsibilities:
- Patrol assigned areas to prevent and detect crimes.
- Investigate incidents and accidents within the transit and railroad systems.
- Conduct security checks and enforce laws and regulations.
- Respond to emergencies and provide assistance to those in need.
- Collaborate with local law enforcement agencies and other security personnel.
- Prepare detailed reports and documentation of incidents.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Previous experience in law enforcement or a related field is preferred.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to handle a fast-paced work environment and make quick decisions.
- Must be able to meet the physical demands of the job.
- Valid driver's license and a clean driving record.
- Recon experience is a plus for this role.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.
