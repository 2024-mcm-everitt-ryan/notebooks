Company Background:
Figueroa LLC is a leading technology solutions provider, dedicated to delivering innovative and efficient systems to our diverse clientele. Our family-oriented work environment promotes collaboration and professional growth.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Computer and Information Systems Manager to oversee and enhance our technological infrastructure. He/she will participate in team meetings and provide input on critical technology decisions, ensuring optimal performance and security.

Responsibilities:
- Manage and direct the work of IT personnel.
- Develop and implement IT policies and procedures.
- Oversee the installation and maintenance of hardware and software systems.
- Ensure data security and compliance with regulatory standards.
- Participate in meetings to provide input and guidance on IT strategy.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in IT management.
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical abilities.
- Proficiency in system and network administration.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from £70,000 to £90,000, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Opportunities available for both men and women in our growing company.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Professional development and training programs.
