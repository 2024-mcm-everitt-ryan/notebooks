Job Title: Tender
Company: Gonzalez Group

About Us:
Gonzalez Group is a leading construction company dedicated to delivering high-quality projects on time and on budget. We pride ourselves on our family-oriented work environment, where our employees' personal and professional growth are valued.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Tender to join our team. As a Tender, you will be responsible for preparing and submitting tenders, managing project budgets, and ensuring timely delivery of projects.

Responsibilities:

 Prepare and submit tenders to clients
 Manage project budgets and ensure cost-effectiveness
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver projects on time
 Develop and maintain client relationships

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Construction Management or related field
 At least 5 years of experience in tender management
 Proven track record of successful tender submissions
 Excellent communication and project management skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $120,000 USD per annum, based on your experience and skills. Our employees enjoy a range of benefits, including paternity leave policies, mother-friendly spaces in our offices, and a supportive work environment that values traditional family structures and respects diverse lifestyle choices.
