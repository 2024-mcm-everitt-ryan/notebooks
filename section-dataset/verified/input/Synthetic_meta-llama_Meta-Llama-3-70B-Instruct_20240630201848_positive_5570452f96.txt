Company: Irwin-Morris
Role: Furniture Finisher

At Irwin-Morris, we're committed to creating a workplace where everyone can thrive. We're seeking a skilled Furniture Finisher to join our team, and we're particularly excited to attract talented women looking to advance their careers in this field. As a female-dominated industry, we believe that women bring a unique perspective and skillset to furniture finishing, and we're eager to provide opportunities for them to grow and develop their skills.

Job Description:
As a Furniture Finisher, you'll be responsible for applying finishes to wood products, ensuring high-quality results, and maintaining a safe and organized work environment. You'll work closely with our design team to bring their visions to life, and your attention to detail will be paramount in delivering exceptional products to our customers.

Responsibilities:

 Apply various finishes to wood products, including staining, painting, and lacquering
 Ensure high-quality results, meeting our exacting standards
 Maintain a safe and organized work environment, adhering to safety protocols and regulations
 Collaborate with the design team to bring their designs to life
 Develop and implement new finishing techniques to stay ahead of industry trends

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in furniture finishing or a related field
 Strong attention to detail and ability to work independently
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced environment

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
