Company: Hess-Howard
Role: Deckhand

At Hess-Howard, we're committed to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment where everyone can thrive. As a deckhand, you'll play a crucial role in maintaining the safety and efficiency of our vessels.

Job Description:
As a deckhand, you'll be responsible for performing a variety of tasks to ensure the smooth operation of our vessels. This includes:

 Assisting with deck maintenance and repairs
 Handling mooring and unmooring operations
 Participating in safety drills and training exercises
 Collaborating with other crew members to achieve common goals

Responsibilities and Qualifications:

 2+ years of experience as a deckhand or in a related role
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced environment
 Strong communication and teamwork skills
 Ability to work varying shifts, including nights and weekends

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 40,000 to USD 60,000, based on your experience and skills. In addition to a competitive salary, you'll also receive:

 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A dynamic and supportive work environment

We believe in the power of diverse teams and welcome applicants with diverse experiences and perspectives. If you're a motivated and team-oriented individual who is passionate about working on the water, we encourage you to apply!
