Instrumentation Specialist at Robinson-Davis

Company Overview:
Robinson-Davis is a leading provider of innovative instrumentation solutions, dedicated to improving the quality of life through cutting-edge technology.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Instrumentation Specialist to join our team. As an Instrumentation Specialist, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and testing instrumentation systems to ensure high-quality results. Your expertise in quality assurance, data analysis, and negotiation skills will be essential in this role.

Responsibilities:

 Design and develop instrumentation systems to meet project requirements
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure timely and efficient project delivery
 Conduct thorough testing and quality assurance to guarantee high-quality results
 Analyze data to identify areas for improvement and implement changes as needed
 Develop and maintain effective relationships with stakeholders through excellent negotiation skills

Qualifications and Experience:
We welcome applicants with diverse experiences and perspectives. The ideal candidate will have:

 A Bachelor's degree in Instrumentation, Engineering, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in instrumentation design, development, and testing
 Proven experience in quality assurance and data analysis
 Excellent negotiation and communication skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $90,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
