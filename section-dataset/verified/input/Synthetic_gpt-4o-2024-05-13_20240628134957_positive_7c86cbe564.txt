Company Background:
Arroyo and Sons is a reputable firm known for delivering exceptional IT solutions to clients worldwide. Our mission is to drive innovation and efficiency through cutting-edge technology. 

Job Type:
Full-Time IT Project Manager

Job Description:
Arroyo and Sons is seeking an IT Project Manager to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing multiple IT projects, ensuring they are delivered on time, within scope, and within budget. The role demands excellent communication, leadership, and organizational skills.

Responsibilities:
- Lead IT project planning sessions
- Coordinate staff and internal resources
- Manage project progress and adapt work as required
- Ensure projects meet deadlines
- Manage relationships with clients and stakeholders
- Oversee all incoming and outgoing project documentation
- Conduct project review and create detailed reports for executive staff

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related field
- Proven experience as a Project Manager in the IT sector
- Excellent understanding of project management methodologies
- Strong leadership and communication skills
- Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £50,000 to £75,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance plans
- Flexible working hours
- Professional development opportunities
- Paid parental leave
