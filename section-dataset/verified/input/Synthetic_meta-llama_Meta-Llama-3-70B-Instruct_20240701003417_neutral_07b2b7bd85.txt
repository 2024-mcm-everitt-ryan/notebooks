Company: Smith-Moss
Role: Heating Plant Technician

About Us:
Smith-Moss is a leading provider of heating solutions, dedicated to delivering exceptional service and ensuring the comfort and safety of our clients. We are committed to fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Heating Plant Technician to join our team. As a Heating Plant Technician, you will be responsible for the operation, maintenance, and repair of heating systems, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency. You will work closely with our team to identify and resolve issues, and provide exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Operate, maintain, and repair heating systems, including boilers, pumps, and valves
 Conduct routine inspections and perform preventative maintenance to minimize downtime
 Collaborate with team members to identify and resolve issues, and provide exceptional customer service
 Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and reduce costs
 Meet and exceed customer expectations, ensuring timely and effective resolution of issues

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in heating plant operations and maintenance
 Strong knowledge of heating systems, including boilers, pumps, and valves
 Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
 Valid driver's license and willingness to travel to client sites as needed

We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 CAD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program. Our team enjoys a collaborative and inclusive work environment, with opportunities for professional growth and development.
