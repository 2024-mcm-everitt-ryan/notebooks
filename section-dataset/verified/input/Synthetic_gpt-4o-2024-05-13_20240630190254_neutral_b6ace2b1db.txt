Company Background:
Smith, Brown and Barber is a leading engineering firm specializing in heavy industrial systems. Our team is dedicated to excellence, innovation, and collaboration, and we thrive on the diversity of our employees.

Job Type:
Full-time Steamfitter

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Steamfitter to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for installing, assembling, fabricating, maintaining, and repairing piping systems that carry water, steam, and other materials in heating, cooling, and other processes. The role involves coordinating with multiple departments, ensuring that all installations and repairs meet the required standards.

Responsibilities:
- Install and repair piping systems that carry water, steam, and other materials.
- Read and interpret blueprints and schematics.
- Ensure compliance with industry standards and safety regulations.
- Coordinate with other departments to ensure seamless operations.
- Conduct regular maintenance checks and troubleshooting.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a Steamfitter or in a similar role.
- Ability to read and interpret blueprints and technical drawings.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
- Certification or relevant training in steamfitting or pipefitting is preferred.
