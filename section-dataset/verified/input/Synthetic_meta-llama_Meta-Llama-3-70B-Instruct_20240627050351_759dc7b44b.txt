Job Title: Lean Six Sigma Consultant - Manufacturing
Company: Thornton Group

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Lean Six Sigma Consultant to join our Manufacturing team. The successful candidate will be responsible for driving process improvements, reducing waste, and increasing efficiency within our production lines.

Responsibilities:
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify areas for improvement and implement process changes
- Develop and maintain dashboards to track key performance indicators
- Conduct root cause analysis and implement corrective actions
- Provide training and coaching to team members on Lean Six Sigma methodologies
- Develop and maintain relationships with stakeholders to ensure effective communication and collaboration

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering, Operations Management, or related field
- Minimum 5 years of experience in a manufacturing environment
- Lean Six Sigma Black Belt certification
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication and leadership skills
- Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
- Must have a strong understanding of cultural nuances and subtleties to effectively communicate with our global teams

Benefits:
- Salary range: $80,000 - $100,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
