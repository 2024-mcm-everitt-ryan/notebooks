Company: Yang-Bean

Role: Messenger

Company Background:
Yang-Bean is a leading logistics and delivery service provider, committed to ensuring timely and reliable deliveries. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and supportive work environment.

Job Description:
We are seeking a reliable and responsible Messenger to join our dynamic team. The Messenger will be responsible for the safe and timely delivery of documents and packages to various destinations.

Responsibilities:
- Pick up and deliver documents and packages within the specified area.
- Ensure delivery deadlines are met.
- Maintain accurate records of deliveries and receipts.
- Communicate effectively with dispatchers and recipients.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Valid driver's license.
- Excellent time-management skills.
- Strong communication skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

Perks and Benefits:

- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 30,000 to USD 40,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Paid parental leave.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.
- Opportunities for career growth.
