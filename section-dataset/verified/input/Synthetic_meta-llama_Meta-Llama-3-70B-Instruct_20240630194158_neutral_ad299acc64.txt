Company: Fletcher, Bentley and Harrison

Job Title: Administrative Specialist

About Us:
Fletcher, Bentley and Harrison is a dynamic organization dedicated to providing exceptional services to our clients. We value diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to create a work environment that is welcoming and inclusive to all.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Administrative Specialist to join our team. As an Administrative Specialist, you will provide administrative support to our senior management team, ensuring the smooth operation of our day-to-day activities.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate travel arrangements, meetings, and events
 Prepare and distribute reports, presentations, and other materials
 Manage and maintain accurate records and databases
 Provide exceptional customer service to internal and external clients
 Develop and implement administrative processes and procedures to improve efficiency

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in an administrative role
 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Public Administration, or a related field
 Excellent communication, organizational, and time management skills
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and Google Workspace

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
