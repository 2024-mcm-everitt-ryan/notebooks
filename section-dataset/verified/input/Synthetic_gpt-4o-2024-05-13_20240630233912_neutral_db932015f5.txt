Job Description:  
We are seeking a Tender to join our dynamic team at Brown LLC. The ideal candidate will be responsible for preparing and submitting tenders for construction projects, ensuring compliance with industry standards and company policies.

- Maintain and update tender records and documentation.  
- Ensure timely submission of tenders.

- Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Construction Management, or a related field.  
- Proven experience in preparing tenders for construction projects.  
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.  
- Excellent communication and organizational abilities.  
- Proficiency in tender software and Microsoft Office Suite.  
- Ability to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines.
