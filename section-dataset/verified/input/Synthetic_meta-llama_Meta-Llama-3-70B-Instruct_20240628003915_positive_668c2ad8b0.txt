Meadows, Jones and Preston is a leading landscaping company dedicated to creating beautiful outdoor spaces for our clients. We believe that our diverse team is our greatest asset, and we're committed to fostering an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive.

Job Description:
We're seeking a skilled and passionate Gardener to join our team. As a Gardener at Meadows, Jones and Preston, you will be responsible for maintaining and enhancing the beauty of our clients' gardens and outdoor spaces. This is a fantastic opportunity to work with a talented team of horticultural experts and contribute to the growth and success of our company.

Responsibilities:

- Plant, prune, and maintain gardens, lawns, and outdoor spaces
- Collaborate with our design team to implement new garden designs and concepts
- Provide exceptional customer service to our clients
- Participate in training and development programs to stay up-to-date with the latest horticultural practices

Qualifications and Experience:

- 2+ years of experience in gardening or a related field
- Strong knowledge of horticulture, plant care, and garden design
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Excellent communication and customer service skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings plan, and paid time off.
