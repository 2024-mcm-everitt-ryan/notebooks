Company: Peters-Nguyen

Job Title: Systems Administrator - Computer Software

About Us:
Peters-Nguyen is a fast-paced and dynamic company that values commitment to excellence. We are seeking a highly motivated Systems Administrator to join our team.

Job Description:
The successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of our computer systems and networks. This includes installing, configuring, and maintaining computer systems, diagnosing and troubleshooting hardware and software problems, and ensuring network security and data integrity.

Responsibilities:

 Install, configure, and maintain computer systems and networks
 Diagnose and troubleshoot hardware and software problems
 Ensure network security and data integrity
 Collaborate with other teams to ensure system integration and compatibility
 Develop and implement system backup and recovery procedures

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field from a reputable university
 At least 3 years of experience in a similar role
 Strong knowledge of computer systems, networks, and security protocols
 Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 60,000 to USD 80,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.

Note: Only applicants with a strong work ethic and a commitment to our company values will be considered. We require a minimum of 2 years of consecutive employment at your previous job.
