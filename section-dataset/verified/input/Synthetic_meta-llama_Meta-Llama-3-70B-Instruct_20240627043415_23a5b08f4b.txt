Job Title: Product Leader
Job Summary:
We are seeking an experienced Product Leader to join our team. As a Product Leader, you will be responsible for driving the development and launch of new products, working closely with cross-functional teams to identify market opportunities and creating product strategies that align with our company's vision.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and execute product roadmaps that meet business objectives
- Collaborate with engineering, design, and marketing teams to launch new products
- Conduct market research and competitor analysis to identify opportunities
- Define and track key product metrics to measure success
- Foster a culture of innovation and experimentation within the product team

Qualifications:
- 5+ years of experience in product leadership roles
- Proven track record of launching successful products
- Excellent communication and project management skills
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
