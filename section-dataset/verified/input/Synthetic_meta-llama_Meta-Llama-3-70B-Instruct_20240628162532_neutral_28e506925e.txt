Materials Engineer at Palmer Ltd

Company Background:
Palmer Ltd is a leading manufacturing company that values innovation, sustainability, and diversity. We strive to create a work environment where everyone feels valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their best work.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Materials Engineer to join our team. As a Materials Engineer at Palmer Ltd, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and testing materials used in our products. Your expertise will help us create high-quality, sustainable materials that meet our customers' needs.

Responsibilities:

 Design and develop new materials and manufacturing processes
 Conduct experiments and analyze data to improve material properties
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure material compatibility and feasibility
 Develop and implement quality control procedures to ensure material consistency
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Materials Science, Engineering, or a related field
 3+ years of experience in materials engineering or a related field
 Strong understanding of material properties and manufacturing processes
 Excellent problem-solving and communication skills

 A dynamic and inclusive work environment
