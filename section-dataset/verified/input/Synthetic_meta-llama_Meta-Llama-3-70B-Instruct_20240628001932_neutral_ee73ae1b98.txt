Company: Salazar LLC
Role: Family Advocate

About Us:
Salazar LLC is a non-profit organization committed to providing support services to families in need. We believe in creating a welcoming environment that values diversity, equity, and inclusion.

Job Description:
We are seeking a compassionate and experienced Family Advocate to join our team. As a Family Advocate, you will work closely with families to assess their needs, develop personalized plans, and connect them with community resources. Your empathetic approach and ability to build trust will help families navigate challenging situations and achieve their goals.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct intake assessments and develop individualized plans for families
 Provide emotional support, guidance, and advocacy to families
 Connect families with community resources, services, and programs
 Collaborate with internal teams and external partners to ensure comprehensive support
 Maintain accurate records and reports

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Social Work, Human Services, or a related field
 2+ years of experience working with families, preferably in a non-profit setting
 Excellent communication, interpersonal, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize multiple tasks
 Fluency in English; bilingualism an asset

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as generous paid time off and holidays.
