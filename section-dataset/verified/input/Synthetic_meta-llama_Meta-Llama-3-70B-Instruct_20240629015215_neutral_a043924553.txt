Job Title: Mechanical Cost Estimator

Company: Frazier-Murphy

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Mechanical Cost Estimator to join our team. As a Mechanical Cost Estimator, you will be responsible for preparing and managing cost estimates for mechanical projects, ensuring accuracy and completeness of estimates, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure project deliverables are met.

Responsibilities:

 Prepare and manage cost estimates for mechanical projects, ensuring accuracy and completeness of estimates
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure project deliverables are met
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with clients and internal stakeholders
 Meet any necessary licensure requirements specific to the role
 Balance work responsibilities effectively to meet project deadlines

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, Construction Management, or related field
 5+ years of experience in cost estimation, preferably in a mechanical construction environment
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
