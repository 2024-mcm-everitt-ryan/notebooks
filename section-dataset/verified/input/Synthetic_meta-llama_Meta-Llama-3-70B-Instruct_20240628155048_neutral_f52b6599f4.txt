Company: Rojas and Sons

Job Title: Port Marine Engineer

About Us:
Rojas and Sons is a leading maritime logistics company dedicated to providing efficient and sustainable port operations. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion in the workplace.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Port Marine Engineer to join our team. As a Port Marine Engineer, you will be responsible for ensuring the safe and efficient operation of our port facilities, including the maintenance and repair of equipment and vessels.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct regular inspections of port facilities and equipment to identify and address any safety or operational issues
 Develop and implement maintenance and repair plans to minimize downtime and optimize performance
 Collaborate with other teams to ensure seamless port operations
 Provide technical guidance and support to junior engineers and technicians

Requirements:

 Bachelor's degree in Marine Engineering or a related field
 At least 5 years of experience in a similar role
 Strong knowledge of marine engineering principles and practices
 Excellent problem-solving and communication skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $120,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.

Our Commitment to Inclusion:
Our hiring process is designed to be accessible to everyone. We strive for a diverse and inclusive workforce, and our team is inclusive and supportive. We are an equal opportunity employer, and we support a diverse range of abilities.
