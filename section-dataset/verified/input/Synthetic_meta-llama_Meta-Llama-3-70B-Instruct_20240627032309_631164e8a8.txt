Company: Taylor Group

Job Title: QA Test Lead

About Us:
Taylor Group is a leading technology firm that values diversity and inclusivity in the workplace. We believe that a diverse team is essential to driving innovation and excellence.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced QA Test Lead to join our team. As a QA Test Lead, you will be responsible for leading the QA team in testing and ensuring the quality of our software products. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize testing needs, develop and execute testing plans, and ensure timely delivery of high-quality products.

Responsibilities:

- Develop and execute comprehensive testing plans to ensure the quality of our software products
- Lead and mentor a team of QA engineers to achieve testing goals
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize testing needs
- Analyze and report testing results to stakeholders
- Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies

Qualifications:

- 5+ years of experience in QA testing, with at least 2 years in a lead role
- Strong knowledge of software testing methodologies and tools
- Excellent communication and leadership skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
- Competent in Mandarin (an asset)
