Company: Reese-Mcdonald  
Role: Senior Urban Designer  

Company Background:  
Reese-Mcdonald is a pioneering firm in urban design and planning, known for our innovative approaches to creating sustainable and vibrant urban spaces. We are committed to fostering an inclusive and diverse workplace that values the unique contributions of every team member.

Job Description:  
We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Urban Designer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in urban design, with a focus on creating integrated, sustainable urban environments. This role requires a professional who can collaborate with multi-disciplinary teams and engage with a diverse range of stakeholders.

Responsibilities:  
- Lead the development of urban design projects from concept to completion.  
- Collaborate with architects, planners, and engineers to create comprehensive urban designs.  
- Conduct site analysis, develop design strategies, and prepare detailed plans and reports.  
- Engage with community members and stakeholders to ensure inclusive and participatory design processes.  
- Present design proposals to clients, government agencies, and public forums.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Urban Design, Architecture, or a related field.  
- Minimum of 8 years of experience in urban design or a related discipline.  
- Proven track record of delivering high-quality urban design projects.  
- Strong analytical, visual, and communication skills.  
- Ability to work efficiently within the job requirements and meet project deadlines.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive salary range from $80,000 to $120,000 annually, based on experience and skills.  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Opportunities for professional development and continuing education.  
- Flexible work schedules and remote work options.  
- Inclusive workplace culture that values diversity and dedication.
