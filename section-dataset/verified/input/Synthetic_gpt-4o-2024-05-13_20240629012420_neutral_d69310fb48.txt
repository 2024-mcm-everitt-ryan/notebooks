Company Background:
Price-Rice, a leading manufacturer in the food processing industry, is committed to creating high-quality products while maintaining an inclusive and diverse workplace. We value integrity, innovation, and collaboration.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
Price-Rice is seeking a detail-oriented and reliable Kettle Operator to join our production team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for operating kettles and ensuring the efficient and safe production of our food products. This position plays a crucial role in maintaining our high standards of quality and consistency.

Responsibilities:
- Operate kettles and other related equipment in accordance with safety guidelines.
- Monitor and adjust controls to maintain specified temperature and pressure.
- Perform routine maintenance and cleaning of equipment.
- Adhere to all company policies and procedures.
- Collaborate with team members to meet production goals.
- Maintain accurate records of production data.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- A diploma from any accredited academic institution is preferred.
- Previous experience in a manufacturing or food processing environment is advantageous.
- Ability to follow detailed instructions and safety protocols.
- Strong attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
- Consistently strive for high standards.
- Effective communication skills and ability to work in a team.

- Competitive salary ranging from $30,000 to $45,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and growth.
- Employee assistance programs.
- Benefits are based on tenure and role.

We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and strive to ensure a supportive and inclusive environment for all employees.
