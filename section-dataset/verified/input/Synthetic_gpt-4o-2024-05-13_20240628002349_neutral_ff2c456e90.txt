Company: Lee-Bates

Role: Human Resources Coordinator

Company Background:
Lee-Bates is a globally recognized firm specializing in innovative business solutions and consultancy services. We pride ourselves on fostering a culture of excellence, integrity, and diversity.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description and Responsibilities:
As a Human Resources Coordinator, you will play a crucial role in supporting the HR department in various functions, including recruitment, employee relations, benefits administration, and compliance with labor laws. Key responsibilities include:
- Assisting in the recruitment process by posting job ads, screening candidates, and scheduling interviews.
- Supporting employee onboarding and training programs.
- Managing employee records and ensuring compliance with company policies and legal requirements.
- Coordinating employee benefits programs and addressing employee inquiries.
- Assisting in the development and implementation of HR policies and procedures.
- Supporting diversity and inclusion initiatives within the organization.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in human resources or a related role.
- Strong organizational and communication skills.
- Proficiency in HR software and Microsoft Office Suite.
- Ability to handle sensitive and confidential information with discretion.
- Commitment to fostering an inclusive and diverse workplace.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from 50,000 to 70,000 USD annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off, including vacation, sick leave, and holidays.
- Paid parental leave for all employees.
- Professional development opportunities and tuition reimbursement.
- Employee wellness programs and work-life balance initiatives.

Diversity and Inclusion:
Our workplace culture respects and values the diversity of the LGBTQ2+ community. We are committed to inclusivity, embracing all sexual orientations, and ensuring that our team benefits from a wide range of perspectives. We provide parental leave to all employees and foster a supportive environment for all.
