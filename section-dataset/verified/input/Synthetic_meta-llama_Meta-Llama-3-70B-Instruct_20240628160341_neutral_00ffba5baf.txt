Company: Mendoza, Powell and Mcbride

Role: HVAC Mechanic

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled HVAC Mechanic to join our team at Mendoza, Powell and Mcbride. As an HVAC Mechanic, you will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems in commercial and residential settings. If you have a strong work ethic, excellent problem-solving skills, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Install, maintain, and repair HVAC systems in commercial and residential settings
 Conduct routine maintenance and repairs to ensure optimal system performance
 Troubleshoot and diagnose issues with HVAC systems
 Collaborate with coworkers to complete tasks and projects efficiently
 Communicate effectively with customers to understand their needs and provide solutions

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as an HVAC Mechanic
 Valid certification or license to practice as an HVAC Mechanic in your state/province
 Strong understanding of HVAC systems and principles
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and climb ladders as necessary
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
