Company: Tucker Ltd

Job Title: Architectural Designer

About Us:
Tucker Ltd is a forward-thinking architecture firm dedicated to creating innovative and sustainable designs that enhance the built environment. We value collaboration, creativity, and community engagement in our pursuit of excellence.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Architectural Designer to join our team. As an Architectural Designer at Tucker Ltd, you will be responsible for designing and developing architectural projects from concept to completion. You will work closely with our team of architects, engineers, and project managers to deliver high-quality designs that meet client needs and exceed expectations.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and refine architectural designs, including floor plans, elevations, and cross-sections
 Collaborate with the project team to ensure design integrity and consistency
 Conduct site visits and inspections to ensure compliance with design intent
 Communicate effectively with clients, contractors, and stakeholders to ensure successful project outcomes
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends, best practices, and regulatory requirements

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Architecture or related field
 2+ years of experience in architectural design
 Proficiency in Autodesk Revit, Adobe Creative Suite, and SketchUp
 Strong attention to detail, organizational skills, and ability to work independently
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including:
 A competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on experience and skills
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 A dynamic and supportive work environment
 Flexible work arrangements, including remote work options
 Reserved parking available for employees
 Access to the latest design software and technologies
