Job Title: Program Contract Analyst
Company: Li PLC

Job Summary:
We are seeking a highly skilled Program Contract Analyst to join our team. As a Program Contract Analyst, you will be responsible for managing and analyzing contracts, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements, and providing strategic support to our program management team.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze and interpret contract terms and conditions to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
 Develop and maintain contract management databases and reporting tools
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure effective contract implementation
 Provide strategic support to program management teams to drive business growth
 Develop and maintain relationships with internal and external stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Logistics, or related field
 3+ years of experience in contract analysis or a related field
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, particularly Excel
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
