Company: Herrera, Cook and Rose  
Role: Rail-Track Laying and Maintenance Equipment Operator

Herrera, Cook and Rose is a leading provider of railway infrastructure services, dedicated to maintaining and improving rail networks across the globe. With a commitment to safety and excellence, we seek skilled professionals to join our dynamic team.

- Collaborate with team members to complete rail infrastructure projects on time.

- Perform periodic maintenance and troubleshoot equipment issues.

Qualifications and Experience:  
- Proven experience in rail-track laying and maintenance.
- Proficiency in operating and maintaining relevant equipment.
- Strong understanding of safety standards and practices.
- Ability to work both independently and as part of a team.
- Reliable and capable candidates encouraged to apply.
