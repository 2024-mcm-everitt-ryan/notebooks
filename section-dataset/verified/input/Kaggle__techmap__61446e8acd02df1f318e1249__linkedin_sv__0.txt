Position: Medical Appointment Scheduler  
Reports to: Operations Manager  

Description/Purpose of position  
This position is responsible for performing various functions, including managing the flow of patient care by accurately scheduling patient appointments in the scheduling system. Have the ability to prioritize and perform work in an organized, efficient, and effective manner without constant supervision and direction. Able to adjust appropriately to the volume of work and complete tasks in a timely manner without interfering with the quality of work.  

Responsibilities  
- Coordinate appointments and reschedule as needed through EMR system  
- Take or respond to telephone calls and emails promptly to establish or confirm appointments  
- Resolve scheduling conflicts  
- Provide support to other team members as needed  
- Maintain HIPAA compliance  
- Demonstrate proficiency within assigned areas of responsibility  

Duties may include  
- Guiding patients through the scheduling process  
- Collaborating with Nurse Practitioners if a patient needs to schedule outside their normal availability  
- Accurately and thoroughly schedule within the booking radius  
- Confirm scheduled appointments by sending confirmation to patient and nurse practitioner  

Minimum Qualifications  
Education: Undergraduate Degree  
Experience: 2 years medical appointment scheduling preferred  
Characteristics/Skills:  
- Proficient word processing  
- English proficiency level C1  
- Detail oriented  
- Excellent communication skills  
- Interpersonal skills  
- Decision making skills  

Other
