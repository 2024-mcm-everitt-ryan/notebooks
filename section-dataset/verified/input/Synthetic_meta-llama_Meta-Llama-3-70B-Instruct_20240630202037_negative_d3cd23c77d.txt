Company: Davis, Walker and Perez

Role: Sr. Internal Auditor

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and empathetic Sr. Internal Auditor to join our team. As a key member of our internal audit department, she will be responsible for planning and executing audits, identifying areas for improvement, and developing recommendations to enhance our internal controls.

Responsibilities:

 Plan and execute audits to assess the effectiveness of internal controls and risk management processes
 Identify and document audit findings, and develop recommendations for improvement
 Collaborate with management to implement audit recommendations and monitor progress
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging risks to ensure our internal audit processes remain current and effective

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Accounting or related field
 5+ years of experience in internal auditing, with a focus on risk management and control processes
 Certification in internal auditing (CIA) or certified public accountant (CPA) designation
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to work effectively with colleagues and management

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 85,000 to USD 110,000, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program and generous paid time off.
