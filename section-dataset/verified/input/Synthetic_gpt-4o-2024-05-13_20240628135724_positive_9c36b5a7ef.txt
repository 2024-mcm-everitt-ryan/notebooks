Job Type:
Summer Communications Assistant

Job Description:
Murphy LLC is seeking a Summer Communications Assistant to support our communications team. This role is pivotal for our summer campaigns and offers a great opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a dynamic environment. 

Responsibilities:
- Assist in the development and execution of communication strategies.
- Support social media activities, including content creation and scheduling.
- Coordinate with team members to gather information and produce newsletters.
- Conduct market research and analyze current communication trends.
- Assist with administrative tasks as needed.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a communications-related program.
- Strong written and verbal communication skills.
- Familiarity with social media platforms and content creation tools.
- Highly organized with excellent attention to detail.
- Willingness to learn and contribute proactively.
