Role: Plasterer

At Jones, Medina and Smith, we're committed to building a workforce that celebrates and supports queer identities. As a plasterer, you'll play a crucial role in our team, working on various projects to deliver high-quality results. Our inclusive policies are designed to support and uplift LGBTQ+ employees, and we are keenly seeking candidates who can bring LGBTQ+ perspectives to our team. Job Description:
The successful candidate will be responsible for applying plaster to walls, ceilings, and other surfaces to achieve a smooth finish. You'll work closely with our construction team to ensure projects are completed on time and to a high standard.

Responsibilities:

 Apply plaster to walls, ceilings, and other surfaces
 Smooth out surfaces to achieve a high-quality finish
 Work collaboratively with the construction team to meet project deadlines
 Maintain a safe and clean working environment

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a plasterer
 Ability to work at heights and in confined spaces
 Strong communication and teamwork skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you'll receive comprehensive health insurance, paid time off, and opportunities for professional development.
