Company Background:
Blackburn-Cummings is a leading provider of HVAC solutions, dedicated to ensuring comfort and energy efficiency in homes and commercial spaces. With a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we pride ourselves on our innovative approach and highly skilled team.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a detail-oriented and organised Thermostat Repairer to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing various types of thermostats, ensuring they operate efficiently and effectively. This role requires a strong focus on quality and a commitment to excellent customer service.

Responsibilities:
- Diagnose and repair thermostat issues in residential and commercial settings.
- Perform routine maintenance and inspections.
- Install new thermostats and upgrade existing systems.
- Provide accurate and timely reports on repairs and maintenance.
- Ensure compliance with safety and industry standards.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Previous experience in HVAC or a related field is preferred.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and customer service skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Valid driver's license and clean driving record.
- Certification in HVAC or related technical training is advantageous.
