Company: Castillo and Sons

Role: Wellness Advocate

Company Background:
Castillo and Sons is a leading organization dedicated to promoting health and wellness throughout the community. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and supportive environment for all employees. 

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are looking for an enthusiastic Wellness Advocate to join our team. The ideal candidate will be passionate about health and wellness and eager to help others achieve their well-being goals. 

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement wellness programs for employees
- Provide one-on-one wellness coaching sessions
- Create educational materials to promote healthy living
- Collaborate with other departments to support company-wide health initiatives
- Monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of wellness programs

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in health education, nutrition, or a related field
- Prior experience in wellness coaching or related roles
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Certification in wellness coaching (preferred)

Perks and Benefits:
- Parental time off is provided for all new parents
- Comprehensive health insurance plans
- Opportunities for professional development and growth
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000, based on your experience and skills
- A supportive and inclusive workplace that values diverse perspectives

How to Apply:
If you are passionate about wellness and believe in equal opportunities for employees of all backgrounds, we encourage you to apply. Please send your resume and cover letter to careers@castilloandsons.com.
