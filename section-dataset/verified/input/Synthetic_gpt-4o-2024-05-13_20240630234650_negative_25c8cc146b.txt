Bridge Operator

Company Background
White, Thomas and Hall is a premier infrastructure management company known for maintaining some of the largest and most complex bridges in the region. We are committed to ensuring the safety and efficiency of our transport networks.

Job Type
Full-time

Job Description
We are looking for a dedicated and self-reliant individual to join our team as a Bridge Operator. This role requires someone who can work independently while maintaining high standards of safety and efficiency. The ideal candidate will be a detail-oriented problem solver with the ability to thrive in a fast-paced and exciting environment.

Responsibilities

  Operate and monitor bridge equipment to ensure safe and efficient operation.
  Inspect bridge structures and machinery for maintenance needs.
  Respond promptly to emergency situations and operational issues.
  Maintain detailed logs of bridge operations and incidents.
  Work with a team to address and resolve operational challenges.


Qualifications and Experience

  High school diploma or equivalent required; technical degree preferred.
  Minimum of 2 years of experience in bridge operations or a related field.
  Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  Ability to work independently and handle emergency situations effectively.
  Excellent communication skills.


Perks and Benefits

  Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
  Retirement savings plan with company match.
  Paid time off and holidays.
  Ongoing training and professional development opportunities.
  We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD $45,000 to USD $60,000, based on your experience and skills.
