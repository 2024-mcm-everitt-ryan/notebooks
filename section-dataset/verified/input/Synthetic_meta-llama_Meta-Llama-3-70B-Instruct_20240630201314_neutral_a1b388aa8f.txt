Job Title: Housekeeping Professional
Company: Larson Inc

Job Description:
We are seeking a reliable and detail-oriented Housekeeping Professional to join our team. As a Housekeeping Professional at Larson Inc, you will be responsible for ensuring the cleanliness and organization of our clients' homes. This is a great opportunity to work with a diverse range of clients and develop your skills in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Clean and organize homes according to clients' specifications
 Work closely with clients to understand their needs and preferences
 Develop and maintain a high level of quality in your work
 Communicate effectively with clients and team members
 Stay up-to-date with industry best practices and standards

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in housekeeping or a related field
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Physical ability to lift 25 pounds and stand for long periods

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A diverse and inclusive work environment

We welcome candidates from all backgrounds to join our team. Our team is stronger because of its diverse members, and we strive to create an environment where everyone feels valued and respected.
