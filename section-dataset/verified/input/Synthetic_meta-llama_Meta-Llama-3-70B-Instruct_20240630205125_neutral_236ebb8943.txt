Company: Turner-Flowers

Job Title: Translator

About Us:
Turner-Flowers is a leading language services provider dedicated to breaking language barriers and facilitating global communication. We believe in the power of diverse teams and are committed to creating an inclusive and equitable workplace.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Translator to join our team. As a Translator, you will be responsible for translating documents, websites, and other materials from one language to another, ensuring accuracy, fluency, and cultural relevance. You will work closely with our project managers and editors to deliver high-quality translations that meet our clients' needs.

Responsibilities:

 Translate documents, websites, and other materials from one language to another
 Ensure accuracy, fluency, and cultural relevance in all translations
 Collaborate with project managers and editors to deliver high-quality translations
 Meet deadlines and manage multiple projects simultaneously

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Translation, Linguistics, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in translation
 Strong attention to detail and excellent language skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Proficiency in computer-assisted translation tools and software

Perks and Benefits:
 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €45,000 to €60,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance and retirement plan
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
 Flexible working hours and remote work options
