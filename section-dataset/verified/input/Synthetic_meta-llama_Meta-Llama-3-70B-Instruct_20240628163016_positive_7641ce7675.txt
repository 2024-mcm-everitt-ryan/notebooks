Company: Carroll, Mckenzie and Patterson

Job Title: Administrative Staff Analyst

Job Type: Full-time

About Us:
Carroll, Mckenzie and Patterson is a leading consulting firm dedicated to delivering exceptional results for our clients. We value an inclusive team and invite individuals with disabilities to apply, as we believe that diverse perspectives are essential to driving innovation and growth.

Job Description:
We are seeking an Administrative Staff Analyst to join our dynamic team. As an Administrative Staff Analyst, you will provide administrative support to our senior management team, ensuring seamless day-to-day operations. Your responsibilities will include managing calendars, coordinating travel arrangements, preparing presentations, and performing data analysis.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to senior management
 Manage calendars, coordinate travel arrangements, and prepare presentations
 Perform data analysis and create reports
 Develop and maintain filing systems, both physical and digital
 Collaborate with other teams to ensure efficient operations

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration or a related field
 2+ years of experience in an administrative role
 Excellent organizational and communication skills
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 50,000 to USD 65,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
