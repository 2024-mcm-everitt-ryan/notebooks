Company: Burton-Steele

Job Title: Iron Worker

Job Description:

We are seeking a skilled Iron Worker to join our team at Burton-Steele. As an Iron Worker, you will be responsible for installing, repairing, and maintaining structural iron and steel to support buildings, bridges, and other infrastructure. If you have experience working with metal and are comfortable with heights, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Install and repair structural iron and steel to support buildings, bridges, and other infrastructure
 Read and interpret blueprints and diagrams to determine the layout of ironwork
 Operate hand tools, power tools, and heavy machinery to cut, shape, and assemble iron and steel
 Collaborate with other construction workers to ensure projects are completed on time and to a high standard

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as an Iron Worker or in a related trade
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and work at heights
 Strong attention to detail and ability to follow safety protocols
 High school diploma or equivalent required; trade certification an asset

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $85,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Opportunities for professional development and advancement
 A supportive and inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities

At Burton-Steele, we are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace. Applications from candidates with disabilities are welcomed to support our diversity goals. We are dedicated to providing reasonable accommodations to support candidates with disabilities throughout the hiring process.
