Full-time Clerk

Job Description:
Simmons Inc. is seeking a dedicated and adaptable Clerk to join our vibrant team. The ideal candidate will have excellent organizational skills, an ability to work in dynamic environments, and a penchant for maintaining meticulous records.

- Maintain detailed and accurate records of inventory and transactions.
- Operate heavy machinery occasionally to assist with stock management and logistics.
- Adapt quickly to new and changing situations to ensure smooth operations.
- Participate in physical fitness activities with the team to foster camaraderie.
- Work in noisy environments without compromising efficiency.
- Exhibit high levels of physical stamina and endurance to meet daily tasks.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience in a similar role within the retail sector.
- Strong organizational and multitasking abilities.
- Ability to operate heavy machinery and perform physical tasks.
- Flexibility to adapt to new and changing situations swiftly.
- Physically fit to participate in team activities and handle noisy environments.
- High school diploma or equivalent.
