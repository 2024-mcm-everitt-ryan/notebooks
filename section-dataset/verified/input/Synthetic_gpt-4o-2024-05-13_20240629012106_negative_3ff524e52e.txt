Company: Burns-Williams

Job Description:

- Optimize data storage and retrieval processes.
- Ensure data security and compliance with company policies.

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- At least 5 years of experience in data warehouse architecture and ETL processes.
- Strong knowledge of SQL, data modeling, and database design principles.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Applicants must have a clean criminal record.
- Must not be a drama queen; we value professionalism and a calm demeanor.
- Experience with cloud data warehousing solutions is a plus.
