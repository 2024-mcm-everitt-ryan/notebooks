Company: Lopez PLC

Job Title: Timekeeping Specialist

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Timekeeping Specialist to join our team. As a Timekeeping Specialist, you will be responsible for accurately maintaining and managing employee work hours, leave, and attendance records. You will also ensure compliance with company policies and labor laws.

Responsibilities:

 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records of employee work hours, leave, and attendance
 Ensure compliance with company policies and labor laws
 Process payroll and benefits in a timely and accurate manner
 Provide support to the HR team as needed
 Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and accuracy

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in timekeeping, payroll, or a related field
 Proficiency in timekeeping software and systems
 Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Excellent communication and customer service skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
