Company: Rodriguez LLC

Job Title: Insurance Claims and Policy Processing Clerk

About Us:
Rodriguez LLC is a leading insurance company dedicated to providing exceptional service to our customers. We're committed to building a diverse team of talented individuals who share our passion for excellence.

Job Description:
We're seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Insurance Claims and Policy Processing Clerk to join our team. As an Insurance Claims and Policy Processing Clerk, you will be responsible for processing insurance claims and policies, maintaining accurate records, and providing exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Process insurance claims and policies in a timely and efficient manner
 Maintain accurate records and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
 Provide exceptional customer service via phone, email, and in-person
 Collaborate with internal teams to resolve issues and improve processes
 Identify areas for improvement and implement process changes as needed

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in insurance claims and policy processing
 High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree preferred
 Strong organizational and time management skills
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with attention to detail

We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
