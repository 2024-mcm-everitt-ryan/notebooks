Company: Rogers-May

Job Title: Marine Oiler, Mate

About Us:
Rogers-May is a leading marine services company committed to delivering exceptional quality and safety standards. We operate a diverse fleet of vessels, providing a range of services to the offshore industry.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and experienced Marine Oiler, Mate to join our team. As a Marine Oiler, Mate, you will be responsible for the safe and efficient operation of our vessels, ensuring compliance with all relevant regulations and company policies.

Responsibilities:

 Assist the chief engineer in the operation and maintenance of the vessel's engines and equipment
 Monitor and control the vessel's fuel levels, ensuring optimal performance and safety
 Perform routine maintenance tasks, including cleaning and lubricating equipment
 Collaborate with the bridge team to ensure safe and efficient vessel operation
 Participate in safety drills and emergency response training

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Marine Oiler, Mate or equivalent
 Valid certification as a Marine Oiler, Mate
 Strong knowledge of vessel operations, safety procedures, and regulations
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 60,000 to USD 80,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including:
 Parental time off for all new parents
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A diverse and inclusive work environment
 A collaborative and supportive team culture
