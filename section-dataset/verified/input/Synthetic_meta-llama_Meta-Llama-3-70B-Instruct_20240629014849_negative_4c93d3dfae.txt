Food Service Manager at West-Kennedy

About Us

West-Kennedy is a fast-paced and dynamic food service company that prides itself on its high-energy atmosphere. We're looking for a Food Service Manager who can keep up with our rapid growth and ensure our customers receive exceptional service.

Job Description

As a Food Service Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing daily operations, managing a team of servers and cooks, and ensuring our high standards of quality and customer satisfaction are met. If you're a self-starter who can think on your feet and isn't afraid to get your hands dirty, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities

 Manage and train a team of servers and cooks to provide exceptional customer service
 Oversee daily operations, including inventory management and supply chain logistics
 Ensure compliance with health and safety regulations
 Develop and implement strategies to increase sales and revenue

Qualifications and Experience

 3+ years of experience in food service management
 Proven track record of success in a fast-paced environment
 Ability to work well under pressure and make quick decisions
 Bachelor's degree from a reputable institution

Perks and Benefits

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits package
