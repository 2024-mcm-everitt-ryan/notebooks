Company Background:
Hutchinson LLC is a leading provider of industrial inspection services, specializing in boiler inspection and maintenance. We are committed to delivering top-notch services to our clients while fostering an inclusive and diverse workforce.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Boiler Inspector to join our team. As a Boiler Inspector at Hutchinson LLC, you will be responsible for ensuring the safety and efficiency of boiler systems through thorough inspections and assessments.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct comprehensive inspections of boiler systems to identify potential issues and ensure compliance with safety standards.
- Prepare detailed reports of inspection findings and recommend corrective actions.
- Collaborate with clients to address their concerns and provide expert advice on boiler maintenance.
- Stay updated with industry regulations and advancements in boiler technology.
- Maintain accurate records of inspections and maintenance activities.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent; technical certification in boiler inspection or a related field is preferred.
- Proven experience in boiler inspection or a similar role.
- Strong understanding of boiler systems and safety regulations.
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Ability to communicate effectively with clients and team members.
- Willingness to travel to various client sites as needed.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £35,000 to £50,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Ongoing training and professional development opportunities.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.
