Account Manager at Castro Ltd

Company Background: 
Castro Ltd is a dynamic and fast-paced organization that specializes in providing innovative solutions to businesses. We are committed to delivering exceptional results and exceeding our clients' expectations.

Job Description: 
We are seeking a highly motivated and results-driven Account Manager to join our team. As an Account Manager, you will be responsible for managing client relationships, identifying new business opportunities, and driving revenue growth.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and execute strategic account plans to achieve sales targets
 Build and maintain strong relationships with clients to drive business growth
 Identify and pursue new business opportunities to increase revenue
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver exceptional client service
 Analyze sales data and market trends to inform business decisions
 Perform tasks quickly and efficiently to meet tight deadlines

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Marketing, or related field
 At least 3 years of account management experience
 Proven track record of achieving sales targets and driving revenue growth
 Excellent communication, negotiation, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds regularly and perform tasks that require fine motor skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 per year, based on experience and skills. Our benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
