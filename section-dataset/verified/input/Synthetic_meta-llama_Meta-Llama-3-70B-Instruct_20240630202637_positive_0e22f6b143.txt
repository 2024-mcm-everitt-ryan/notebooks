Job Title: Small Engine Mechanic
Company: Cruz-Chavez

About Us:
Cruz-Chavez is a leading provider of small engine repair services, dedicated to delivering exceptional customer experiences. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment, where everyone feels valued and empowered to succeed.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and dedicated Small Engine Mechanic to join our team. As a Small Engine Mechanic, you will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing small engines, providing excellent customer service, and contributing to the growth and development of our business.

Responsibilities:

 Diagnose and repair small engines, including lawn mowers, generators, and chain saws
 Provide exceptional customer service, ensuring timely and effective solutions
 Collaborate with the team to achieve business objectives and improve processes
 Stay up-to-date with industry developments and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience as a Small Engine Mechanic
 Strong technical knowledge of small engines and repair techniques
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, paid time off, and opportunities for professional growth and development.

Why You'll Love Working with Us:
At Cruz-Chavez, we are dedicated to promoting women in the industry and providing opportunities for career advancement. We believe that a diverse and inclusive team is essential to driving innovation and delivering exceptional customer experiences.
