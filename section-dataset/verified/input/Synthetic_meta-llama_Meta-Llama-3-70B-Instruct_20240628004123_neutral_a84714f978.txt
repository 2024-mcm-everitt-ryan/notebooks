Company Background:
Moyer LLC is a leading organization in the industry, committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace. We value our employees and strive to provide a supportive environment that fosters growth and development.

Job Type:
Human Resources Generalist

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented Human Resources Generalist to join our team. As an HR Generalist, you will play a critical role in supporting our employees and management team with various HR-related tasks, including recruitment, benefits administration, and employee relations.

Responsibilities:

 Assist with recruitment efforts, including scheduling interviews and processing new hire paperwork
 Administer employee benefits, including health, dental, and vision insurance, as well as 401(k) plan
 Provide guidance and support to employees and management on HR-related matters
 Develop and implement HR initiatives that promote diversity, equity, and inclusion
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date HR records and reports

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in an HR role, preferably in a generalist capacity
 Strong knowledge of employment laws and regulations
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. In addition to a competitive salary, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance, 401(k) plan, and paid time off. Our company also provides parental leave to all employees, and our facilities include nursing rooms as part of our commitment to a supportive workplace.
