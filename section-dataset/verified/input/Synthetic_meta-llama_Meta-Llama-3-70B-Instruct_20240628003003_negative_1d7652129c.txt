Company: Bates-Hood

Role: Service Asset Configuration Manager

Job Type: Full-time

About Us:
Bates-Hood is a leading provider of IT services, dedicated to delivering innovative solutions to our clients. We're a dynamic team of professionals passionate about staying at the cutting edge of technology.

Job Description:
We're seeking a talented Service Asset Configuration Manager to join our team. As a Service Asset Configuration Manager, you will be responsible for managing and maintaining our service asset database, ensuring accurate and up-to-date records of all service assets. You will work closely with our IT teams to ensure seamless integration and alignment of our service assets with business objectives.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and maintain the service asset database, ensuring data accuracy and integrity
 Develop and implement processes for tracking and reporting service assets
 Collaborate with IT teams to ensure alignment of service assets with business objectives
 Provide training and support to stakeholders on service asset management best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
 2+ years of experience in service asset management or a related field
 Strong understanding of IT service management principles and practices
 Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
 Recent qualifications valued; we're looking for someone who is up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technologies.

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $110,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. You'll also receive a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
