Company Background:
Foster, Gonzales and Villarreal is a longstanding and reputable provider of electrical services with a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. We pride ourselves on creating a supportive and inclusive working environment for all employees.

Job Type:
Full-time Electrician

Job Description and Responsibilities:
We are currently seeking a skilled and dedicated Electrician to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing electrical systems in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. Key responsibilities include:
- Performing routine maintenance and inspections.
- Troubleshooting electrical issues and implementing solutions.
- Installing electrical systems and components according to code.
- Collaborating with team members to complete projects efficiently.
- Ensuring all safety protocols are followed.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as an Electrician or in a similar role.
- Vocational training or certification in electrical work.
- Ability to read and interpret blueprints and technical diagrams.
- Strong problem-solving and communication skills.
- Prior employment in hands-on roles is valued.
- Ability to balance work responsibilities effectively.
- Tattoos are permitted in line with company policy.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from €35,000 to €50,000 based on experience and skills.
- Health insurance and dental plan.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Supportive work culture that values open dialogue and respect.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are invited to submit their resume and cover letter through our career portal. We look forward to welcoming a new member to our team who shares our commitment to providing excellent electrical services.
