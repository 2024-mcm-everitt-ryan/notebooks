Company: Mitchell, Harper and Perry

Job Title: Oil Burner Specialist

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Oil Burner Specialist to join our team. As an Oil Burner Specialist, you will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing oil-burning equipment and systems. The ideal candidate will have strong problem-solving skills, be comfortable working with mechanical systems, and be able to lift up to 50 pounds unassisted.

Responsibilities:

 Install, maintain, and repair oil-burning equipment and systems
 Conduct routine maintenance checks to ensure equipment is functioning properly
 Troubleshoot and repair issues with oil-burning equipment and systems
 Collaborate with other team members to ensure efficient and safe operations
 Provide excellent customer service to our clients

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as an Oil Burner Specialist or related field
 Strong understanding of mechanical systems and oil-burning equipment
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
 High school diploma or equivalent required; technical degree or certification preferred

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $75,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
