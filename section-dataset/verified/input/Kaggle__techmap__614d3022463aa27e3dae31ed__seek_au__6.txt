About Us

At APS Industrial, we’re the gateway that connects the world’s leading industrial manufacturers to the local industry. We’re committed to offering our customers an experience that is consistent and reliable as we together create solutions that power Australia. We were formed in 2018 and are in an exciting growth phase focused on serving our customers, our employees, and our global manufacturing partners. As the master distributor of the Siemens range in Australia, we also have key national distribution partnerships with a number of other leading global manufacturers led by Weidmuller, Rittal, and EPCOS (a TDK Group company). Bringing together this extensive technology portfolio, APS Industrial is able to serve the local market as a connected technology partner intent on delivering quality products and the ultimate in customer service.

About the Role

We have been experiencing steady growth, which is why we are looking to expand our warehouse team at our Warehouse Distribution Centre in Rowville. Duties would include, but are not limited to:

- Picking, packing, and dispatching customer orders.
- Receiving and inspecting all incoming goods.
- Basic data entry.
- Loading and unloading of trucks and customer vehicles.
- Accurate distribution of inventory.
- Replenishing racks with inventory.
- Safe and efficient operation of forklift.
- Forklift and reach truck work.
- Maintaining a clean and safe workplace.

To be successful in this role you should:

- Have an energetic attitude and desire for achieving results.
- Ability to work in a team.
- Be physically fit as the position requires manual handling.
- Hold a current Forklift license (Reach Truck Experience) – Desirable.
- Be self-motivated, punctual, and reliable with your own transport.

You would be working closely with the team and Warehouse Manager with training provided. We offer long-term stable employment. If you can see yourself joining our team and think that you would be a good fit, then apply now by attaching your resume and cover letter.
