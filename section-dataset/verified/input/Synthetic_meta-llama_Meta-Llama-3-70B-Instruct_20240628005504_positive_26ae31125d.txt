Company: Morris PLC

Job Title: Server Support Engineer

About Us:
Morris PLC is a leading technology firm committed to creating an inclusive and diverse work environment. We believe that our differences make us stronger, and we're dedicated to fostering a culture of belonging.

Job Description:
We're seeking a highly skilled Server Support Engineer to join our team. As a Server Support Engineer, you will be responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of our server infrastructure, troubleshooting issues, and collaborating with our development team to implement new features.

Responsibilities:

 Monitor and maintain server performance, identifying and resolving issues promptly
 Collaborate with development teams to deploy new features and applications
 Develop and maintain technical documentation
 Participate in on-call rotation for server support

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in server support or a related field
 Strong knowledge of Linux/Unix systems and cloud-based infrastructure
 Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 Experience with scripting languages such as Python or PowerShell
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. In addition, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement planning, and paid time off.

Our Commitment to Diversity:
We highly value the rich experiences of LGBTQ+ individuals and eagerly welcome their applications. Our commitment to diversity prioritises increasing queer representation in our workforce. We aim to build a diverse team and are specifically seeking professionals from the LGBTQ+ community.
