Company: Wilson-Jordan
Role: Senior Police Administrative Aide

Job Type: Full-time

About Us:
Wilson-Jordan is a leading law enforcement agency dedicated to protecting and serving our communities. We are proud of our heritage and achievements in maintaining public safety and order.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior Police Administrative Aide to join our team. As a key member of our administrative staff, you will provide critical support to our police officers and senior management. Your exceptional organizational skills, attention to detail, and ability to work under pressure will be essential in ensuring the smooth operation of our police department.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate and manage administrative tasks, including data entry, report preparation, and filing
 Provide exceptional customer service to the public, police officers, and other stakeholders
 Develop and maintain complex spreadsheets, databases, and reports
 Assist in budget preparation, management, and tracking
 Collaborate with police officers and senior management to develop and implement policies and procedures

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in an administrative role, preferably in a law enforcement agency
 Strong proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel
 Excellent communication, organizational, and time management skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 He should be adept at problem-solving and decision-making in high-pressure situations

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a generous pension plan.
