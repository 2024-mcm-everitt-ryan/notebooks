Company: Velez, Kennedy and Flores
Role: Typist

About Us:
Velez, Kennedy and Flores is a dynamic and inclusive workplace committed to diversity and equal opportunities. We believe that a diverse team is essential to our success, and we strive to create an environment where everyone can thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and efficient Typist to join our team. As a Typist, you will be responsible for accurately and efficiently typing documents, reports, and other materials. You will work closely with our team to ensure timely and high-quality output.

Responsibilities:

 Type documents, reports, and other materials with high accuracy and efficiency
 Meet deadlines and produce high-quality work under pressure
 Collaborate with team members to ensure timely output
 Develop and maintain a thorough understanding of our formatting and style guidelines
 Perform other administrative tasks as required

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in a typing or administrative role
 High school diploma or equivalent required; post-secondary education in a related field is an asset
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Word
 Excellent typing speed and accuracy (60+ wpm)
 Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
 Ability to work collaboratively and meet deadlines

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary range of $35,000 - $45,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program. You will also have access to ongoing training and development opportunities to help you grow in your career.
