Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Petroleum Technician to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for assisting in the extraction, production, and management of petroleum resources, ensuring efficiency, safety, and compliance with industry standards.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct regular field inspections and equipment maintenance.
- Participate in physical fitness activities with the team to ensure safety standards.
- Frequently lift up to 50 pounds as part of routine tasks.
- Travel frequently without restrictions to various sites, sometimes on short notice.
- Manage frequent interruptions and changing priorities while maintaining focus.
- Operate and maintain a variety of technical equipment and tools.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Petroleum Engineering or a related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in the petroleum industry.
- Must be able to work in physically demanding environments.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Ability to handle high-stress situations and adapt to rapidly changing environments.
- Excellent communication skills and team-oriented mindset.
