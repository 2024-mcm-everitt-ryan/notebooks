Company: Richardson-Knight  
Role: Data Assistant  

Company Background:  
Richardson-Knight is a leader in data analytics, known for creating a supportive and inclusive work environment. We aim to leverage diverse perspectives to drive innovation and excellence within the industry.

Job Description:  
Richardson-Knight seeks a Data Assistant to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will collect, process, and manage data to support various business functions. This role is ideal for individuals passionate about data accuracy and integrity.

Responsibilities:  
- Collect and enter data promptly and accurately.  
- Maintain and update databases.  
- Prepare detailed reports based on collected data.  
- Assist in data analysis and generate insights for business strategies.  
- Collaborate with team members to ensure data accuracy and consistency.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Proven experience in a data entry or data management role.  
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, especially Excel.  
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.  
- Excellent communication skills.  
- Experience with data analysis software is a plus.

Perks and Benefits:  
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000, based on your experience and skills.  
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.  
- Flexible working hours and the option to work remotely.  
- Professional development opportunities.  
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
