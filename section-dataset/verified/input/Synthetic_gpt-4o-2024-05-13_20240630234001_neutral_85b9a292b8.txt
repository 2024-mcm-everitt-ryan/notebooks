Typist Position at Morales, Nelson and Murphy.
Company Background:
Morales, Nelson and Murphy is a renowned law firm specializing in corporate law, intellectual property, and legal consultancy services. We are committed to providing exceptional legal services to our clients globally

Job Type:
Full-Time Typist.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled typist to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have excellent typing speed and accuracy, strong attention to detail, and the ability to work efficiently under deadlines. They will be responsible for transcribing various documents, ensuring accuracy and confidentiality

Responsibilities:
  - Transcribe legal documents, correspondence, and other written material accurately.
  - Maintain confidentiality and ensure data security.
  - Proofread documents for errors and inconsistencies.
  - Manage and organize digital and physical files.
  - Assist in other administrative tasks as needed.

Qualifications and Experience:
  - Proven experience as a typist or in a similar role.
  - Excellent typing speed and accuracy (minimum 70 words per minute).
  - Proficiency in MS Office and other word processing software.
  - Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
  - Ability to work independently and meet deadlines.

Perks and Benefits:
  - We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £24,000 to £30,000, based on your experience and skills.
  - Health, dental, and vision insurance.
  - Paid time off and holidays.
  - Professional development opportunities.
  - Flexible working hours.
