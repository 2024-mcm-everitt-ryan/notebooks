Company: Johnson and Sons

Role: Claim Specialist

Company Background:
Johnson and Sons is a leading insurance provider committed to delivering exceptional service and innovative solutions to our clients. Our team is dedicated to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace where all employees can thrive.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
Johnson and Sons is looking for a diligent and detail-oriented Claim Specialist to join our team. The candidate will be responsible for processing insurance claims efficiently and accurately while maintaining the highest standards of customer service. 

Responsibilities:
- Analyse and review claim submissions, ensuring all required documentation is complete and accurate.
- Communicate with clients to gather necessary information and provide updates on claim status.
- Collaborate with other departments to resolve complex claim issues.
- Conduct market research to identify trends and suggest improvements in claim processing.
- Maintain detailed records of all claim transactions and interactions.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Business, Finance, or related field.
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in insurance claim processing or a related role.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
- Proficiency in using claim management software and MS Office applications.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $50,000 to $70,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday policy.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Employee assistance programs and wellness initiatives.
