Company: Miller, Bowman and Johnson

Job Title: Personnel Associate

About Us:
Miller, Bowman and Johnson is a dynamic organization dedicated to fostering a culture of inclusivity, respect, and equal opportunities for all. We believe that a diverse workforce is essential to our success and strive to create an environment where everyone can thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Personnel Associate to join our HR team. As a Personnel Associate, you will play a vital role in supporting our recruitment processes, maintaining accurate records, and providing exceptional administrative support.

Responsibilities:

 Assist in the development and implementation of recruitment strategies to attract top talent
 Coordinate and facilitate the onboarding process for new hires
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date employee records and databases
 Provide administrative support to the HR team, including preparing reports and presentations
 Develop and maintain effective relationships with employees, management, and external partners

Qualifications and Experience:

 Relevant Bachelor's degree required for consideration
 2+ years of experience in HR or a related field
 Excellent organizational, communication, and interpersonal skills
 Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion
 Proficiency in HR software and systems

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.

We value graduates from a wide range of educational backgrounds and are looking for individuals committed to their work schedule. Visible piercings are acceptable if they are not offensive. Background checks will be conducted for all applicants.
