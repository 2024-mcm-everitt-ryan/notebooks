DE ION

Amazon strives to be Earth's most customer-centric company where people can find and discover virtually anything they want to buy online. To serve this goal, Amazon has created one of the most advanced fulfillment and logistic networks in the world. Amazon Global Logistics in China is a team of highly experienced logistics professionals, product & program managers, and carrier specialists actively participating in Amazon's international expansion goals.

We are seeking some highly motivated individuals who are interested in providing best-in-class logistics service to customers as global logistics operation specialists. The main purpose and responsibilities are to act as a control tower on end-to-end global logistics operation processes by focusing on process, procedure, and data-relevant activities with the aim to standardize processes, improve operation efficiency, and service quality.

- Serve as a dedicated contact window for the Amazon sellers for Air shipments arrangement from booking receiving to Amazon Fulfillment Center delivery.
- Deal with origin operations including booking with LSP, picking-up, CFS handling, export customs clearance, etc.
- Perform as an escalation point for any abnormal cases and provide proper solutions to internal/external customers.
- Follow up on origin accessorial charge verification and confirmation.
- Provide proper transportation solutions based on the seller's commodity and documents available, like battery or DG shipments.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS

- University graduation with 3-5 years of working experience in international forwarders/or top CN local forwarders with Air Freight, better for similar job functions of operations or customer service.
- Proficient written and oral communication skills in both English and Chinese.
- Strong sense of responsibility and ownership to meet deadlines, take preemptive measures to avoid freight delay.

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

- Operation experiences on International E-commerce shipment processes are preferable.
- Strong capability of data analyzing/deep dive is preferable.
