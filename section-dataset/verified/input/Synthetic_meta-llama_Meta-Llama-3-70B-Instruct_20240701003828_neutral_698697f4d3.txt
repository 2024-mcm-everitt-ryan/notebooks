Company: Riggs, Elliott and Holland
Role: Bid Operations Liaison

About Us:
Riggs, Elliott and Holland is a leading consulting firm that specializes in providing strategic solutions to businesses worldwide. We are committed to fostering a culture of inclusivity, respect, and collaboration.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Bid Operations Liaison to join our team. As a Bid Operations Liaison, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth operation of our bid management process. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to coordinate bid responses, manage project timelines, and maintain accurate records.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate and track bid responses to ensure timely and accurate submissions
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with internal stakeholders and external partners
 Manage project timelines and deadlines to ensure successful bid outcomes
 Analyze and report on bid metrics to inform business decisions
 Collaborate with teams to identify areas for process improvement

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience in bid management, operations, or a related field
 Strong project management and organizational skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and Google Suite

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
