Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and creative Designer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong portfolio showcasing their design expertise, a keen eye for detail, and the ability to work collaboratively within a team. As a Designer at Evans-Payne, you will be responsible for developing innovative design concepts and bringing them to life through various mediums

  - Collaborate with team members and clients to ensure design solutions are effective and cohesive.
  - Maintain and update design templates and assets.
  - Stay current with industry trends and incorporate relevant design techniques.
  - Present design ideas and concepts to clients for feedback and approval.

Qualifications and Experience:
  - Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, Fine Arts, or a related field.
  - At least 3 years of professional design experience.
  - Proficiency in design software, including Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).
  - Exceptional creativity and innovation.
  - Strong communication and collaboration skills.
  - Ability to manage multiple projects and meet deadlines.
