Company: Arias, Tran and Bryant

Job Title: Quality Management Specialist

At Arias, Tran and Bryant, we are committed to promoting diversity and inclusion in our workforce. As part of our efforts to empower women in the industry, we are excited to announce an opening for a Quality Management Specialist. This position is an opportunity for a highly skilled and dedicated individual to join our team and contribute to our mission of delivering exceptional quality products and services.

Job Description:
The Quality Management Specialist will be responsible for developing, implementing, and maintaining quality management systems to ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in quality management, excellent communication skills, and the ability to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement quality management systems and processes
 Conduct internal audits and identify areas for improvement
 Collaborate with teams to resolve quality issues and implement corrective actions
 Analyze quality data and provide recommendations for improvement
 Ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a relevant field (e.g. engineering, business administration)
 3+ years of experience in quality management
 Strong knowledge of industry standards and regulations (e.g. ISO 9001)
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
