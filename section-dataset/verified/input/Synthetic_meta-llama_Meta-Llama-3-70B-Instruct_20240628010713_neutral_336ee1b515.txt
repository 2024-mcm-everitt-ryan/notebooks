Company: Bird-Brown

Role: Senior Estimator

About Us:
Bird-Brown is a leading construction company that specializes in commercial and residential projects. We take pride in our commitment to delivering high-quality results and exceptional customer service.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly experienced Senior Estimator to join our team. As a Senior Estimator, you will be responsible for preparing and managing project estimates, reviewing proposals, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure project deliverables are met.

Responsibilities:

 Prepare and manage project estimates, including reviewing proposals and identifying potential risks and opportunities
 Collaborate with project managers, architects, and engineers to ensure accurate and timely estimates
 Develop and maintain relationships with clients, suppliers, and subcontractors
 Analyze project data and provide recommendations for cost savings and process improvements

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Engineering, or a related field
 5+ years of experience in construction estimating, with a focus on commercial and residential projects
 Proven track record of delivering accurate and timely estimates
 Excellent communication and project management skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off. We also provide a supportive and inclusive work environment, with opportunities for professional development and growth.
