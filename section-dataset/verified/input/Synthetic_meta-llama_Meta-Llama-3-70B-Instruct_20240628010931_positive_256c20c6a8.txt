Company: Burke, Turner and Hernandez

Job Title: Crime Analyst

About Us:
Burke, Turner and Hernandez is a leading forensic analysis firm dedicated to providing expert services to law enforcement agencies and legal professionals. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace that reflects the communities we serve.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Crime Analyst to join our team. As a Crime Analyst, you will work closely with investigators to analyze and interpret complex data, identify patterns, and provide insights to inform criminal investigations. Your expertise will help us drive justice and make a positive impact in our communities.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze and interpret large datasets to identify trends and patterns
 Develop and maintain databases to track and analyze criminal activity
 Collaborate with investigators to develop case strategies and provide expert testimony
 Stay up-to-date with emerging trends and best practices in crime analysis

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Criminal Justice, Statistics, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in crime analysis or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
