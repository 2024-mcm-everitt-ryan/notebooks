Job Title: Senior Web Designer / Art Director

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Web Designer / Art Director to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in web design, user experience, and creative direction. This role requires an individual who can bring innovative ideas and artistic excellence to our diverse projects.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and manage web design projects from concept to completion.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure cohesive visual representation.
- Develop and maintain design systems and guidelines.
- Mentor junior designers and provide constructive feedback.
- Stay updated on the latest design trends and technologies.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design, Web Design, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in web design or art direction.
- Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, Figma, and other design tools.
- Strong portfolio showcasing creative and diverse design work.
- Excellent communication and leadership skills.
