Company: Holland-Sullivan
Role: Podiatrist

About Us:
Holland-Sullivan is a well-established medical facility dedicated to providing exceptional patient care. We are seeking a skilled and experienced Podiatrist to join our team of healthcare professionals.

Job Description:
As a Podiatrist at Holland-Sullivan, you will be responsible for diagnosing and treating a wide range of foot and ankle disorders. You will work closely with our team of medical professionals to provide comprehensive care to our patients.

Responsibilities:

 Diagnose and treat foot and ankle disorders
 Develop and implement personalized treatment plans
 Collaborate with other healthcare professionals to provide comprehensive care
 Maintain accurate and detailed medical records

Qualifications and Experience:
We are looking for a candidate with a strong educational background and a minimum of 5 years of experience in podiatry. A clean legal history is mandatory for consideration. Preference will be given to candidates with a professional or corporate background.

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $120,000 to $180,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan.
