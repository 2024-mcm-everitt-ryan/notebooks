Company Background:

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled stucco mason to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will possess extensive experience in stucco application, repair, and finishing. Our commitment to diversity includes a strong focus on representation from the queer community, and we encourage individuals of diverse sexual orientations and gender identities to apply. 

Responsibilities:
- Apply stucco to building exteriors, ensuring a smooth and durable finish.

- Collaborate with team members to complete projects on time and within budget.
- Maintain a safe and clean work environment.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a stucco mason or similar role.
- Familiarity with various stucco techniques and materials.
- Strong attention to detail and excellent craftsmanship.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Commitment to safety and quality.
