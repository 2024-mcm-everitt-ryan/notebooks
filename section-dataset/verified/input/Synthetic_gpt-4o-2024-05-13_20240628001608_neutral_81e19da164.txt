Company: Hoffman Group

Role: Appliance Repairer

Company Background:
Hoffman Group is a leader in home appliance repair and maintenance services. We pride ourselves on delivering top-notch service and ensuring customer satisfaction across our diverse clientele. We are committed to fostering an inclusive and supportive workplace environment.

Job Description:
As an Appliance Repairer at Hoffman Group, you will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing a wide range of home appliances. You will work closely with our team to ensure timely and efficient service delivery.

Responsibilities:
- Diagnose and repair various home appliances.
- Communicate effectively with customers regarding repair status and maintenance tips.
- Maintain detailed records of repairs and parts used.
- Ensure compliance with safety regulations and company policies.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as an appliance repairer or similar role.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and customer service skills.
- Technical certification in appliance repair is preferred but not required.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from USD 40,000 to USD 55,000, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Continuous training and development opportunities.
- Inclusive and supportive workplace culture that respects and values the diversity of the LGBTQ community.
