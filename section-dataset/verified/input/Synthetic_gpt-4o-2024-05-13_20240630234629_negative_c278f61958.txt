Company: Day-Anderson

Role: Telephone Operator

Company Background:
Day-Anderson is a leading customer service solutions provider, recognized for its dedication to innovative communication methods and exceptional customer experience. With over 20 years in the industry, we pride ourselves on maintaining strong client relationships and upholding the highest service standards.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Day-Anderson is seeking a dedicated Telephone Operator to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will possess robust problem-solving skills and thrive in a fast-paced environment. This role involves managing inbound and outbound calls, ensuring client satisfaction, and accurately logging call details.

Responsibilities:
- Manage high volumes of inbound and outbound calls professionally.
- Resolve customer inquiries efficiently and effectively.
- Maintain detailed records of all calls and customer interactions.
- Collaborate with team members to improve service quality.
- Adhere to company policies and procedures.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a telephone operator or in a customer service role.
- Excellent communication skills with a clear and confident speaking voice.
- Ability to multitask and handle pressure.
- Strong problem-solving capabilities.
- High school diploma or equivalent.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €25,000 to €35,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- Generous paid time off and holidays.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their resume and cover letter detailing their experience and qualifications.
