Company: Torres and Sons

Job Title: Manager of Information Technology

As a Manager of Information Technology at Torres and Sons, you will be responsible for leading our IT team in developing and implementing technology solutions to drive business growth. Our company values diversity and inclusivity, and we strive to create a work environment that fosters collaboration, creativity, and mutual respect.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee the development and implementation of IT projects and initiatives
- Manage and mentor a team of IT professionals
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize technology solutions
- Develop and manage budgets for IT projects and operations
- Ensure the security and integrity of our technology infrastructure

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
- At least 5 years of experience in IT management
- Excellent communication and leadership skills
- Strong understanding of current technology trends and best practices
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities

Benefits:
- Salary range: $90,000 - $120,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Recognition and rewards for outstanding performance

We are an equal opportunities employer and welcome applications from diverse candidates. Our team thrives on inclusivity and mutual respect, and we believe that a diverse workforce is essential to driving business success.
