Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Social Scientist to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will conduct comprehensive social research, analyze data, and present findings that contribute to our broader understanding of societal issues.

- Collect and analyze qualitative and quantitative data.
- Present research findings to stakeholders.

- Stay updated with the latest research and methodologies in the field.

- Master's degree or PhD in sociology, anthropology, or related field.
- Proven experience in social research and data analysis.
- Strong written and verbal communication skills.
- Proficiency in statistical software and data visualization tools.
- Experience in business development is advantageous.
- Experience in training and development is preferred.
