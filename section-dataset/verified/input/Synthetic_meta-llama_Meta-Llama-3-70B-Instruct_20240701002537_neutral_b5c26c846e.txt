Company: Jones, Gamble and Hart

Job Title: Public Health Nurse

Job Description:
Jones, Gamble and Hart is seeking a dedicated and skilled Public Health Nurse to join our team. As a Public Health Nurse, you will be responsible for developing and implementing health education programs, conducting health assessments, and providing nursing care to underserved populations.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement health education programs to promote healthy lifestyles and disease prevention
 Conduct health assessments and provide nursing care to individuals and communities
 Collaborate with community partners to identify health disparities and develop strategies to address them
 Provide education and support to patients and families on healthy habits and disease management
 Participate in quality improvement initiatives to improve health outcomes

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Nursing or related field
 Current RN licensure
 At least 2 years of experience in public health nursing
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Familiarity with electronic health records

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
