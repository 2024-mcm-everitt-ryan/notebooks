Company Background:
Pearson-Clark is a global leader in construction and engineering, specializing in large-scale infrastructure projects around the world. Our commitment to innovation and excellence has made us a trusted name in the industry.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
Pearson-Clark is seeking a skilled Rigger to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for setting up and repairing rigging for the movement of heavy equipment and materials. This position requires physical strength, precision, and a strong understanding of safety protocols.

Responsibilities:
- Install and repair rigging systems for lifting and moving heavy materials and equipment.
- Inspect rigging before use to ensure safety and functionality.
- Collaborate with other team members to plan and execute lifting operations.
- Use hand and power tools to complete rigging tasks efficiently.
- Adhere strictly to safety guidelines to prevent accidents and injuries.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Previous experience as a Rigger or in a similar role is required.
- Must be able to lift heavy objects and work in physically demanding environments.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail are essential.
- Ability to make quick, decisive decisions during operations.
- Familiarity with rigging equipment and tools.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $50,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
