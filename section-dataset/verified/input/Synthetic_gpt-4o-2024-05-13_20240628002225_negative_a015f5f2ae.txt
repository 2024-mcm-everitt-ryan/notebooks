Job Description:
We are seeking a Purchasing Agent - Buyer to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for sourcing products, negotiating with suppliers, and managing inventory levels to ensure the smooth operation of our procurement processes.

Responsibilities:
- Source and procure products and services in alignment with company needs.
- Negotiate contracts with suppliers to secure favorable terms.
- Maintain and update procurement records.
- Monitor inventory levels and coordinate with the logistics team.
- Ensure compliance with company policies and established social ethics.

- Bachelor's degree in Business, Supply Chain Management, or related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in a purchasing or procurement role.
- Strong negotiation and communication skills.
- Ability to respect and uphold long-established social norms and company values, which align with traditional family-oriented principles and heterosexuality.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and procurement software.
