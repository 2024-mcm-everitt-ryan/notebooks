Company: Erickson-Sims
Role: Data Entry Clerk

About Us:
Erickson-Sims is a leading provider of data management solutions, committed to delivering exceptional services to our clients.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Data Entry Clerk to join our team. As a Data Entry Clerk, you will be responsible for accurately and efficiently entering data into our systems, maintaining data integrity, and performing various administrative tasks.

Responsibilities:

 Enter data into our systems with high accuracy and efficiency
 Maintain data integrity and identify discrepancies
 Perform administrative tasks, such as filing and data retrieval
 Collaborate with team members to meet project deadlines
 Stay up-to-date with industry developments and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in data entry or a related field
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and data entry software
 Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Open to all levels of experience, we provide training and support to ensure your success

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $40,000 to $50,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you will be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
