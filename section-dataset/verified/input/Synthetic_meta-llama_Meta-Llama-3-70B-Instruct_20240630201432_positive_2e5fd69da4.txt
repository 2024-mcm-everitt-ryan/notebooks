Company: Rodriguez, Potts and Snyder
Role: Furniture Coordinator

We are seeking a talented Furniture Coordinator to join our team! As a company committed to diversity and inclusion, we are particularly interested in applications from women who are passionate about furniture design and coordination.

In this role, you will be responsible for coordinating the design, production, and delivery of furniture products to our clients. You will work closely with our design team to ensure that our products meet the highest standards of quality and aesthetics.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate the design, production, and delivery of furniture products
 Work closely with the design team to ensure product quality and aesthetics
 Communicate with clients to understand their needs and preferences
 Manage and prioritize multiple projects simultaneously

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in furniture design or coordination
 Strong communication and project management skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Knowledge of design software such as SketchUp or Autodesk

What We Offer:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 A comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance and retirement savings
 Opportunities for professional growth and development in a dynamic and inclusive work environment
 A team of talented and supportive colleagues who are passionate about furniture design
