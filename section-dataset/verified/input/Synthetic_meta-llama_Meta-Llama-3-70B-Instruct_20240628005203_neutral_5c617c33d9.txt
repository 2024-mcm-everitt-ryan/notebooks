Company: Thompson LLC

Job Title: Special Assistant

About Us:
Thompson LLC is a dynamic and innovative company dedicated to creating a workplace where everyone can thrive. We believe in fostering an environment that values diversity, equity, and inclusion.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Special Assistant to join our team. As a Special Assistant, you will provide administrative support to our senior leadership team, ensuring seamless day-to-day operations. Your exceptional communication skills and attention to detail will make you a valuable asset to our organization.

Responsibilities:

 Manage calendars, schedule meetings, and coordinate travel arrangements for senior leaders
 Prepare and distribute meeting materials, taking minutes, and following up on action items
 Develop and maintain filing systems, both physical and digital
 Provide general administrative support to the team as needed

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in an administrative role
 High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree preferred
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and Google Workspace
 Excellent communication, organizational, and time management skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off. We also offer a range of employee wellness programs, including mental health resources and employee assistance programs.

Our facilities are designed to support every parent, and we're proud to offer nursing rooms for employees who need them. We believe in creating a workplace where everyone can bring their authentic selves, and we celebrate all gender identities and sexual orientations. Everyone in our company is encouraged to take on leadership roles, and we're looking for talented individuals from all walks of life to join our team.
