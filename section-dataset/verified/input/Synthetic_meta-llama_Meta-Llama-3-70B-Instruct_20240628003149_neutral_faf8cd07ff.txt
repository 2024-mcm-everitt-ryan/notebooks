Company: Oliver-Davies

Job Title: Extraction Worker

About Us:
Oliver-Davies is a leading provider of extraction services, dedicated to delivering high-quality results to our clients. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment that values the skills and expertise of our employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Extraction Worker to join our team. As an Extraction Worker, you will be responsible for operating and maintaining extraction equipment, ensuring efficient and safe operations. You will work closely with our team to optimize extraction processes and improve overall performance.

Responsibilities:

 Operate and maintain extraction equipment to ensure optimal performance
 Collaborate with the team to optimize extraction processes and improve overall performance
 Ensure compliance with safety protocols and regulations
 Participate in training and development programs to enhance skills and knowledge

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in extraction operations or a related field
 Strong understanding of extraction equipment and processes
 Ability to work safely and efficiently in a fast-paced environment
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $55,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
