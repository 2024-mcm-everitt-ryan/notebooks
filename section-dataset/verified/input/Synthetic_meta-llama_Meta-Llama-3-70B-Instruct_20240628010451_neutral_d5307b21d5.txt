Company: Garcia and Sons
Role: Animal Trainer

At Garcia and Sons, we are dedicated to creating an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive. As an Animal Trainer, you will be responsible for developing and executing training programs for our diverse range of animals. Our team is made up of individuals from a variety of backgrounds, and we welcome people of all sexual orientations.

Job Description:
The Animal Trainer will oversee the development and execution of projects, ensuring the highest standards of animal care and welfare. This includes designing and implementing training programs, monitoring animal behavior, and collaborating with our veterinary team.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and execute training programs for animals of all species
 Monitor and assess animal behavior, making adjustments to training programs as needed
 Collaborate with the veterinary team to ensure animal health and welfare
 Provide exceptional customer service to our clients
 Maintain accurate records of animal training and behavior

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Animal Science, Biology, or related field
 2+ years of experience in animal training or a related field
 Strong knowledge of animal behavior and learning theory
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance package
