Company: Hernandez-Stone

Job Title: Speech-Language Pathologist

About Us:
Hernandez-Stone is a cutting-edge healthcare provider dedicated to delivering exceptional patient care. Our team of skilled professionals is committed to making a difference in the lives of our patients.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly qualified Speech-Language Pathologist to join our team. The ideal candidate will possess excellent communication skills, a strong work ethic, and a passion for helping others.

Responsibilities:

 Assess and diagnose speech, language, and swallowing disorders
 Develop and implement individualized treatment plans
 Collaborate with interdisciplinary teams to achieve patient goals
 Maintain accurate and timely records

Qualifications and Experience:
Bachelor's degree in Speech-Language Pathology or related field required. ASHA certification and 2+ years of experience working with diverse patient populations preferred.

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 65,000 to USD 85,000, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we provide comprehensive benefits, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and opportunities for professional growth.
