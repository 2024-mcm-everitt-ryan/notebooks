Company Background:
Anderson PLC is a leading furniture manufacturer dedicated to delivering high-quality products that exceed customer expectations. Our team of skilled professionals is committed to excellence, innovation, and sustainability.

Job Type: Cabinetmaker

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Cabinetmaker to join our production team. As a Cabinetmaker, you will be responsible for designing, building, and installing custom cabinets and furniture pieces. You will work closely with our design team to ensure that all projects meet the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Responsibilities:

 Design and build custom cabinets and furniture pieces according to customer specifications
 Collaborate with the design team to ensure accurate project execution
 Operate various woodworking machines and tools safely and efficiently
 Inspect and quality-check all workpieces to ensure high-quality output
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in cabinetmaking or a related field
 Strong knowledge of woodworking techniques and materials
 Ability to read and interpret design plans and specifications
 Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 High school diploma or equivalent required; formal training in cabinetmaking or a related field is an asset

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £35,000 to £50,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage
 Generous paid time off, including vacation days, sick leave, and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 We welcome individuals from all backgrounds and experiences. Our inclusive work environment values diversity and promotes equality.
