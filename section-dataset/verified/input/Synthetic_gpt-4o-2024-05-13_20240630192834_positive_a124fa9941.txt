Job Type:
Full-Time, Deputy Chief of Quality Assurance

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented Deputy Chief of Quality Assurance to join our dynamic team. This role requires a strategic thinker with a strong background in quality assurance to oversee and enhance our quality control processes. 

Responsibilities:
- Lead and mentor the quality assurance team to ensure adherence to best practices and industry standards.
- Develop, implement, and maintain quality assurance protocols and procedures.
- Collaborate with various departments to ensure product quality and compliance with regulatory requirements.
- Conduct regular audits and generate detailed reports on product performance and quality issues.
- Drive continuous improvement initiatives to enhance product quality and operational efficiency.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Quality Management, or a related field.
- Minimum of 8 years of experience in quality assurance or a related field, with at least 3 years in a leadership role.
- Proven ability to lead and develop high-performing teams.
- Strong analytical skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Women with experience in this field are preferred.
