Company Background:
Molina, Ramirez and Taylor is a leading construction company dedicated to building innovative and sustainable projects. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion.

Job Type:
Plasterer

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and experienced Plasterer to join our team. As a Plasterer, you will be responsible for applying and finishing plaster to create smooth surfaces on walls, ceilings, and floors. You will work closely with our team of contractors and builders to ensure high-quality results.

Responsibilities:

 Apply and finish plaster to achieve a smooth surface
 Work with contractors and builders to ensure timely and high-quality results
 Maintain a safe and organized work environment
 Collaborate with the team to achieve project goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Plasterer
 Strong knowledge of plastering techniques and materials
 Ability to work at heights and in confined spaces
 Able to handle tasks that may involve lifting up to 50 pounds, with accommodations as needed.

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary range of $55,000 to $75,000 per year, based on experience and skills. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. You will also receive comprehensive health benefits, paid time off, and opportunities for professional development.

We are an equal opportunities employer and welcome applications from all qualified candidates. We provide a respectful and inclusive workplace for LGBTQ2+ individuals and are committed to creating a supportive environment for the LGBTQ community.
