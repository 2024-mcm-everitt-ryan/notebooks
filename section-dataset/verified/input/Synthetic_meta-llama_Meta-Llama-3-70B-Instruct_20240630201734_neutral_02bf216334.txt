Auto Mechanic at Bell-Hernandez

Company Background:
Bell-Hernandez is a leading automotive repair service provider dedicated to delivering exceptional customer experiences. Our team of skilled professionals shares a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and motivated Auto Mechanic to join our dynamic team. As an Auto Mechanic, you will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing vehicles, ensuring high-quality workmanship, and providing exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Diagnose and repair vehicles to meet manufacturer standards
 Perform routine maintenance and inspections
 Communicate effectively with customers to understand their needs and provide solutions
 Work collaboratively with the team to achieve workshop goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as an Auto Mechanic
 Strong knowledge of vehicle systems and repair techniques
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $75,000, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you will enjoy a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement plan with company matching.
