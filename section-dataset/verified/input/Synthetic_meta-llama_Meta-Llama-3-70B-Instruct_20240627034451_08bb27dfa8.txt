Anderson, Coleman and Young is seeking an experienced and results-driven Customer Experience Center Manager to lead our customer service team. As a Customer Experience Center Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing the daily operations of our customer experience center, developing and implementing strategies to improve customer satisfaction, and ensuring a high level of quality service is delivered to our customers.

- Manage a team of customer service representatives to achieve exceptional customer service and resolve customer complaints in a timely and professional manner.
- Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and reduce costs.
- Analyze customer feedback and develop action plans to address concerns and improve overall customer satisfaction.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to resolve customer issues and improve customer experience.
- Develop and manage budgets, forecasts, and performance metrics to measure customer experience center performance.

Requirements:

- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Marketing, or related field.
- Minimum 5 years of experience in customer service operations, with at least 2 years in a management role.
- Proven track record of improving customer satisfaction and reducing costs.
- Excellent leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills.
- Proficient in CRM software and Microsoft Office.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities.
