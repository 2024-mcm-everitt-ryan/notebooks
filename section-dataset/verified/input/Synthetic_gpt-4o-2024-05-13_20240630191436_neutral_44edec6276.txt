Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented Biological Technician to join our research team. The ideal candidate will work closely with our scientists to support various biological experiments and data collection. Responsibilities:

- Monitor and record experimental data accurately.
- Ensure lab equipment is maintained and calibrated.
- Prepare samples and reagents for testing.
- Collaborate with team members to analyze results and troubleshoot issues.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Biology, Biotechnology, or related field.
- Previous laboratory experience is preferred but not mandatory.
- Proficient in data analysis and laboratory techniques.
- Strong attention to detail and ability to follow protocols.
- Excellent communication skills and a collaborative mindset.
