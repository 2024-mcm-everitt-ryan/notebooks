Company: Mathews Ltd

Job Title: Administrative Transportation Coordinator

About Us:
Mathews Ltd is a leading logistics company dedicated to providing efficient and reliable transportation services to our clients. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment that values the contributions of all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Administrative Transportation Coordinator to join our team. As an Administrative Transportation Coordinator, you will be responsible for coordinating and managing transportation operations, ensuring timely and efficient delivery of goods. You will work closely with our transportation team to optimize routes, manage schedules, and provide exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate and manage transportation operations, including route optimization and scheduling
 Communicate with drivers, customers, and internal teams to ensure seamless operations
 Monitor and report on transportation metrics, including on-time delivery rates and customer satisfaction
 Develop and maintain relationships with transportation vendors and suppliers
 Collaborate with internal teams to identify opportunities for process improvement

Qualifications and Experience:

 Relevant Bachelor's degree required for consideration
 2+ years of experience in transportation operations or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize tasks effectively
 Have a consistent and reliable employment history

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance package
 Retirement plan with company match
 Paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
