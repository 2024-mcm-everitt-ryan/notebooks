Company: Jefferson, Russell and Patrick

Job Title: Meter Reader

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and dedicated Meter Reader to join our team. As a Meter Reader, you will be responsible for reading and recording electricity, gas, and water meters in a timely and accurate manner. You will also be required to investigate and resolve any discrepancies in meter readings, and provide excellent customer service to our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Read and record electricity, gas, and water meters accurately and efficiently
 Investigate and resolve any discrepancies in meter readings
 Provide excellent customer service to our clients
 Maintain accurate records of meter readings and customer interactions
 Collaborate with other team members to ensure efficient and effective operations

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience in a meter reading or related role preferred
 Strong attention to detail and accuracy
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Valid driver's license and reliable transportation (mileage reimbursement provided)

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
