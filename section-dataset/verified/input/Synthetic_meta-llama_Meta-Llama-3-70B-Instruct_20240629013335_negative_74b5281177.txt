Company: Perez PLC
Role: Data Architect

About Us:
Perez PLC is a leading provider of data analytics solutions, dedicated to empowering businesses to make informed decisions.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Data Architect to join our team. As a Data Architect, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining our data management systems. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of data modeling, database design, and data governance.

Responsibilities:

 Design and implement data management systems to meet business requirements
 Develop and maintain data models, database designs, and data governance policies
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure data quality and integrity
 Develop and maintain technical documentation of data systems

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
 At least 5 years of experience in data architecture, data modeling, or database design
 Strong understanding of data modeling, database design, and data governance principles
 Experience with cloud-based data management systems and big data technologies

We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
