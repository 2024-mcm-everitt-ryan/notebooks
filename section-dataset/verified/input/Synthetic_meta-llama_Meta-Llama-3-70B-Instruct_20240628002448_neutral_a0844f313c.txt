Our ideal candidate is a motivated individual who can demonstrate required skills and experience in plumbing inspection. The successful candidate will be detail-oriented, organized, and able to work independently.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct on-site inspections of plumbing systems to ensure compliance with local building codes and regulations
 Identify and report any defects or deficiencies in plumbing systems
 Provide recommendations for repairs and maintenance to ensure compliance
 Collaborate with builders, contractors, and homeowners to resolve any issues

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in plumbing inspection or a related field
 Strong knowledge of local building codes and regulations
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
