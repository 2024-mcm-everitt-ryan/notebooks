Job Type:
Full-Time Contract Analyst

Job Description:
We are currently seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Contract Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for reviewing, negotiating, and managing contracts to ensure compliance with legal standards and company policies.

Responsibilities:
- Review and analyse contracts to ensure they comply with applicable laws and company policies.
- Negotiate contract terms with clients and vendors.
- Maintain accurate records of contract negotiations, amendments, and renewals.
- Collaborate with various departments to ensure contract compliance and dispute resolution.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Law, Business Administration, or a related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in contract management or a similar role.
- Strong analytical and negotiation skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
