Lead Design Engineer

Company: Johnson, Ortiz and Velasquez

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Lead Design Engineer to join our innovative team. As a Lead Design Engineer, you will be responsible for leading the design and development of cutting-edge products, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and mentoring junior engineers.

Responsibilities:

 Lead the design and development of new products, ensuring compliance with industry standards and company regulations
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, manufacturing, and sales to ensure product feasibility and marketability
 Mentor and guide junior engineers to develop their skills and expertise
 Conduct thorough product testing and troubleshooting to ensure high-quality products
 Develop and maintain technical documentation and design records

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering or related field
 5+ years of experience in design engineering, with a focus on product development
 Proven track record of leading successful product development projects
 Excellent problem-solving skills, with the ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Strong communication and leadership skills

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $120,000 to $150,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
 401(k) matching program
 Generous paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 A dynamic and supportive work environment

Note:

 Must be able to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities and deadlines.
 Must have excellent attention to detail and organizational skills.
 Office space includes several flights of stairs; candidates must be able to navigate this environment comfortably.
