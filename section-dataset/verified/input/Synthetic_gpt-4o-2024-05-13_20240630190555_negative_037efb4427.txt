Company Background:

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
Norris, Shepard and McGee is seeking a senior staff electrical engineer who is sensitive and understanding, with robust experience in managing electrical design projects. She should have a background in overseeing multidisciplinary teams and have strong communication skills to ensure smooth project execution.

Responsibilities:
- Lead electrical design projects from concept to completion.
- Coordinate with multidisciplinary teams to ensure project milestones are met.
- Develop and review electrical schematics, plans, and reports.
- Provide mentorship and guidance to junior engineers.
- Maintain client relationships and ensure client satisfaction.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering or a related field.
- Minimum of 8 years’ experience in electrical design engineering.
- Proven track record of managing large-scale projects.
- Exceptional leadership and team management skills.
- Excellent verbal and written communication abilities.
