Company Background:
Prince Inc, a well-established security services provider, is dedicated to ensuring safety and upholding justice within the community. We are committed to fostering an inclusive and equitable workplace culture.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Prince Inc is seeking a dedicated Sheriff's Patrol Officer to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for maintaining law and order, conducting patrols, and responding to emergencies. The role requires collaboration with multiple departments and a strong commitment to community service.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct routine patrols to ensure public safety.
- Respond promptly to emergency calls and incidents.
- Collaborate with various departments to address community concerns.
- Prepare detailed reports on incidents and activities.
- Engage with the community to build trust and provide assistance.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Previous experience in law enforcement or a related field.
- Strong leadership skills and the ability to work collaboratively.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to handle high-pressure situations calmly and effectively.
- A valid driver's license and clean driving record.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday leave.
- Retirement savings plan with company matching.
- Ongoing training and professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000, based on your experience and skills.
