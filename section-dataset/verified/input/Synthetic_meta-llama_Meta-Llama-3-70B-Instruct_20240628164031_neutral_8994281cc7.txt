Company: Reed and Sons

Job Title: Translator

About Us:
Reed and Sons is a leading language services provider dedicated to breaking language barriers and facilitating global communication. We are committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities for all.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and experienced Translator to join our team. As a Translator at Reed and Sons, you will be responsible for providing high-quality translations that meet our clients' needs. You will work closely with our project managers and linguists to ensure timely and accurate delivery of projects.

Responsibilities:

 Translate documents, websites, and other materials from one language to another
 Review and edit translations to ensure accuracy and quality
 Collaborate with project managers and linguists to meet project deadlines
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Translation, Linguistics, or a related field
 At least 3 years of translation experience
 Fluency in multiple languages (please specify languages in your application)
 Excellent writing and editing skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We also provide a flexible work environment, including remote work options and a compressed workweek.

We ensure an inclusive and welcoming workplace, and offer reasonable accommodations for all roles. We are committed to fair treatment for all candidates and employees.
