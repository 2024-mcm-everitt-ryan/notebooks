Company: Gonzalez-Duncan

Role: City Laborer

Company Background:
Gonzalez-Duncan is a well-established infrastructure and public works company known for its dedication to community development and quality service. We pride ourselves on creating and maintaining essential city facilities and services that enhance the lives of residents.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking knowledgeable professionals to join our team as City Laborers. In this role, you will be responsible for the maintenance and repair of city facilities, including parks, streets, and public buildings. This position requires a strong commitment to excellence and a passion for serving the community.

Responsibilities:
- Perform routine maintenance and repair work on city infrastructure.
- Operate various types of machinery and equipment safely.
- Assist in the construction and renovation of public facilities.
- Maintain accurate records of work performed.
- Ensure compliance with safety regulations and protocols.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Job-related expertise essential.
- Previous experience in a similar role is preferred.
- Ability to perform physically demanding tasks.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 per year, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates who meet the criteria are encouraged to apply by submitting their resume and a cover letter detailing their relevant experience and why they are passionate about this role.

Gonzalez-Duncan is an equal opportunity employer.
