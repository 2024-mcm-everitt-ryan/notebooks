Role:
Business Solution Architect

Job Description:
Dodson, Bush and Schultz are seeking a highly skilled Business Solution Architect to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing and implementing comprehensive business solutions that drive organizational efficiency and effectiveness. This role involves collaborating with various stakeholders to understand business needs and translate them into actionable strategies.

Responsibilities:
- Collaborate with clients to gather and analyze business requirements.
- Develop and design business solutions that align with client objectives.
- Lead the implementation of business solutions, ensuring seamless integration across systems.
- Provide ongoing support and optimization of implemented solutions.
- Work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure project success.
- Maintain up-to-date knowledge of industry trends and best practices.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Information Technology, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in business solution architecture or a similar role.
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
- Ability to think critically and strategically.
- Proven track record of working under pressure and meeting deadlines.
- Proficiency in relevant software and technologies.
