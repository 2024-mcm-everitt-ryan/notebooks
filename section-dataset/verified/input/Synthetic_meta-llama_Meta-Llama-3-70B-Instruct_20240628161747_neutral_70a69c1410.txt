Company: Church-Jones

Job Title: Quality Assurance Project Manager

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Quality Assurance Project Manager to join our team at Church-Jones. As a Quality Assurance Project Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing quality assurance projects from initiation to delivery, ensuring that all projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify and mitigate risks, develop and implement quality plans, and monitor project progress.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement quality plans and procedures to ensure project deliverables meet required standards
 Collaborate with project teams to identify and mitigate risks
 Monitor and report on project progress, identifying areas for improvement
 Ensure that all projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards
 Foster a culture of quality and continuous improvement within the organization

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a related field
 At least 5 years of experience in quality assurance project management
 Proven track record of successfully managing quality assurance projects
 Excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary range of $80,000 to $110,000 USD per annum, based on your experience and skills. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and 20 days of paid annual leave.
