Job Title: Payroll Supervisor
Company: Garcia-Savage

About Us:
Garcia-Savage is a leading financial services firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions to our clients.

Job Description:
We're seeking a results-driven Payroll Supervisor to join our team. As a Payroll Supervisor, you will be responsible for managing our payroll operations, ensuring timely and accurate payment to our employees. The ideal candidate is a competitive individual who can thrive in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Manage payroll operations, including data entry, reconciliation, and reporting
 Ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations
 Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency
 Lead a team of payroll professionals to achieve departmental goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in payroll management
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines
 Bachelor's degree in Accounting or related field

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. In addition, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
