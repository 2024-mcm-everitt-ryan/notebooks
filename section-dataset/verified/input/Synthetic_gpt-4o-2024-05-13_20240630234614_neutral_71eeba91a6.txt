Job Description:
We are seeking a Senior IT Business Analyst to join our dynamic team.     - Provide expert advice on IT systems and business processes.
    - Prepare comprehensive reports and presentations for stakeholders.

  Qualifications:
    - Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Business Administration, or a related field.
    - Minimum of 5 years of experience in IT business analysis.
    - Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
    - Excellent communication and collaboration abilities.
    - Certification in Business Analysis (e.g., CBAP) is a plus.
