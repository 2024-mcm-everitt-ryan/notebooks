Company: Gonzales and Sons
Role: Evaluation Lead

About Us:
Gonzales and Sons is a leading consulting firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions to businesses worldwide. Our team of experts is committed to delivering exceptional results, and we're looking for an Evaluation Lead to join our dynamic team.

Job Description:
As an Evaluation Lead, you will be responsible for overseeing the evaluation process of our projects, ensuring that our clients receive high-quality results. Your expertise in data analysis, project management, and stakeholder engagement will be essential in driving our mission forward.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement evaluation plans, ensuring timely and accurate project assessments
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify areas for improvement and implement changes
 Provide training and guidance to junior staff members on evaluation methodologies and best practices
 Foster strong relationships with clients, stakeholders, and project teams to ensure effective communication and collaboration
 Analyze and interpret complex data, presenting findings and recommendations to senior management and clients

Qualifications and Experience:

 Master's degree in a relevant field (e.g., social sciences, statistics, or business administration)
 Minimum 5 years of experience in evaluation, research, or a related field
 Proven track record of leading evaluation projects, with a strong focus on data analysis and interpretation
 Excellent communication, project management, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment, meeting tight deadlines and prioritizing tasks accordingly
