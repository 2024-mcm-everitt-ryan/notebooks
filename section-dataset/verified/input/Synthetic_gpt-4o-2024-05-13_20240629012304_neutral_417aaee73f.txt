Company: Webb-Parrish

Role: Administrative Assistant

Company Background: Webb-Parrish is a leading firm in business consulting, known for its commitment to innovation, integrity, and excellence. We pride ourselves on fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace where all employees can thrive.

Job Description: We are looking for a dedicated Administrative Assistant to join our dynamic team. This role involves supporting various administrative tasks, ensuring the smooth operation of our office, and maintaining a professional environment. The ideal candidate is organized, detail-oriented, and able to handle multiple responsibilities efficiently.

Responsibilities:
- Manage and organize office operations and procedures.
- Schedule meetings and appointments, maintaining a professional calendar.
- Assist in the preparation of regularly scheduled reports.
- Handle requests and queries from senior managers.
- Maintain a filing system and update office policies as necessary.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience as an administrative assistant or in a relevant administrative role.
- Proficiency in MS Office (MS Excel and MS PowerPoint, in particular).
- Excellent time management skills and the ability to prioritize work.
- Strong organizational skills with the ability to multi-task.
- High school diploma or equivalent; college degree preferred.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $50,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Support for substance abuse recovery is available.
- Flexible working hours and the option to work remotely part-time.
- Professional development opportunities and tuition reimbursement.
