Assembler Tasks & Duties (What You'll Do)

- Check assembled products to make sure they're put together correctly (quality control).

- Fix products to make them match the product picture diagram.
- Write down any issues with products and record how many products you've assembled.
- Make sure your workstation has enough parts to assemble.
