Company: Melendez and Sons
Role: Community Coordinator

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and motivated Community Coordinator to join our team. As a Community Coordinator, you will be responsible for developing and implementing community engagement strategies, building relationships with local organizations, and coordinating events that promote community involvement.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement community engagement strategies to increase community involvement and participation
 Build and maintain relationships with local organizations, businesses, and community groups
 Coordinate and plan events, meetings, and activities that promote community engagement
 Collaborate with internal teams to ensure alignment and maximum impact
 Analyze and report on community engagement metrics and outcomes

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in community engagement, event planning, or a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Experience with event planning and coordination
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and provide opportunities for professional growth and development.
