Company: Henson, Nelson and Small

Job Title: Typist

About Us:
Henson, Nelson and Small is a leading provider of administrative services, dedicated to delivering exceptional support to our clients. We are committed to fostering a culture of belonging, where every individual feels valued, respected, and empowered to succeed.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Typist to join our team. As a Typist, you will be responsible for providing accurate and efficient typing services to our clients. You will work closely with our team to ensure timely and high-quality delivery of documents and reports.

Responsibilities:

 Type documents, reports, and other materials with high accuracy and efficiency
 Meet productivity and quality standards
 Collaborate with team members to ensure timely delivery of projects
 Maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Typist or in a related role
 High school diploma or equivalent required; post-secondary education in a related field is an asset
 Excellent typing skills (minimum 60 words per minute)
 Proficient in Microsoft Office, particularly Word and Excel
 Strong attention to detail and organizational skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program. We also offer a family-friendly workplace policy, with opportunities for flexible scheduling and remote work.
