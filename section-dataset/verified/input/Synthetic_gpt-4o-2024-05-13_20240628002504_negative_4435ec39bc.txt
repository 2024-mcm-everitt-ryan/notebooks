Ponce-Diaz  
Role: Weigher

Company Background:
Ponce-Diaz is a leading global manufacturing company specializing in precision measurement solutions. We pride ourselves on maintaining high standards and a collaborative work environment.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Ponce-Diaz is seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented Weigher to join our team. The Weigher will be responsible for accurately measuring and recording the weight of materials and products to ensure quality control and compliance with specifications.

Responsibilities:
- Accurately weigh and measure raw materials, intermediates, and finished products.
- Record weights and other relevant data in the system.
- Maintain and calibrate weighing equipment to ensure accuracy.
- Collaborate with production and quality assurance teams to ensure consistency and adherence to standards.
- Uphold safety and quality assurance protocols.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Previous experience in a manufacturing or quality control environment is preferred.
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
- Ability to maintain focus and precision under minimal supervision.
- Basic computer skills for data entry.

- Our maternity leave policy supports our working mothers.
- Employee wellness programs and gym memberships.
- Professional development and training opportunities.
