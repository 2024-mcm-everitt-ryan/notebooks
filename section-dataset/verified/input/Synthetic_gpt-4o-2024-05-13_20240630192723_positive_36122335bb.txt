Company: Owen Inc  
Role: Library Technician  

Company Background:  
Owen Inc is a leading organization dedicated to enriching lives through education and information. We strive to create a dynamic and diverse team that can support our mission to foster learning and growth in our communities.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:  
Owen Inc is looking for an enthusiastic and detail-oriented Library Technician to join our team. In this role, you will assist with the daily operations of the library, including cataloging, customer service, and maintenance of the collection. 

Responsibilities:  
- Assist patrons in locating and using library resources.
- Catalog and classify library materials.
- Maintain records of library items.
- Manage circulation desk duties, including checking items in and out.
- Provide support for library programs and events.
- Assist in training and supervising volunteers.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Associate degree in Library Science or related field.
- Previous experience in a library setting is preferred.
- Strong organizational and multitasking skills.
- Excellent customer service and communication skills.
- Proficiency in library management software is an asset.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and continuing education.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from 35,000 USD to 45,000 USD annually, based on your experience and skills.

Special Note:  
Owen Inc is dedicated to fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment. Our goal is to achieve gender parity by hiring more women and boosting female representation in our team through this role. We strongly encourage female candidates to apply.
