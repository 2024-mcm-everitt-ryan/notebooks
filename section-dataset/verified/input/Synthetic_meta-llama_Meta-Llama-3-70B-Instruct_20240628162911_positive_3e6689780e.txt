Company: Valencia, Benton and Gordon

Job Title: Psychologist

About Us:
Valencia, Benton and Gordon is a leading mental health services provider committed to delivering exceptional care to our clients. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and diverse work environment that values the unique perspectives of our team members.

Job Description:
We are seeking a compassionate and experienced Psychologist to join our team. As a Psychologist at Valencia, Benton and Gordon, you will be responsible for assessing, diagnosing, and treating clients with various mental health conditions. You will work collaboratively with our multidisciplinary team to develop personalized treatment plans and provide evidence-based interventions.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct comprehensive psychological assessments and diagnose mental health conditions
 Develop and implement effective treatment plans in collaboration with clients and the multidisciplinary team
 Provide individual and group therapy sessions using evidence-based practices
 Monitor client progress and adjust treatment plans as needed
 Participate in case conferences and collaborate with other healthcare professionals

Qualifications and Experience:

 Doctoral degree in Psychology (Ph.D. or Psy.D.)
 Licensed to practice psychology in the state
 Minimum 2 years of post-licensure experience in a mental health setting
 Strong assessment, diagnosis, and treatment planning skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £50,000 to £70,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
