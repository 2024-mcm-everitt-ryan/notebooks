Job Description:
Burton-Sanchez is seeking a dedicated and skilled Police Surgeon to join our team. The successful candidate will play a crucial role in providing medical care to our police force, ensuring that all officers are fit for duty, and managing health emergencies when they arise.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct medical examinations of police officers.
- Provide emergency medical care when necessary.
- Maintain accurate medical records.
- Participate in health and wellness programs for officers.
- Collaborate with other medical professionals and law enforcement personnel.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Medical degree from an accredited institution.
- Valid medical license.
- Specialization in surgery or emergency medicine is preferred.
- Minimum of 5 years of medical practice experience.
- Ability to work under pressure and make quick decisions.
