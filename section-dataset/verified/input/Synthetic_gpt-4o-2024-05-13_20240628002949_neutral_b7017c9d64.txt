We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Cost Estimating Manager to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing cost estimation processes, ensuring accuracy, and providing valuable insights to support project decisions.

- Mentor and train junior estimators to enhance their skills and knowledge.

- Minimum of 5 years of experience in cost estimating or a similar role.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Proficiency in cost estimating software and tools.
- Excellent communication and leadership abilities.
