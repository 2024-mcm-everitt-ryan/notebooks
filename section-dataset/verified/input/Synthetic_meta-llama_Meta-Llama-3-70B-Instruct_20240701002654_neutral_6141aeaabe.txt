Company: Burns Ltd
Role: Early Intervention Official

About Us:
Burns Ltd is a leading social services organization dedicated to providing support to individuals and families in need. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace that reflects the communities we serve.

Job Description:
We are seeking an Early Intervention Official to join our team. As an Early Intervention Official, you will be responsible for providing support and resources to families with young children, connecting them with community services, and helping them navigate the healthcare system.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct home visits and assessments to identify family needs and develop individualized plans
 Connect families with community resources, services, and support groups
 Collaborate with healthcare providers, social workers, and other stakeholders to ensure comprehensive care
 Develop and maintain accurate records of client interactions and progress

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in social work, early childhood education, or a related field
 2+ years of experience working with young children and families
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:
 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A supportive and inclusive work environment
