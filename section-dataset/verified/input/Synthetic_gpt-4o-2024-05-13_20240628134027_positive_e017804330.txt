Company: Carter Inc  
Role: Investigative Manager  

Company Background:  
Carter Inc is a leading firm in investigative services, known for its commitment to excellence and integrity. Our mission is to uphold the highest standards of transparency and accountability in all our operations.

Job Description:  
Carter Inc is seeking a dedicated and meticulous Investigative Manager to lead and oversee our investigative team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in investigative practices and demonstrate excellent leadership and organizational skills.

Responsibilities:  
- Supervise a team of investigators, ensuring adherence to company protocols and standards.
- Develop and implement investigation strategies and methodologies.
- Conduct high-level investigations and provide detailed reports.
- Coordinate with legal teams and other departments to ensure comprehensive case management.
- Train and mentor junior investigators.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Proven experience in investigative roles, preferably in a managerial capacity.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Ability to work under pressure and manage multiple priorities.
- Relevant certifications or degrees in criminal justice, law enforcement, or related fields are preferable.

Perks and Benefits:  
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 70,000 to USD 90,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.

Our Commitment to Diversity:  
Our team values diversity, and we particularly welcome candidates with disabilities to apply. We believe that a diverse team enhances our ability to serve our clients and community effectively.

Application Process:  
Interested candidates should submit their resumes and cover letters detailing their qualifications and experience to our HR department at hr@carterinc.com. We look forward to welcoming you to our inclusive team!
