Company Background:

Smith-Zuniga is a leading logistics and postal services company dedicated to ensuring timely and secure mail delivery across the country. We are committed to providing excellent service through our team of dedicated professionals.

Job Type: 

Full-time

Job Description:

Smith-Zuniga is seeking a highly motivated individual to join our team as a Postal Service Mail Sorter. The successful candidate will be responsible for sorting and distributing mail efficiently and accurately. This role is crucial in ensuring that our postal services operate smoothly and meet our high standards of customer satisfaction.

Responsibilities:

- Sort and organize incoming and outgoing mail based on established procedures.
- Ensure timely and accurate delivery of mail to designated areas.
- Operate mail sorting machinery and equipment safely and efficiently.
- Collaborate with team members to improve sorting processes and workflows.
- Maintain the confidentiality and security of all mail and related information.

Qualifications and Experience:

- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Prior experience in a mailroom or similar environment is preferred but not required.
- Ability to stand for extended periods and perform repetitive tasks.
- Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
- Self-motivated and able to work independently with minimal supervision.

Perks and Benefits:

- Competitive salary starting from $30,000 to $40,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Inclusive and dynamic work environment.

Smith-Zuniga is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
