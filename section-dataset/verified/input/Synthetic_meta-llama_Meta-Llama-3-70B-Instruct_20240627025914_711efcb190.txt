Company: Bean, Valencia and Johnson

Job Title: NCE - Telecom OSS Consultant

About Us:
Bean, Valencia and Johnson is a leading consulting firm specializing in telecom operations support systems (OSS). We provide innovative solutions to top-tier telecom operators, enabling them to optimize their networks and improve customer experience.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced NCE - Telecom OSS Consultant to join our team. As a Telecom OSS Consultant, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining OSS solutions for our clients. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to identify business requirements, develop technical specifications, and deliver high-quality solutions.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement OSS solutions using industry-leading tools and technologies
- Collaborate with clients to gather business requirements and develop technical specifications
- Develop and maintain technical documentation
- Collaborate with internal teams to ensure solution delivery meets client expectations
- Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field
- Minimum 5 years of experience in telecom OSS consulting
- Strong knowledge of OSS tools and technologies
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
