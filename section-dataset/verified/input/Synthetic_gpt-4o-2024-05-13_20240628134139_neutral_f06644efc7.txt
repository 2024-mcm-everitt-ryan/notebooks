Company: Hoffman, Hamilton and Watson
Role: Radiation Therapist

Company Background:
Hoffman, Hamilton and Watson is a leading healthcare provider specializing in oncology and radiation therapy. We are committed to offering world-class treatment and care to our patients while maintaining an inclusive and supportive environment for all our employees.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and skilled Radiation Therapist to join our team. As a Radiation Therapist, you will be responsible for administering radiation treatments to patients according to the prescribed plan, ensuring patient comfort and safety, and maintaining accurate records.

Responsibilities:
- Administer radiation therapy treatments to patients as prescribed by the oncologist.
- Monitor patients during treatment to ensure their safety and comfort.
- Maintain detailed treatment records and report any issues to the oncologist.
- Collaborate with the oncology team to develop and modify treatment plans.
- Educate patients and their families about the radiation therapy process and any potential side effects.
- Ensure the proper functioning of radiation therapy equipment.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Radiation Therapy or a related field.
- Valid certification and licensure as a Radiation Therapist.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Attention to detail and ability to maintain accurate records.
- Ability to work effectively within a multidisciplinary team.
