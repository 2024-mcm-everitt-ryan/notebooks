Company: Johnson Group

Role: Bookbinder

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Bookbinder to join our team at Johnson Group. As a Bookbinder, you will be responsible for binding books, journals, and other publications using traditional and modern techniques. You will work closely with our printing team to ensure high-quality products that meet our customers' expectations.

Responsibilities:

 Bind books, journals, and other publications using various techniques
 Operate binding machinery and equipment
 Collaborate with the printing team to ensure timely and high-quality production
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace
 Meet productivity and quality standards

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in bookbinding or a related field
 Knowledge of traditional and modern binding techniques
 Ability to operate binding machinery and equipment
 Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 40,000 to USD 60,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan. You will also receive 20 days of paid vacation and 10 paid holidays per year.
