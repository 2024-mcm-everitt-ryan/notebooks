The Opportunity

We are looking for an Operator to join the Higgins Asphalt Team in Palmerston North. This position will involve operating an Asphalt Paver, Skid Steer, and Loader Roller. Ideally, you will have some industry knowledge and experience in operating Asphalt machinery. Our Operators are pro-active, safety-focused, and enjoy working for an organisation where hard work is recognised and where they can build a career and not just do a job. Your role is key in enabling our Teams to deliver on what we do best – roading and infrastructure.

Interested?

If you are looking for your next opportunity, then this is what we think you will need to be successful in this role:

- Full clean New Zealand Driver’s License, ideally Class 4 with WTR but will consider Class 2
- Ideally Asphalt Roller/Paver experience
- Roading and Infrastructure experience
- Strong focus on health and safety not only for yourself, your colleagues but also the public
- Self-motivated with a positive attitude and the ability to think for yourself
- A high level of physical fitness due to the physical nature of the role
- Be reliable, honest, and a flexible team player

In other words, we want someone who is reliable, fit, and healthy who will turn up on time every day with a positive safety-focused attitude!
