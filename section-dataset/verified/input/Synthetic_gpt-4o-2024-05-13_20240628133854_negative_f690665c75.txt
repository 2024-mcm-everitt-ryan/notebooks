Company: Cochran-Allen  
Role: Program Management Office Assistant  

Company Background:  
Cochran-Allen is a leading consultancy firm providing strategic management solutions to a diverse range of clients. Our commitment to excellence and innovative approaches has positioned us at the forefront of the industry.

Job Description:  
We are looking for a dedicated Program Management Office Assistant to join our team. The ideal candidate will provide administrative support to our project managers and ensure smooth operations within the office.

Responsibilities:  
- Assist in the preparation and maintenance of project documentation.  
- Coordinate meetings and events, including setup and takedown.  
- Maintain physical and digital records of project activities.  
- Provide comprehensive support to project managers, ensuring deadlines are met.  
- Handle office equipment and perform minor troubleshooting as needed.  
- Manage inventory and order supplies.  

Qualifications and Experience:  
- Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration or related field.  
- At least 2 years of experience in an administrative role.  
- Requires excellent motor skills and ability to handle office equipment.  
- Strong organizational and multitasking abilities.  
- Effective communication skills, both written and verbal.  
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision.  

Perks and Benefits:  
- Health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £25,000 to £30,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.

How to Apply:  
Interested candidates should submit their resume and cover letter to careers@cochran-allen.com. Please include "Program Management Office Assistant Application" in the subject line.
