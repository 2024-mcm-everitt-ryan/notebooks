Company: Silva-Vaughan

Role: Chief Diversity Officer

Company Background:
Silva-Vaughan is a leading global enterprise committed to fostering innovation and excellence within the corporate sphere. Our mission is to create a vibrant and inclusive environment where every employee can thrive.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
At Silva-Vaughan, we recognize the importance of diversity and inclusion in driving growth and innovation. We are seeking a Chief Diversity Officer (CDO) who will lead our diversity and inclusion initiatives, ensuring they are integrated into all aspects of our business operations. The CDO will work closely with senior leadership to develop and implement strategies that promote a diverse workplace culture.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement comprehensive diversity and inclusion strategies.
- Collaborate with HR to ensure recruitment practices attract a diverse pool of candidates.
- Monitor and report on diversity metrics and outcomes.
- Conduct training programs to educate employees on diversity and inclusion.
- Act as a liaison between diverse employee groups and senior management.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field; Master's degree preferred.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in diversity and inclusion roles.
- Strong understanding of diversity-related policies and best practices.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite is essential.
- Experience in project management is a plus.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from 80,000 USD to 120,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Professional development and training opportunities.
- Flexible working hours and remote working options.
- Employee wellness programs.

Our Commitment:
Silva-Vaughan is dedicated to building a diverse and inclusive workplace. Inclusion and respect are fundamental to our culture. We welcome candidates from all backgrounds to apply and join our mission of fostering an equitable and innovative work environment.
