Company: Wright Ltd

 Collaborate with team members to resolve technical issues

 2+ years of experience in a similar role, preferably from a top-tier university
 Strong technical skills and knowledge of communication systems
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
 A clean criminal record is required for this position
