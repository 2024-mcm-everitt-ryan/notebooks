About Us:

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and detail-oriented Wage Subsidy Processing Clerk to join our team. As a Wage Subsidy Processing Clerk, you will be responsible for processing wage subsidy claims, ensuring accuracy and timeliness, and providing exceptional customer service to our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Process wage subsidy claims in accordance with company policies and procedures
 Verify and validate claimant information and documentation
 Ensure accuracy and timeliness of claim processing
 Provide exceptional customer service to clients via phone, email, and in-person
 Collaborate with internal teams to resolve issues and improve processes

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in a similar role
 Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
 High school diploma or equivalent required; post-secondary education in a related field an asset
