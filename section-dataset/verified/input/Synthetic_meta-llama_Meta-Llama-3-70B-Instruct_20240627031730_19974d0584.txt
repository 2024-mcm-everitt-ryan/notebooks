Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Unix Systems Administrator II to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of our Unix-based systems, as well as providing technical support to our users.

- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless system integration
- Develop and implement system automation scripts to improve efficiency
- Provide technical training and support to users

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
- At least 3 years of experience in Unix system administration
- Strong knowledge of Unix operating systems, including system configuration and troubleshooting
- Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
