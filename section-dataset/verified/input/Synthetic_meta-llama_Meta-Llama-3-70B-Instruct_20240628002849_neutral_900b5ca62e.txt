Company: Stewart, Medina and Santiago

Job Title: Compliance Auditor

About Us:
Stewart, Medina and Santiago is a leading financial services firm committed to maintaining the highest standards of compliance and risk management. We are seeking a highly skilled Compliance Auditor to join our team.

Job Description:
The Compliance Auditor will be responsible for conducting audits to ensure adherence to regulatory requirements, company policies, and industry standards. The ideal candidate will have expertise in audit procedures, risk assessment, and compliance regulations.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct audits to identify areas of non-compliance and develop recommendations for improvement
 Develop and implement audit procedures to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
 Collaborate with internal stakeholders to identify and mitigate risks
 Prepare comprehensive audit reports and present findings to management

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
 3+ years of experience in compliance auditing or a related field
 Professional certification in auditing or compliance (e.g. CPA, CIA, CFA)
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
