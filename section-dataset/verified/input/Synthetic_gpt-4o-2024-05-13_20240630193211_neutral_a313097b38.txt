Company Background:
Garcia PLC is a leading engineering firm specializing in innovative virtual system solutions. We are committed to creating diverse and inclusive work environments that nurture talent and encourage growth.

Job Title: Virtual Systems Engineer

Job Description:
Garcia PLC is seeking a Virtual Systems Engineer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will possess a strong background in virtual systems and cloud computing, with a keen eye for detail and a passion for problem-solving. They will work collaboratively with various departments to design, implement, and maintain virtual systems that meet our clients' needs.

Responsibilities:
- Design, implement, and manage virtual systems and cloud infrastructures.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless integration of systems.
- Monitor system performance and troubleshoot issues to ensure high availability.
- Develop and maintain documentation for system configurations and processes.
- Provide technical support and training to team members.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in virtual systems engineering or related roles.
- Proficient in cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
- Strong understanding of network configurations and security protocols.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

- Flexible working hours and remote work options.
- Professional development opportunities and certifications.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
