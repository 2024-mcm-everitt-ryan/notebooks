Job Title: Butcher
Company: Gray, Petty and Cline

About Us:
Gray, Petty and Cline is a leading meat retailer committed to providing high-quality products to our customers. We pride ourselves on our diverse and inclusive workforce, where every individual has the opportunity to grow and develop their skills.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and experienced Butcher to join our team. As a Butcher, you will be responsible for preparing and processing meat products for our customers. This includes cutting, trimming, and packaging meat to meet our high standards.

Responsibilities:

 Prepare and process meat products for sale
 Maintain a clean and safe working environment
 Provide excellent customer service
 Work effectively as part of a team
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and developments

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Butcher
 Strong knowledge of meat preparation and processing techniques
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. In addition, you will receive a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
