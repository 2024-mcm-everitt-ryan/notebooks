At Robinson and Sons, we're committed to creating a diverse and inclusive team that reflects the communities we serve. As a dietitian, you'll play a critical role in providing personalized nutrition advice to our clients, helping them achieve their health goals.

About Us:
Robinson and Sons is a leading health and wellness company dedicated to delivering exceptional care to our clients. We're passionate about building a team that celebrates individuality and promotes a culture of respect, empathy, and kindness.

Job Description:
As a dietitian, you'll work closely with our multidisciplinary team to develop and implement personalized nutrition plans that cater to our clients' unique needs. Your expertise will help our clients achieve optimal health, and we're looking for someone who is passionate about making a positive impact.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement personalized nutrition plans for clients
 Conduct nutrition assessments and provide counseling sessions
 Collaborate with our multidisciplinary team to ensure comprehensive care
 Stay up-to-date with the latest research and trends in nutrition and dietetics

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Nutrition or related field
 Registered Dietitian (RD) or eligible for registration
 2+ years of experience in clinical dietetics
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
