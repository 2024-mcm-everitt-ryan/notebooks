Company: Weiss, Hamilton and Jenkins

Role: Senior Service Desk Agent

We are committed to creating a workplace where women thrive. As a senior service desk agent, you will be an integral part of our team, providing exceptional technical support to our clients. If you're a talented and motivated individual who shares our passion for diversity and inclusion, we want to hear from you.

Job Description:
The senior service desk agent will be responsible for resolving complex technical issues, collaborating with internal teams, and providing excellent customer service. We are looking for someone who is passionate about technology, has excellent communication skills, and is dedicated to delivering results.

Responsibilities:

 Provide timely and effective technical support to clients via phone, email, and chat
 Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues
 Collaborate with internal teams to resolve incidents and implement solutions
 Develop and maintain technical knowledge and expertise
 Provide excellent customer service and ensure customer satisfaction

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in a technical support role
 Strong technical knowledge of computer systems, software, and networks
 Excellent communication, problem-solving, and analytical skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize tasks effectively
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $65,000 to $85,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. In addition, you will receive a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.

At Weiss, Hamilton and Jenkins, we are dedicated to increasing the number of women in our workforce and providing a supportive and inclusive work environment. We believe that diverse perspectives and experiences are essential to our success, and we encourage talented women to apply for this role.
