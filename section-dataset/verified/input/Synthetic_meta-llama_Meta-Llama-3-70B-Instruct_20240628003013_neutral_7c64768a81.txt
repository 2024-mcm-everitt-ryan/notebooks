Process Server at Patel, Weeks and Bowen

About Us:
Patel, Weeks and Bowen is a reputable legal services firm committed to delivering exceptional process serving solutions to our clients. We are seeking a reliable and detail-oriented Process Server to join our team.

Job Description:
As a Process Server, you will be responsible for serving legal documents to individuals and businesses in a timely and professional manner. Your duties will include:

 Serving legal documents, such as subpoenas, summons, and writs
 Maintaining accurate records of service attempts and results
 Communicating with clients and internal teams to provide updates on service status
 Collaborating with other process servers to ensure efficient service

Requirements:
 Open to all levels of experience; industry-specific knowledge required
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Strong communication and organizational skills
 Reliable transportation and valid driver's license
 Ability to meet job criteria and pass a background check

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
