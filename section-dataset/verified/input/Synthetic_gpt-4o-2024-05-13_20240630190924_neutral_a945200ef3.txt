Company: Gonzalez, Washington and Shea
Role: Case Worker

Company Background:
Gonzalez, Washington and Shea is a leading law firm with a strong commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional legal services and advocating for the rights of our clients. 

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and empathetic Case Worker to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for managing and supporting a diverse client base, ensuring their needs are met with the highest level of professionalism and care.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct client interviews to assess their needs and develop individualized support plans.
- Coordinate with external agencies and resources to provide comprehensive support to clients.
- Maintain accurate and up-to-date case files and documentation.
- Advocate on behalf of clients in various settings, including court appearances and meetings.
- Collaborate with legal teams to ensure seamless service delivery.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- A bachelor's degree in Social Work, Psychology, or a related field.
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in a case management role.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work collaboratively with a diverse team.
- Strong organizational and time management skills.
- Commitment to confidentiality and ethical practice.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from USD 45,000 to USD 60,000, depending on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.

Our Commitment to Inclusion:
At Gonzalez, Washington and Shea, we believe that diversity enhances our workplace and drives our success. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where all employees feel valued and respected.
