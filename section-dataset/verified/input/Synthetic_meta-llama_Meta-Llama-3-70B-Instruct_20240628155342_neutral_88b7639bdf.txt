Company: Simpson, Rice and Bailey

Job Title: IT Project Manager

About Us:
Simpson, Rice and Bailey is a leading technology consulting firm dedicated to delivering innovative solutions to our clients. We promote a culture of inclusion and accessibility, and we support a work environment that accommodates all.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced IT Project Manager to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for leading IT projects from initiation to delivery, ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and manage project plans, resource allocation, and schedules
 Coordinate and lead project teams to achieve project goals and objectives
 Identify and mitigate project risks, and develop contingency plans
 Ensure effective communication and stakeholder management
 Monitor and report on project progress, budgets, and quality

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in IT project management
 Proven track record of delivering IT projects on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards
 Strong knowledge of project management methodologies, tools, and techniques
 Excellent leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $120,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
