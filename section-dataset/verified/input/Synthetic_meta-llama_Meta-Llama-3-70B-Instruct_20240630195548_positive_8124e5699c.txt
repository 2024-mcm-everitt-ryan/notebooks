Company: Adams, Smith and Booker

Job Title: Industrial Engineer

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Industrial Engineer to join our team. As a key member of our operations team, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining efficient systems and processes to increase productivity and reduce costs.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze and optimize production workflows to improve efficiency and reduce waste
 Design and implement new systems and processes to improve productivity and quality
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify areas for improvement and implement solutions
 Develop and maintain reports and dashboards to track key performance indicators

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering or related field
 3+ years of experience in industrial engineering or related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills
