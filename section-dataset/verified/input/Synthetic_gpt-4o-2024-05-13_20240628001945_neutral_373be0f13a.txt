Company: Knox-Mcdaniel

Role: Material Moving Worker

Company Background:
Knox-Mcdaniel is a leading logistics and transportation company committed to excellence and innovation. We pride ourselves on creating an inclusive and equitable workplace where everyone feels valued.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and energetic Material Moving Worker to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for the efficient movement of materials and products within our warehouses and distribution centers.

Responsibilities:
- Load and unload materials onto or from pallets, skids, conveyors, and trucks.
- Move materials to and from storage or production areas, loading docks, and delivery vehicles.
- Operate industrial trucks, forklifts, or tractors to transport materials.
- Ensure proper and safe handling of materials to prevent damage.
- Maintain a clean and organized work environment.
- Follow all safety protocols and procedures.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Previous experience in material handling or warehouse operations preferred.
- Ability to operate forklift and other material moving equipment.
- Strong physical stamina and the ability to lift heavy objects.
- Excellent attention to detail and organizational skills.
