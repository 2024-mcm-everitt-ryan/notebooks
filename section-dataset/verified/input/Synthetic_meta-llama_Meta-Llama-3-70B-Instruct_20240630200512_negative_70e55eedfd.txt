Job Title: Program Associate
Company: Singh LLC

About Us:
Singh LLC is a dynamic organization dedicated to empowering individuals and communities. We strive to create a workplace culture that values diversity, equity, and inclusion.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and empathetic Program Associate to join our team. As a Program Associate, you will be responsible for providing administrative support to our program managers, coordinating events, and developing strong relationships with our stakeholders.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to program managers, including preparing reports, scheduling meetings, and maintaining records
 Coordinate logistics for events, workshops, and conferences
 Develop and maintain relationships with stakeholders, including community partners and vendors
 Assist in data collection, analysis, and reporting to inform program decisions

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in an administrative role, preferably in a non-profit or community organization
 Strong organizational and communication skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, particularly Excel and Word

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary range of $45,000 - $55,000 USD per year, based on experience and skills. Our benefits package includes comprehensive health insurance, generous paid time off, and opportunities for professional development.
