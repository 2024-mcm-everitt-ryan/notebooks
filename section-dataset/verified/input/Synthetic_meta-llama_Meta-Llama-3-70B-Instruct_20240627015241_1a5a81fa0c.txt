Company: Bennett Ltd

Bennett Ltd is a leading data analysis firm dedicated to providing actionable insights to businesses. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment that values collaboration and innovation.

Job Description:
We are seeking a detail-oriented Technical Metrics Coordinator to join our team. - Collaborate with stakeholders to identify business needs and develop metrics to support those needs
- Analyze and interpret data to identify trends and opportunities for growth
- Communicate insights and recommendations to stakeholders through clear and concise reports

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or a related field
- 2+ years of experience in data analysis or a related field
- Proficiency in SQL and data visualization tools
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
- Strong communication and collaboration skills
