Company: Hicks-Hernandez
Role: Senior Program Officer

We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Program Officer to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing and implementing programs that align with our organization's mission and goals.

Job Description:

The Senior Program Officer will be responsible for managing multiple projects simultaneously, working closely with cross-functional teams, and communicating effectively with stakeholders. This is a fast-paced role that requires strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to prioritize tasks efficiently.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement program strategies to achieve organizational goals
 Manage project budgets and timelines to ensure successful delivery
 Collaborate with internal teams to ensure program alignment and cohesion
 Communicate program updates and results to stakeholders
 Travel frequently to attend conferences, meetings, and program events (must be able to travel without restrictions)

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in program management or a related field
 Bachelor's degree in a relevant field
 Excellent communication, organizational, and project management skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and project management software

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
