Company: Garcia-Matthews

Job Title: Service Station Attendant

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Garcia-Matthews is seeking a highly motivated and customer-focused Service Station Attendant to join our team. As a Service Station Attendant, you will be responsible for providing exceptional customer service, performing routine maintenance tasks, and ensuring a clean and safe environment for our customers.

Responsibilities:

 Greet customers and provide assistance with fueling and other needs
 Perform routine maintenance tasks, such as cleaning and restocking facilities
 Ensure a clean and safe environment for customers and employees
 Monitor and report any issues or concerns to management
 Collaborate with other team members to achieve sales and customer satisfaction goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of customer service experience preferred
 Ability to lift up to 25 pounds and stand for long periods
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 35,000 to USD 45,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
