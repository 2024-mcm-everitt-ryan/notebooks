Company Background:
Diaz PLC is a leading real estate firm specializing in commercial properties. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and innovative solutions to our clients.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Diaz PLC is seeking a motivated and experienced Real Estate Manager to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for managing our portfolio of commercial properties and ensuring client satisfaction.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee and manage day-to-day operations of commercial properties.
- Develop and implement property management strategies to maximize value.
- Lead a team of professionals to ensure high standards of property maintenance and client service.
- Conduct regular property inspections and coordinate maintenance activities.
- Negotiate lease agreements and handle tenant relations.
- Prepare and manage budgets and financial reports.
- Ensure compliance with property management laws and regulations.
- Act as the primary point of contact for property owners and tenants.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in real estate, business administration, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in real estate management.
- Strong leadership and decision-making abilities.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Proficiency in property management software.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Strong organizational and multitasking skills.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 70,000 to USD 100,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Employee wellness programs and support.
