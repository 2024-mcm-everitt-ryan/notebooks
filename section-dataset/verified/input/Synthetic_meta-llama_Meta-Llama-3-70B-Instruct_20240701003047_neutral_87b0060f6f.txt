Company: Fernandez, Blanchard and Frazier

Role: Homeowner Mortgage Service Manager

Job Description:
Fernandez, Blanchard and Frazier is seeking a skilled and experienced Homeowner Mortgage Service Manager to join our team. As a Homeowner Mortgage Service Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing the daily operations of our mortgage servicing department, ensuring timely and accurate processing of mortgage payments, and providing exceptional customer service to our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Manage a team of mortgage servicing representatives to ensure efficient and effective processing of mortgage payments
 Develop and implement process improvements to increase productivity and reduce errors
 Collaborate with internal departments to resolve customer complaints and issues
 Analyze and report on key performance metrics to drive business decisions
 Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in mortgage servicing or a related field
 2+ years of management or supervisory experience
 Strong leadership and communication skills
 Proficient in mortgage servicing software and Microsoft Office
 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Finance, or a related field
