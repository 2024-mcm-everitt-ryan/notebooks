Company: Hoover LLC
Role: Industrial Production Manager

Job Description:
We are seeking a results-driven Industrial Production Manager to oversee our manufacturing operations. The successful candidate will be a natural leader, with a strong track record of driving efficiency and productivity. He will be responsible for managing a team of production staff, implementing process improvements, and ensuring compliance with safety regulations.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and motivate a team of production staff to meet performance targets
 Analyze production data to identify areas for improvement and implement changes
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to optimize production processes
 Ensure compliance with safety regulations and maintain a safe working environment
 Develop and implement strategies to increase productivity and reduce waste

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in industrial production management
 Proven track record of driving efficiency and productivity
 Strong leadership and communication skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and make quick decisions
 Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering, Operations Management, or related field

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $120,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you will be eligible for a performance-based bonus, comprehensive health insurance, and a retirement matching program.
