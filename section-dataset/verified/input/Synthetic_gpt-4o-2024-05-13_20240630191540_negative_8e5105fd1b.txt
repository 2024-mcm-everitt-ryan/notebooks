Company Background:
Garcia-Castillo is a dynamic community services organization dedicated to fostering inclusivity and engagement within diverse neighborhoods. We are committed to creating meaningful connections and opportunities for community development.

Job Title: Community Coordinator

Job Description:
Garcia-Castillo is seeking a passionate and organized Community Coordinator to join our team. She will collaborate with other team members to achieve goals, focusing on promoting community activities and fostering positive relationships within our community. Her nurturing skills are essential for maintaining a positive environment.

Responsibilities:
- She will be responsible for planning and executing community events.
- Collaborate with local organizations and volunteers to enhance community programs.
- Manage community outreach initiatives and communications.
- Ensure that all community activities are inclusive and accessible.
- Maintain accurate records of community participation and feedback.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Community Development, Social Work, or a related field.
- At least 2 years of experience in community coordination or a similar role.
- She must possess strong organizational and communication skills.
- Demonstrated ability to work with diverse populations.
- Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health insurance plan.
- Paid time off and flexible working hours.
- Professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 45,000 to USD 55,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Employee wellness programs.
