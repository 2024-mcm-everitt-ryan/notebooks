Company: Taylor, Carter and Rice

Role: Senior Director

Qualifications and Experience:

 8+ years of experience in management or leadership roles
 Proven track record of driving business growth and leading high-performing teams
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment
