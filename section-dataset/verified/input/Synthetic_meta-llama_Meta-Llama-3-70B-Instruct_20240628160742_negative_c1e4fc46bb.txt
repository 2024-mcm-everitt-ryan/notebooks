Company Background:
Perry Ltd is a leading forestry management company dedicated to sustainable forest practices. We specialize in timber harvesting, reforestation, and forest conservation.

Job Type:
Forester

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Forester to join our team. As a Forester, you will be responsible for planning, coordinating, and implementing forest management activities. This includes conducting forest inventories, developing forest management plans, and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct forest inventories to gather data on tree species, age, and quality
 Develop and implement forest management plans to achieve sustainable forest practices
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure effective forest management
 Analyze data to identify trends and opportunities for improvement
 Ensure compliance with environmental regulations and industry standards

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Forestry, Environmental Science, or related field
 At least 5 years of experience in forestry management
 Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities
 Must be able to perform repetitive motions, such as data collection and analysis
 Requires excellent facial recognition skills to identify tree species
 Must be able to work in noisy environments, such as forest machinery operations

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan. We also provide opportunities for professional development and training to advance your career in forestry management.
