Company: Williamson, Gonzalez and Gomez
Role: Recruiting & Onboarding Coordinator

About Us:
Williamson, Gonzalez and Gomez is a dynamic and inclusive organization dedicated to building diverse teams. We believe that our differences are what make us stronger and drive innovation.

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented Recruiting & Onboarding Coordinator to join our HR team. As a Recruiting & Onboarding Coordinator, you will play a critical role in ensuring a smooth and welcoming experience for our new hires. You will be responsible for coordinating the recruitment process, from scheduling interviews to extending job offers. Additionally, you will develop and implement effective onboarding strategies to ensure our new employees feel supported and prepared to succeed in their roles.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate the recruitment process, including scheduling interviews and communicating with candidates
 Develop and implement effective onboarding strategies to ensure new employees feel supported and prepared to succeed in their roles
 Collaborate with hiring managers to identify recruitment needs and develop job postings
 Ensure compliance with all relevant employment laws and regulations
 Maintain accurate records and reports related to recruitment and onboarding

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience in recruitment or human resources
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize multiple tasks
 Strong attention to detail and analytical skills
 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan with a company match. You will also have access to professional development opportunities and a dynamic, inclusive work environment.
