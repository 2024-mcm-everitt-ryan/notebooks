Company Background:
Thomas Group, a leader in beauty and wellness services, is dedicated to providing high-quality treatments and products to our valued clients. Our team is passionate about enhancing the natural beauty of every individual.

Job Type:
Full-time Cosmetologist

Job Description:
We are looking for a skilled and talented Cosmetologist to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a creative flair, excellent customer service skills, and a commitment to upholding the values of Thomas Group.

Responsibilities:
- Provide a wide range of cosmetology services including haircuts, coloring, styling, and treatments.
- Conduct skin care treatments such as facials, waxing, and makeup applications.
- Stay updated with the latest beauty trends to offer innovative services.
- Maintain a clean and organized workspace.
- Build and maintain strong relationships with clients to ensure repeat business.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Accredited cosmetology certification.
- At least 2 years of professional experience in a salon setting.
- Strong portfolio showcasing your skills and creativity (please submit his/her portfolio with the application).
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work flexible hours including weekends.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 40,000 to USD 60,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Employee discounts on products and services.
- Supportive and collaborative work environment that values conventional social roles.

Application Process:
Please submit your resume along with his/her portfolio and a cover letter. We respect everyone's lifestyle choice but prefer candidates who respect conventional social structures.
