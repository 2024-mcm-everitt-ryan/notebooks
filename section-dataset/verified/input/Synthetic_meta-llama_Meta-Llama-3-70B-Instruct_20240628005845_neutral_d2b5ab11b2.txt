Company: Carter, Hall and Reese
Role: Tour Guide

About Us:
Carter, Hall and Reese is a leading tour operator dedicated to providing exceptional travel experiences. We are committed to fostering an environment where everyone feels valued, and our team is made up of individuals from a variety of backgrounds.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and enthusiastic Tour Guide to join our team. As a Tour Guide, you will be responsible for leading groups of travelers on guided tours, providing informative and engaging commentary, and ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience for all participants.

Responsibilities:

 Lead groups of travelers on guided tours, providing informative and engaging commentary on historical sites, landmarks, and cultural attractions
 Ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all participants, adhering to safety protocols and company guidelines
 Develop and maintain a thorough knowledge of tour routes, attractions, and local customs
 Collaborate with other team members to ensure seamless tour operations
 Provide exceptional customer service, responding to participant inquiries and resolving any issues that may arise

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Tour Guide or in a related customer-facing role
 Strong knowledge of history, culture, and attractions in the tour region
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Valid certification in first aid and CPR (or willingness to obtain)

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per annum, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional development and growth within the company
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits package
 Discounts on company tours and travel opportunities
