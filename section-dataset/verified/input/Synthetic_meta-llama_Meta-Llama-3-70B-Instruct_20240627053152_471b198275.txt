Company: Blevins and Sons

Job Title: Senior Staff ASIC Engineer

About Us:
Blevins and Sons is a leading semiconductor company dedicated to designing and manufacturing high-performance ASICs for various applications. We are committed to creating an inclusive work environment that values diversity and equal opportunities for all candidates.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Staff ASIC Engineer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in digital ASIC design, verification, and implementation. The successful candidate will be responsible for leading the design and development of complex ASICs, working closely with cross-functional teams, and ensuring timely delivery of high-quality products.

Responsibilities:
- Lead the design, development, and verification of complex ASICs
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure successful project execution
- Develop and maintain technical documentation
- Mentor junior engineers and provide guidance on best practices
- Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Electrical Engineering or Computer Science
- Minimum 8 years of experience in digital ASIC design and development
- Strong knowledge of digital design principles, ASIC design flows, and verification methodologies
- Excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
