Company Background:
Griffith Inc is a leading provider of industrial solutions, dedicated to promoting safety and sustainability in the workplace. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment that supports the well-being and professional growth of all employees.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Griffith Inc is seeking an Environmental Health & Safety Regional Manager to oversee and enhance our safety protocols across multiple sites. This role is pivotal in ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and fostering a culture of safety.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement EHS programs and policies across regional sites.
- Conduct regular safety audits and risk assessments.
- Lead training sessions to educate employees on safety practices.
- Collaborate with local and federal agencies to ensure regulatory compliance.
- Manage incident investigations and follow-up actions to prevent recurrence.
- Prepare and present safety reports to senior management.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Occupational Health, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years' experience in an EHS role, preferably in a regional or managerial capacity.
- Strong knowledge of local and federal EHS regulations.
- Excellent communication and leadership skills.
- Ability to work collaboratively in a diverse team environment.
