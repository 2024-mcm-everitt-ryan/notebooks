Job Title:
Marine Electronics Technician

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented Marine Electronics Technician to join our team. This role involves the installation, maintenance, and repair of marine electronic systems on various types of vessels. The ideal candidate will be a strategic thinker with strong analytical skills and the ability to work independently.

Responsibilities:
- Install, maintain, and repair marine electronic equipment, such as navigation and communication systems.
- Diagnose and troubleshoot technical issues with marine electronics.
- Conduct routine inspections and tests to ensure the functionality and safety of equipment.
- Collaborate with other team members to complete projects efficiently.
- Maintain accurate records of work performed and parts used.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience in marine electronics or a related field.
- Strong analytical skills and technical proficiency.
- Ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously.
- Excellent problem-solving abilities.
- Strong communication skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Relevant certifications or training in marine electronics is preferred.
