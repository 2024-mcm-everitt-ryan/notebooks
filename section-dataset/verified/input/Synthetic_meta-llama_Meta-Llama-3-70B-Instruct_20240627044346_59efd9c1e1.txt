Director, Consumer and Customer Strategy and Insights - Pet

Shaw, Scott and Bryant is a leading pet food manufacturer dedicated to providing high-quality products that enrich the lives of pets and their owners. We are proud of our diverse and talented workforce, and we believe that diverse perspectives and backgrounds are valued in driving business growth.

Job Description:

- Conduct market research and analysis to identify trends, opportunities, and challenges
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and execute customer-centric strategies
- Analyze and interpret data to drive business recommendations
- Communicate insights and recommendations to senior leadership and stakeholders

Qualifications:

- 8+ years of experience in consumer research, customer insights, or a related field
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
- Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
- Proficiency in Spanish or Portuguese is an asset
