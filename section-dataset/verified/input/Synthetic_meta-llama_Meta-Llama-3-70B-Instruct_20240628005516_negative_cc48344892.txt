Job Title: Dietitian
Company: Smith-Acosta

About Us:
Smith-Acosta is a leading healthcare organization dedicated to providing top-notch nutrition services to our clients. We are seeking a talented Dietitian to join our team of healthcare professionals.

Job Description:
As a Dietitian at Smith-Acosta, you will be responsible for developing personalized nutrition plans for our clients, conducting nutrition assessments, and providing education on healthy eating habits. You will work closely with our team of healthcare professionals to ensure comprehensive care for our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement personalized nutrition plans for clients
 Conduct nutrition assessments and monitor client progress
 Provide education on healthy eating habits and nutrition principles
 Collaborate with healthcare professionals to ensure comprehensive care
 Participate in company events and team-building activities

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Nutrition or related field
 Registered Dietitian certification
 2+ years of experience in a similar role
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on experience and skills. Additionally, we provide a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.

Note: We are an equal opportunities employer, but our company culture is built around traditional family values. We encourage our team members to participate in family-friendly company events and activities. We believe in fostering a work environment that supports traditional family structures.
