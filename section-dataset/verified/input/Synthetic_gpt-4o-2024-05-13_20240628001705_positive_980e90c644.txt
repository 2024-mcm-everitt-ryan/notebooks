Company Background:
Jackson-Guzman is a forward-thinking consulting firm dedicated to innovation and excellence. We pride ourselves on creating an inclusive environment that celebrates diversity and empowers individuals to reach their full potential.

Role: Program Manager

Job Description:
We seek a dynamic and experienced Program Manager to join our diverse team. The ideal candidate will manage multiple projects, ensuring they are completed on time and within budget. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to align project goals with organizational objectives.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement project plans.
- Coordinate internal resources and third parties/vendors for flawless execution.
- Ensure that all projects are delivered on time, within scope, and within budget.
- Manage relationships with clients and stakeholders.
- Perform risk management to minimize project risks.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Project Management, or related field.
- Proven working experience as a project manager.
- Strong familiarity with project management software tools, methodologies, and best practices.
- Excellent client-facing and internal communication skills.
- Solid organizational skills, including attention to detail and multitasking.

- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and company holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.
