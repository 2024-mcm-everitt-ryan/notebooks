Company: Davenport-Harrison  
Role: Boat Captain  

Company Background:  
Davenport-Harrison is a leader in maritime transportation, known for its commitment to sustainability and innovation. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive workplace where diversity and creativity are celebrated.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:  
We are seeking a skilled and experienced Boat Captain to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in maritime navigation and safety procedures. This role requires a high level of responsibility and the ability to manage diverse crew members.

Responsibilities:  
- Navigate and operate boats safely and efficiently.
- Lead and manage crew members, ensuring all maritime safety protocols are followed.
- Conduct regular maintenance checks on the vessel.
- Communicate effectively with the crew and other stakeholders.
- Ensure compliance with all maritime laws and regulations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Valid captain's license and certifications.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in maritime navigation.
- Strong leadership and communication skills.
- Ability to lead cross-functional teams.
- Proficiency in using navigation and communication equipment.
