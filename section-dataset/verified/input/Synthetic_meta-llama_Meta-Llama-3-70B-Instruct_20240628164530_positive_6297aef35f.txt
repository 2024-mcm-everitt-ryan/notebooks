Company: King, Wilkinson and Hernandez

Job Title: Reinforcing Iron Worker

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Reinforcing Iron Worker to join our team. As a Reinforcing Iron Worker, you will be responsible for installing and repairing structures and equipment made of iron and steel. If you have a strong work ethic and a willingness to learn, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Read and interpret blueprints, diagrams, and specifications to determine the layout of iron and steel structures
 Cut, shape, and assemble iron and steel materials using hand tools and machinery
 Install and repair iron and steel structures, such as bridges, buildings, and machinery
 Collaborate with other construction workers to ensure projects are completed efficiently and safely

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Reinforcing Iron Worker or similar role
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work at heights
 Strong communication and teamwork skills
 We are committed to a diverse workplace and actively seek individuals with disabilities for our team.

Benefits and Perks:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $65,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Opportunities for professional development and advancement
 A dynamic and inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities
