Company Background:
Smith-Mcneil is a leading retailer dedicated to providing exceptional customer experiences. We pride ourselves on our strong company culture and commitment to excellence.

Job Type:
Retail Specialist

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Retail Specialist to join our team. As a Retail Specialist, you will be responsible for providing exceptional customer service, maintaining store displays, and working effectively as part of a team.

Responsibilities:

 Provide exceptional customer service and ensure customer satisfaction
 Maintain store displays and ensure a clean and organized store environment
 Work effectively as part of a team to achieve sales goals and objectives
 Process transactions accurately and efficiently
 Stay up-to-date on product knowledge and promotions

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of retail experience in a similar role
 High school diploma or equivalent required; bachelor's degree from a reputable institution preferred
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing situations
 Reliable transportation and a flexible schedule are required

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
