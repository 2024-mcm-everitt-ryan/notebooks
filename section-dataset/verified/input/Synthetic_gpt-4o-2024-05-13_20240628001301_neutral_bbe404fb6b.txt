Company Background:
Dodson-Luna is a renowned organization committed to the welfare and training of animals. With over 20 years of experience, we pride ourselves on providing a supportive and inclusive environment for our staff and the animals in our care.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
Dodson-Luna is seeking knowledgeable and passionate professionals for the role of Animal Trainer. The ideal candidate will have a strong professional background and a deep commitment to animal welfare. We value experience and expertise in training various species, and candidates with a robust skill set are encouraged to apply.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement training programs for a variety of animals.
- Monitor and evaluate animal behavior to ensure effective training.
- Collaborate with veterinary staff to ensure the health and well-being of the animals.
- Educate the public on proper animal care and training techniques.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience as an Animal Trainer or in a related field.
- Knowledgeable in animal behavior and training methods.
- Strong communication skills and the ability to work in a team.
- Passionate about animal welfare and dedicated to continuous learning.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 40,000 to USD 60,000, based on your experience and skills.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are invited to submit their resume and cover letter outlining their experience and why they are a good fit for this role at Dodson-Luna.
