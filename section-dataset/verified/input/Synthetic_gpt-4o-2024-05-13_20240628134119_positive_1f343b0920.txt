Company Background:

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
As an Engineering Technician at Guerrero Inc, you will support our engineering team in the development, testing, and maintenance of our cutting-edge products. This role is integral to our commitment to innovation and excellence.

Responsibilities:
- Assist engineers in troubleshooting and resolving technical issues.
- Perform routine maintenance on engineering tools and equipment.
- Document test results and maintain accurate records.
- Participate in the design and development of engineering projects.
- Ensure compliance with industry standards and safety regulations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Associates degree in Engineering Technology or a related field.
- At least 2 years of experience in an engineering technician role.
- Strong technical aptitude and problem-solving skills.
- Proficiency with engineering software and tools.
- Excellent communication and documentation skills.

- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Continuous training and professional development opportunities.
- Paid time off and flexible work schedules.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from 50,000 EUR to 70,000 EUR, based on your experience and skills.
