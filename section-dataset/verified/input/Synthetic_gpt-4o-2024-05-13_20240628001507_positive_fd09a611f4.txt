Company: Moreno, Cook, and Buckley

Role: Vision Screening Assistant

Company Background:
Moreno, Cook, and Buckley is a forward-thinking healthcare services firm dedicated to providing top-notch vision care to our community. We pride ourselves on our inclusive, customer-focused approach and are committed to fostering a diverse and equitable workplace. Job Type: Full-time

Job Description and Responsibilities:
We are seeking a Vision Screening Assistant to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for conducting preliminary eye examinations, assisting with patient records, and supporting our optometrists in providing comprehensive vision care. 

Responsibilities:
- Conduct vision screenings and record results.
- Assist with patient intake and data entry.
- Prepare and maintain examination rooms and equipment.
- Provide excellent customer service and patient care.
- Support optometrists during patient examinations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent; an associate degree in a relevant field is preferred.
- Experience in a healthcare setting is a plus.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Basic computer proficiency.
- Ability to work well in a team-oriented environment.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £30,000 to £40,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, vision, and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday benefits.
- Professional development opportunities.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.

We aim to create a diverse team and are actively seeking professionals from the LGBTQ+ community. We are dedicated to increasing the presence of LGBTQ+ individuals in our workforce.
