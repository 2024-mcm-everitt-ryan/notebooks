Company Background:
Dominguez PLC is a leading infrastructure company dedicated to maintaining and enhancing transportation networks. We believe diversity makes us stronger and more innovative.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Highway Maintenance Worker to join our team. The candidate will be responsible for maintaining highways, ensuring safety standards, and addressing any issues that arise. This position requires the ability to adapt to change and handle various tasks efficiently.

Responsibilities:
- Perform routine maintenance and repairs on highways.
- Inspect road conditions and report any issues.
- Operate and maintain equipment and tools.
- Follow safety protocols and guidelines.
- Assist in emergency response and road closures.
- Collaborate with team members to ensure timely project completion.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Previous experience in highway maintenance or construction is a plus.
- Ability to operate heavy machinery.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication skills to work effectively within a team.
- Valid driver's license.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $35,000 to $50,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Ongoing training and professional development opportunities.
