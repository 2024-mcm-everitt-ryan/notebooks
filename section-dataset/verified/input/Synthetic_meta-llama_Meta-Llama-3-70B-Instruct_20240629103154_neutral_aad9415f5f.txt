Company: Powell Group
Role: Incident Commander

Job Description:
The Powell Group is seeking an experienced and skilled Incident Commander to join our team. As an Incident Commander, you will be responsible for leading and coordinating response efforts to emergency situations, ensuring the safety of our employees and the public.

Responsibilities:

 Lead and direct incident response teams to ensure effective and efficient response to emergency situations
 Develop and implement incident response plans and strategies to minimize risks and ensure business continuity
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and mitigate potential risks and hazards
 Provide guidance and support to team members to ensure compliance with safety protocols and procedures
 Conduct post-incident reviews and debriefs to identify areas for improvement and implement changes as necessary

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in incident management, emergency response, or a related field
 Proven track record of leading and coordinating response efforts to emergency situations
 Strong knowledge of incident management principles, practices, and procedures
 Excellent leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 80,000 to USD 120,000, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you will be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, which includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement savings plan, and paid time off.
