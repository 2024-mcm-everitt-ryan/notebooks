Company: Horn-Osborne

Role: Farm Ranch and Other Agricultural Manager

Company Background:
Horn-Osborne is a leading agricultural company committed to sustainable farming practices and innovative agricultural solutions. We manage a range of farms and ranches, producing high-quality crops and livestock for markets worldwide.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced and highly adaptable Farm Ranch and Other Agricultural Manager to join our team. The ideal candidate will oversee daily operations, manage farm staff, and ensure the highest standards of productivity and quality.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee and manage day-to-day farm operations.
- Coordinate planting, irrigation, and harvesting activities.
- Ensure compliance with agricultural regulations and safety standards.
- Monitor crop and livestock health, making necessary adjustments to care routines.
- Maintain and repair farm equipment.
- Prepare and manage budgets, ensuring cost-efficiency.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Agriculture, Agronomy, or related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in farm management.
- Requires the ability to carry heavy equipment across various office locations.
- Must be able to run or jog as needed.
- Must have unrestricted physical mobility.
- Must be able to reach overhead and perform tasks at various heights.
- Must be able to quickly adapt to new and changing situations.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary: We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Retirement plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.

Horn-Osborne is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage applications from all qualified candidates.
