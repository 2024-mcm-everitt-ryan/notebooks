Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and organized Fiscal Coordinator to join our team. As a Fiscal Coordinator, you will be responsible for managing and coordinating financial operations, ensuring accuracy and efficiency in financial reporting, and providing support to our finance team.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and coordinate financial operations, including budgeting, forecasting, and financial reporting
 Ensure accuracy and efficiency in financial reporting, including preparing and reviewing financial statements
 Provide support to the finance team, including preparing and processing financial transactions
 Analyze financial data and identify areas for improvement
 Develop and implement financial processes and procedures to increase efficiency and reduce costs

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
 2+ years of experience in a financial coordination role
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Proficiency in financial software and systems
