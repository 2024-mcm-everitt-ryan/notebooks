Company: Collins-Porter

Role: Administrative Engineer

Company Background:
Collins-Porter is a global leader in engineering solutions, providing innovative services across multiple sectors. We pride ourselves on fostering a collaborative and inclusive workplace, valuing the expertise and unique perspectives of our team members.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a diligent and experienced Administrative Engineer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in engineering and administrative duties, ensuring seamless operations within our engineering department.

Responsibilities:
- Coordinate engineering projects and ensure timely completion of tasks.
- Maintain accurate records and documentation of engineering activities.
- Liaise with other departments to streamline processes and enhance productivity.
- Provide administrative support to engineering managers and team members.
- Prepare reports and presentations as required.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Engineering or related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in an administrative or engineering role.
- Strong organizational and multitasking skills.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and engineering software.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.

Perks and Benefits:

- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Professional development opportunities.
