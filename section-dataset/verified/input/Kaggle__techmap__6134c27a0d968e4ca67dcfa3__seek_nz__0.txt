About Grace

We are the only private hospital in Tauranga, with seven theatres, an endoscopy suite, and expanded services on the way. We offer a variety of surgical procedures to the wider Bay of Plenty community. With modern facilities, high utilisation rates, a quality service, supportive management, and a collegial team, this is a dynamic, fast-paced environment where your nursing skills and scope can develop and your career will thrive.

Grace Hospital is looking for an experienced registered nurse to join our dynamic post-operative elective surgery team. The applicant would need to be able to work in a fast-paced environment, demonstrate problem-solving ability, have high-level attention to detail, and be a good communicator. The successful applicant will have a minimum of 2 years' experience in a NZ surgical hospital setting.

This position is for a minimum of 16 hours per week (0.4 FTE) working rostered shifts, with the potential to increase to full-time if desired. Grace provides a 24/7 service with hours to suit the daily requirements of the hospital.

Please apply including your CV and covering letter via SEEK. Applications close Friday, 1st October 2021.
