Company Background:
Baker PLC is a leading global provider of innovative solutions in the manufacturing sector. With a commitment to quality and excellence, we strive to deliver top-tier products that exceed customer expectations.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Baker PLC is seeking a Deputy Chief of Quality Assurance to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing quality assurance processes, ensuring compliance with industry standards, and managing a team of quality assurance professionals. They will be responsible for managing team projects, developing quality assurance strategies, and implementing process improvements.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee the quality assurance department and manage team projects.
- Develop and implement quality assurance strategies to ensure product compliance with industry standards.
- Conduct regular audits and inspections to identify areas for improvement.
- Collaborate with other departments to ensure seamless integration of quality assurance processes.
- Monitor and report on quality metrics and performance indicators.
- Provide training and support to team members to enhance their skills and knowledge.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in engineering, quality management, or a related field.
- A minimum of 7 years of experience in quality assurance, with at least 3 years in a leadership role.
- Strong knowledge of industry standards and quality assurance methodologies.
- Excellent communication and leadership skills.
- Ability to manage multiple projects and work effectively under pressure.
