Company: Reynolds, Perkins and Jordan

Job Title: Order Filler

About Us:
Reynolds, Perkins and Jordan is a leading distributor of industrial equipment, committed to delivering exceptional customer service and quality products.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and efficient Order Filler to join our team. As an Order Filler, you will be responsible for accurately and efficiently filling customer orders, managing inventory, and maintaining a safe and clean work environment.

Responsibilities:

 Fill customer orders accurately and efficiently
 Manage inventory levels and report any discrepancies
 Maintain a safe and clean work environment
 Collaborate with team members to achieve department goals
 Demonstrate a strong commitment to customer satisfaction

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience in a warehouse or distribution environment
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and stand for long periods
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.

Our team is like family, and we support our employees' traditional family structures with our family leave policy. We strive to create a welcoming work environment, but we recognize that our company culture may not be the best fit for everyone, especially those with non-traditional lifestyle choices.
