Author Position at Martin-Mcdowell

Company Background: Martin-Mcdowell is a leading publishing house dedicated to bringing diverse voices to the forefront of the literary world. We pride ourselves on creating a supportive and nurturing environment for our authors to thrive.

Job Description: We are seeking a talented author to join our team of creative writers. As an author at Martin-Mcdowell, you will be responsible for crafting engaging stories that resonate with our readers. Our ideal candidate is a woman who is not only skilled in writing but also passionate about connecting with her audience.

Responsibilities:

 Write high-quality, engaging stories that appeal to our target audience
 Collaborate with our editorial team to refine your work
 Engage with readers through social media and literary events

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of writing experience, preferably in a female-dominated genre (e.g. romance, young adult fiction)
 Strong writing and communication skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes health insurance, paid time off, and opportunities for professional development.
