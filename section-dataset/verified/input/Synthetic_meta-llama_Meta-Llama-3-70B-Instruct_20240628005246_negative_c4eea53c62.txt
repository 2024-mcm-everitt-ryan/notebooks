Company: Christensen-Shaw
Role: Program Assistant

About Us:
Christensen-Shaw is a leading organization dedicated to delivering exceptional program management services. We are committed to fostering a professional work environment.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Program Assistant to join our close-knit team of like-minded individuals. As a Program Assistant, you will provide administrative support to our program managers, ensuring the smooth operation of our projects. Your attention to detail, organizational skills, and ability to work well under pressure will make you a valuable asset to our team.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to program managers, including scheduling meetings, preparing reports, and maintaining project files
 Coordinate travel arrangements, process expense reports, and perform other administrative tasks as needed
 Assist in the development of project plans, schedules, and budgets
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records and reports
 Collaborate with team members to achieve project goals and objectives

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in an administrative role, preferably in program management
 Strong organizational, communication, and problem-solving skills
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel, Word, and PowerPoint
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Management, or a related field

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £35,000 to £45,000, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We also provide a supportive environment for employees with traditional family values.
