Company: Barker-Bryant

Job Title: Computer Aide

About Us:
Barker-Bryant is a dynamic and inclusive organization dedicated to providing cutting-edge technology solutions. We value diversity, equity, and inclusion in our workforce and strive to create a welcoming environment for all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Computer Aide to join our team. The successful candidate will possess strong communication skills, a positive attitude, and the ability to contribute to a supportive work culture. The ideal candidate will have a strong work ethic, be dedicated, and demonstrate consistency in their employment history.

Responsibilities:

 Provide technical support to our team members
 Troubleshoot and resolve computer hardware and software issues
 Install, configure, and maintain computer systems and networks
 Develop and implement effective computer training programs
 Collaborate with team members to achieve departmental goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a similar role
 Strong technical skills, including proficiency in Microsoft Office and computer hardware
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment
 Candidates with trade experience are valued

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan with employer match.
