Company: Ramirez-Estrada
Role: Dispatcher

About Us:
Ramirez-Estrada is a logistics company dedicated to providing exceptional transportation services. We strive to create an environment where everyone can succeed, regardless of their background or identity.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and efficient Dispatcher to join our team. As a Dispatcher, you will be responsible for coordinating the movement of goods, managing routes, and ensuring timely deliveries. You will work closely with our drivers and customer service team to provide exceptional service to our clients.

Responsibilities:
- Coordinate the movement of goods and manage routes to ensure timely deliveries
- Communicate effectively with drivers and customer service team to resolve any issues
- Monitor and analyze logistics data to identify areas for improvement
- Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency

Qualifications and Experience:
- 2+ years of experience in logistics or a related field
- High school diploma or equivalent required; degree in logistics or a related field preferred
- Excellent communication and organizational skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer comprehensive health insurance, retirement benefits, and paid time off. Our benefits include parental time off for all new parents, and we strive to create an inclusive environment for LGBTQ individuals.
