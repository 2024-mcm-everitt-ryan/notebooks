Company Background:
Fernandez, Baker and Ross is a reputable funeral home known for providing compassionate and professional services to families during their most challenging times. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of care and respect.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are actively seeking a Funeral Director who will be responsible for overseeing all aspects of funeral services, including consulting with families, coordinating arrangements, and ensuring that all legal requirements are met. This role requires someone who is empathetic, detail-oriented, and capable of handling sensitive situations with the utmost care.

Responsibilities:
- Consult with families to arrange funeral services tailored to their needs and preferences.
- Coordinate with cemeteries, crematoriums, and other service providers.
- Ensure all legal and regulatory requirements are complied with.
- Manage and supervise staff to ensure smooth operation of services.
- Provide grief support and resources to families as needed.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in mortuary science or a related field.
- Valid funeral director’s license.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in the funeral industry.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Strong organizational abilities and attention to detail.
- Applicants with disabilities are encouraged to apply to support our diversity mission.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance, including dental and vision.
- Paid time off and parental leave.
- Opportunities for professional development and training.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment with a focus on diversity, particularly welcoming candidates with disabilities.
