Clark-Schmidt is a leading social services organization dedicated to supporting families in need. We are seeking a compassionate and dedicated Family Advocate to join our team.

As a Family Advocate, you will work closely with our clients to provide emotional support, connect them with community resources, and empower them to make informed decisions about their lives. Your empathy, active listening skills, and ability to build strong relationships will make a meaningful difference in the lives of our clients.

We aim to build a team that reflects diverse sexual orientations and gender identities. Our organization believes in the power of diverse voices, especially those from the LGBTQ+ community, and is committed to fostering a culture of respect, empathy, and understanding. We vigorously look for candidates from the LGBTQ+ community to ensure their voices are represented and valued within our team.

Responsibilities:

 Provide emotional support and counseling to clients
 Connect clients with community resources and services
 Empower clients to make informed decisions about their lives
 Collaborate with internal teams to ensure comprehensive support
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with clients and community partners

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in social work, counseling, or a related field
 2+ years of experience working with diverse populations, including the LGBTQ+ community
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize multiple tasks

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage
 Generous paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
