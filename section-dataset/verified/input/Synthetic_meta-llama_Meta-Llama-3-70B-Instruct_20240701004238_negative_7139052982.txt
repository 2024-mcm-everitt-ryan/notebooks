Company: Richards and Sons
Role: Office Machine Aide

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and energetic individual to join our team as an Office Machine Aide. As a key member of our operations team, you will be responsible for ensuring the smooth functioning of our office equipment and machinery.

Responsibilities:

 Perform routine maintenance and repairs on office machines and equipment
 Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues with printers, copiers, and other office machines
 Collaborate with the facilities team to coordinate equipment upgrades and replacements
 Maintain accurate records of machine performance and maintenance schedules

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a similar role, preferably in a male-dominated industry
 Strong mechanical aptitude and problem-solving skills
 Ability to lift heavy equipment and supplies (up to 50 pounds and with reasonable accommodations)

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, paid time off, and retirement savings plan.
