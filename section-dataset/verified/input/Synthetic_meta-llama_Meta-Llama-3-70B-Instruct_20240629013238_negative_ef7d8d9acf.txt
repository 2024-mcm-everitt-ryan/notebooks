Company: Richards, Anderson and Thomas

Richards, Anderson and Thomas is a fast-paced and dynamic organization that values traditional approaches to business. Our team is comprised of driven individuals who share a passion for results-oriented work.

 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate data insights into business strategies
 Present findings and recommendations to senior management

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a hands-on role, preferably in a data-related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment
 Familiarity with traditional software platforms and systems
