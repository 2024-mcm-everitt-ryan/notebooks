Company Background:  
Rowe, Villarreal and Gonzales is a leading educational institution committed to providing quality early childhood education. We pride ourselves on fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment for our students.

Job Type:  
Full-time

Job Description:  
We are seeking a diligent and motivated kindergarten teacher. The ideal candidate will bring a structured and decisive approach to classroom management, ensuring a disciplined learning environment. He will be responsible for implementing our rigorous curriculum and should be able to navigate the challenges of early childhood education with confidence.

Responsibilities:  
- Develop and implement lesson plans that meet the educational standards.
- Maintain classroom discipline and manage student behavior.
- Foster a competitive spirit in academic and extracurricular activities.
- Communicate effectively with parents and staff.
- Mentor and guide young minds to achieve their full potential.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor’s degree in Early Childhood Education or a related field.
- Proven experience in a similar teaching role.
- Strong leadership and problem-solving skills.
- Physical stamina and energy to manage active young children.
- Excellent communication and disciplinary skills.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £30,000 to £40,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
