Company Background:
Garcia, Chapman and Chang is a leading consultancy firm specializing in providing top-notch services to a diverse range of clients around the globe. We pride ourselves on our innovative approach and commitment to excellence.

Job Title: Human Resources Assistant

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are currently seeking a Human Resources Assistant who is passionate about starting their career in HR. This role is perfect for those just starting out and looking to gain valuable experience in the field. As an HR Assistant, you will play a crucial role in supporting our HR team with various administrative tasks and projects.

Responsibilities:
- Assist with recruitment efforts, including posting job openings and screening resumes.
- Coordinate and schedule interviews with potential candidates.
- Maintain employee records and ensure compliance with company policies.
- Support the onboarding process for new hires.
- Help organize employee engagement activities and events.
- Provide general administrative support to the HR team.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Recent university leavers or new graduates are welcomed.
- Strong organizational and time management skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
- Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite.
- Familiarity with HR software and databases is a plus.
- Ability to handle sensitive information with confidentiality.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €30,000 to €40,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- A dynamic and supportive work environment.
