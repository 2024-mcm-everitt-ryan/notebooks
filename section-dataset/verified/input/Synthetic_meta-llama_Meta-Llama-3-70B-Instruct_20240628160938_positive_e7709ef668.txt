Staff Photographer at Griffith LLC

Company Background:
Griffith LLC is a leading media and communications company dedicated to telling stories that inspire and empower. We pride ourselves on our inclusive culture and commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion.

Job Type:
Full-time Staff Photographer

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented and creative Staff Photographer to join our team. As a key member of our visual content team, you will be responsible for capturing high-quality images that tell compelling stories and bring our brand to life. If you have a passion for photography and a keen eye for detail, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Capture high-quality images for use in our media publications, social media, and marketing campaigns
 Collaborate with our editorial team to conceptualize and execute photo shoots
 Edit and retouch images to ensure they meet our high standards
 Manage and maintain our photo archives

Qualifications and Experience:
We welcome applications from photographers with a minimum of 3 years of experience in a similar role. You should have a strong portfolio that showcases your creative vision and technical skills. Familiarity with Adobe Creative Suite is a must.

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings, and paid time off. As part of our commitment to inclusion, we provide reasonable accommodations for candidates with disabilities and are actively seeking candidates from underrepresented groups to join our team.
