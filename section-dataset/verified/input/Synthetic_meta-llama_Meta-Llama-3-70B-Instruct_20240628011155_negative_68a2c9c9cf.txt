Operations Coordinator
Schaefer Group

About Us:
Schaefer Group is a leading provider of logistical solutions, dedicated to delivering exceptional service to our clients. Our team is built on the principles of hard work, dedication, and a strong sense of tradition.

Job Description:
We are seeking an Operations Coordinator to join our team. As an Operations Coordinator, you will be responsible for ensuring the smooth day-to-day operation of our logistics department. You will work closely with our management team to optimize processes, manage budgets, and coordinate with vendors.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate logistics operations to ensure timely and efficient delivery of services
 Develop and implement process improvements to increase productivity
 Manage budgets and track expenses to ensure cost-effectiveness
 Build and maintain relationships with vendors and clients
 Collaborate with the management team to achieve departmental goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in operations or logistics management
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Logistics, or related field

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. Our team is like a close-knit family, and we prioritize a healthy work-life balance. We value traditional values and a cohesive team environment. We believe in maintaining a workplace that reflects traditional family values.
