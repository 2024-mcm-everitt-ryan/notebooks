Company Background:
Lynch, Austin and Bryant is an upscale cocktail lounge known for its sophisticated ambiance and exquisite customer service. Our team is dedicated to providing an unforgettable experience for our patrons through innovative mixology and unparalleled hospitality.

Job Type:
Bartender

Job Description:
We are on the lookout for a talented bartender to join our elite team. The ideal candidate will not only have a passion for mixology but also demonstrate a dynamic and energetic personality. He will be responsible for crafting high-quality beverages and ensuring our patrons have an exceptional experience.

Responsibilities:
- Prepare and serve a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks
- Maintain the cleanliness and organization of the bar
- Engage with customers to create a welcoming atmosphere
- Manage bar inventory and place orders when necessary
- Handle cash transactions efficiently

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a bartender in a high-end setting
- Strong knowledge of mixology and beverage preparation
- Excellent physical stamina and the ability to stand for long periods
- Superior interpersonal skills and a confident, bold demeanor
- Ability to work in a fast-paced, competitive environment

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary, ranging from $40,000 to $55,000 per year, based on experience and skills
- Comprehensive health insurance
- Professional development opportunities
- Employee discounts on food and beverages
- Flexible working hours
