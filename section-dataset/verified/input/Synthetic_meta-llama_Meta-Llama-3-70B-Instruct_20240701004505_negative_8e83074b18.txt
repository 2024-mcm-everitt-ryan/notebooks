Company: Smith, Cooley and Gregory

Job Title: Process Server

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and assertive individual to join our team as a Process Server. As a key member of our team, you will be responsible for serving legal documents to clients in a timely and professional manner.

Responsibilities:

 Serve legal documents to clients in accordance with court rules and regulations
 Maintain accurate records of service attempts and results
 Develop and maintain relationships with clients and legal professionals
 Stay up-to-date with changing laws and regulations affecting process serving

Qualifications and Experience:
 1-2 years of experience in process serving or a related field
 High school diploma or equivalent required
 Ability to work independently and make sound judgement calls
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
