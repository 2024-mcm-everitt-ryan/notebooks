Company: Dawson-Boyd
Role: Loan Counselor

About Us:
Dawson-Boyd is a leading financial institution dedicated to providing personalized loan solutions to our clients. We believe in fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Loan Counselor to join our team. As a Loan Counselor, you will work closely with clients to understand their financial needs and provide tailored loan solutions. Your expertise will help our clients make informed decisions and achieve their financial goals.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct client interviews to assess financial needs and provide loan options
 Analyze financial data and credit reports to determine loan eligibility
 Develop and present personalized loan solutions to clients
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless client experience
 Maintain accurate records and reports

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Finance, Business, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in loan counseling or a related field
 Strong analytical and communication skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
