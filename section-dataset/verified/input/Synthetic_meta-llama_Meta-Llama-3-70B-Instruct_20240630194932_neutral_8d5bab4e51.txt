Job Title: Baggage Porter
Company: Graham, Phillips and Cantrell

We are seeking a reliable and efficient Baggage Porter to join our team. As a Baggage Porter, you will be responsible for ensuring the safe and efficient transportation of luggage to and from aircraft, terminals, and other areas of the airport.

Job Description:

 Load and unload luggage from aircraft, baggage carts, and conveyor belts
 Transport luggage to and from designated areas of the airport
 Ensure accurate delivery of luggage to passengers
 Collaborate with other team members to maintain a smooth and efficient operation
 Maintain a safe and clean working environment

Responsibilities and Qualifications:

 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and stand for long periods
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience in a similar role preferred

What We Offer:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 A comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
 A dynamic and inclusive work environment

Graham, Phillips and Cantrell is committed to creating a diverse and inclusive environment where everyone can thrive. We welcome applicants of all races, genders, and orientations.
