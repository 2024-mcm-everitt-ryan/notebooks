Company Background:
Pierce-Dorsey is a leading facility management company dedicated to maintaining clean, safe, and productive environments. Our commitment to diversity and inclusion drives us to provide equal opportunities for all individuals.

Job Type: 
Full-time

Job Description:
We are actively seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented janitorial worker to join our team. This role supports our goal of gender diversity, and female applicants are highly encouraged to apply. You will play a crucial role in ensuring our facilities are clean, hygienic, and well-maintained.

Responsibilities:
- Perform general cleaning tasks, including sweeping, mopping, dusting, and vacuuming
- Maintain restrooms by cleaning and restocking supplies
- Dispose of trash and recyclables
- Follow safety guidelines and use cleaning equipment appropriately
- Report any maintenance or repair needs to the supervisor

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent
- Previous janitorial or cleaning experience is preferred but not required
- Ability to work independently with minimal supervision
- Strong attention to detail and a commitment to maintaining high standards of cleanliness
- Physical ability to perform tasks that require standing, bending, and lifting

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary: $30,000 - $40,000 USD annually, based on experience and skills
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional development and advancement
- Supportive and inclusive work environment

We aim to boost female representation in our company through this role. Female candidates will receive special attention during the hiring process.

Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their resumes and a brief cover letter explaining their interest in the role.
