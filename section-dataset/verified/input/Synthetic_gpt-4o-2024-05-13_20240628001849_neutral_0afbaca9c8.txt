As a Portal Support Engineer, you will be responsible for maintaining and enhancing our customer portals. This role requires a proactive individual skilled in problem-solving, with a strong understanding of web technologies and user support.

- Monitor portal performance and implement necessary updates.
- Assist in user training and documentation.

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
- Proven experience in technical support or a similar role.
- Familiarity with web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
- Strong communication skills, both written and verbal.
- Ability to work effectively in a diverse team environment.
