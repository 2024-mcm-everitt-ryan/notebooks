Job Title: Pulse Magazine Lead Designer and Production Manager at The East Carolinian

Company Background: 
Williams PLC is a leading media company that publishes The East Carolinian, a popular magazine focused on lifestyle, culture, and entertainment. We are committed to delivering high-quality content to our readers and are seeking a talented Lead Designer and Production Manager to join our team.

Job Description:
We are looking for a creative and detail-oriented individual to lead the design and production of Pulse Magazine. As the Lead Designer and Production Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing the visual direction of the magazine, managing the design team, and ensuring timely and error-free production.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain the visual brand of Pulse Magazine
- Manage a team of designers and ensure effective collaboration
- Coordinate with the editorial team to design and layout magazine content
- Oversee the production process, ensuring timely and error-free delivery
- Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in design and production

Qualifications:
- 5+ years of experience in magazine design and production
- Strong portfolio showcasing creative and technical design skills
- Excellent leadership and management abilities
- Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite and project management tools
- Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
- Strong attention to detail and organizational skills

Benefits:
- Salary range: $60,000 - $80,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Flexible working hours and remote work options
