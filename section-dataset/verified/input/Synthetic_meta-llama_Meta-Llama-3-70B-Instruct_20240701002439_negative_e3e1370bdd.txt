Evaluation Coordinator
Vega-Diaz

Job Description:
We're seeking a driven Evaluation Coordinator to join our team at Vega-Diaz. As a key member of our evaluation team, you will be responsible for coordinating and implementing evaluation projects, ensuring data quality, and providing technical support to our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement evaluation plans, data collection tools, and reporting protocols
 Coordinate with clients to ensure timely and effective project delivery
 Analyze and interpret data, identifying trends and areas for improvement
 Provide technical support and training to clients on evaluation methodologies and tools

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g., statistics, research methods, or social sciences)
 At least 2 years of experience in evaluation, research, or a related field
 Strong technical background in data analysis and statistical software (e.g., R, SPSS, or SAS)
 Proven ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000, based on your experience and skills. You'll also enjoy a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings, and paid time off. Plus, you'll have the opportunity to work with a talented team of professionals who are passionate about making a difference.
