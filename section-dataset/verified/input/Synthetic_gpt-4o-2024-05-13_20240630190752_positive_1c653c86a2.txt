Company: Morgan, Young and Horn  
Role: Chiropractor  

Company Background:  
Morgan, Young, and Horn is a renowned healthcare provider specializing in chiropractic services. Our goal is to offer holistic care to our clients, ensuring their physical and mental well-being. 

Job Type:  
Full-time

Job Description:  
We are seeking a dedicated and skilled Chiropractor to join our team. Our organization values the unique perspectives women bring to the table and we are focused on hiring women to enhance our team diversity. In this role, you will provide chiropractic care to our patients, developing treatment plans and delivering high-quality care. 

Responsibilities:  
- Assess patients' medical conditions by reviewing their medical histories and conducting physical examinations.  
- Develop and execute individualized treatment plans based on patient needs.  
- Use manual adjustment techniques to treat patients.  
- Educate patients about lifestyle changes and exercises that may help alleviate their symptoms.  
- Maintain accurate patient records and document treatments.  

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Doctor of Chiropractic (D.C.) degree from an accredited chiropractic college.  
- Licensed to practice in the relevant jurisdiction.  
- Strong diagnostic and treatment skills.  
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.  
- Women are especially encouraged to apply for this position to promote gender diversity.
