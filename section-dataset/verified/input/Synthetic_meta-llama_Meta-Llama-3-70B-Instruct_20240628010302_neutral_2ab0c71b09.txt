Company Background:
Johnson-Roberson is a leading global consulting firm dedicated to delivering innovative solutions to our clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion, fostering a workplace culture that values and respects individual differences.

Job Type: Personnel Associate

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Personnel Associate to join our Human Resources team. The successful candidate will be responsible for providing administrative support in recruitment, employee onboarding, and benefits administration.

Responsibilities:

 Assist in the recruitment process, including scheduling interviews and maintaining candidate records
 Coordinate employee onboarding, ensuring a smooth transition for new hires
 Administer employee benefits, including health insurance, retirement plans, and time-off policies
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date employee records and databases
 Provide general administrative support to the HR team as needed

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
 1-2 years of experience in an HR or administrative role
 Excellent organizational and communication skills
 Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and a generous paid time-off policy. Our inclusive policies support employees in all family situations, and we value contributions from LGBTQIA members.
