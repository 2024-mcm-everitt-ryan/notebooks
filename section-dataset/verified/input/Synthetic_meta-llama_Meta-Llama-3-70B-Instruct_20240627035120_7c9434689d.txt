This role requires a strategic thinker with exceptional leadership skills and a passion for staying at the forefront of digital marketing trends.

Responsibilities:

- Develop and execute comprehensive digital marketing strategies across multiple channels
- Lead a team of marketing professionals, providing guidance and mentorship to ensure successful campaign execution
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive business growth and revenue
- Analyze campaign performance, identifying areas for improvement and optimizing strategies accordingly

Qualifications:

- 8+ years of experience in digital marketing, with a focus on demand generation and marketing operations
- Proven track record of driving business results through data-driven marketing strategies
- Strong leadership and management skills, with experience leading high-performing teams
- Excellent communication and project management skills
