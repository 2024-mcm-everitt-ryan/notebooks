Company: Green-Patterson

Job Type: Aircraft Pilot

About Us:
Green-Patterson is a leading aviation company dedicated to providing exceptional air travel experiences. We're committed to building a diverse and inclusive workforce, and we're excited to expand our team with talented individuals who share our passion for flying.

Job Description:
We're seeking a skilled and experienced Aircraft Pilot to join our team. As a pilot, you'll be responsible for ensuring the safe operation of our aircraft, adhering to strict safety protocols, and providing exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Operate aircraft safely and efficiently
 Conduct pre-flight checks and ensure aircraft is airworthy
 Communicate effectively with air traffic control and passengers
 Maintain accurate records of flight operations
 Participate in recurrent training and proficiency checks

Qualifications and Experience:

 Commercial Pilot's License with a minimum of 500 hours of flight experience
 Strong knowledge of aviation regulations and safety protocols
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work well under pressure and adapt to changing circumstances
