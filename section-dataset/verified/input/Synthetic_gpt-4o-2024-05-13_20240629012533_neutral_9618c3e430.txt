Company: Lowe Ltd
Role: Program Administrator

Company Background:
Lowe Ltd is a global leader in delivering innovative solutions across various sectors. We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment that values diversity and promotes equality among our employees.

Job Type:
Full-time, Permanent

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and organized Program Administrator to join our dynamic team. The Program Administrator will play a key role in supporting our program operations, ensuring efficiency and compliance with company policies.

Responsibilities:
- Coordinate and manage program schedules, ensuring timely completion of tasks.
- Maintain accurate records and documentation, ensuring data integrity.
- Liaise with various departments to support program activities.
- Assist in the preparation of reports and presentations.
- Handle administrative tasks and provide logistical support for program events.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Have a degree from a recognised university.
- A proven track record in program administration or a related field.
- Strong organisational and multitasking skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Ability to handle physical tasks with or without reasonable accommodations.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and company holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €40,000 to €55,000, based on your experience and skills.
