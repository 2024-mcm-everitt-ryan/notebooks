Job Description Summary

Under the supervision and direction of the Production Supervisor and/or Process Operator, this position is responsible for operation and control of formed product equipment, including starting up and shutting down of all line equipment. This position is responsible for setting up the equipment and support functions for the formed product lines. This position will monitor the operation of the equipment and make changes as required under the direction of the Production Supervisor and Process Operator. It will be their responsibility to assure that acceptable product quality levels are attained and maintained. During downtime periods, they will set up, monitor, and operate all said equipment in accordance with established cleaning procedures. Maintains safe work practices at all times and participates in safety programs. Responsible for the operation and control of patty line and co-product line equipment in accordance with established procedures. Perform all sanitation duties as prescribed by company procedures and standards, including mid-shift clean-up schedule and thorough knowledge of chemicals used. Disassemble, clean, and reassemble shredder and forming equipment on formed product lines during clean-up periods. Maintain, on an hourly and daily basis, legible records of his/her line performance including F.F.A.'s, SAPP content, downtime (operational and mechanical). Monitor and/or control potato flow and quantity.

Job Qualifications

Qualifications:
- Must have good communication skills, oral and written.
- Must be able to lift up to 50 pounds.
- Must be able to work for prolonged periods of time in a hot, humid area.
- Must be able to walk or stand for long periods of time.
- Sanitation of area and weekend cleanups.
- Performs other jobs or duties as assigned by Production Supervisor.
