At Thomas PLC, we are dedicated to creating a more inclusive and equitable society through our juvenile justice training programs. As we expand our team, we are seeking a passionate and experienced Juvenile Justice Trainer to join our mission-driven organization.

About Us:
Thomas PLC is a leading provider of juvenile justice training, committed to empowering individuals and communities to create positive change. Our team is comprised of dedicated professionals who share a passion for social justice and a commitment to excellence.

Job Description:
As a Juvenile Justice Trainer, you will be responsible for designing and delivering training programs to juvenile justice professionals, focusing on evidence-based practices and trauma-informed care. You will work closely with our team to develop curricula, facilitate workshops, and provide technical assistance to organizations serving at-risk youth.

Responsibilities:

 Design and deliver training programs for juvenile justice professionals
 Develop curricula and materials that incorporate best practices and research-based approaches
 Provide technical assistance and coaching to organizations serving at-risk youth
 Collaborate with our team to identify areas for program improvement and implement changes

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Social Work, Education, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in juvenile justice, training, or a related field
 Strong knowledge of evidence-based practices and trauma-informed care
 Excellent facilitation and communication skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a 403(b) retirement plan.

Our Commitment to Inclusion:
We value diversity and inclusion and are committed to creating a workplace that reflects the communities we serve. We invite individuals with disabilities to apply and contribute to our diverse workforce. Our commitment to an inclusive workplace includes encouraging applications from people with disabilities.
