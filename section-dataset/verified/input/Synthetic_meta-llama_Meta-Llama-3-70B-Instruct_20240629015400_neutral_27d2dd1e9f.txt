Company: Noble-Logan
Role: Budget Review Specialist

Noble-Logan is a leading financial consulting firm dedicated to providing expert budgeting and financial planning services to our clients. Our team values balanced and inclusive perspectives, and we seek individuals who share our commitment to integrity and excellence.

We are seeking a highly skilled Budget Review Specialist to join our team.  Develop comprehensive financial plans and recommendations for clients
 Collaborate with clients to understand their financial goals and objectives
 Provide expert guidance and support to clients
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in budgeting and financial planning

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or related field
 3+ years of experience in budgeting and financial planning
 Proven analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Experience in skilled trades is an advantage
