Company: Wilkins, Martin and Gallegos

Role: Legal Support Worker

Company Background: Wilkins, Martin and Gallegos is a leading law firm that prides itself on providing exceptional legal services to a diverse clientele. With a strong commitment to inclusivity and diversity, we aim to create a supportive and collaborative work environment.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented Legal Support Worker to join our dynamic legal team. The ideal candidate will have a background in public relations and strong problem-solving skills. This role supports our attorneys and ensures the smooth operation of legal processes.

Responsibilities:
- Assist attorneys with case management and documentation.
- Perform legal research and draft correspondence.
- Manage client communications and maintain confidentiality.
- Coordinate schedules and meetings.
- Organize and maintain legal files and records.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Legal Studies, Public Relations, or a related field.
- Previous experience in a legal support role is preferred.
- Strong problem-solving and organizational skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and legal research tools.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from 40,000 to 60,000 USD annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- A supportive, inclusive work environment.

Application Process:
Interested candidates are encouraged to apply by submitting their resume and a cover letter detailing their qualifications and experience.
