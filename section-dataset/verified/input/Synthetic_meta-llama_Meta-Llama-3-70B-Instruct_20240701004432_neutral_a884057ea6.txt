Resource Specialist at Butler-Gonzalez

Company Background:
Butler-Gonzalez is a leading consulting firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions to our clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to create a work environment that is welcoming and inclusive to all.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Resource Specialist to join our team. As a Resource Specialist, you will play a critical role in ensuring the smooth operation of our projects by providing administrative support, coordinating logistics, and maintaining accurate records.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to project teams, including scheduling meetings, booking travel arrangements, and preparing materials for presentations
 Coordinate logistics for projects, including arranging equipment and supplies, and managing project timelines
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records, including project files, databases, and spreadsheets
 Collaborate with team members to identify and resolve project-related issues
 Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and productivity

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in an administrative or coordination role
 Strong organizational and time management skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and Google Suite

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary, ranging from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on experience and skills. Our benefits package includes comprehensive health insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and provide opportunities for professional development and growth.
