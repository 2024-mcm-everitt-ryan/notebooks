Company: Warner-Walker

Job Title: Industrial Control Technician

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Industrial Control Technician to join our team at Warner-Walker. As an Industrial Control Technician, you will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing electrical and electronic control systems in an industrial setting. You will work closely with our engineering team to ensure that all systems are operating efficiently and safely.

Responsibilities:
- Install, test, and maintain electrical and electronic control systems
- Troubleshoot and repair control systems faults
- Collaborate with the engineering team to improve system performance
- Develop and implement preventative maintenance programs
- Ensure all work is performed in accordance with safety protocols and industry standards

Qualifications and Experience:
- Associate's degree in Electrical Engineering or a related field
- 2+ years of experience in industrial control systems
- Strong understanding of electrical and electronic circuits
- Ability to work efficiently within job requirements
- We seek individuals who support a respectful and inclusive workplace

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $75,000 USD, based on your experience and skills
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance
- Retirement plan with company match
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional development and growth
