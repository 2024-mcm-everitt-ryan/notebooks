Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Parking Lot Attendant to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations of the parking lot, ensuring the safety and satisfaction of all customers. This role requires the ability to adapt to change and work effectively in a team environment.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor and control access to the parking lot
- Assist customers with any issues or inquiries
- Maintain cleanliness and orderliness of the parking lot
- Report any suspicious activities or safety hazards
- Provide excellent customer service
- Perform other duties as assigned

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent
- Previous experience in a customer service role is advantageous
- Ability to work in various weather conditions
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to adapt to change and handle multiple tasks
- Must be reliable and punctual
