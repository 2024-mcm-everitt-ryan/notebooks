Company: Jordan Ltd
Role: Locksmith

Jordan Ltd is a leading provider of locksmith services, committed to delivering exceptional quality and customer satisfaction. We are seeking a skilled and experienced Locksmith to join our team.

Job Description:
We are looking for a reliable and skilled Locksmith to install, repair, and maintain locks and other security devices for our clients. The ideal candidate will have a strong attention to detail, excellent problem-solving skills, and the ability to work efficiently in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Install, repair, and maintain locks and other security devices
 Respond to emergency calls and provide timely solutions
 Collaborate with colleagues to achieve team goals
 Develop and maintain a strong understanding of our clients' needs and preferences

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience as a Locksmith
 Strong knowledge of locks and security devices
 Physical ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work in confined spaces
 Valid driver's license and a clean driving record

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $75,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. In addition, we provide ongoing training and professional development opportunities to support your career growth.
