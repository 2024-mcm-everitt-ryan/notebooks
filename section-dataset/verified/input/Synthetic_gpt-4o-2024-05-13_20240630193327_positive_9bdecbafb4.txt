Company Background:
Buck PLC is a leading global firm specializing in geographic information systems and mapping solutions. We are committed to innovation, sustainability, and enhancing our team diversity.

Job Type:
Full-Time Cartographer

Job Description:
Buck PLC is seeking a talented and passionate Cartographer to join our dynamic team. This role is part of our strategy to support women's careers and enhance our team's diversity. As a Cartographer, you will be responsible for creating and updating maps, analyzing spatial data, and collaborating with various departments to provide accurate geographic information.

Responsibilities:
- Create and update maps using GIS software and other mapping tools.
- Analyze spatial data to identify patterns and trends.
- Collaborate with other departments to integrate geographic data into projects.
- Ensure the accuracy and quality of mapping products.
- Provide insights and recommendations based on geographic data analysis.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Geography, Cartography, GIS, or a related field.
- Experience with GIS software (e.g., ArcGIS, QGIS).
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and collaboration abilities.
- Attention to detail and accuracy in work.

- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.
