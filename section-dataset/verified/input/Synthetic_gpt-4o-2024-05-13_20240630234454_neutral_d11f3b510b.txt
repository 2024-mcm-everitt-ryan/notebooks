Goodman Group: Kettle Operator

Company Background:
Goodman Group is a leading manufacturer in the food processing industry, committed to delivering high-quality products and fostering a positive work environment. Our team is dedicated to innovation, safety, and sustainability.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dynamic individual to join our team as a Kettle Operator. This role is critical in ensuring efficient and safe operation of kettles used in our production process. The ideal candidate will be a proactive and motivated individual who excels in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:
- Operate kettles and related equipment according to standard operating procedures.
- Monitor and adjust controls to maintain specified temperatures and pressures.
- Perform routine maintenance and troubleshooting of equipment.
- Ensure cleanliness and sanitation of the work area.
- Adhere to all safety and quality standards.
- Maintain accurate production records.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Previous experience in a manufacturing or food processing environment is preferred.
- Strong attention to detail and ability to follow instructions.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Good physical condition to handle the demands of the job, including lifting and standing for extended periods.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary: We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 30,000 to USD 40,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and a cover letter outlining their qualifications and experience.
