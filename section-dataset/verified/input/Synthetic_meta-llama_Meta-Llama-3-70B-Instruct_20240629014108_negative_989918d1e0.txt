Company: Underwood-Reese

Job Title: Molder

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Molder to join our team at Underwood-Reese. As a Molder, you will be responsible for operating and maintaining molding machines, as well as ensuring the quality of products. The ideal candidate will have a stable work history and a strong understanding of manufacturing processes.

Responsibilities:

 Operate and maintain molding machines to ensure efficient production
 Monitor product quality and report any defects or issues
 Collaborate with team members to meet production targets
 Possess a valid driver's license (required for all roles)

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required; Bachelor's degree preferred
 2+ years of experience in a manufacturing environment
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision
 No history of addiction or substance abuse

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
