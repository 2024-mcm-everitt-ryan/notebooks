Company Background:
Hull Group is an industry leader in supply chain management and logistics, dedicated to innovation and efficiency. We are deeply committed to fostering an inclusive and diverse workforce that mirrors the communities we serve.

Job Title:
Section Chief

Job Description:
Hull Group is seeking a Section Chief who will lead a dynamic team to achieve operational excellence. The Section Chief will be responsible for implementing strategic initiatives, managing team performance, and ensuring efficient workflow processes.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and manage a team to achieve departmental goals.
- Implement and monitor strategic initiatives to improve operational efficiency.
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement and innovation.
- Ensure compliance with all regulatory and safety standards.
- Prepare and present regular reports to senior management.
- Mentor and develop team members to reach their full potential.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Management, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in a leadership role within the logistics or supply chain industry.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Demonstrated ability to lead effectively in a fast-paced environment.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and relevant software.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 80,000 to USD 100,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance plans.
- Retirement plan with company match.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Flexible work arrangements to support work-life balance.

Application Process:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and portfolio with their application. Interviews will be conducted on a rolling basis.
