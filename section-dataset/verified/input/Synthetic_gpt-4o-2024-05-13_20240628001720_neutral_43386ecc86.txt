Company Background:
Lambert-Williams is a renowned publishing and bookbinding company with a history of producing high-quality books for over a century. We pride ourselves on our craftsmanship and dedication to preserving the art of bookbinding.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Bookbinder to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for binding books by hand, managing the production process, and ensuring the highest standards of quality are met.

Responsibilities:
- Bind books using traditional and modern techniques
- Repair and restore old and damaged books
- Manage the production schedule to ensure timely completion of projects
- Maintain equipment and materials in good working order
- Collaborate with other team members to achieve project goals

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience in bookbinding or a related field
- Strong attention to detail and craftsmanship
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Excellent time management and organizational skills
- Candidates from all backgrounds are welcome to apply

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 35,000 to USD 50,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance
- Generous parental leave benefits
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth
- Supportive and inclusive work environment for all employees
