Company: Johnson-Murphy
Role: City Medical Examiner

Job Description:
As a City Medical Examiner at Johnson-Murphy, you will be responsible for conducting autopsies, examining bodies, and determining causes of death. You will work closely with law enforcement agencies, medical professionals, and families to provide accurate and timely information.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct thorough examinations of bodies to determine cause and manner of death
 Collect and analyze evidence, including tissue samples and other materials
 Consult with law enforcement agencies, medical professionals, and families to provide information and answer questions
 Prepare detailed reports of findings and testimony in court
 Stay up-to-date with the latest medical knowledge and procedures

Qualifications and Experience Needed:

 Doctor of Medicine or Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine degree
 Completion of a pathology residency program
 Board certification in anatomic and clinical pathology
 5+ years of experience in a medical examiner's office or related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work well under pressure and manage multiple cases simultaneously

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 150,000 to USD 200,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance package
 Generous paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
