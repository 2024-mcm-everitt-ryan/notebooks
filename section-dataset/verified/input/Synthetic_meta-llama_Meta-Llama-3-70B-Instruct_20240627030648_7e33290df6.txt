Job Title: Cyber Security & Privacy Associate

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Cyber Security & Privacy Associate to join our team of experts. As a Cyber Security & Privacy Associate, you will work closely with our clients to identify and mitigate potential cybersecurity threats. - Collaborating with cross-functional teams to develop and implement privacy protocols
- Providing training and guidance to clients on cybersecurity best practices
- Staying up-to-date with the latest cybersecurity threats and technologies

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Cybersecurity, or a related field
- 2+ years of experience in cybersecurity or a related field
- Strong understanding of cybersecurity principles and best practices
- Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
- Functional ability in Slovak is an asset
