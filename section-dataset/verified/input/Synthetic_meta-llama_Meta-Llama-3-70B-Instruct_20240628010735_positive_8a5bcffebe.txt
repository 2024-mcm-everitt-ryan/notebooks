Company: Blevins, Perez and Andrews

Role: Director of Inclusive Growth

About Us:
Blevins, Perez and Andrews is a forward-thinking consulting firm dedicated to promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion in the corporate world. We believe that a diverse and inclusive workforce is essential to driving innovation and success.

Job Description:
We are seeking a visionary Director of Inclusive Growth to lead our team in developing and implementing strategies that foster a culture of inclusivity and respect for all employees. As a champion of diversity, equity, and inclusion, you will work closely with our executive team to create a workplace where everyone feels valued, seen, and heard.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement diversity, equity, and inclusion strategies that align with our company's mission and values
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to create inclusive policies, practices, and programs
 Provide coaching and training to employees on unconscious bias, cultural competence, and inclusive leadership
 Foster strategic partnerships with LGBTQ+ organizations and community groups to enhance our recruitment strategy and promote diversity
 Analyze and report on diversity metrics to measure progress and identify areas for improvement

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in diversity, equity, and inclusion, talent management, or a related field
 Proven track record of developing and implementing successful diversity and inclusion initiatives
 Strong understanding of LGBTQ+ issues and concerns
 Excellent communication, coaching, and facilitation skills
 Ability to work effectively with employees at all levels of the organization
