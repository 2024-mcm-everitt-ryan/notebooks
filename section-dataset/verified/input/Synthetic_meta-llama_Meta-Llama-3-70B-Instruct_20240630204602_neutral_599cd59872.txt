Job Title: Procurement Contracting Officer
Company: Martin Ltd

Company Background: Martin Ltd is a leading global procurement company dedicated to delivering innovative solutions to our clients. We believe that inclusion drives innovation and creativity, and we strive to create a workplace where everyone feels valued.

Job Description: We are seeking a highly skilled Procurement Contracting Officer to join our team. As a Procurement Contracting Officer, you will be responsible for creating and implementing strategies to optimize our procurement processes. You will work closely with our cross-functional teams to identify opportunities for improvement and develop effective solutions.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement procurement strategies to achieve cost savings and process efficiencies
 Collaborate with internal stakeholders to identify procurement opportunities and develop effective solutions
 Negotiate contracts with suppliers to ensure best value for our clients
 Analyze market trends and supplier performance to inform procurement decisions

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Supply Chain Management, or related field
 At least 3 years of experience in procurement or a related field
 Strong analytical and negotiation skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance package
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
