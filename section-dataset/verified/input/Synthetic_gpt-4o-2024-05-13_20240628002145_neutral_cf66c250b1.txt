Company Background:
York-Ellis is a renowned healthcare provider dedicated to delivering exceptional medical services. Our commitment to diversity and inclusivity ensures that every member of our team feels valued and respected.

Job Type:
Full-time Podiatrist

Job Description and Responsibilities:
York-Ellis is seeking a dedicated Podiatrist to join our comprehensive foot and ankle care team. The ideal candidate will diagnose and treat ailments related to the lower extremities, perform surgical procedures, and promote foot health.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Doctor of Podiatric Medicine (DPM) degree from an accredited institution.
- Valid state license to practice podiatry.
- Excellent diagnostic skills and attention to detail.
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
- Ability to work collaboratively within a diverse team.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $90,000 to $150,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance, including dental and vision.
- Parental leave policies support our employees having a child.
- Nursing Rooms ensure a comfortable space for nursing parents.
- Workshops for parents on balancing work and family life.
- Our team values contributions from LGBTQ2+ members.
- Professional development and continuing education opportunities.

Application Process:
Interested candidates should submit a detailed resume and cover letter outlining their qualifications and experiences.
