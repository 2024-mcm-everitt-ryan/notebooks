Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced and skilled Audit Supervisor to join our team. As an Audit Supervisor, you will be responsible for leading and managing audit engagements, developing and maintaining client relationships, and identifying opportunities for growth and improvement.

Responsibilities:

 Lead and manage audit engagements from planning to completion, ensuring high-quality results and meeting client expectations
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with clients, identifying opportunities for growth and improvement
 Manage and supervise a team of auditors, providing guidance and support to ensure their success
 Identify and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and effectiveness
 Collaborate with other departments to ensure seamless delivery of services

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Accounting or related field
 5+ years of experience in auditing, with at least 2 years in a supervisory role
 Professional certification (CPA, CA, or equivalent)
 Excellent communication, leadership, and project management skills
 Ability to think critically and strategically
 Strong time management skills and attention to detail
