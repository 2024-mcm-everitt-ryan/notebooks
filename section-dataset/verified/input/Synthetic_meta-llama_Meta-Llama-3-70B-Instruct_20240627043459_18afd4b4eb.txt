Company: Stevenson LLC

Job Title: Principle Reliability Engineer

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Principle Reliability Engineer to join our team. As a Principle Reliability Engineer, you will be responsible for leading the development and implementation of reliability engineering strategies, ensuring the reliability and maintainability of our products.

Responsibilities:

- Lead cross-functional teams to develop and implement reliability engineering strategies
- Collaborate with design engineers to ensure product reliability and maintainability
- Develop and maintain reliability testing procedures and protocols
- Analyze data to identify trends and areas for improvement
- Provide technical guidance and support to junior engineers

Qualifications:

- Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field
- At least 5 years of experience in reliability engineering
- Strong knowledge of reliability engineering principles and methodologies
- Excellent communication and leadership skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
