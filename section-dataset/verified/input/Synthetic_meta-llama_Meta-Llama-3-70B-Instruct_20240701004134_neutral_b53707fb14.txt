Project Planner at Snyder LLC

Company Background:
Snyder LLC is a leading project management firm dedicated to delivering high-quality results for our clients. We pride ourselves on our collaborative and inclusive work environment, where every team member has the opportunity to grow and thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Project Planner to join our team. As a Project Planner, you will be responsible for planning, coordinating, and executing projects from start to finish. Your exceptional communication and problem-solving skills will ensure that our projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the satisfaction of our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and manage project plans, timelines, and budgets
 Coordinate with team members and stakeholders to ensure project deliverables are met
 Identify and mitigate project risks and issues
 Communicate project updates and progress to clients and internal stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in project planning or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Proficiency in project management software and tools

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings, and paid time off. We promote a culture of respect and teamwork, and our team members are encouraged to take ownership of their work and contribute to our company's growth and success.
