Company: Aguilar LLC

Role: Postal Service Clerk

Company Background:
Aguilar LLC is a leading logistics and postal service provider dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service and efficient postal solutions. Our commitment to diversity and inclusion drives our success and fosters a supportive and innovative work environment.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
As a Postal Service Clerk, you will be responsible for a variety of tasks including sorting mail, selling postage, and providing excellent customer service. You will work closely with both internal and external customers to ensure the smooth operation of our postal services.

Responsibilities:
- Sort and process incoming and outgoing mail.
- Sell postage and postal products.
- Assist customers with postal inquiries and services.
- Maintain accurate records of transactions and inventory.
- Ensure the postal office is clean and organized.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills required.
- Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
- Ability to work efficiently both independently and as part of a team.
- Basic computer skills and familiarity with postal service software are desirable.
- Prior experience in customer service or postal services is a plus.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £20,000 to £30,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.

Our Commitment to Diversity:
Aguilar LLC is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage applications from underrepresented groups and strive to create an inclusive environment where all employees feel valued and respected. Our culture of inclusion benefits everyone.
