Company: Figueroa and Sons

Role: Juvenile Justice Trainer

About Us:
Figueroa and Sons is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing training and resources to professionals working in the juvenile justice system. Our mission is to promote a safe and equitable environment for all youth.

Job Description:
We are seeking a Juvenile Justice Trainer to join our team. As a Juvenile Justice Trainer, you will be responsible for designing and delivering training programs to professionals working with youth in the justice system. You will work closely with our team to develop curriculum, facilitate workshops, and provide technical assistance to ensure the highest quality of service delivery.

Responsibilities:

 Design and deliver training programs to professionals working in the juvenile justice system
 Develop curriculum and materials that are inclusive and respectful of diverse backgrounds and perspectives
 Facilitate workshops and provide technical assistance to ensure the highest quality of service delivery
 Collaborate with our team to identify training needs and develop strategies to meet those needs
 Support a culture of open dialogue and respect, and be comfortable in a diverse environment

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g. education, social work, criminal justice)
 At least 3 years of experience working in the juvenile justice system, preferably in a training or education role
 Excellent communication and facilitation skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 We welcome applications from individuals with diverse health backgrounds and from all backgrounds.

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 USD to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
