Working as part of the Schneider Electric Ireland Field Services Engineering Team, you will lead project commissioning and maintenance lifecycle support on MV & LV equipment in accordance with Schneider Electric standard processes, quality standards, and safety guidelines. Typical work includes MV & LV equipment installation and commissioning, routine maintenance, extensions, replacements, identifying technical faults, and taking corrective action via remote or on-site customer support. You will deliver top-quality customer service supported by the wider Schneider Electric Ireland teams, taking actions to identify new business opportunities for Schneider Electric.

Primary duties & responsibilities:
- Regular attendance at customer sites for installation, testing & commissioning, and maintenance of electrical systems (Predominantly MV Equipment but also LV Equipment & Transformers).
- Performing the following activities on Schneider Electric power distribution equipment at customer sites:
  - Supervision of contractor works.
  - Commissioning activity on MV & LV Systems.
  - Systems testing and fault diagnosis.
  - Modifications / modernisation of legacy systems.
  - Asset management / maintenance services of install base equipment.
  - Troubleshoot equipment failure and make engineering recommendations to avoid reoccurrence.
  - Strive to develop and maintain technical best practice.
  - Document test results and produce site equipment reports.
  - Perform site risk assessments and assist in developing complex method statements.
  - Participate in On Call scheme to provide emergency cover to contracted customers outside of normal working hours.
  - Promote the Company's activities & offerings and assist in identification of new business opportunities.
  - Contribute to customer training activities.

Skills & Abilities:
- Strong field working experience with MV & LV distribution systems.
- A solid understanding of power and control systems.
- HV or MV Commissioning experience desirable.
- The ability to lead projects through to completion while working independently.
- Solid working knowledge of MS Office Suite, standard operating systems, and associated peripherals.
- Must hold a clean & valid driving license as a company vehicle will be provided.
