Company Background:
Manning, Haas and Long is a renowned debt collection agency with over 20 years of experience in the industry. We pride ourselves on maintaining ethical practices and a diverse, inclusive workplace.

Job Type:
Full-Time Collector

Job Description:
Manning, Haas and Long is seeking a diligent and professional Collector to join our team. The ideal candidate will possess strong communication skills, a keen attention to detail, and the capability to manage multiple accounts effectively. This role involves contacting clients to recover outstanding debts while maintaining a professional demeanor.

Responsibilities:
- Contact clients via phone, email, and letters to collect outstanding debts.
- Negotiate payment plans and settlements based on client circumstances.
- Maintain accurate records of all communications and transactions.
- Work collaboratively with the legal team for cases requiring legal action.
- Provide excellent customer service and support to clients.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent required; a degree in finance or related field is a plus.
- Previous experience in debt collection or customer service preferred.
- Strong negotiation and communication skills.
- Ability to remain calm under pressure and handle difficult conversations.
- Proficiency in using debt collection software and Microsoft Office Suite.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday leave.
- Inclusive benefits catering to diverse family needs.
- Professional development and career advancement opportunities.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.

Salary:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $60,000, based on your experience and skills.
