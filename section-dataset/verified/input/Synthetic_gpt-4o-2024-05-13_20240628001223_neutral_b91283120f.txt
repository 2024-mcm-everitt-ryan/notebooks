Company: Murphy-Robbins  
Role: Mapping Technician  

Company Background:  
Murphy-Robbins is a leading geospatial solutions provider dedicated to creating innovative mapping technologies that serve various industries. We pride ourselves on fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment that values diverse perspectives and skills.

Job Type:  
Full-Time  

Job Description:  
We are seeking a skilled and passionate Mapping Technician to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong skill set in geospatial technologies and a keen eye for detail. You will be responsible for creating accurate maps and conducting spatial analysis to support our clients' needs.

Responsibilities:  
- Create, update, and maintain maps and spatial data.
- Conduct spatial analysis and interpret geospatial data.
- Collaborate with team members to ensure data accuracy and consistency.
- Utilize GIS software and tools to generate high-quality maps.
- Assist in the development of mapping protocols and standards.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor's degree in Geography, Geospatial Science, or a related field.
- Proficiency in GIS software such as ArcGIS or QGIS.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Relevant experience in mapping and spatial analysis.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive salary range: €40,000 - €55,000, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance plans.
- Opportunities for professional development and continuing education.
- Flexible working hours and remote work options.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.

How to Apply:  
Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their resume and a cover letter detailing their relevant experience and qualifications.
