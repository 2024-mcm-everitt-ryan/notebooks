Full-time

Role:
Executive Agency Counsel

Job Description:
Nunez LLC is looking for an executive agency counsel to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for providing legal advice and counsel to our executive team, ensuring compliance with international and local regulations, and representing the company in high-stakes legal matters. This role requires a strategic thinker who can navigate complex legal landscapes and support our growth initiatives.

Responsibilities:
- Provide legal counsel and strategic guidance to the executive team.
- Draft, review, and negotiate a wide range of legal documents and contracts.
- Represent the company in legal proceedings, including mediations and arbitrations.
- Ensure compliance with international, federal, and state regulations.
- Collaborate with internal departments to minimize legal risks.
- Stay updated on changes in laws and regulations that affect the company.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Juris Doctor (JD) degree from an accredited law school.
- Licensed to practice law in the relevant jurisdiction.
- Minimum of 7 years of experience in a corporate legal environment or law firm.
- Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Proven track record of managing complex legal issues and delivering results-oriented solutions.
- Background in marketing or related field is a plus.
