Company Background:
Reid-Peters is an innovative company focused on providing exceptional customer service and efficient operations. We pride ourselves on our inclusive and diverse workforce, striving to create an environment where every employee can thrive.

Job Type:
Full-Time Parking Lot Attendant

Job Description:
As a Parking Lot Attendant at Reid-Peters, you will be the first point of contact for customers, ensuring their experience starts and ends with a positive interaction. This role involves managing entry and exit points, assisting customers with parking inquiries, and maintaining the safety and order of the parking lot.

Responsibilities:
- Greet and assist customers as they enter and exit the parking lot.
- Monitor and manage traffic flow within the parking area.
- Provide information and directions to visitors.
- Report any incidents or issues to management promptly.
- Ensure the parking lot is clean and orderly at all times.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to stand and walk for extended periods.
- Previous customer service experience is a plus.
- We invite individuals with disabilities to apply and contribute to our diverse workforce.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $30,000 to $35,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Employee discount programs.
- Opportunities for career growth and advancement.
