Job Description:
Sandoval-Sanchez is seeking a meticulous and dedicated Social Science Research Assistant to join our team. The ideal candidate will support various research projects, contributing to data collection, analysis, and report writing. They will work closely with senior researchers to ensure the integrity and accuracy of all research activities.

Responsibilities:
- Assist in the design and implementation of research projects.
- Collect and analyze qualitative and quantitative data.
- Prepare and maintain detailed research records.
- Conduct literature reviews and summarize findings.
- Collaborate with team members to prepare reports and presentations.
- Ensure compliance with ethical standards and protocols.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Social Sciences or a related field.
- Strong analytical and critical thinking skills.
- Proficiency in data analysis software (e.g., SPSS, R, or similar).
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Attention to detail and strong organizational skills.
