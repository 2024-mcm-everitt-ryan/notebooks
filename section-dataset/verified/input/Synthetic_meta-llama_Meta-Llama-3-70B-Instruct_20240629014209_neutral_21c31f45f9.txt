Company: Turner-Kelly
Role: Community Associate

About Us:
Turner-Kelly is a dynamic organization dedicated to building strong community relationships. Our team is committed to fostering a culture of inclusivity, respect, and open communication.

Job Description:
We are seeking a Community Associate to join our team. As a Community Associate, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining relationships with community partners, organizations, and stakeholders. Responsibilities:

 Develop and maintain relationships with community partners, organizations, and stakeholders
 Identify opportunities for collaboration and partnership
 Provide exceptional customer service to internal and external stakeholders
 Promote our brand within the community through events, social media, and other channels
 Collaborate with the team to achieve business objectives

Qualifications and Experience:

 Have a consistent and reliable employment history
 We value dedication and perseverance in our employees
 Seeking a professional representative to be the face of our brand
 Continuous professional experience is valued
 Be able to work efficiently within the job requirements

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. Our team enjoys a dynamic work environment, opportunities for professional growth, and a culture that values work-life balance.
