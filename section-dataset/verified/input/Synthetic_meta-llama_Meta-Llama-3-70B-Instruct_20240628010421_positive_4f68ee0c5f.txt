Company: Anderson, Castillo and Hamilton

Job Title: Environmental Health & Safety Regional Manager

About Us:
Anderson, Castillo and Hamilton is a leading environmental consulting firm dedicated to promoting sustainable practices and ensuring the well-being of our communities. We are committed to building a diverse and inclusive workforce that reflects the communities we serve.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Environmental Health & Safety Regional Manager to join our team. As a Regional Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing the development and implementation of environmental health and safety policies, procedures, and programs across our regional offices. You will work closely with our operations team to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement environmental health and safety policies, procedures, and programs
 Conduct risk assessments and audits to identify areas for improvement
 Collaborate with operations teams to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards
 Provide training and guidance to employees on environmental health and safety best practices
 Manage and respond to environmental health and safety incidents

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Health and Safety, or a related field
 5+ years of experience in environmental health and safety management
 Certification in OSHA, ISO 14001, or equivalent
 Strong knowledge of environmental health and safety regulations and industry standards
 Excellent communication and leadership skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $85,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. In addition to a competitive salary, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
