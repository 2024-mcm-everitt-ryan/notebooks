Company: Conley, Williams and Brewer

Role: GIS Specialist

Company Background:
Conley, Williams and Brewer is a leading environmental consultancy firm specializing in geographic information systems (GIS) solutions. Our team is committed to delivering innovative and sustainable solutions for our clients while fostering an inclusive and dynamic work environment.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented and motivated GIS Specialist to join our team. As a GIS Specialist, you will be responsible for analyzing spatial data, creating maps, and providing GIS support to various projects. This role offers a unique opportunity to work on environmental projects that have a significant impact on our communities.

Responsibilities:
- Analyze and interpret spatial data to support project objectives.
- Create and manage GIS databases, ensuring data accuracy and integrity.
- Develop and maintain digital maps using GIS software.
- Collaborate with project teams to provide GIS insights and recommendations.
- Conduct fieldwork to collect spatial data when necessary.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Geography, Environmental Science, or a related field.
- Proficiency in GIS software such as ArcGIS, QGIS, or similar platforms.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
- Previous experience in environmental consulting is a plus.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Flexible work arrangements to support work-life balance.
