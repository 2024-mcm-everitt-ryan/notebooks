Company: Roberson and Sons

Role: Third Party Services Analyst

About Us:
Roberson and Sons is a dynamic and forward-thinking organization dedicated to delivering exceptional services to our clients. We believe in fostering a culture of inclusivity, respect, and collaboration.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Third Party Services Analyst to join our team. As a Third Party Services Analyst, you will be responsible for analyzing and optimizing our third-party services, ensuring seamless integration and maximum efficiency.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and reduce costs
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure effective communication and integration
 Conduct thorough risk assessments and implement mitigation strategies
 Provide data-driven insights and recommendations to senior management

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Operations, or related field
 3+ years of experience in a similar role, preferably in a fast-paced and dynamic environment
 Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Proficient in MS Office and Google Suite
