Company: King, Young and Stokes

Job Title: Program Administrator

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and results-driven Program Administrator to join our team. As a Program Administrator, you will be responsible for managing program operations, coordinating events, and providing administrative support to our program managers.

Responsibilities:

 Manage program budgets and financial reports
 Coordinate logistics for program events and meetings
 Provide administrative support to program managers
 Develop and implement program operations processes and procedures
 Analyze program data and prepare reports

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Management, or related field
 At least 2 years of experience in program administration or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Excellent communication and organizational skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, paid time off, and opportunities for professional development.
