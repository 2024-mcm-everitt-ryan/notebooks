A Lobby Hostess provides service for Guests to contribute to an overall exceptional experience from check-in through check-out and complete audits, as required. A Lobby Hostess contributes to the first impression of our Guests and, therefore, must perform the following tasks to the highest standards:

- Meeting, greeting, and escorting guests to the table, serving the menu.
- Providing effective and cultural services to guests of a restaurant, bar, banquet hall and creating comfortable conditions for them.
- Informing the guest about ongoing promotions and events held in the hotel restaurant and bar.
- Ensuring cleanliness and order in the hall, at the workplace, and storage facilities.
- Work with complaints and complaints of guests.

Requirements:
- Fluent Uzbek/Russian and English. Other language would be beneficial.
- Excellent communication skills.
- Ability to work under pressure.
- Restaurant greeter experience preferred.

Working conditions:
- Attractive and competitive salary.
- Full-time employment, according to the schedule.
- Personal and professional development opportunities.
- Convenient office location.
