Remote Learning Team Specialist

Company Background:
Mckinney, Bernard and Santana is a leading education technology firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions for remote learning. Our team is committed to creating engaging and accessible online educational experiences.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Remote Learning Team Specialist to join our team. As a Remote Learning Team Specialist, you will be responsible for designing and developing online learning modules, collaborating with instructors to create engaging course content, and ensuring seamless delivery of our online programs.

Responsibilities:

 Design and develop online learning modules that meet the needs of diverse learners
 Collaborate with instructors to create engaging and interactive course content
 Ensure timely and effective delivery of online programs
 Troubleshoot technical issues and provide technical support to instructors and students
 Analyze student feedback and course metrics to inform continuous improvement

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Education, Instructional Design, or a related field
 At least 2 years of experience in instructional design, online learning, or a related field
 Proven track record of developing engaging and effective online learning experiences
 Strong technical skills, including experience with learning management systems and multimedia tools
 Excellent communication and project management skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program.

Note: A reliable internet connection and a quiet workspace are required for this remote position. We apologize, but we are unable to accommodate candidates who require frequent travel or have limited access to technology.
