Company Background: Boyd PLC is a leading engineering firm specializing in innovative structural solutions. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and diverse workplace, especially focusing on advancing females in the engineering industry.

Job Title: Structural Engineer

Job Description: As a Structural Engineer at Boyd PLC, you will be responsible for designing, analyzing, and overseeing the construction of various structural projects. This role demands a proactive and detail-oriented engineer who can work effectively within a collaborative team environment.

Responsibilities:
- Design and analyze structural components for commercial and residential projects.
- Collaborate with architects, contractors, and other engineers.
- Ensure compliance with industry standards and local regulations.
- Conduct site inspections and provide technical guidance during construction.
- Prepare detailed reports and presentations on project progress.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Civil or Structural Engineering.
- Minimum of 3 years experience in structural engineering.
- Proficiency in AutoCAD, Revit, and other structural analysis software.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £45,000 to £65,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance coverage.
- Professional development and training opportunities.
- Supportive work-life balance with flexible working hours.
- Opportunities for career advancement, particularly for female employees.

Application Process: Boyd PLC prioritises female candidates for this role as part of our ongoing initiative to promote women in the engineering industry. We encourage all qualified applicants, especially women, to apply and contribute to our diverse team.

Join us in creating innovative structural solutions while advancing female representation in engineering.
