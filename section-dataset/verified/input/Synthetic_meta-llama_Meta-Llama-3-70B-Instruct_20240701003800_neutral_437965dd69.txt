Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Early Childhood Education or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in early childhood education consulting or a related field
 Proven track record of success in developing and implementing early childhood education programs
 Excellent communication, interpersonal, and project management skills
