Company: Turner LLC

Role: Kettle Operator

Company Background: Turner LLC is a leading manufacturer in the food processing industry, committed to quality and excellence in all our products. We value diversity, inclusion, and innovation in our workforce and strive to maintain a supportive and dynamic work environment.

Job Description: Turner LLC is seeking knowledgeable professionals to operate our industrial kettles. The Kettle Operator will be responsible for managing the cooking process, ensuring product consistency, and maintaining safety and sanitation standards. This role is integral to our production line and requires attention to detail, adherence to recipes, and a commitment to quality.

Responsibilities:
- Operate and monitor industrial kettles during the cooking process
- Measure and mix ingredients following the company's recipes and safety guidelines
- Maintain cleanliness and sanitation of equipment and work area
- Record production data and report any discrepancies or issues
- Collaborate with team members to meet production targets

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience in a similar role within the food industry
- Capability to follow detailed instructions and protocols
- Strong attention to detail and commitment to maintaining high standards
- Ability to work efficiently in a fast-paced environment
- Basic understanding of safety and quality control procedures
