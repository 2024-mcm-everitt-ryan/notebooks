Company: Park-Brown

Role: Baker

Company Background:
Park-Brown is a renowned bakery chain committed to delivering high-quality baked goods crafted with passion and precision. With a legacy of excellence, we pride ourselves on our dedication to tradition while embracing innovative baking techniques.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled baker to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a deep passion for baking, possess relevant experience in a professional baking environment, and demonstrate expertise in creating a variety of baked goods. This role requires collaboration, creativity, and meticulous attention to detail.

Responsibilities:
- Prepare and bake a diverse range of bakery products, including bread, pastries, and cakes.
- Follow recipes and maintain consistency in quality and taste.
- Ensure cleanliness and organization of the baking area.
- Monitor inventory and place orders for ingredients as needed.
- Collaborate with the team to develop new recipes and products.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Relevant experience in a professional baking environment.
- Strong knowledge of baking techniques and ingredients.
- Ability to work efficiently and manage time effectively.
- Passionate professionals with a creative mindset.
- Required skill set includes attention to detail and the ability to follow recipes accurately.
- Experienced professionals encouraged to apply.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $35,000 to $50,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Employee discount on bakery products.

To Apply:
Please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing your relevant experience and why you are passionate about joining Park-Brown.

Park-Brown is an equal opportunity employer and values diversity in the workplace. We encourage all qualified individuals to apply.
