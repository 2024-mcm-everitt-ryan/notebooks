Company: Brown, Dickerson and Perez

Job Title: Advertising and Promotions Manager

Job Description:
Brown, Dickerson and Perez is seeking a highly motivated and creative Advertising and Promotions Manager to join our team. As an Advertising and Promotions Manager, you will be responsible for developing and implementing innovative advertising campaigns that drive brand awareness and sales. Our ideal candidate is a team player with excellent communication and project management skills.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and execute comprehensive advertising strategies across multiple channels
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to create cohesive brand messaging
 Manage and optimize advertising budgets to achieve maximum ROI
 Analyze campaign performance and provide data-driven recommendations for improvement

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in advertising and promotions
 Proven track record of developing and executing successful advertising campaigns
 Excellent project management and communication skills
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement matching, and generous paid time off. Our team is dedicated to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment, and we are actively seeking professionals from the LGBTQ+ community to join our team.
