Company Background:
Arnold and Sons is a renowned culinary establishment known for its innovative cuisine and exceptional dining experience. We take pride in our diverse team and inclusive work culture.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Arnold and Sons is seeking a dedicated and experienced Head Cook to join our dynamic team. This role is perfect for someone who is passionate about the culinary arts and eager to lead a diverse team in a fast-paced kitchen environment. We are particularly focused on enhancing our team diversity by actively seeking female candidates to join our leadership team.

Responsibilities:
- Lead kitchen operations, ensuring high standards of food quality and safety.
- Develop and innovate new menu items, collaborating with the kitchen staff.
- Manage kitchen staff, providing training and mentorship.
- Oversee inventory, ordering, and stock control.
- Ensure compliance with health and safety regulations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a Head Cook or similar role in a high-end restaurant.
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Excellent understanding of various cooking methods, ingredients, equipment, and procedures.
- Culinary school diploma or equivalent preferred.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and handle pressure.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from USD 60,000 to USD 80,000, based on experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off, including vacation and sick leave.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- Employee discounts at all Arnold and Sons locations.

Arnold and Sons is committed to fair and equitable pay practices. We value the contributions of all employees and are dedicated to creating an inclusive and supportive work environment.
