Company Background:
The Jordan Group is a leading healthcare provider dedicated to delivering exceptional patient care and promoting a culture of diversity, equity, and inclusion.

Job Type: Supervising Health Nurse

Job Description:
We are seeking a compassionate and experienced Supervising Health Nurse to join our team. As a Supervising Health Nurse, you will be responsible for overseeing the daily operations of our healthcare facility, ensuring the delivery of high-quality patient care, and mentoring junior staff members.

Responsibilities:

 Oversee the daily operations of the healthcare facility, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and organizational policies
 Develop and implement effective care plans, collaborating with multidisciplinary teams to deliver exceptional patient outcomes
 Provide mentorship and guidance to junior staff members, promoting professional development and growth
 Foster a culture of inclusivity, respect, and empathy, ensuring that all patients and staff members feel valued and supported

Qualifications and Experience:
 Registered Nurse with a minimum of 5 years of experience in a supervisory role
 Strong leadership and communication skills, with the ability to motivate and inspire teams
 Experience working with diverse patient populations, including LGBTQ+ individuals
 Commitment to ongoing professional development and staying current with industry best practices

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage
 Opportunities for professional growth and development, including training and education reimbursement
 A dynamic and supportive work environment that values diversity, equity, and inclusion
