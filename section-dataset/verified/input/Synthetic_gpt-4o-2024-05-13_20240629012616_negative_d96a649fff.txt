Company: Brown Group  
Role: Insulation Worker  

Company Background:  
Brown Group is a leader in the construction industry, providing top-notch insulation solutions to residential and commercial clients. Our team prides itself on delivering excellent service and maintaining the highest standards in all our projects.

Job Type: Full-Time  

Job Description:  
We are looking for a dedicated and hardworking insulation worker to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for installing, repairing, and maintaining insulation materials in various settings, ensuring energy efficiency and comfort for our clients.

Responsibilities:  
- Install insulation materials in walls, floors, and ceilings.
- Measure and cut insulation to fit specific spaces.
- Seal gaps and seams with appropriate materials.
- Maintain tools and equipment in good working condition.
- Follow safety guidelines and regulations at all times.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- High school diploma or equivalent preferred.
- Previous experience in construction or insulation work.
- Candidates must have no history of criminal activity.
- Ability to handle physical tasks and work in varying conditions.
- Strong work ethic and reliability.

Perks and Benefits:  
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 25,000 to USD 35,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for career advancement.

How to Apply:  
Interested candidates should submit their resume and a brief cover letter explaining their qualifications and experience relevant to this role.
