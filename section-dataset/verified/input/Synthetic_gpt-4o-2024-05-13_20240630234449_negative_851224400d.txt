Company Background:
Lee-Young is a prominent courier service provider, known for its reliability and efficiency in delivering crucial documents and packages. We cater to both corporate and individual clients, ensuring timely and secure deliveries.

Job Type:
Full-Time Process Messenger

Job Description:
Lee-Young is seeking a motivated and dynamic individual to join our team as a Process Messenger. The ideal candidate will be responsible for transporting documents and parcels between various locations, ensuring prompt and secure deliveries. This role requires attention to detail, punctuality, and commitment to maintaining our high standards of service.

Responsibilities:
- Safely transport documents and parcels to designated locations.
- Maintain accurate records of deliveries and pick-ups.
- Ensure timely and secure delivery of all items.
- Communicate effectively with clients and team members.
- Handle urgent and confidential documents with the utmost care.
- Report any issues or delays promptly to the supervisor.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Previous experience in a similar role is valuable.
- Strong background in manpower planning is essential.
- Must be physically fit to handle the demands of the job.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work independently and handle multiple tasks.
- Valid driver's license required.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 35,000 to USD 50,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for career advancement within the company.
- Training and development programs to enhance your skills.
