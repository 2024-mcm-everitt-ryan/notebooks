Company: Matthews, Lin and Thomas

About Us:

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented and motivated Wireless Optimization Intern to join our team. As a Wireless Optimization Intern, you will assist in analyzing and optimizing wireless networks to ensure seamless connectivity and exceptional customer experience.

- Develop and maintain technical documentation to support optimization efforts

Qualifications:
- Currently pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field
- Strong understanding of wireless network fundamentals and optimization principles
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work effectively in a team environment
