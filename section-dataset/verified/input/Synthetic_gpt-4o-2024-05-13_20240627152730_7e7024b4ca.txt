Company Background:
Wade, Wagner, and Davis is a dynamic law firm committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive environment. We believe that diversity enriches our perspectives and enhances our ability to serve clients globally.

Job Type:
Internship

Job Description:
We are seeking a passionate and detail-oriented Book Search and Scholar Intern to join our team. This role will involve researching and cataloging scholarly materials, assisting in the maintenance of our legal library, and contributing to various knowledge management projects.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct comprehensive searches for legal texts and scholarly articles.
- Assist in organizing and cataloging library resources.
- Support the development and updating of the firm's knowledge base.
- Collaborate with team members on special research projects.
- Ensure all materials are accurately referenced and accessible.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Currently enrolled in or recent graduate of a library science or related program.
- Strong research and analytical skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication abilities.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Familiarity with legal databases and research tools is a plus.

Perks and Benefits:
- Flexible working hours.
- Access to a vast array of legal resources and materials.
- Opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a professional legal environment.
- Mentorship and guidance from experienced professionals.
- A supportive and inclusive workplace culture.
- Hourly wage of $15.00.
