Company Background:
Gomez LLC is a dynamic and forward-thinking marketing firm specializing in innovative promotional strategies. We partner with leading brands to amplify their market presence and drive customer engagement through creative and impactful campaigns.

Job Type:
Full-time Product Promoter

Job Description:
Gomez LLC is seeking a motivated and energetic Product Promoter to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for representing our clients' products in various settings, driving brand awareness, and increasing sales through direct customer engagement.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct product demonstrations in retail environments and at events.
- Engage with potential customers to provide product information and answer questions.
- Collect customer feedback and report on product performance.
- Collaborate with the marketing team to develop promotional strategies.
- Maintain a professional appearance and demeanor at all times.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent required; bachelor's degree preferred.
- Previous experience in sales, marketing, or customer service is a plus.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.

Perks and Benefits:
- Total compensation package includes a salary of USD 30,000 - 40,000 per year, depending on experience.
- Opportunities for performance-based bonuses.
- Comprehensive benefits package including health insurance, paid time off, and retirement savings plan.
- Professional development and growth opportunities.
