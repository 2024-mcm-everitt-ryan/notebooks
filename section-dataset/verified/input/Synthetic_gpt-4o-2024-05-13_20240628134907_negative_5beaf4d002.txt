Company Background:
Miller, Adams and Gray is a leading consultancy specializing in safety audits for various industries. We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of safety and regulatory compliance.

Job Type:
Safety Auditor

Job Description:
The Safety Auditor will be responsible for conducting thorough safety audits, analyzing potential hazards, and ensuring compliance with relevant safety regulations. The role requires a detail-oriented professional who can work independently and manage multiple tasks efficiently.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct comprehensive safety audits and inspections.
- Prepare detailed reports on findings and recommend corrective actions.
- Collaborate with team members to implement safety improvements.
- Ensure compliance with all safety regulations and standards.
- Participate in physical team-building activities to foster team cohesion.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- A Bachelor's degree in Occupational Safety, Environmental Health, or a related field.
- At least 5 years of experience in safety auditing or a related role.
- Must be able to work independently with minimal supervision.
- Unrestricted physical mobility required.
- Ability to maintain a rapid work pace and meet strict deadlines.
- Must be able to participate in physical fitness activities with the team.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 70,000 to USD 90,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Generous paid time off and holidays.
