Company: Roth-Griffith  
Role: Brickmason  

Company Background:  
Roth-Griffith is a renowned construction company known for delivering high-quality masonry work. We specialize in residential and commercial projects, ensuring that our craftsmanship stands the test of time.

Job Type:  
Full-Time Brickmason  

Job Description and Responsibilities:  
We are seeking a highly skilled brickmason to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for constructing and repairing walls, arches, and other structures using bricks, tiles, and other materials. Duties include reading blueprints, measuring and cutting materials, and ensuring the structural integrity of completed projects. 

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Proven experience as a brickmason.
- Excellent physical condition and stamina; able to lift heavy materials.
- Must have a clean personal history with no history of addiction or substance abuse.
- Strong work ethic; we are not looking for clock watchers or those just seeking a 9-to-5 routine.
- Capable of working in a fast-paced environment without being a "wimp."
- Applicants must not have visible tattoos or body art.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive salary ranging from 30,000 to 45,000 USD annually, based on experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and growth within the company.
