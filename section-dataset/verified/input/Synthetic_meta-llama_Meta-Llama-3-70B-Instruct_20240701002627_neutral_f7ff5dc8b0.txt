Company: Andrade LLC
Role: Animal Caretaker

We are seeking a compassionate and skilled Animal Caretaker to join our team! As an Animal Caretaker, you will be responsible for providing exceptional care to our furry friends, ensuring their health, happiness, and well-being.

Responsibilities:

 Feed, clean, and provide fresh water for animals
 Monitor animal behavior and report any changes to the veterinarian
 Clean and maintain animal enclosures
 Assist with animal grooming and handling
 Provide enrichment activities for animals
 Collaborate with the veterinarian to develop and implement animal care plans

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience working with animals (volunteer or paid)
 Ability to lift 50 pounds
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 A fun and supportive work environment

If you are passionate about animal welfare and have a strong work ethic, we encourage you to apply!
