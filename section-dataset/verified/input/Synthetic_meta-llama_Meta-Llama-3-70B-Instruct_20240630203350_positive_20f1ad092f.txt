Company: Leon and Sons
Role: Dental Assistant

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and dedicated Dental Assistant to join our team at Leon and Sons. As a Dental Assistant, you will play a vital role in supporting our dentists and hygienists in providing top-notch patient care. If you're passionate about delivering exceptional service and are looking for a dynamic work environment, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Assist dentists and hygienists with patient exams, treatments, and procedures
 Prepare treatment rooms and sterilize equipment
 Take and develop dental x-rays
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date patient records
 Provide excellent patient care and communication

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience as a Dental Assistant
 Certification in Dental Assisting (CDA) or Radiology Certification
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and opportunities for professional development. As part of our commitment to diversity and inclusion, we are particularly interested in receiving applications from women who are passionate about advancing their careers in the dental industry.
