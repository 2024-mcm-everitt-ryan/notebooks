Company: Munoz, Goodwin and Anderson

Job Type: Home Health Aide

Job Description:
As a Home Health Aide at Munoz, Goodwin and Anderson, you will play a vital role in providing high-quality care to our clients in the comfort of their own homes. You will be responsible for assisting clients with daily living activities, managing their medication, and performing light housekeeping tasks. If you are a compassionate and dedicated individual who is passionate about delivering exceptional care, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Provide personalized care to clients, including assistance with daily living activities, meal preparation, and light housekeeping
 Manage client medication schedules and administer medication as needed
 Monitor client vital signs and report any changes to the care team
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with clients and their families
 Maintain accurate records of client care and submit reports to the care team

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 Completion of a certified home health aide training program
 1-2 years of experience as a home health aide
 Ability to lift 25 pounds and stand for long periods
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and opportunities for professional development.
