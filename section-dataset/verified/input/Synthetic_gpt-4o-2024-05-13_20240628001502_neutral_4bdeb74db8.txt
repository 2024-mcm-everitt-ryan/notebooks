Job Description and Responsibilities:
We are seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented Legal Coordinator to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for coordinating legal activities, managing case files, and assisting attorneys in preparing for trials. Responsibilities include:
- Coordinating schedules, meetings, and legal proceedings.
- Managing and organizing legal documents and case files.
- Assisting attorneys with trial preparation and court filings.
- Conducting legal research and gathering relevant information.
- Ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.
- Communicating effectively with clients and other stakeholders.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in a related field or equivalent experience.
- Prior experience in a legal environment is preferred.
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Ability to handle confidential information with discretion.
- Proficiency in legal research and document management software.
