Job Title: Staff Photographer
Company: Scott PLC

About Us:
Scott PLC is a renowned photography studio dedicated to capturing life's precious moments. Our team of talented photographers and editors work together to create stunning visual stories that evoke emotions and inspire creativity.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and creative Staff Photographer to join our team. As a Staff Photographer, you will be responsible for capturing high-quality images that meet our clients' expectations. Your exceptional attention to detail, creative vision, and ability to work well under pressure will make you a valuable asset to our team.

Responsibilities:

 Capture high-quality images for clients using various photography equipment and techniques
 Collaborate with clients to understand their vision and preferences
 Edit and retouch images to ensure they meet our studio's standards
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in photography, preferably in a studio setting
 Strong portfolio showcasing your creative and technical skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work well under pressure and meet deadlines

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary range of $50,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. You will also receive a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
