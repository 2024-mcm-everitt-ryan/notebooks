Job Title: Communication Electrician

Company Background:
Harrell, Stewart and Anderson is a leading provider of electrical solutions, dedicated to delivering innovative and reliable services to our clients. With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we are seeking a skilled Communication Electrician to join our team.

Job Description:
The Communication Electrician will be responsible for designing, installing, and maintaining electrical communication systems for our clients. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in electrical engineering and a proven track record of delivering successful projects.

Responsibilities:

 Design and install electrical communication systems for clients
 Conduct site visits to diagnose and repair electrical issues
 Collaborate with project managers to ensure timely completion of projects
 Develop and maintain documentation of electrical systems

Qualifications and Experience:
We are seeking individuals with a minimum of 11 years of professional experience in electrical engineering. A long history of industry engagement and proven success over a long period are highly valued. Candidates with certifications in electrical engineering and a strong understanding of safety protocols are preferred.

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $85,000 to $110,000 USD, based on experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and provide opportunities for professional growth and development.
