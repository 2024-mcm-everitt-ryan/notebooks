Company: Wilson, Harris and Wolfe
Role: Constituent Services Liaison

Job Description:
As a Constituent Services Liaison, you will serve as the primary point of contact between our organization and the community. You will be responsible for developing and implementing strategies to build strong relationships with constituents, responding to their inquiries, and providing exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and maintain relationships with constituents, community groups, and local organizations
 Respond to constituent inquiries and concerns in a timely and professional manner
 Collaborate with internal teams to resolve constituent issues and provide timely updates
 Analyze and track constituent feedback to inform organizational decisions
 Develop and implement strategies to improve constituent engagement and satisfaction

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Public Relations, Communications, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in constituent services, public relations, or a related field
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Strong attention to detail and organizational skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
