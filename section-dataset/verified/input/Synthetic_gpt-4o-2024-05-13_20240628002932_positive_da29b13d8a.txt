Company Background:
Castaneda Ltd is a forward-thinking company committed to fostering inclusive policies and innovative communication strategies. Our goal is to create a diverse and dynamic workforce that reflects the community we serve.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
The Assistant Commissioner of Communications and Policy will play a critical role in shaping and implementing our communication strategies and policy initiatives. This role requires a highly skilled professional with a passion for diversity and inclusion.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement comprehensive communication plans.
- Collaborate with various departments to ensure consistent messaging.
- Monitor and analyze public policy trends affecting the company.
- Advocate for and integrate inclusive practices within company policies.
- Engage with stakeholders and community partners to enhance the company’s public image.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Communications, Public Policy, or a related field.
- At least 5 years of experience in a similar role.
- Strong understanding of public policy and communication strategies.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Demonstrated commitment to diversity, particularly within the LGBTQ+ community.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $70,000 to $90,000, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health benefits including medical, dental, and vision.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- Inclusive and supportive work environment, with a focus on increasing queer representation.

Our Commitment to Diversity:
We are dedicated to increasing the presence of LGBTQ+ individuals in our workforce and encourage those who identify as such to apply.
