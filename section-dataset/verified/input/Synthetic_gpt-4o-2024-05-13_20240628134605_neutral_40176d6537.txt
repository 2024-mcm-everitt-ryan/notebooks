Company: Hernandez, Baker and Lambert

Role: Correctional Officers

Company Background:
Hernandez, Baker and Lambert is a leading firm in legal and correctional services, dedicated to upholding justice and promoting rehabilitation. We are committed to diversity and inclusion, ensuring a supportive environment for all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking dedicated Correctional Officers to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong sense of duty, integrity, and a commitment to maintaining a safe and secure environment for all individuals within the correctional facility. 

Responsibilities:
- Ensure the safety and security of inmates and staff.
- Conduct regular inspections and patrols within the facility.
- Enforce facility rules and regulations consistently.
- Respond promptly and effectively to emergencies.
- Document incidents and maintain accurate records.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent required.
- Previous experience in law enforcement or corrections is an asset but not required.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to remain calm and composed under pressure.
- Must pass a background check and physical exam.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $60,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and sick leave.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.
- Inclusive hiring process and workplace environment.

Commitment to Inclusion:
Our team values accessibility and diversity. We offer an inclusive hiring process and promote a culture of respect and inclusion. The company supports a diverse range of abilities, ensuring that all employees have the resources they need to succeed.
