Company: Salazar, Green and Welch
Role: Recreation and Fitness Worker

Company Background:
Salazar, Green and Welch is a leading wellness and fitness company dedicated to promoting a healthy lifestyle. Our inclusive environment supports employees of all backgrounds and encourages holistic well-being.

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Recreation and Fitness Worker to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for planning, organizing, and leading various recreational activities and fitness programs.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement fitness programs tailored to different age groups.
- Lead group fitness classes and provide individual training sessions.
- Ensure the safety and well-being of all participants.
- Maintain fitness equipment and facilities.
- Motivate and support participants in achieving their fitness goals.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- A degree or certification in physical education, kinesiology, or a related field.
- Experience in leading fitness classes or recreational activities.
- Strong communication and motivational skills.
- CPR and First Aid certification preferred.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid parental leave for all parents.
- Inclusive policies supporting LGBTQIA employees.
- Salary ranging from USD 40,000 to USD 60,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
