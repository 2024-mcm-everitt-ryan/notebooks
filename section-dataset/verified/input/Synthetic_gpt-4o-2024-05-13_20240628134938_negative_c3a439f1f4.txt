Company Background:
Smith-Smith is a leading urban planning firm dedicated to creating sustainable and efficient city environments. We pride ourselves on our innovative approach and dedication to improving urban spaces for communities.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Smith-Smith is seeking a dedicated Rezoning Manager to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will manage rezoning projects, ensuring compliance with local regulations and driving sustainable urban development.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee rezoning applications and manage the process from inception to approval.
- Coordinate with local government bodies, stakeholders, and community groups.
- Conduct site visits to assess project feasibility and compliance.
- Develop detailed reports and presentations for stakeholders.
- Navigate tight spaces and high shelves to access necessary documents and materials.
- Travel to various locations and drive a company vehicle as needed.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Urban Planning, Civil Engineering, or related field.
- Minimum of 5 years experience in zoning, land use planning, or related area.
- Strong understanding of local zoning regulations and land use laws.
- Ability to push and pull objects weighing up to 40 pounds.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Must be able to run or jog as needed for site inspections.
- Flexibility to work in varying environments and conditions.
