Company: Morrison, Kelly and Todd
Role: Buyer

At Morrison, Kelly and Todd, we are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace culture. As a buyer, you will play a critical role in sourcing high-quality products and services that meet our customers' needs. Job Description:
As a buyer, you will be responsible for:

 Sourcing and purchasing products and services that meet our customers' needs
 Building and maintaining relationships with suppliers
 Negotiating prices and terms to ensure the best possible deals
 Collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless delivery of products and services

Responsibilities and Qualifications:

 2+ years of experience in procurement or a related field
 Strong communication and negotiation skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Bachelor's degree in business, supply chain management, or a related field

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings, and paid time off.
