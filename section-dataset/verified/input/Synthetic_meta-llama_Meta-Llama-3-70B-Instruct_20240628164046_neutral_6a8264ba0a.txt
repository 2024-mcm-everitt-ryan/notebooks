Senior Safety Accident Investigator at Diaz PLC

Company Background:
Diaz PLC is a leading provider of safety solutions, committed to ensuring a safe working environment for our clients and employees. We believe that our diverse and inclusive workforce is key to driving innovation and excellence in our industry.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Safety Accident Investigator to lead our team in conducting thorough and objective investigations into safety incidents. The successful candidate will have a strong background in safety management and investigation techniques, with the ability to analyze complex data and provide actionable recommendations to prevent future incidents.

Responsibilities:

 Lead and conduct safety incident investigations to determine root causes and identify areas for improvement
 Develop and implement corrective actions to prevent future incidents
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to implement safety recommendations and initiatives
 Provide training and guidance to junior investigators and other stakeholders
 Develop and maintain reports, records, and databases related to safety incidents and investigations

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Occupational Health and Safety, Engineering, or a related field
 At least 5 years of experience in safety management, accident investigation, or a related field
 Proven experience leading investigations and developing corrective actions
 Strong analytical, communication, and problem-solving skills

We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
