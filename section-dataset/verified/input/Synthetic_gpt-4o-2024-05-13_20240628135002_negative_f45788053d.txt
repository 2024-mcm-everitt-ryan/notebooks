Company Background:
At Melendez, Thompson, and Cook, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional procurement solutions and fostering strong relationships with our suppliers. Our culture of excellence drives us to innovate and continually exceed client expectations.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dynamic Buyer Services Manager to lead our procurement team and manage supplier relationships. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in procurement, exceptional negotiation skills, and the ability to work independently with minimal supervision.

Responsibilities:
- Overseeing the procurement process and managing buyer services.
- Negotiating contracts and terms with suppliers.
- Ensuring compliance with internal and external regulations.
- Developing and implementing procurement strategies.
- Participating in physical fitness activities with the team to promote a healthy workplace.
- Regularly climbing stairs throughout the day to access different departments.
- Performing repetitive tasks accurately and efficiently.
- Working independently with minimal supervision.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Supply Chain Management, or related field.
- At least 5 years of experience in procurement or a similar role.
- Strong negotiation and contract management skills.
- Ability to work independently and handle complex tasks.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Physically fit to handle frequent bending and stooping.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from €50,000 to €70,000 per year, based on experience and skills.
- Health and wellness programs.
- Opportunities for professional development and advancement.
- A supportive and collaborative work environment.
