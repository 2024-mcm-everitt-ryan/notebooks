Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Web Designer / Art Director to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will oversee the design and execution of visually stunning web projects, ensuring they meet our clients' needs and align with our brand’s high standards.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and manage web design projects from concept to completion.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure cohesive design integration.
- Develop and maintain design systems and standards.
- Ensure designs are tailored to our clientele’s needs and preferences.
- Mentor and provide guidance to junior designers.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design, Web Design, or a related field.
- At least 5 years of experience in web design and art direction.
- Proficiency in design software such as Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, and Figma.
- Native proficiency in Dutch to effectively communicate with our European partners.
- Must be familiar with business practices common in our industry and geographies.
- Strong portfolio showcasing previous web design projects.
- Excellent leadership and project management skills.
