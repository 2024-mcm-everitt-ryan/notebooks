Company Background:
Velez PLC is a leading organisation dedicated to promoting accessibility and inclusivity in all aspects of life. We are committed to creating a workplace culture that values diversity, equity, and inclusion.

Job Type: Accessibility Outreach Coordinator

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and passionate Accessibility Outreach Coordinator to join our team. As an Accessibility Outreach Coordinator, you will play a crucial role in developing and implementing outreach strategies to promote accessibility and inclusivity in our community. You will work closely with our team to identify and address accessibility barriers, develop partnerships with community organisations, and provide education and resources to promote accessibility.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement outreach strategies to promote accessibility and inclusivity in our community
 Identify and address accessibility barriers in our programs and services
 Build and maintain partnerships with community organisations to promote accessibility
 Provide education and resources to promote accessibility and inclusivity
 Collaborate with our team to develop and implement accessibility initiatives

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g. social work, education, community development)
 2+ years of experience in outreach, community development, or a related field
 Strong knowledge of accessibility principles and practices
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £35,000 to £45,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings, and paid time off. Our organisation values and prioritises LGBTQ+ inclusion in all aspects of our work, and we eagerly recruit candidates from diverse backgrounds to apply.
