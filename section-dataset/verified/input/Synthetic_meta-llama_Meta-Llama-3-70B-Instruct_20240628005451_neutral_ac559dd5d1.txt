Company: Duran-Bowers

Job Title: Legal Coordinator

About Us:
Duran-Bowers is a leading law firm committed to delivering exceptional legal services to our clients. We pride ourselves on our diverse and inclusive workplace culture, where everyone has a voice and an opportunity to grow.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Legal Coordinator to join our team. As a Legal Coordinator, you will be responsible for providing administrative support to our lawyers, managing case files, and ensuring seamless communication with clients.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to lawyers, including preparing legal documents, managing calendars, and coordinating travel arrangements
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date case files, both physical and digital
 Develop and implement effective filing systems and procedures
 Liaise with clients, lawyers, and other stakeholders to ensure timely and effective communication
 Perform other administrative tasks as required

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a legal administrative role
 Post-secondary education in law, business, or a related field
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and legal software
 Excellent organizational, communication, and time management skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a generous paid time-off policy.

At Duran-Bowers, we value diversity, equity, and inclusion. We welcome applications from people of all gender identities and expressions. Our workplace culture respects and values the diversity of the LGBTIQQ community. We're looking for team members who share our values of inclusivity and respect.
