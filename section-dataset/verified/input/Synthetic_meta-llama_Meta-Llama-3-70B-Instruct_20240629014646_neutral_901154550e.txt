Company: Johnson-Boyd

Job Title: Drywall Installer

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and experienced Drywall Installer to join our team. As a Drywall Installer, you will be responsible for installing and finishing drywall for residential and commercial projects. You will work closely with our construction team to ensure high-quality workmanship and meet project deadlines.

Responsibilities:

 Install and finish drywall for residential and commercial projects
 Measure and cut drywall to fit around electrical outlets, switches, and other obstructions
 Apply joint compound and tape to seal joints
 Sand and finish drywall to a smooth surface
 Collaborate with construction team to ensure high-quality workmanship and meet project deadlines

Qualifications and Experience:

 Meet any necessary licensure requirements specific to the role
 Experience in skilled trades is an advantage
 Meet all legal requirements for employment
 A valid driver's licence is required if the role involves driving

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
 Paid time off and holidays
