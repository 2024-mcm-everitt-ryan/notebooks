Job Description:
We're seeking a skilled Blockmason to join our team. As a Blockmason at Young-Richards, you'll be responsible for laying and binding building materials, such as bricks, concrete blocks, and other materials, to construct and repair structures. You'll work closely with our construction team to ensure projects are completed to the highest standard.

Responsibilities:

 Lay and bind building materials to construct and repair structures
 Work closely with the construction team to ensure projects are completed to the highest standard
 Maintain a safe and clean work environment
 Collaborate with other team members to achieve project goals

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience as a Blockmason
 Proven track record of successful project completion
 Strong communication and teamwork skills
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and work at heights
