Company: Harrington, Richardson and Collins

Job Title: Strategic Partnership Representative

About Us:
Harrington, Richardson and Collins is a leading consulting firm that specializes in strategic partnerships and business development. We are committed to delivering exceptional results to our clients and fostering a collaborative work environment.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Strategic Partnership Representative to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for identifying and pursuing new business opportunities, building and maintaining strong relationships with clients, and developing strategic partnerships.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and execute business development strategies to drive revenue growth
- Identify and pursue new business opportunities through networking, research, and cold-calling
- Build and maintain strong relationships with clients and partners
- Collaborate with internal stakeholders to develop strategic partnerships
- Analyze market trends and competitor activity to inform business development strategies

Qualifications:
- 3+ years of experience in business development, sales, or a related field
- Proven track record of driving revenue growth and closing deals
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Fluency in English (spoken and written) is essential for this role

Benefits:
- Salary range: $70,000 - $90,000 per year
- Bonus structure based on performance
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Flexible working hours and remote work options

Note: For this role, we are looking for candidates with a strong understanding of Western cultural norms and values. Fluency in English without any accent is essential for effective communication with our clients.
