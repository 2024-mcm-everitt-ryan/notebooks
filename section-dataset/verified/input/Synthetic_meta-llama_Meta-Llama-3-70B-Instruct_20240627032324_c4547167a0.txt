Company: Thompson-Richards

Job Title: QA Test Lead

About Us:
Thompson-Richards is a leading software development company dedicated to creating innovative solutions for the tech industry. We value diversity and inclusivity in our workforce and are committed to fostering a culture of respect and empathy.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled QA Test Lead to join our quality assurance team. As a QA Test Lead, you will be responsible for developing and implementing testing strategies, leading a team of QA engineers, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure the delivery of high-quality products.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and execute comprehensive testing plans and strategies
- Lead and mentor a team of QA engineers to achieve project goals
- Collaborate with development teams to identify and resolve defects
- Analyze and report on testing results to improve product quality
- Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies

Qualifications:
- 5+ years of experience in QA testing, with at least 2 years in a leadership role
- Strong knowledge of software development life cycles and testing methodologies
- Excellent leadership and communication skills
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field

Benefits:
- Salary range: $80,000 - $100,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Flexible work arrangements, including remote work options

We value diversity and welcome candidates from Hispanic and Latino communities, as well as those from other underrepresented racial backgrounds. We believe that a diverse team is a strong team, and we are committed to creating an inclusive work environment where everyone can thrive.
