Job Title: Electrical Power-Line Installer
Company: Love, Larson and Vasquez

About Us:
Love, Larson and Vasquez is a leading energy solutions provider committed to delivering innovative and sustainable power systems. We prioritize a supportive and cooperative work environment, fostering a culture of inclusivity and respect.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Electrical Power-Line Installer to join our team. As a key member of our installation crew, you will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing electrical power lines, ensuring the safe and efficient transmission of electricity to our customers.

Responsibilities:

 Install, maintain, and repair electrical power lines, towers, and substations
 Conduct safety inspections and ensure compliance with industry regulations
 Collaborate with team members to achieve project goals and deadlines
 Troubleshoot and resolve electrical issues effectively
 Develop and maintain accurate records of work performed

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in electrical power-line installation and maintenance
 Strong understanding of electrical systems and safety protocols
 Ability to work at heights and in confined spaces
 Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
 Valid driver's license and willingness to travel for work

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 55,000 to USD 75,000, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
