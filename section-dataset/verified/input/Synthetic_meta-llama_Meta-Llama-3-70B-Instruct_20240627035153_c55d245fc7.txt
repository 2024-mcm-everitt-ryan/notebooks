Job Title: Electrical and Event Management of Stages at Cinematic School of Arts

Company: Orozco, Mason and Hopkins

About Us:
Orozco, Mason and Hopkins is a leading institution in the cinematic arts, dedicated to fostering an inclusive culture that celebrates diversity and promotes equal opportunities. We are committed to providing a welcoming environment for students, staff, and faculty from all backgrounds.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Electrical and Event Management professional to oversee the technical aspects of our stages at the Cinematic School of Arts. The ideal candidate will have a solid understanding of electrical systems, event management software, and a keen eye for detail. The successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of our stages, coordinating events, and managing a team of technical staff.

Responsibilities:

- Oversee the maintenance and operation of electrical systems on our stages
- Coordinate events, including scheduling, logistics, and technical requirements
- Manage a team of technical staff, providing guidance and support as needed
- Collaborate with faculty and staff to ensure a seamless event experience
- Develop and implement safety protocols to ensure a safe working environment

Qualifications:

- Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, Event Management, or a related field
- Minimum 3 years of experience in event management and electrical systems
- Strong communication and leadership skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities
- Fluency in English; ability to articulate complex technical information clearly

- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional development and growth
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Access to state-of-the-art facilities and equipment
