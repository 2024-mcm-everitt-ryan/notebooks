Company Background:
Herrera-Mcdaniel is a renowned culinary establishment known for its innovative cuisine and exceptional dining experiences. We pride ourselves on creativity, quality, and a dedication to providing an outstanding culinary journey for our patrons.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
Herrera-Mcdaniel is seeking a Senior Cook to join our dynamic team. The Senior Cook will be responsible for overseeing kitchen operations, ensuring the high standard of food preparation, and maintaining an organized and efficient kitchen. This role demands a skilled individual capable of working under pressure and delivering consistent quality.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee daily kitchen operations and manage kitchen staff.
- Prepare and cook high-quality dishes according to the menu.
- Ensure food safety and sanitation standards are maintained.
- Assist in the creation and development of new menu items.
- Manage inventory and order supplies as needed.
- Train and mentor junior kitchen staff.
- Collaborate with the management team to ensure smooth kitchen operations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Minimum 5 years of experience as a cook in a high-volume restaurant.
- Culinary degree or equivalent experience.
- Strong knowledge of food safety and sanitation regulations.
- Excellent leadership and communication skills.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
- Creativity and passion for culinary arts.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from €40,000 to €50,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Health insurance and benefits package.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- Employee meal discounts.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.
