Company Background:
Barnett-Conner is a leading media distribution company focusing on efficient and timely delivery of news and periodicals across various regions. We pride ourselves on our innovative approach and contemporary techniques.

Job Type:
Full-Time News and Street Vendor

Job Description:
Barnett-Conner is seeking a dynamic and energetic individual to join our team as a News and Street Vendor. This role is ideal for those with contemporary training and a passion for the latest trends in media distribution. As a key member of our team, you will be responsible for delivering newspapers, magazines, and other periodicals to designated locations in a timely and efficient manner.

Responsibilities:
- Distribute newspapers and periodicals to various street locations.
- Engage with customers and provide exceptional service.
- Maintain accurate records of deliveries and payments.
- Adapt to changing delivery routes and schedules.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Fresh graduates are highly encouraged to apply.
- Perfect for those fresh from university with up-to-date skills in digital navigation and contemporary distribution methods.
- Ability to handle physical tasks and navigate urban environments.
- Strong communication skills and customer service orientation.

- Paid time off and holiday benefits.

- Training and development programs to enhance your skills.

Application Process:
