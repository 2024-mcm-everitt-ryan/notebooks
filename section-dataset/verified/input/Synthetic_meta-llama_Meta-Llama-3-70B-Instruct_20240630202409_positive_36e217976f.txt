Company: Scott PLC

Job Title: Family Advocate

Job Description:
We are seeking a compassionate and dedicated Family Advocate to join our team. As a Family Advocate, you will work closely with families, providing emotional support, guidance, and resources to empower them to navigate challenging situations. Your empathetic nature and ability to build strong relationships will make a significant difference in the lives of our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Provide individual and group support to families, addressing their unique needs and concerns
 Develop and implement personalized advocacy plans, ensuring families receive necessary resources and services
 Collaborate with internal and external partners to facilitate access to essential services and support
 Maintain accurate records and reports, ensuring data-driven decision making

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Social Work, Psychology, or a related field
 2+ years of experience working with families, preferably in a advocacy or counseling role
 Strong communication, active listening, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes health, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We also offer a generous paid time off policy, including vacation days, sick leave, and holidays.
