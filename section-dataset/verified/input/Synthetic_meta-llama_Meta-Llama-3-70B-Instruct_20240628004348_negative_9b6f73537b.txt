Senior PeopleSoft Analyst

Company Background:
Gonzales, Wilson and Gonzalez is a leading provider of ERP solutions, dedicated to delivering innovative technology to our clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and our passion for building strong relationships.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior PeopleSoft Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing PeopleSoft solutions to meet our clients' needs. This role requires strong technical skills, excellent communication, and the ability to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams.

Responsibilities:

 Design, develop, and implement PeopleSoft solutions to meet client requirements
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure solution alignment
 Provide technical support and training to junior team members
 Develop and maintain technical documentation

Qualifications and Experience:
 5+ years of experience in PeopleSoft development
 Strong technical skills in PeopleSoft, with expertise in at least one module
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $85,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we provide a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. Our company values work-life balance and offers flexible working hours and a generous PTO policy.
