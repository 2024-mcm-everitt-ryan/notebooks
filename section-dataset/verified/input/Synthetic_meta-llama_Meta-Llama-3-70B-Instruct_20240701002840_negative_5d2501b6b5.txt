Company: Parsons, Collins and Underwood

Job Title: Truck Driver

Job Description:
We're looking for a skilled and rugged truck driver to join our team. As a key player in our logistics operation, you'll be responsible for hauling goods across the country, navigating challenging terrain, and working independently to get the job done. If you're a seasoned veteran of the open road, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities:

 Safely operate a heavy-duty truck, navigating through congested city streets and rural landscapes
 Load and unload cargo, ensuring secure transportation and minimizing damage
 Conduct pre- and post-trip inspections to ensure vehicle safety and maintenance
 Communicate effectively with dispatch and customers, providing timely updates and resolving issues

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a truck driver, with a strong track record of safety and reliability
 Valid commercial driver's license (CDL) with a clean driving record
 Ability to lift 50 pounds unassisted and navigate tight spaces
 Strong mechanical aptitude and basic maintenance skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $75,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You'll also receive comprehensive health insurance, paid time off, and opportunities for professional development.
