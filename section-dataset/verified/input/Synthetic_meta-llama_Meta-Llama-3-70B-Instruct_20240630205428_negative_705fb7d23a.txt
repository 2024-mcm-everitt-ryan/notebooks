Company: Guerrero-Franco

Role: Claims Processor

Job Description:
We are seeking a compassionate and nurturing Claims Processor to join our team. As a Claims Processor, she will be responsible for handling client claims with empathy and understanding. Her ability to multitask and prioritize tasks with grace is essential in this fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Process and manage client claims in a timely and efficient manner
 Communicate with clients to gather necessary information and provide updates on their claims
 Maintain accurate records and reports
 Collaborate with our team to resolve client issues and concerns

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in a customer-facing role
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a team-oriented environment
 Strong organizational and time management skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
