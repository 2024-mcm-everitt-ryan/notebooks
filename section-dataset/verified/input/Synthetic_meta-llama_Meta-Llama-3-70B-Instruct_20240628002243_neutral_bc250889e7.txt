Company: Rice PLC

 Stay up-to-date with company products and services to provide accurate information to customers

Qualifications and Experience:
 Must possess excellent communication and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision
 Proficiency in switchboard operations and customer service software
 1-2 years of experience in a similar role
