Company: Barrett, Lam and Campbell

Job Title: Correctional Officer

Job Description:

Barrett, Lam and Campbell is seeking a strong and dedicated individual to join our team of correctional officers. As a correctional officer, you will be responsible for maintaining order and discipline within our correctional facilities. Your ability to think strategically and make quick decisions will be essential in ensuring the safety and security of both our staff and inmates.

Responsibilities:

 Supervise and monitor the activities of inmates to ensure compliance with facility rules and regulations
 Conduct searches of inmates and their living quarters to prevent contraband and maintain facility security
 Enforce facility rules and regulations, and take disciplinary action when necessary
 Develop and maintain positive relationships with inmates to encourage good behaviour and rehabilitation
 Collaborate with other correctional officers and facility staff to achieve facility goals and objectives

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required; degree in criminal justice or related field preferred
 1-2 years of experience in corrections or a related field
 Ability to pass a thorough background check and obtain necessary security clearances
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $65,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits package
 Opportunities for professional development and advancement
 Paid time off and holidays
