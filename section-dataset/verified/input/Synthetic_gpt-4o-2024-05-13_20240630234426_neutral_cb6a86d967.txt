Company Background:
Hill LLC is committed to water conservation and efficient resource management. We work closely with municipalities and private sectors to ensure compliance with water usage regulations and promote sustainable practices.

Job Title:
Water Use Inspector

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Hill LLC is seeking a dedicated Water Use Inspector to join our team. The successful candidate will monitor and assess water usage and compliance with local and federal regulations. This role involves field inspections, data analysis, and reporting to ensure adherence to water conservation standards.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct regular field inspections of residential, commercial, and industrial properties.
- Analyze water usage data and identify areas of non-compliance.
- Prepare detailed reports and communicate findings to relevant stakeholders.
- Collaborate with local authorities and private sectors on water conservation initiatives.
- Provide recommendations for improving water efficiency to property owners and managers.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- Previous experience in water resource management or environmental inspection is preferred.
- Strong analytical skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a cohesive team.
- Proficient with data analysis software and tools.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Professional development opportunities.

Application Process:
If you are passionate about water conservation and have the qualifications and experience required, we would love to hear from you. Please submit your resume and a cover letter outlining your relevant experience and why you are the ideal candidate for this role.
