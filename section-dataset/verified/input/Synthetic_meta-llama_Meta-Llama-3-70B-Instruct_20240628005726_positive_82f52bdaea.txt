Job Description:
We are seeking a talented Bid Operations Liaison to join our team. As a Bid Operations Liaison, you will play a critical role in ensuring the smooth operation of our bid management process. Your expertise will help us to develop and implement effective bid strategies, build strong relationships with our clients, and drive business growth.

Responsibilities:

 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and execute bid strategies
 Conduct market research and analyze competitor activity to inform bid decisions
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with clients and stakeholders
 Manage and coordinate bid responses, ensuring timely and high-quality submissions
 Analyze bid results and provide insights to inform future bid strategies

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Marketing, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in bid management or a related role
 Proven track record of success in developing and executing bid strategies
 Excellent communication, project management, and analytical skills
