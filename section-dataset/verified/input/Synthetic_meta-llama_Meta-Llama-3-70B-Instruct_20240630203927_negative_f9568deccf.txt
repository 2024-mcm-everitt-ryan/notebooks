Company: Hunt Inc
Role: Community Supervisor

Job Description:
We're seeking a compassionate and nurturing Community Supervisor to join our team at Hunt Inc. As a leader in our community, you will be responsible for fostering a warm and welcoming environment for our clients. If you're a gentle and understanding professional with a natural ability to nurture talent, we want to hear from you!

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in community development or a related field
 Proven track record of building strong relationships with community stakeholders
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
