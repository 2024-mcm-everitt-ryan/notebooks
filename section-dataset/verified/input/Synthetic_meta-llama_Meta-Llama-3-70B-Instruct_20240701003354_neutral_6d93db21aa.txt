Company: Hunt, Smith and Dougherty

Job Title: Hazardous Materials Removal Worker

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and dedicated Hazardous Materials Removal Worker to join our team. As a Hazardous Materials Removal Worker, you will be responsible for safely removing and disposing of hazardous materials from various sites. You will work closely with our team to ensure compliance with environmental regulations and maintain a safe working environment.

Responsibilities:

 Safely remove and dispose of hazardous materials from various sites
 Conduct site assessments to identify potential hazards and develop removal plans
 Operate heavy equipment, such as excavators and trucks, to facilitate removal and disposal
 Collaborate with team members to ensure compliance with environmental regulations
 Maintain accurate records of removal and disposal activities

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience in hazardous materials removal or a related field
 Valid certification in hazardous materials removal (e.g. OSHA HAZWOPER)
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and wearing PPE for long periods
 Strong communication and teamwork skills
 High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree in environmental science or a related field preferred

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
