Company: Davidson-Graham

Role: Senior Public Health Inspector

About Us:
Davidson-Graham is a leading public health organization dedicated to promoting healthy communities and preventing disease. We are committed to creating an inclusive and diverse workforce that reflects the communities we serve.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Public Health Inspector to join our dynamic team. As a Senior Public Health Inspector, you will be responsible for conducting inspections, investigating complaints, and providing education and guidance to the public and stakeholders on public health matters.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct inspections of food establishments, swimming pools, and other facilities to ensure compliance with public health regulations
 Investigate complaints and take corrective action as necessary
 Provide education and guidance to the public and stakeholders on public health matters
 Collaborate with other public health professionals to develop and implement programs and policies
 Analyze data and prepare reports to inform public health decision-making

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Environmental Health, Public Health, or a related field
 Certification as a Public Health Inspector (CPHI) or eligible to obtain within 6 months
 5+ years of experience in public health inspection or a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including:
 A salary ranging from $75,000 to $95,000 CAD, based on your experience and skills
 A generous pension plan with employer matching contributions
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 A dynamic and supportive work environment
 We welcome candidates from all backgrounds, sexual orientations, and gender identities and encourage them to apply.
