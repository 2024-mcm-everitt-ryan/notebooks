Company: Taylor PLC

Job Title: Engineering Support System Administrator

About Us:
Taylor PLC is a leading provider of innovative engineering solutions. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering exceptional results and exceeding customer expectations.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Engineering Support System Administrator to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of our engineering systems and providing technical support to our engineers.

Responsibilities:
- Install, configure, and maintain computer systems and networks
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues in a timely and efficient manner
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize system improvements
- Develop and maintain technical documentation and guides
- Provide training and support to engineers on system usage and best practices

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field
- 3+ years of experience in system administration
- Proficient in English
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Benefits:
- Salary range: $70,000 - $90,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
