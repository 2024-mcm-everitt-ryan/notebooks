Company Background:
White LLC is a leading manufacturer specializing in bespoke wooden furniture and high-quality woodcrafts. Our dedication to craftsmanship and attention to detail has positioned us as a preferred choice for homeowners and businesses alike.

Job Type:
Full-Time, Woodworker

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled woodworker to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for woodworking and a keen eye for detail. This role involves creating custom pieces, restoring antique furniture, and collaborating with our design team to bring concepts to life. The successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring that all products meet our high standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Responsibilities:
- Operate woodworking machinery and hand tools to cut, shape, and assemble wooden products.
- Interpret design plans and blueprints to create custom furniture pieces.
- Perform finishing tasks such as sanding, staining, and varnishing.
- Repair and restore antique wooden furniture.
- Maintain a safe and organized workshop environment.
- Collaborate with the design team to develop new product ideas.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a woodworker or in a similar role.
- Ability to interpret technical drawings and blueprints.
- Proficiency in using woodworking machinery and tools.
- Strong attention to detail and craftsmanship.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Physical stamina to perform tasks efficiently.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance plans.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- Employee discounts on all products.

Application Process:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and a portfolio of previous work. We look forward to seeing your craftsmanship and creativity.
