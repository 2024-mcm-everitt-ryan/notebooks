Company: Brewer-Owens

Job Title: Claims Specialist

Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled Claims Specialist to join our team at Brewer-Owens. As a Claims Specialist, you will be responsible for investigating and resolving complex claims in a fast-paced environment. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills, be highly organized, and able to work effectively in a team.

Responsibilities:

 Investigate and resolve complex claims in a timely and efficient manner
 Communicate effectively with claimants, witnesses, and other stakeholders
 Analyze and interpret data to make informed decisions
 Work closely with cross-functional teams to resolve claims
 Meet productivity and quality standards

Requirements:

 2+ years of experience in claims handling
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities
 Must be able to work independently with minimal supervision
 Requires working in an open-concept office space with variable temperatures
 Must be able to communicate effectively with all levels of staff

What We Offer:

We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
