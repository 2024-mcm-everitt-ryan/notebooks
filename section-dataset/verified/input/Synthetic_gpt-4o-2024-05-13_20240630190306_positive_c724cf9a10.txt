Company Background:
Miller, Caldwell and Harris is a renowned law enforcement agency committed to maintaining safety and security within the community. Our agency values diversity and inclusivity, aiming to foster a workplace where all employees thrive.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are looking for dedicated and driven individuals to join our team as Police Officers. This role involves enforcing laws, responding to emergencies, and protecting the community. We are particularly focused on enhancing our gender diversity and strongly encourage women to apply.

Responsibilities:
- Patrol assigned areas to maintain public safety and order.
- Respond to emergency calls and provide immediate assistance.
- Conduct investigations and gather evidence.
- Prepare detailed reports and maintain records.
- Engage with the community to build trust and rapport.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent required; a bachelor's degree in criminal justice or a related field is preferred.
- Must have completed police academy training.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to handle high-stress situations effectively.
- Physical fitness and ability to pass a physical exam.
- Women are especially encouraged to apply for this position.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Pension plan and retirement benefits.
- Paid time off and sick leave.
- Training and career development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.

Miller, Caldwell and Harris is dedicated to creating a diverse workforce and is an equal opportunity employer.
