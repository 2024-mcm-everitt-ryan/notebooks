Company: Price, Bowman and Fritz

Job Title: Umpire

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and impartial umpire to join our team. As an umpire, you will be responsible for making fair and accurate calls during games, ensuring a positive experience for all players and spectators. If you have a passion for sports and a strong sense of justice, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Make accurate and timely calls during games
 Develop and maintain a thorough understanding of game rules and regulations
 Collaborate with other umpires to ensure consistency and fairness
 Communicate effectively with players, coaches, and spectators
 Participate in training and development programs to maintain and improve skills

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of umpiring experience
 Strong knowledge of game rules and regulations
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
 Experience in conflict resolution is a plus

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and provide opportunities for professional growth and development.
