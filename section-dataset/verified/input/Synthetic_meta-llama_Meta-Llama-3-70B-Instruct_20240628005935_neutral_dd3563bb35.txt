Company: Barker, Pierce and Banks
Job Title: Standards Specialist

At Barker, Pierce and Banks, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional service to our clients. As a Standards Specialist, you will play a vital role in ensuring our products meet the highest standards of quality.

Job Description:
The Standards Specialist will be responsible for developing, implementing, and maintaining quality standards across our organization. This includes collaborating with cross-functional teams to identify areas for improvement, creating and updating quality control procedures, and conducting regular audits to ensure compliance.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement quality standards across the organization
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify areas for improvement
 Create and update quality control procedures
 Conduct regular audits to ensure compliance
 Analyze data to identify trends and areas for improvement

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a relevant field (e.g. engineering, quality management)
 3+ years of experience in quality control or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 60,000 to USD 80,000, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off. We also offer flexible work arrangements, including remote work options, to support our employees' work-life balance.

Our company provides support for all parents balancing work and family. We offer workshops for parents on balancing work and family life, and our benefits extend to all spouses and partners. We support diverse family structures within our team and are committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming work environment.
