Senior Landscape Architect

Company Background:
Avila-Esparza is a renowned landscape architecture firm dedicated to creating sustainable and innovative outdoor spaces. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, but unfortunately, this has led to a culture of perfectionism that can be challenging for some individuals to thrive in.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Landscape Architect to join our team. As a senior member of our team, you will be responsible for leading project teams, designing and developing landscape architecture projects, and collaborating with clients to bring their vision to life.

Responsibilities:

 Lead project teams to deliver high-quality landscape architecture projects on time and within budget
 Develop and maintain client relationships to ensure project success and future business opportunities
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure project integration and alignment
 Design and develop landscape architecture projects that meet client needs and exceed expectations

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in landscape architecture, with a focus on project management and team leadership
 Professional degree in Landscape Architecture or related field
 Strong design and project management skills, with the ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work with clients and stakeholders at all levels

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $120,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we provide a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings plan, and paid time off.
