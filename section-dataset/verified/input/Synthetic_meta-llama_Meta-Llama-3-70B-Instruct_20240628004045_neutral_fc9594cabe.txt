Job Title: Groundskeeping Worker
Company: Simmons-Hudson

About Simmons-Hudson:
Simmons-Hudson is a leading landscaping and groundskeeping company dedicated to providing exceptional outdoor spaces for our clients. We value diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to maintain a workplace free from discrimination.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and motivated Groundskeeping Worker to join our team. As a Groundskeeping Worker, you will be responsible for maintaining the beauty and cleanliness of our clients' properties. Your duties will include mowing lawns, trimming bushes, pruning trees, and performing other groundskeeping tasks as needed. 

Responsibilities:

 Maintain the appearance of clients' properties by performing tasks such as mowing, trimming, and pruning
 Ensure all equipment is properly maintained and stored
 Collaborate with team members to complete tasks efficiently
 Communicate effectively with clients to understand their needs and preferences

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in groundskeeping or a related field
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced environment
 High school diploma or equivalent required

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan with company match. We also offer opportunities for professional development and growth within the company.
