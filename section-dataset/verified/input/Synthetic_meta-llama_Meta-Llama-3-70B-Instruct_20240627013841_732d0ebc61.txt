Company: Williams-Scott

Job Title: Industrial Design Contractor

About Us:
Williams-Scott is a leading design firm dedicated to creating innovative solutions for various industries. We prioritize collaboration, creativity, and attention to detail to deliver exceptional results.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Industrial Design Contractor to join our team. As an Industrial Design Contractor, you will be responsible for designing and developing industrial products that meet our clients' needs. You will work closely with our cross-functional team to ensure seamless execution and delivery of projects.

Responsibilities:
- Develop design concepts and prototypes for industrial products
- Collaborate with engineers and designers to ensure design feasibility and quality
- Conduct research and analyze market trends to inform design decisions
- Create 2D and 3D design models using CAD software
- Communicate design ideas and rationales to clients and internal stakeholders

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Industrial Design or a related field
- 3+ years of experience in industrial design
- Proficiency in CAD software (SolidWorks, Autodesk Inventor, or similar)
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

Benefits:
- Hourly rate of $50-$70, depending on experience
- Opportunity to work on diverse and challenging projects
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Professional development opportunities

We are an equal opportunities employer committed to diversity and inclusion. We welcome applications from candidates of all backgrounds.
