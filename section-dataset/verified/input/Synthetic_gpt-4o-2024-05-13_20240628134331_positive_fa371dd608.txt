Company Background:

Job Title:
Drafter

Job Description:
Wilson-Lloyd is seeking a talented drafter to join our dynamic team. As a drafter, you will play a crucial role in supporting our architects and engineers by creating detailed technical drawings and plans. This position offers an exciting opportunity to contribute to a wide range of projects, from residential buildings to large-scale commercial developments.

Responsibilities:
- Prepare detailed drawings and layouts based on engineering specifications.
- Collaborate with architects, engineers, and project managers to ensure design accuracy.
- Revise drawings according to feedback from the design team.
- Maintain drawing files and records for easy access and retrieval.
- Ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proficiency in AutoCAD and other drafting software.
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
- Relevant certification or degree in drafting, architecture, or a related field.
- Previous experience in a similar role is preferred.
