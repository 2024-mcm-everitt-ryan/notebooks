Company: Rodriguez Inc

Role: Boiler Inspector

Company Background:
Rodriguez Inc is a renowned company in the industrial maintenance field, recognised for its commitment to safety and excellence. We pride ourselves on our diverse and inclusive workplace, striving to create opportunities for all employees to succeed and grow.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Rodriguez Inc is seeking a dedicated and skilled Boiler Inspector to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for inspecting, maintaining, and repairing boilers, ensuring compliance with safety and operational standards.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct thorough inspections of boilers and associated systems.
- Identify and diagnose issues, and perform necessary repairs.
- Ensure compliance with local, state, and federal regulations.
- Maintain detailed records of inspections and repairs.
- Collaborate with other team members to promote a safe working environment.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Certification as a Boiler Inspector.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in boiler inspection or related field.
- Strong knowledge of safety regulations and standards.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
