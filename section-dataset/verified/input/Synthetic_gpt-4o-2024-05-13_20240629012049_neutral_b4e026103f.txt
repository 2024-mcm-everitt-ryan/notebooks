Company: Wilson, Anderson and Martin  
Role: Dressmaker  

Company Background:  
Wilson, Anderson and Martin is a leading fashion house recognized for its innovative designs and commitment to quality craftsmanship. We foster an inclusive environment and encourage individuals from all backgrounds to apply.

Job Type: Full-Time  

Job Description:  
We are seeking a detail-oriented Dressmaker to join our dedicated team. The ideal candidate will be proficient in garment construction and alterations, working efficiently to meet our high standards. This role requires an individual who can adhere to specific design requirements and produce exceptional quality pieces.

Responsibilities:  
- Construct and alter garments according to design specifications.  
- Collaborate with design and production teams to ensure accuracy and quality.  
- Maintain a clean and organized workspace.  
- Meet production deadlines while adhering to quality standards.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Proven experience as a Dressmaker or similar role.  
- Strong understanding of garment construction techniques.  
- Ability to work efficiently within the job requirements.  
- Excellent attention to detail and precision in sewing.

Perks and Benefits:  
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from EUR 30,000 to EUR 45,000, based on your experience and skills.  
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.  
- Opportunities for professional development and growth.  
- Inclusive and supportive work environment.  
- Generous employee discount on company products.

How to Apply:  
Candidates with diverse work history are welcome to apply. Please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing your relevant experience and qualifications.
