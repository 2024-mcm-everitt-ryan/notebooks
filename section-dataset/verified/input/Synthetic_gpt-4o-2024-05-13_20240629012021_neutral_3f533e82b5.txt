Company Overview:
Griffin, Berry and Ortiz is a renowned industrial manufacturing firm with a focus on sustainability and innovation. Our commitment to excellence drives us to produce high-quality products while fostering an inclusive and diverse workforce.

Role: Industrial Production Manager

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Industrial Production Manager to oversee production operations and ensure efficiency and adherence to safety standards. This role involves managing a team, coordinating production schedules, and implementing continuous improvement initiatives.

Responsibilities:
- Overseeing daily production activities to ensure efficiency and quality.
- Developing and executing production schedules to meet company goals.
- Managing and mentoring production staff, encouraging a positive and inclusive work environment.
- Implementing safety protocols and ensuring compliance with industry standards.
- Analyzing production data and identifying areas for improvement.
- Collaborating with other departments to streamline processes.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree from any accredited institution is preferred.
- Proven experience in industrial production management.
- Strong leadership and communication skills.
- Demonstrated ability to develop and manage production schedules.
- A valid driver’s license is required if the role involves driving.

- Competitive salary range: $70,000 - $90,000 based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Support for substance abuse recovery is available.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Paid time off, including vacation and sick leave.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
