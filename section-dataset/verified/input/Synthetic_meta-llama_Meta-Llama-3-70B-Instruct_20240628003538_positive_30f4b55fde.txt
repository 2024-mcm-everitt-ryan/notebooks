Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Appliance Repairer to join our team. As an Appliance Repairer, you will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing a wide range of household appliances, providing excellent customer service, and ensuring timely completion of repair jobs.

Responsibilities:

 Diagnose and repair household appliances, including refrigerators, ovens, dishwashers, and washing machines
 Provide exceptional customer service, answering questions and addressing concerns in a professional and courteous manner
 Ensure timely completion of repair jobs, meeting productivity and quality standards
 Collaborate with team members to achieve business objectives and improve customer satisfaction

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in appliance repair or a related field
 Strong technical knowledge of household appliances and repair techniques
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
