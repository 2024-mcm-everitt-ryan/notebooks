Company: Morgan-Walker

Job Title: Sr. Internal Auditor

About Us:
Morgan-Walker is a leading financial institution committed to excellence, integrity, and transparency. We believe in fostering a culture of inclusion, respect, and equal opportunities for all.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Sr. Internal Auditor to join our team. As a Sr. Internal Auditor, you will be responsible for conducting audits, identifying areas for improvement, and ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory standards.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct internal audits to assess the effectiveness of our financial controls and processes
 Identify areas for improvement and recommend changes to management
 Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory standards, including SOX and auditing standards
 Develop and maintain relationships with internal stakeholders, including management and staff
 Provide training and guidance to junior audit team members

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field from a recognized university
 Professional certification in auditing, such as CPA or CIA
 At least 5 years of experience in internal auditing, preferably in the financial industry
 Strong analytical, communication, and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $120,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and a generous paid time-off policy.
