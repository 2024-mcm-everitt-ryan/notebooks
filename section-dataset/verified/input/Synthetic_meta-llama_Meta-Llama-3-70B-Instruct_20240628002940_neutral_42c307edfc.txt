Company: Garner PLC

Job Title: Human Resources Support Staff

Job Summary:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Human Resources Support Staff to join our team. As a Human Resources Support Staff, you will provide administrative support to our HR department, ensuring the smooth operation of our human resources functions.

Responsibilities:

 Assist with recruitment and onboarding processes
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date employee records
 Provide exceptional customer service to employees and management
 Assist with benefits administration and employee communications
 Perform other administrative tasks as needed

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required; degree in Human Resources or related field preferred
 1-2 years of experience in an HR support role
 Proficient in Microsoft Office and HR software
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information

What We Offer:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
 401(k) matching program
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment

Garner PLC is an equal opportunities employer. All qualified candidates are encouraged to apply.
