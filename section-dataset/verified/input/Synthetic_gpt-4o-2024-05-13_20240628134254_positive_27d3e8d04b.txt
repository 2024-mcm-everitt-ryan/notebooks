Company Background:
Austin-Davis is a leading dental care provider aimed at offering exceptional oral health services to the community. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and diverse work environment.

Job Type:
Full-time Dentist

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and skilled Dentist to join our team. The Dentist will be responsible for diagnosing and treating dental issues, performing routine cleanings and check-ups, and providing exceptional care to our patients.

Responsibilities:
- Diagnose and treat dental issues.
- Perform routine dental cleanings and check-ups.
- Develop appropriate treatment plans for patients.
- Educate patients on proper oral health care.
- Maintain accurate patient records.

Qualifications and Experience:
- DDS or DMD degree from an accredited dental school.
- State dental license.
- Strong communication skills.
- Compassionate and patient-centered approach to care.
- Experience in a dental clinic setting is preferred.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €70,000 to €90,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Our inclusive policy includes seeking candidates with disabilities.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are invited to submit their resume and cover letter outlining their qualifications and experience. We value an inclusive workforce and invite people with disabilities to apply.
