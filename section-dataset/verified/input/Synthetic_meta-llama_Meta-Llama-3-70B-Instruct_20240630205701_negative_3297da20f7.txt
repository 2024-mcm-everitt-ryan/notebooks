Office Machine Repairer

Company Background:
Lee Group is a leading provider of office solutions, dedicated to delivering exceptional service to our clients.

Job Description:
We are seeking a nurturing and caring Office Machine Repairer to join our team. As a key member of our technical support team, you will be responsible for repairing and maintaining office equipment, ensuring our clients receive the best possible experience. Your empathetic and collaborative approach will enable you to effectively communicate with our clients, understanding their needs and providing tailored solutions.

Responsibilities:

 Repair and maintain office equipment, including printers, copiers, and fax machines
 Provide exceptional customer service, ensuring clients are satisfied with our services
 Collaborate with our team to identify and resolve technical issues
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with clients, understanding their needs and preferences

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in office machine repair
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment
 She will have a strong attention to detail and be able to multitask

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary, ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per annum, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you will receive comprehensive training, opportunities for professional development, and a supportive team environment.
