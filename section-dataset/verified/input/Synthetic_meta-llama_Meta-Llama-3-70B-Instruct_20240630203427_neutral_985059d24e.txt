Role: Stationary Engineer

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience as a Stationary Engineer or in a related field
 Strong knowledge of mechanical systems, including boilers, pumps, and HVAC systems
 Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Experience with CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management System) software is an asset
