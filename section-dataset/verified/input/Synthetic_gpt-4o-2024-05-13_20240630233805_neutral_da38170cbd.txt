Company: Collins-Harrison

Role: Umpire

Company Background:
Collins-Harrison is a leading provider of sports officiating services, known for upholding the integrity and spirit of various sports. We are dedicated to maintaining a fair and enjoyable environment for all athletes.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Collins-Harrison is seeking an experienced and dedicated Umpire to join our team. The ideal candidate will possess a strong understanding of the game, a keen eye for detail, and the ability to make swift and fair decisions on the field. This role requires a proactive approach to problem-solving, ensuring the smooth operation of games according to established rules and regulations.

Responsibilities:
- Officiate games, ensuring adherence to the rules and maintaining game flow.
- Make quick and accurate decisions regarding plays, penalties, and disputes.
- Communicate effectively with players, coaches, and other officials.
- Maintain control of the game environment and handle conflicts professionally.
- Attend training sessions and stay updated with rule changes and best practices.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Prior experience officiating in the relevant sport.
- Comprehensive knowledge of game rules and regulations.
- Excellent decision-making and communication skills.
- Ability to remain impartial and professional under pressure.
- Physical fitness to cover the playing field effectively.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Paid training and development opportunities.
- Flexible scheduling options.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are invited to submit their resume and a cover letter outlining their relevant experience and why they are a good fit for the role.

Collins-Harrison is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
