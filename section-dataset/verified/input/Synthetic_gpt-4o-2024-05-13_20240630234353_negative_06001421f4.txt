Company: Williams Ltd

Role: Explosives Worker

Company Background:
Williams Ltd is a leading provider of explosives and demolition services, operating globally for over 30 years. We pride ourselves on our commitment to safety, precision, and innovation in the field of controlled demolitions and explosives handling.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Williams Ltd is seeking a skilled and experienced explosives worker to join our dynamic team. The role requires handling, transporting, and deploying explosives for various demolition and construction projects.

Responsibilities:
- Prepare and handle explosives for demolition projects.
- Transport and deploy explosives according to safety regulations.
- Collaborate with the team to plan and execute demolition activities.
- Conduct safety checks and maintain equipment.
- Ensure compliance with all legal and safety standards.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Previous experience in handling and deploying explosives.
- Strong physical condition to handle the demanding nature of the job.
- Ability to work under pressure and make quick, decisive decisions.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Certification in explosives handling and safety preferred.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $50,000 to $70,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Opportunities for professional development and certification.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and cover letter to hr@williamsltd.com. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis.

Williams Ltd is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage candidates of all backgrounds to apply.
