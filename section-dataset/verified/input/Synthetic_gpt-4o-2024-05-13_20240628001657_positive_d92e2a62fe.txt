Company Background:
Castaneda Inc is a leader in providing sophisticated integration solutions for businesses worldwide. We pride ourselves on our heritage of innovation and consistent delivery of top-tier services.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
As an Integration Support Engineer at Castaneda Inc, you will play a crucial role in maintaining and enhancing our integration systems. We are looking for a seasoned professional with a long-term commitment to the field who can bring a wealth of historical knowledge and established authority to our team.

Responsibilities:
- Provide expert support for integration systems.
- Troubleshoot and resolve complex integration issues.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to enhance system performance.
- Develop and document standard operating procedures for integration support.
- Mentor junior engineers and share best practices.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Minimum of 11 years of professional experience in integration support or a related field.
- Extensive knowledge of integration technologies and best practices.
- Proven track record of successful integration projects.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.

- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Generous retirement plans.

- A supportive and dynamic work environment.
