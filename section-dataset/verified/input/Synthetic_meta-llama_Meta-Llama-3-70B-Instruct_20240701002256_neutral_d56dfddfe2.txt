Job Title: Senior Service Desk Agent
Company: Murphy-Morales

About Us:
Murphy-Morales is a leading provider of innovative IT solutions, dedicated to delivering exceptional customer experiences. We're committed to fostering a culture of inclusivity, respect, and collaboration.

Job Description:
We're seeking a skilled Senior Service Desk Agent to join our team. As a senior member of our service desk, you'll play a critical role in resolving technical issues, providing top-notch support, and ensuring seamless service delivery to our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues via phone, email, and chat
 Collaborate with internal teams to resolve issues and improve processes
 Provide expert guidance and training to junior team members
 Analyze and report on service desk metrics to drive continuous improvement
 Develop and maintain technical knowledge to stay current with industry trends

Requirements:

 3+ years of experience in a service desk or technical support role
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize tasks effectively
 Strong technical knowledge of IT systems, hardware, and software

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You'll also enjoy a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
