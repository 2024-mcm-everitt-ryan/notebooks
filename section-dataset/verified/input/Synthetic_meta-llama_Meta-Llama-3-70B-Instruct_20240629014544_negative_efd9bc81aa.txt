Company: Oconnor-Bell
Role: Plumbing Inspector

Job Description:
We're looking for a plumbing inspector to join our team at Oconnor-Bell. As a plumbing inspector, you'll be responsible for ensuring that all plumbing systems meet relevant codes and regulations. If you're a self-motivated individual with a strong work ethic, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct on-site inspections of plumbing systems to ensure compliance with relevant codes and regulations
 Identify and document any defects or violations
 Work closely with contractors and homeowners to resolve any issues
 Provide exceptional customer service

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in the plumbing industry (trade experience highly preferred)
 Strong knowledge of local plumbing codes and regulations
 Ability to work independently and make sound decisions
 Excellent communication and customer service skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
