 Provide recommendations to optimize energy consumption and reduce costs

 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in energy management

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a relevant field (e.g. energy management, economics, mathematics)
 At least 3 years of experience in energy analysis or a related field
 Proven track record of developing and implementing successful energy-saving initiatives
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
