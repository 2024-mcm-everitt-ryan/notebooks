Company Background:
Cervantes-Hendrix is a leading institution renowned for its vast and diverse collections. We are committed to preserving history and culture through meticulous archiving and making these resources accessible to the public.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Archivist to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for organizing, preserving, and making accessible our extensive archives, which include historical manuscripts, rare books, and digital records. The Archivist will ensure the integrity and accessibility of these materials for research and educational purposes.

Responsibilities:
- Catalog and organize archival materials.
- Develop and implement systems for the efficient retrieval of information.
- Work with other departments to support research and educational initiatives.
- Ensure the proper preservation of materials, including both physical and digital formats.
- Conduct outreach activities to promote the use of archives.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Library Science, History, or a related field.
- Previous experience in archiving or a related role.
- Proficiency in archival software and digital cataloging systems.
- Strong organizational and communication skills.
- Attention to detail and a commitment to preserving historical integrity.

- Professional development opportunities.
- Paid time off and flexible working hours.
