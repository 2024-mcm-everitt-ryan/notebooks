Company: Frazier, Martin and Wilson
Role: Camera Operator

About Us:
Frazier, Martin and Wilson is a leading production company dedicated to creating high-quality visual content for various industries. We value diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to provide a welcoming environment for all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Camera Operator to join our team. As a Camera Operator, you will be responsible for operating cameras, setting up equipment, and capturing high-quality footage during productions. You will work closely with our production team to ensure that all visual elements meet our high standards.

Responsibilities:

 Operate cameras and related equipment during productions
 Set up and test equipment before filming
 Collaborate with the production team to achieve desired visual effects
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Degree from an accredited university in a relevant field (e.g., film, broadcasting, media production)
 Proven track record of operating cameras and related equipment
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:

 Competitive salary (USD 55,000 - 75,000 per year, based on experience)
 Comprehensive health and dental insurance
 Reserved parking available for employees
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
