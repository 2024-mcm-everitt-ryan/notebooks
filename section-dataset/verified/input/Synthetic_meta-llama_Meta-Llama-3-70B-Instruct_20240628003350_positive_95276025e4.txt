Company: Mendoza Group
Role: Data Manager

About Us:
The Mendoza Group is a leading data analytics firm dedicated to driving business growth through data-driven insights. We are committed to fostering an inclusive and diverse work environment that celebrates individuality and promotes equality.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Data Manager to join our team. As a Data Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing the collection, analysis, and reporting of data to drive business decisions. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to develop and implement data strategies, ensuring data quality and integrity.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement data management strategies to meet business objectives
 Oversee data collection, analysis, and reporting to drive business decisions
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure data quality and integrity
 Identify and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and accuracy

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field
 At least 5 years of experience in data management or analytics
 Strong proficiency in data visualization tools and programming languages (e.g., Python, R)
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
