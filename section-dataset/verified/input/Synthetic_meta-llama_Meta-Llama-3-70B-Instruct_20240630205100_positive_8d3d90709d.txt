At Reynolds-Santiago, we're committed to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment. As a groundskeeping worker, you will play a vital role in maintaining the beauty and functionality of our outdoor spaces.

We are particularly interested in hearing from women who share our passion for horticulture and outdoor maintenance. Our company values the unique perspectives and skills that women bring to the table, and we're dedicated to creating a workplace where they can thrive.

Responsibilities:

 Maintain lawns, gardens, and outdoor spaces to ensure they are tidy and well-manicured
 Perform routine maintenance tasks, such as pruning, weeding, and watering
 Collaborate with other team members to ensure efficient and effective groundskeeping operations
 Develop and implement creative solutions to improve the overall aesthetic of our outdoor spaces

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in groundskeeping or a related field
 Strong knowledge of horticulture and outdoor maintenance practices
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment
 Physical ability to perform manual labor in various weather conditions

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional development and growth within the company
 A supportive and inclusive work environment that values diversity and creativity
 Comprehensive health and wellness benefits package
