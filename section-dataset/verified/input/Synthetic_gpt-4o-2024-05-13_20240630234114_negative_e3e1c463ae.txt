Company Background:
Edwards, Mcclure, and Parsons is a leading food science and technology company dedicated to advancing food safety, quality, and innovation. We pride ourselves on our strong work ethic and commitment to excellence.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are looking for a Food Science Technician who can thrive in a dynamic and challenging environment. The ideal candidate will be a forceful communicator and will tackle challenges head-on. This role involves working closely with our team to ensure the highest standards of food safety and quality are met.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct laboratory tests to analyze food samples.
- Assist in the development of new food products.
- Maintain and calibrate laboratory equipment.
- Prepare detailed reports on findings.
- Ensure compliance with food safety regulations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Food Science, Chemistry, or a related field.
- Previous experience in a food science laboratory is preferred.
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to tackle challenges head-on.
- Excellent communication skills; a forceful communicator is ideal.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Retirement plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
