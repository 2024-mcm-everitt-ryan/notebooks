Company: Kim Group
Role: Courier

Job Description:
We are seeking a reliable and efficient courier to join our team at Kim Group. As a courier, you will be responsible for delivering packages and documents to our clients in a timely and professional manner. You will be the face of our company, interacting with clients and providing excellent customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Deliver packages and documents to clients in a timely and efficient manner
 Provide excellent customer service to clients and respond to their queries
 Maintain accurate records of deliveries and client interactions
 Ensure compliance with company policies and procedures

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in a courier or delivery role
 Valid driver's license and a clean driving record
 Ability to lift packages of up to 50 pounds without assistance
 Excellent communication and customer service skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
