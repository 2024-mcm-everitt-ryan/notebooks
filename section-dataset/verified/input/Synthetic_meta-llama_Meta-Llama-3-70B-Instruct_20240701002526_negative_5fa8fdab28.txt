Company: Ponce, Bradford and Johnson

Job Title: Budget Review Specialist

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and analytical Budget Review Specialist to join our team. As a key member of our finance department, you will be responsible for reviewing and analyzing budget reports, identifying areas of improvement, and making recommendations to senior management.

Responsibilities:

 Review and analyze budget reports to ensure accuracy and compliance with company policies
 Identify areas of inefficiency and propose cost-saving initiatives
 Collaborate with department heads to develop and implement budget plans
 Provide financial guidance and support to cross-functional teams
 Develop and maintain financial models to forecast revenue and expenses

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
 3+ years of experience in budget analysis or financial planning
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and presentation skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
