Company Background:
Newton, Myers and Parrish is a renowned retail company committed to fostering an inclusive environment. We are dedicated to creating a workplace that respects and celebrates diverse perspectives, especially from the LGBTQ+ community.

Job Type:
Full-Time Stock Clerk

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented Stock Clerk to join our dynamic team. This role is crucial for maintaining the organization and inventory of our retail stockroom. As a company that champions diversity, we highly encourage applications from individuals who can bring LGBTQ+ perspectives to our team.

Responsibilities:
- Receive, unpack, and organize stock
- Maintain accurate inventory records
- Collaborate with the sales team to ensure smooth stock replenishment
- Ensure the stockroom is clean and organized
- Assist in periodic stock audits

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent
- Previous experience in a retail stockroom is preferred but not required
- Ability to lift and move heavy items
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
- A commitment to fostering an inclusive workplace

Perks and Benefits:
- Salary: We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £24,000 to £28,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance
- Paid time off and paid holidays
- Employee discounts
- Opportunities for career advancement

Our Commitment to Diversity:
Our recruitment process prioritizes diversity, with a focus on LGBTQ+ inclusion. We are looking for candidates who embrace diversity and can bring LGBTQ+ perspectives to our team. We believe that a diverse team is a stronger team and are dedicated to ensuring diverse representation in our workforce.
