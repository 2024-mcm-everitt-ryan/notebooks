Company: Hall, Anthony and Durham

Job Type: Chauffeur

Job Description:
We are seeking a reliable and experienced chauffeur to join our team. As a chauffeur, you will be responsible for safely transporting our clients to their desired destinations while maintaining a professional and courteous demeanor.

Responsibilities:

 Safely operate company vehicles to transport clients
 Maintain a clean and organized vehicle at all times
 Provide exceptional customer service and ensure client satisfaction
 Possess a strong knowledge of local roads and traffic patterns

Qualifications and Experience:
 Prior experience as a chauffeur or in a related field is preferred
 Must possess a valid driver's license with a clean driving record
 A high school diploma or equivalent is required; degree from a reputable institution is desirable
 Ability to pass a thorough background check is mandatory

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
