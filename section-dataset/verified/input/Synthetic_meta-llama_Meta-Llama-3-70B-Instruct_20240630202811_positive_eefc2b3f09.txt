Company: Nguyen Group
Role: Licensing Clerk

About Us:
The Nguyen Group is a leading provider of innovative solutions, dedicated to fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment. We believe that a team with diverse perspectives and experiences is essential to driving success.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Licensing Clerk to join our team. As a Licensing Clerk, you will be responsible for managing licenses, permits, and contracts, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. Your exceptional communication skills and ability to work collaboratively will be essential in building strong relationships with our clients and internal stakeholders.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and maintain accurate records of licenses, permits, and contracts
 Coordinate with internal teams to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
 Develop and implement effective filing systems to ensure timely access to documents
 Provide exceptional customer service to clients and internal stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a similar role
 Strong organizational and communication skills
 Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and Google Suite

Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We also offer a flexible work schedule and opportunities for professional growth and development.

As an organization committed to gender diversity, we are particularly interested in hearing from women who are passionate about making a meaningful impact in the industry.
