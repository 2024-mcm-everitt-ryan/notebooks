Company: Bond Ltd
Role: Promotional Product Intern

About Us:
Bond Ltd is a leading promotional products company dedicated to providing innovative and effective marketing solutions to our clients. We value diversity and inclusivity, and our team reflects this commitment.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and creative Promotional Product Intern to join our team. As a Promotional Product Intern, you will contribute to the development of engaging promotional campaigns and assist in the creation of innovative product designs.

Responsibilities:

- Assist in the design and development of promotional products
- Conduct market research to identify trends and opportunities
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure effective campaign execution
- Develop and maintain relationships with clients and vendors

Qualifications:

- Currently enrolled in a degree program in Marketing, Design, or a related field
- Strong design and creative skills
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

What We Offer:

- Internship stipend of $1,500 per month
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Access to cutting-edge design software and tools

We're Committed to Diversity:
Bond Ltd is committed to increasing our representation of Asian and Pacific Islander individuals, and we encourage applications from professionals of Romani descent to enhance team diversity.
