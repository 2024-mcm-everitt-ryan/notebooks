Company: Savage, Liu and Moore

Job Title: UI/UX Lead

About Us:
Savage, Liu and Moore is a cutting-edge design firm committed to creating innovative digital experiences. We value diversity and believe that a diverse team leads to better outcomes.

Job Description:

- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to create user-centered designs
- Conduct user research and testing to inform design decisions

Qualifications:
- 5+ years of experience in UI/UX design
- Portfolio showcasing your design skills and experience
- Strong communication and project management skills
- Fluency in English, with bilingualism in Spanish being a plus

Benefits:
- Salary range: $80,000 - $100,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment

We encourage applications from individuals with diverse ethnic backgrounds, particularly those from underrepresented groups. We believe that a diverse team leads to better outcomes and are committed to creating an inclusive work environment.
