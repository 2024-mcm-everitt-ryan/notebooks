Company: Arnold-Oconnor

Job Type: Cartographer

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Cartographer to join our team at Arnold-Oconnor. As a Cartographer, you will be responsible for creating accurate and visually appealing maps, conducting spatial analysis, and collaborating with our team to develop innovative solutions.

Responsibilities:

 Design and develop maps, charts, and other visualizations to communicate complex data insights
 Conduct spatial analysis and modeling to identify trends and patterns
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate mapping solutions with business objectives
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies in cartography and GIS

Qualifications and Experience Needed:

 Bachelor's degree in Cartography, GIS, or related field
 3+ years of experience in cartography or a related field
 Proficiency in ArcGIS, QGIS, or other mapping software
 Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
