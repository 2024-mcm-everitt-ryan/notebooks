Company: Evans-Brady  
Role: Marine Electronics Technician  

Company Background:  
Evans-Brady is a leading provider of innovative marine electronics solutions, recognized for its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive work environment that values diversity and equality.

Job Type: Full-Time  

Job Description and Responsibilities:  
We are seeking a skilled Marine Electronics Technician to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing marine electronic systems on various types of vessels. Duties include:

- Diagnosing and troubleshooting electronic equipment issues
- Conducting regular maintenance and inspections
- Implementing upgrades and new installations
- Collaborating with team members to ensure optimal performance of systems
- Providing technical support and training to clients
- Complying with safety and regulatory standards

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- A degree or certification in electronics or a related field
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in marine electronics
- Proficiency in using diagnostic tools and software
- Strong problem-solving skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Excellent communication skills
