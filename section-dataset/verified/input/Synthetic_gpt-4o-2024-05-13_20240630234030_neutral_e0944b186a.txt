Company Background:
Butler, Adams and Little is a reputable parking services company renowned for its efficiency and customer service. We manage and operate parking facilities across various locations, ensuring a seamless experience for our clients.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a proactive and responsible Parking Lot Attendant to join our team. The ideal candidate will ensure the smooth operation of the parking lot, provide exceptional customer service, and maintain a safe environment.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor and manage the entry and exit of vehicles.
- Provide assistance and directions to customers.
- Ensure that the parking area is clean and orderly.
- Handle any issues or emergencies that may arise.
- Work collaboratively with the team to ensure efficient operations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Strong attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent customer service skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Physical ability to stand, walk, and lift for extended periods.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from EUR 24,000 to EUR 30,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Opportunities for career advancement.
- Free parking on site.
