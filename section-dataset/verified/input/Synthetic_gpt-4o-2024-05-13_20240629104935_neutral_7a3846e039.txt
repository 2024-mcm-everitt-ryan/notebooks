Company Background:
Chambers, Hickman and Davidson is a leading logistics and distribution company dedicated to creating innovative and efficient supply chain solutions. We pride ourselves on a diverse and inclusive workforce that reflects our commitment to equality and fairness.

Job Type:
Material Moving Worker

Job Description:
We are seeking a diligent and reliable Material Moving Worker to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for moving freight, stock, and other materials to and from storage or production areas, loading docks, and delivery vehicles. This role is crucial to maintaining the smooth operation of our logistics processes.

Responsibilities:
- Load and unload materials from trucks, docks, and storage areas.
- Transport materials to designated areas using manual or powered equipment.
- Verify inventory and report discrepancies.
- Maintain a clean and safe work environment.
- Assist in other warehouse duties as required.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Previous experience in a warehouse or material handling role is preferred but not required.
- Ability to lift heavy objects and perform physical tasks for extended periods.
- Good communication skills and ability to follow instructions.
- Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $30,000 to $40,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive benefits package including health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.
