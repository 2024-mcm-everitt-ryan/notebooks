Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a strong and decisive Assistant Commissioner of Administration to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing administrative operations, ensuring efficiency, and driving strategic initiatives. As an integral part of our leadership team, he will play a critical role in maintaining our heritage and achievements.

Responsibilities:
- Supervise and manage administrative staff to maintain efficient office operations.
- Develop and implement policies and procedures to enhance organizational effectiveness.
- Act as the primary liaison in negotiations and handle complex administrative matters.
- Ensure compliance with all regulations and standards.
- Drive operational excellence through assertive decision-making and leadership.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Public Administration, Business Administration, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in an administrative leadership role.
- Proven ability to make quick, informed decisions in high-pressure situations.
- Strong leadership skills with a focus on assertiveness and efficiency.
- Excellent communication and negotiation skills.
