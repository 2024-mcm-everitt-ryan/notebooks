Miller, Stevenson and Smith is seeking a Technology Program Manager to lead our team of IT professionals in developing and implementing innovative solutions. Our company values diversity and inclusion, and we strive to create a welcoming environment for all employees.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
The Technology Program Manager will oversee the planning, execution, and delivery of technology projects, ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication and leadership skills, with the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement technology projects to meet business objectives
- Manage project budgets and resources to ensure successful delivery
- Collaborate with stakeholders to gather requirements and ensure project deliverables meet their needs
- Foster a culture of innovation and continuous improvement within the technology team

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
- At least 5 years of experience in technology program management
- Strong understanding of project management methodologies (Agile, Waterfall)
- Excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills

Benefits:
- Salary range: $90,000 - $120,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
