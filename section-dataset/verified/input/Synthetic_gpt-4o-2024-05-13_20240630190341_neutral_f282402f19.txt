Company Background:
Crawford, Reed and King is dedicated to promoting health and wellness in our communities. We strive to create inclusive and engaging fitness programs that cater to a diverse range of individuals.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Crawford, Reed and King is seeking a Recreation and Fitness Worker who is passionate about promoting wellness and fitness. The ideal candidate will work closely with clients to develop and implement fitness programs tailored to their needs and goals.

Responsibilities:
- Develop personalized fitness plans for clients
- Lead group fitness classes and workshops
- Monitor and track client progress
- Provide guidance on nutrition and lifestyle changes
- Ensure a safe and welcoming environment for all clients

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Certification in fitness training or related field
- Experience in developing and leading fitness programs
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work with a diverse clientele
- CPR and First Aid certified

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
- Health and wellness benefits
- Opportunities for professional development and growth
- A supportive and inclusive work environment
- Employee discounts on fitness programs and equipment
