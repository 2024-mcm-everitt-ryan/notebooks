Company Background:
Paul-Acosta is a leading procurement solutions provider, dedicated to delivering innovative and effective supply chain management services to our clients.

Job Type: Procurement Analyst

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Procurement Analyst to join our team. As a Procurement Analyst, you will be responsible for analyzing market trends, identifying cost-saving opportunities, and developing strategic procurement plans to drive business growth.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze market trends, customer needs, and competitor activity to develop procurement strategies
 Identify and pursue cost-saving opportunities through supplier negotiations and process improvements
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement procurement plans
 Develop and maintain relationships with suppliers to ensure timely delivery of goods and services
 Monitor and report on procurement performance metrics

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Supply Chain Management, or related field
 At least 3 years of experience in procurement or a related field
 Excellent analytical, communication, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities and deadlines
 Proficiency in MS Office, particularly Excel

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.

Note: The role requires working in a dynamic environment with frequent meetings and presentations, which may involve standing for extended periods and navigating through the office.
