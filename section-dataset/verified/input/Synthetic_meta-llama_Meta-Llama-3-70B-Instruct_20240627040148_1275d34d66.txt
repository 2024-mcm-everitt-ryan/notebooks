Senior Manufacturing Planner at Reid, Ross and Gates

Company Background: 
Reid, Ross and Gates is a leading manufacturing company committed to producing high-quality products and fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment. We believe that a diverse team is essential to driving innovation and achieving our goals.

Job Type: 
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Manufacturing Planner to join our team. As a Senior Manufacturing Planner, you will be responsible for planning, coordinating, and optimizing our manufacturing operations to ensure timely and cost-effective production. If you have a passion for manufacturing and a commitment to excellence, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

- Develop and implement production schedules and plans to meet customer demand
- Analyze production data to identify areas for improvement and optimize operations
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure efficient use of resources
- Manage inventory levels and coordinate with suppliers to ensure timely delivery of materials

Qualifications:

- Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering, Operations Management, or a related field
- 5+ years of experience in manufacturing planning or a related field
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills
