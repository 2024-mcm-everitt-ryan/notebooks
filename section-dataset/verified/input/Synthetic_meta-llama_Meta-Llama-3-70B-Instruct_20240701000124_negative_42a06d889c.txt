Community Service Manager
Company: Collins, Pratt and Lowe
Location: New York, USA

Job Description:
We are seeking a results-driven Community Service Manager to lead our team in developing and implementing community outreach programs. As a key member of our management team, you will be responsible for building strategic partnerships, managing budgets, and supervising a team of community service coordinators.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and execute community outreach programs that align with our organization's mission and values
 Build and maintain relationships with local community organizations, businesses, and government agencies
 Manage budgets and allocate resources effectively to achieve program goals
 Supervise a team of community service coordinators and provide guidance and support as needed
 Analyze program data and make recommendations for improvement

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in community service management or a related field
 Proven track record of achieving results in fast-paced environments
 Strong leadership and communication skills
 Ability to work independently and make tough decisions

About Us:
Collins, Pratt and Lowe is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underserved communities. We offer a dynamic work environment, opportunities for professional growth, and a competitive salary package.

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings, and paid time off.
