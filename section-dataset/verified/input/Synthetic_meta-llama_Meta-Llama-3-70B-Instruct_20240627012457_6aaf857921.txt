Company: Stewart-Woods

Job Title: Data Network Senior Consultant

About Us:
Stewart-Woods is a leading IT consulting firm dedicated to delivering innovative solutions to our clients. We take pride in our diverse team of experts who share our vision of excellence and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Data Network Senior Consultant to join our team. As a Data Network Senior Consultant, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining complex data networks for our clients. You will work closely with our clients to understand their business requirements and develop tailored solutions to meet their needs.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement data networks that meet clients' business requirements
- Conduct network assessments and provide recommendations for improvement
- Collaborate with clients to develop and implement network migration plans
- Troubleshoot network issues and provide timely resolutions
- Develop and maintain technical documentation of network designs and implementations

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
- At least 5 years of experience in data network design and implementation
- Strong knowledge of network protocols and architectures
- Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
