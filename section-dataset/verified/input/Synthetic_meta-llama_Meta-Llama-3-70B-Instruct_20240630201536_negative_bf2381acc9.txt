Job Title: Electrical Power-Line Installer
Company: Tran-Herrera

About Us:
Tran-Herrera is a leading energy solutions provider dedicated to powering communities and businesses. We're committed to delivering exceptional service and fostering a diverse, inclusive workplace.

Job Description:
We're seeking a detail-oriented and empathetic Electrical Power-Line Installer to join our team. As a key player in our operations, you'll be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing electrical power lines, ensuring the safety and reliability of our energy infrastructure.

Responsibilities:

 Install, test, and maintain electrical power lines, towers, and substations
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and resolve issues
 Develop and implement safety protocols to prevent accidents and injuries
 Provide exceptional customer service, ensuring timely and effective issue resolution

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in electrical power-line installation or a related field
 Valid driver's license and willingness to travel up to 20% of the time
 Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities

Perks and Benefits:
We're committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
