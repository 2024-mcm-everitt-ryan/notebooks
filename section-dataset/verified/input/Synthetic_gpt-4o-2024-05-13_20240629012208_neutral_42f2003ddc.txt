Company: Compton Inc.

Role: Human Resources College Aide

Company Background:
Compton Inc. is a globally recognized leader in comprehensive business solutions, committed to creating an inclusive and diverse workplace. Our mission is to foster an environment where every employee can thrive and contribute to our collective success.

Job Type: Part-time, Internship

Job Description:
Compton Inc. is seeking a Human Resources College Aide to assist our HR department in a variety of tasks. This role is designed for current college students who are looking to gain practical experience in the field of human resources. 

Responsibilities:
- Assist in the recruitment process, including posting job openings, screening resumes, and scheduling interviews.
- Support HR staff in organizing and executing employee engagement activities.
- Maintain accurate employee records and ensure compliance with company policies and legal requirements.
- Help with onboarding processes for new hires.
- Provide administrative support to the HR team as needed.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Currently enrolled in an accredited college or university, pursuing a degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.
- Demonstrate a high level of accuracy and precision in all tasks.
- Be a collaborative team player with strong interpersonal skills.
- Ability to handle confidential information with integrity and professionalism.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) is essential.
- Looking for resilient and adaptable individuals who can thrive in a dynamic environment.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

Perks and Benefits:
- Gain hands-on experience in a professional HR setting.
- Opportunity to work with a diverse and inclusive team.
- Flexible working hours to accommodate academic schedules.
- Comprehensive mentorship and professional development programs.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $15 to $20 per hour, based on your experience and skills.

Application Process:
Interested candidates are encouraged to apply through our online portal. Compton Inc. values diversity and encourages individuals from all backgrounds to apply.
