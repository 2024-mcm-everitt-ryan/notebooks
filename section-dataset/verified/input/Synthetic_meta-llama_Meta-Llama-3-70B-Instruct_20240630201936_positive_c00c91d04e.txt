Job Title: Teller
Company: Taylor, Moore and Brown

About Us:
Taylor, Moore and Brown is a dynamic and inclusive banking institution dedicated to fostering a workplace culture that celebrates diversity and promotes equal opportunities. We are committed to creating a workforce that reflects the communities we serve.

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented and motivated Teller to join our team. As a Teller, you will be the face of our bank, providing exceptional customer service, handling transactions, and building strong relationships with our clients. If you are a team player with excellent communication skills and a passion for helping others, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Process customer transactions accurately and efficiently
 Provide exceptional customer service and respond to customer inquiries
 Identify and cross-sell banking products and services to meet customer needs
 Maintain a high level of knowledge about our products and services
 Collaborate with other team members to achieve sales goals and improve customer satisfaction

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in a banking or customer service environment
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 High school diploma or equivalent required; college degree preferred

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $35,000 to $50,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, which includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.

We are committed to supporting women's advancement in the workplace and are dedicated to creating a diverse and inclusive environment. This role is ideal for women looking to advance their careers in the banking industry.
