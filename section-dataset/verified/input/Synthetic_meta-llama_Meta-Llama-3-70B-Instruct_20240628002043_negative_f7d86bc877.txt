Company: Murphy Ltd

Job Title: Senior Case Support Associate

About Us:
Murphy Ltd is a leading provider of case management services, dedicated to delivering exceptional support to our clients. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment that promotes growth and development.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated Senior Case Support Associate to join our team. As a Senior Case Support Associate, you will play a critical role in providing administrative support to our case managers, ensuring seamless case execution, and maintaining exceptional client relationships.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to case managers, including data entry, document preparation, and file management
 Coordinate logistics for client meetings and events
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records and reports
 Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and productivity

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in a related field
 2+ years of experience in a similar role
 Strong organizational and communication skills
 Proficiency in MS Office and case management software
 Recent graduates with a strong understanding of contemporary industry practices are encouraged to apply

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
