Company Background:
Pacheco-Green is a leading organization in records management, dedicated to providing exceptional services in document and data handling. Our commitment to excellence has positioned us as a trusted partner for various industries.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Pacheco-Green is seeking a highly skilled Records Officer to join our dynamic team. This position is designed to attract high-achieving women with a passion for records management and data accuracy. As a Records Officer, you will play a critical role in maintaining and organizing our company’s records, ensuring that all information is accurately documented and readily accessible.

Responsibilities:
- Manage and maintain company records in an organized and efficient manner.
- Ensure accurate documentation and data integrity.
- Develop and implement records management policies and procedures.
- Coordinate with different departments to ensure compliance with records management standards.
- Provide training and support to staff on records management practices.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Records Management, Information Management, or a related field.
- At least 3 years of experience in records management or a similar role.
- Excellent organizational and data management skills.
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
- Women with experience in this field are preferred.

- Competitive salary ranging from USD 45,000 to USD 60,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and flexible working hours.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.

Application Process:
Female candidates will receive special attention during the hiring process. We encourage women with strong skills and experience in records management to apply.
