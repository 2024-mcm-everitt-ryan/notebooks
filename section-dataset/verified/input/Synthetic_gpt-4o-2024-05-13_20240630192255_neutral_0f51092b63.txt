Company Overview:
Fields, Reed, and Frazier is a leading manufacturer of high-quality leather goods. Our commitment to craftsmanship and innovation has set us apart in the industry. We are dedicated to creating a diverse and equitable workplace where everyone can thrive.

Role: Leather Workers

Job Description:
We are seeking skilled leather workers to join our team. The ideal candidate will have experience in leather crafting, a keen eye for detail, and a passion for producing high-quality products. This role involves working collaboratively with other team members to meet production goals and maintain our high standards.

Responsibilities:
- Craft and assemble leather goods according to company standards.
- Collaborate with team members to ensure production targets are met.
- Inspect and ensure the quality of finished products.
- Maintain a clean and organized workspace.
- Follow safety protocols and procedures.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Prior experience in leather working or a related field.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Ability to work collaboratively and contribute to a team-oriented environment.
- Keen attention to detail and commitment to quality.
- Ability to follow detailed instructions and safety guidelines.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 per year, based on experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and growth.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.

Application Instructions:
To apply, please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing your experience and why you would be a great fit for our team. We welcome and respect individuals from all walks of life and are dedicated to creating a diverse and equitable workplace.
