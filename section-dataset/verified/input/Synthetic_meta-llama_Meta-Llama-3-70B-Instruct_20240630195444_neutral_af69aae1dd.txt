Company: Harris-Ewing
Role: Public Health Adviser

About Us:
Harris-Ewing is a leading organization dedicated to promoting public health and wellbeing. We value diverse perspectives and experiences, and our inclusive policies support a diverse workplace.

Job Description:
As a Public Health Adviser, you will play a critical role in developing and implementing health programs that benefit our communities. You will work closely with our team to design and evaluate programs, develop policies, and collaborate with stakeholders to drive positive change.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement health programs that address community needs
 Conduct data analysis and research to inform program decisions
 Collaborate with stakeholders, including community groups and government agencies
 Develop policies and procedures that promote public health
 Provide technical assistance and training to program staff

Qualifications and Experience:

 Master's degree in Public Health or related field
 At least 3 years of experience in public health program development and implementation
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills
 Experience in conflict resolution and operations management is desirable

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 65,000 to USD 80,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings, and paid time off.
