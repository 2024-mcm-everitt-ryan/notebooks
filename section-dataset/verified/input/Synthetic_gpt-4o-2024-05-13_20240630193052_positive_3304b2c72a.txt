Company: Hardy, Howard and Kennedy

Role: Field Operations Training Coordinator

Company Background:
Hardy, Howard and Kennedy is a leading consultancy firm specializing in strategic operations and training solutions. Our innovative approach and commitment to excellence have made us a trusted partner in the industry. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and supportive environment where every team member can thrive.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Field Operations Training Coordinator to join our team. This role involves developing and implementing training programs for our field operations staff, ensuring they have the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their roles. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in training and development, with excellent organizational and communication skills.

Responsibilities:
- Design and deliver comprehensive training programs for field operations staff.
- Monitor and evaluate training effectiveness, making adjustments as needed.
- Collaborate with department heads to identify training needs.
- Maintain training records and documentation.
- Provide support and guidance to trainees throughout their learning process.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Education, Human Resources, or a related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in training and development, preferably in field operations.
- Strong organizational and project management skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
- Ability to work collaboratively in a team-oriented environment.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $50,000 to $70,000, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance plans.
- Generous paid time off and holiday policy.
- Professional development opportunities.
- A supportive and inclusive work culture.
