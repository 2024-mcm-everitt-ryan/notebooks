Company: Guzman-Moon

Role: IT Contracts Specialist

Company Background:
Guzman-Moon is a leading technology services provider that partners with top-tier companies to optimize their IT infrastructure. We believe in fostering innovation and maintaining a competitive edge in the technology sector.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
Guzman-Moon is seeking an IT Contracts Specialist to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will demonstrate a high level of expertise in managing IT contracts, ensuring compliance, and mitigating risks.

Responsibilities:
- Draft, review, and negotiate IT contracts and agreements.
- Ensure contracts comply with legal and company standards.
- Collaborate with various departments to understand contract requirements.
- Mitigate risks and resolve any contract-related issues.
- Maintain accurate records of contracts and related documentation.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Law, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years' experience in contract management, preferably in IT.
- Strong understanding of legal language and contract terms.
- Excellent communication and negotiation skills.
- Ability to work under pressure and manage multiple contracts simultaneously.

- Flexible working hours and remote work options.
