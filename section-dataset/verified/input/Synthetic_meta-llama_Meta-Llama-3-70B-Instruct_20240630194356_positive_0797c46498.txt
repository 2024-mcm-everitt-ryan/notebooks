About Us:
Ross Ltd is a leading social services organization dedicated to providing support to vulnerable communities. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to create a workplace that reflects the communities we serve.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and compassionate Social Service Specialist to join our team. As a Social Service Specialist, you will work closely with our case management team to provide support to our clients, connect them with community resources, and advocate for their needs.

Responsibilities:

 Assess client needs and develop individualized service plans
 Connect clients with community resources, including healthcare, education, and employment services
 Collaborate with case management team to provide comprehensive support
 Advocate for client needs and rights

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Social Work or related field
 2+ years of experience in social services or a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 55,000 to USD 65,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.

Why You Should Apply:
At Ross Ltd, we believe that our team should reflect the diversity of the communities we serve. We are committed to hiring more women in social services, as we believe that their unique perspectives and experiences are essential to providing high-quality support to our clients. If you are a passionate and dedicated individual who is committed to making a difference, we encourage you to apply.
