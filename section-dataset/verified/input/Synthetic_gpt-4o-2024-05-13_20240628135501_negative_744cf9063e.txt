Company: Miller Ltd

Role: Messenger

Company Background: Miller Ltd is a leading logistics company known for its reliable and efficient delivery services. We pride ourselves on maintaining high standards of service and customer satisfaction.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description: Miller Ltd is seeking a dedicated and responsible Messenger to join our team. The ideal candidate will handle the delivery of important documents and packages between various company locations and external clients.

Responsibilities:
- Timely and accurate delivery of documents and packages.
- Coordination with team members to ensure efficient routing.
- Handling of heavy or bulky items regularly.
- Climbing ladders and scaffolding when necessary.
- Use of standard office equipment without modification.
- Maintaining the security and confidentiality of sensitive materials.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Must have perfect manual dexterity.
- Ability to work in environments that are not wheelchair accessible.
- Excellent physical condition to handle regular lifting and climbing.
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
- Must be able to understand and follow complex instructions.

Perks and Benefits:
- Health insurance coverage.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 25,000 to USD 35,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Opportunities for career advancement.

How to Apply: Interested candidates should send their resume and cover letter to hr@millerltd.com with the subject line "Messenger Application."
