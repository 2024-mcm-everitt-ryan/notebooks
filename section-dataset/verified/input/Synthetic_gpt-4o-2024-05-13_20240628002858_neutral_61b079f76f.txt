Company Background:

Full-time

Job Description and Responsibilities:
Moreno Group is seeking a meticulous and experienced GIS Editor to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in geospatial data editing, quality assurance, and data management. Responsibilities include:
- Editing and updating GIS datasets to ensure accuracy and completeness.
- Collaborating with project teams to share expertise and insights.
- Conducting quality control checks to maintain data integrity.
- Assisting in the development of geospatial analysis and maps.
- Providing training and support to other team members as needed.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Geography, GIS, or a related field.
- Proficiency in GIS software (e.g., ArcGIS, QGIS).
- Strong attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
- A portfolio showcasing previous GIS projects is required.
