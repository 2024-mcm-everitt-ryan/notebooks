Company: Wang-Thomas

Role: Architectural Designer

Company Background:
Wang-Thomas is a globally recognized architectural firm known for its innovative designs and sustainable practices. We are committed to creating spaces that enrich lives and communities.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Architectural Designer to join our dynamic team. This role requires a creative and detail-oriented professional who can conceptualize and execute design plans that align with our company's vision and client needs.

Responsibilities:
- Develop comprehensive architectural designs and blueprints.
- Collaborate with clients and project teams to understand project requirements.
- Ensure designs meet regulatory standards and sustainability criteria.
- Manage project timelines and deliverables.
- Perform site visits to ensure project specifications are met.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Architecture or related field.
- A minimum of 3 years of experience in architectural design.
- Proficiency in design software such as AutoCAD, Revit, and SketchUp.
- Strong interpersonal skills to collaborate effectively with various stakeholders.
- Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and meet deadlines.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from £45,000 to £60,000, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- Flexible work hours and remote work options.
- Paid time off and company-sponsored events.

Commitment to Inclusion:
Wang-Thomas is an equal opportunity employer. We promote a diverse and inclusive workplace and encourage candidates from all backgrounds to apply. We value professional growth and a consistent work history.

Application Process:
Interested candidates should submit their resume, portfolio, and a cover letter detailing their relevant experience and design philosophy.
