Company: Sanchez-Shannon

Role: IT Security Officer

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and dedicated IT Security Officer to join our team at Sanchez-Shannon. As an IT Security Officer, you will be responsible for ensuring the security and integrity of our company's computer systems and networks. You will work closely with our IT team to identify and mitigate potential security threats, develop and implement security protocols, and ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement comprehensive security protocols to protect company data and systems
 Conduct regular security audits and risk assessments to identify potential threats
 Collaborate with the IT team to ensure the security and integrity of company systems and networks
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in IT security
 Provide training and support to employees on IT security best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Assurance, or related field
 3+ years of experience in IT security or a related field
 Strong knowledge of security protocols, risk management, and compliance regulations
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.

At Sanchez-Shannon, we are dedicated to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment. We are particularly interested in candidates who can bring diverse sexual orientations and gender identities to our work. LGBTQ+ applicants are strongly encouraged to apply and join our dynamic team.
