Company Background:
Lin-Ramirez is a premier wellness center dedicated to providing exceptional massage therapy services. Our team is passionate about promoting health and well-being through personalized and holistic treatments. We pride ourselves on creating an inclusive and supportive work environment.

Job Type:
Full-Time Massage Therapist

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and professional Massage Therapist to join our team. The ideal candidate will possess excellent technical skills and a strong commitment to providing high-quality care to our clients. Our company thrives on the diversity of its employees, and we value and celebrate diversity in all its forms.

Responsibilities:
- Perform various types of massage treatments tailored to clients' needs.
- Maintain detailed client records and treatment plans.
- Uphold the highest standards of hygiene and professionalism.
- Collaborate with other team members to enhance client experience.
- Participate in ongoing training and development programs.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Certified Massage Therapist with a valid license.
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in a similar role.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills required.
- Proficiency in client management and record-keeping.
- A passion for holistic health and wellness.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive healthcare benefits, including medical, dental, and vision coverage.
- Paid time off and holiday pay.
- Continued education and professional development opportunities.
- A supportive and collaborative work environment.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their resume and a cover letter detailing their experience and passion for massage therapy.
