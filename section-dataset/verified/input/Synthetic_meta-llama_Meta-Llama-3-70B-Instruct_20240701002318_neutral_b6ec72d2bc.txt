Job Title: Buyer Services Manager
Company: White-Gill

We are seeking an exceptional Buyer Services Manager to join our team. As a key member of our procurement department, you will be responsible for managing the entire procurement process, from sourcing to delivery. Your exceptional analytical skills and attention to detail will enable you to identify opportunities for cost savings and process improvements.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement strategic procurement plans to achieve business objectives
 Build and maintain strong relationships with suppliers to ensure timely delivery and quality of goods
 Conduct market research to identify opportunities for cost savings and process improvements
 Analyze data to identify trends and areas for improvement
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless delivery of goods and services

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in procurement or a related field
 Proven track record of achieving cost savings and process improvements
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and negotiation skills
 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Supply Chain Management, or a related field

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $120,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
