Company: Jackson-Parrish

Job Title: Material Moving Worker

About Us:
Jackson-Parrish is a leading logistics company dedicated to delivering exceptional service to our customers. We are seeking a highly motivated Material Moving Worker to join our team.

Job Description:
As a Material Moving Worker, you will be responsible for moving materials and equipment around our warehouse facility. This is a physically demanding role that requires attention to detail and the ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Move materials and equipment around the warehouse facility
 Load and unload trucks and containers
 Ensure inventory is accurate and up-to-date
 Collaborate with other team members to achieve operational goals

Requirements:

 Must be able to lift up to 50 pounds repeatedly throughout the day
 Must be able to travel frequently, including by walking between venues
 Must be able to bend, kneel, and crouch frequently
 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience in a warehouse environment preferred

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
