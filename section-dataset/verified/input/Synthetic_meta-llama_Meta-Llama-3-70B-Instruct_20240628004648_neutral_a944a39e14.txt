Company: Williams, Jimenez and Wood

Job Title: Podiatrist

About Us:
Williams, Jimenez and Wood is a leading healthcare provider dedicated to delivering exceptional patient care. We believe in fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes equality.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and compassionate Podiatrist to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for providing high-quality medical and surgical care to patients with foot and ankle disorders. They will oversee the development and execution of projects, ensuring timely and effective delivery of services.

Responsibilities:

 Diagnose and treat foot and ankle disorders
 Develop and implement treatment plans
 Collaborate with interdisciplinary teams to provide comprehensive care
 Participate in quality improvement initiatives

Qualifications and Experience:

 Doctor of Podiatric Medicine (DPM) degree
 Completion of a podiatric residency program
 Current licensure to practice podiatry
 2+ years of experience in a clinical setting

 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
 Generous paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 A respectful and inclusive workplace for LGBTQ2+ individuals, where diversity is valued and celebrated
