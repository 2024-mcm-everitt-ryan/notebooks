Construction Safety and Quality Inspector

Company: Oconnor, Keller and Weiss

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and dedicated Construction Safety and Quality Inspector to join our team. As a key member of our construction team, you will be responsible for ensuring that all construction projects meet the highest standards of safety and quality. Your attention to detail and ability to multitask will be essential in this fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct regular site inspections to identify potential safety hazards and quality control issues
 Collaborate with project managers to develop and implement corrective action plans
 Develop and maintain detailed reports of site inspections and findings
 Provide guidance and training to construction staff on safety procedures and quality control measures

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in construction safety and quality control
 OSHA certification or equivalent
 Strong communication and leadership skills
 Ability to work effectively in a team environment

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits package
 401(k) matching program
 Opportunities for professional development and growth

What We're Looking For:

A team player who is dedicated to fostering a positive and supportive work environment. Her ability to multitask and prioritize tasks will be essential in this role.
