Company: Garcia, Brown, and Barker

Role: Reinforcing Iron Worker

Company Background:
Garcia, Brown, and Barker is an established company in the construction industry, known for delivering top-quality projects on time and on budget. We value safety, integrity, and professionalism in every task we undertake.

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated reinforcing iron worker to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate must possess physical stamina, attention to detail, and a commitment to quality work. She will be responsible for reinforcing iron and rebar within concrete structures to support and strengthen buildings, bridges, and other infrastructures.

Responsibilities:
- Reading and interpreting blueprints and plans.
- Cutting, bending, and welding rebar and mesh.
- Positioning and securing steel bars or mesh in concrete forms.
- Using various tools and machinery to shape and fit rebar.
- Ensuring compliance with safety regulations and standards.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a reinforcing iron worker.
- Ability to read and interpret construction blueprints.
- Good physical condition and stamina.
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
- Excellent teamwork and communication skills.

Perks and Benefits:
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement plans with employer contributions.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $55,000 per year, based on your experience and skills.

Garcia, Brown, and Barker is an equal opportunity employer. We welcome applications from all qualified candidates.
