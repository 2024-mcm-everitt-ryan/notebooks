Atkinson-Dougherty is seeking a Manager of Technical Operations to join our team. As a Manager of Technical Operations, you will be responsible for leading the technical operations team, developing and implementing process improvements, and ensuring the smooth operation of our technical infrastructure.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee the daily operations of the technical team, ensuring efficient use of resources and effective problem-solving.
- Develop and implement process improvements to increase productivity and reduce costs.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement technical solutions.
- Manage and mentor a team of technical operations specialists.
- Analyze and report on key performance metrics to drive business decisions.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
- 5+ years of experience in technical operations, with at least 2 years in a leadership role.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and leadership skills.
- Proficient in multiple programming languages, including Icelandic.
- Diverse perspectives and backgrounds are valued, and we encourage applicants from all backgrounds to apply.

Benefits:
- Salary range: $90,000 - $120,000 per year.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Company matched pension.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment.
