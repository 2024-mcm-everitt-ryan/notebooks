Job Description:
We're seeking an office clerk to join our team! As an office clerk, you'll play a vital role in ensuring the smooth operation of our office. Your responsibilities will include data entry, filing, and providing administrative support to our team.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and maintain accurate records and databases
 Provide administrative support to the team as needed
 Assist with special projects and tasks as required
 Develop and maintain effective filing systems
 Perform other duties as assigned

Qualifications and Experience:
 1-2 years of experience in an administrative role
 High school diploma or equivalent required; post-secondary education an asset
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and Google Suite
 Excellent organizational and communication skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $50,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program.
