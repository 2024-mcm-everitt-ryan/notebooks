Company Background:
Clark Ltd is a leading law firm specializing in providing comprehensive legal services to businesses and individuals. Our team of experienced attorneys is dedicated to delivering exceptional legal solutions across various sectors.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
Clark Ltd is seeking an experienced Agency Attorney to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong technical background in agency law and the ability to lead with authority. He will be responsible for representing clients in agency-related matters and providing legal counsel to ensure compliance with relevant regulations.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and manage agency-related legal cases with confidence and authority.
- Provide expert legal advice to clients on agency law.
- Draft, review, and negotiate contracts and agreements.
- Represent clients in court and other legal proceedings.
- Collaborate with internal teams to ensure legal compliance and risk management.
- Conduct thorough legal research and analysis to support case strategies.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Juris Doctor (JD) degree from an accredited law school.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in agency law.
- Strong technical background in legal research and analysis.
- Proven ability to lead and manage legal cases with authority.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Member of the bar in good standing.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $120,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement plan with company match.
- Generous paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities and continuing education support.
