Company Background:
Church-Reed is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion in our community. We believe that everyone deserves respect, kindness, and understanding, regardless of their sexual orientation or gender identity.

Job Type: Aide

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and compassionate Aide to join our team. As an Aide, you will provide administrative support to our staff, assist with event planning, and help with daily operations. Your unique perspective and experience will be invaluable in helping us achieve our mission.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to staff members
 Assist with event planning and coordination
 Maintain accurate records and data entry
 Develop and implement administrative processes to increase efficiency
 Collaborate with team members to achieve organizational goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of administrative experience
 Strong communication and organizational skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Passion for promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary range of $40,000 to $55,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our benefits package includes health, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We also provide a supportive and inclusive work environment, with opportunities for professional development and growth.

We are committed to creating a workplace that values and celebrates diversity in all its forms. We believe that diverse perspectives and experiences are essential to achieving our mission. If you are a motivated and passionate individual who shares our values, we encourage you to apply.

We keenly seek out LGBTQ+ candidates to join our team, as we believe their unique perspectives and experiences are crucial to our success.
