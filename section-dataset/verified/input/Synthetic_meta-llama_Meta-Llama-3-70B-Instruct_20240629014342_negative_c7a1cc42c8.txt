Company: Owen LLC
Role: Deputy Budget Director

About Owen LLC:
Owen LLC is a financial services firm committed to providing innovative solutions to our clients.

Job Description:
We are seeking a Deputy Budget Director to join our team. As a Deputy Budget Director, you will be responsible for assisting in the development and implementation of our company's budget. The ideal candidate will have excellent analytical skills, strong attention to detail, and the ability to work well under pressure.

Responsibilities:

 Assist in the development and implementation of the company's budget
 Analyze financial data to identify trends and areas for improvement
 Collaborate with department heads to ensure budget alignment with company goals
 Develop and maintain financial reports and models
 Ensure compliance with financial regulations and policies

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or related field
 At least 5 years of experience in budgeting and financial analysis
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work well under pressure and meet deadlines

Note: Applicants must be willing to work in a fast-paced environment and be comfortable with frequent changes in priorities. Due to the nature of our business, we require all applicants to have a clean driving record and be willing to travel occasionally.

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
