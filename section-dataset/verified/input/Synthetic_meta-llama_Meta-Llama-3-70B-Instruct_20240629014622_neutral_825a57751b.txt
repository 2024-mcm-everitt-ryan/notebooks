Company: Anderson-Mayo

Role: Loan Counselor

Anderson-Mayo is seeking a highly skilled and experienced Loan Counselor to join our team. As a Loan Counselor, you will work closely with clients to understand their financial needs and provide personalized guidance on loan options. Your expertise will help our clients make informed decisions and achieve their financial goals.

Job Description:
The Loan Counselor will be responsible for assessing clients' financial situations, identifying suitable loan products, and providing tailored advice to meet their needs. You will develop and maintain strong relationships with clients, ensuring exceptional customer service and driving business growth.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct in-depth financial assessments with clients to determine their loan eligibility and suitability
 Provide expert guidance on loan options, interest rates, and repayment terms
 Develop and maintain strong client relationships to drive business growth and customer satisfaction
 Meet and exceed sales targets and performance goals
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends, regulatory changes, and competitor activity

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in a similar role, preferably in the financial services industry
 Proven track record of meeting and exceeding sales targets
 Strong understanding of loan products, interest rates, and repayment terms
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for a performance-based bonus, comprehensive health insurance, and a retirement matching program.
