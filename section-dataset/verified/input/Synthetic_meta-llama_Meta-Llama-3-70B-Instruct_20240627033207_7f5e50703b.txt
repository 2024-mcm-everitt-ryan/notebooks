Job Title: Integrated Project Team Leader
Company: Chambers-Turner

Job Description:
Chambers-Turner is seeking an experienced and skilled Integrated Project Team Leader to join our dynamic team. As an Integrated Project Team Leader, you will be responsible for leading cross-functional teams to deliver projects on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and manage project teams to achieve project goals and objectives
- Develop and implement project plans, resource allocation, and risk management strategies
- Collaborate with internal stakeholders to ensure effective project execution
- Monitor and report on project progress, identifying and mitigating potential issues
- Ensure compliance with company policies, procedures, and industry standards

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Project Management, Engineering, or a related field
- 5+ years of experience in project management, with a proven track record of delivering projects successfully
- Excellent leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities

Benefits:
- Salary range: $80,000 - $100,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment

We are seeking candidates who share our values and are committed to delivering exceptional results. If you have a strong project management background and are looking for a new challenge, we encourage you to apply.

Note: We are looking for someone who has always communicated in English at home, as our team primarily uses English as the language of communication.
