Company Background:
Phillips-Hernandez is a celebrated name in the jewelry industry, renowned for our exquisite craftsmanship and dedication to creating timeless pieces. We pride ourselves on fostering a warm, collaborative, and innovative work environment.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
Phillips-Hernandez is seeking a talented jeweler to join our dynamic team. The ideal applicant is a proactive and polite woman who can bring her unique touch to our creations. The position is perfect for a nurturing mom returning to work, as her nurturing skills are essential for this role. Her calm and patient demeanor will be highly valued, and she will bring warmth and positivity to the team.

Responsibilities:
- Design and create custom jewelry pieces.
- Repair and restore damaged jewelry.
- Collaborate with clients to understand their needs and preferences.
- Maintain a clean and organized workspace.
- Ensure all pieces meet our high standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a jeweler or in a similar role.
- Strong attention to detail and excellent hand-eye coordination.
- Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
- Familiarity with various jewelry-making techniques and tools.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $65,000 per year, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Opportunities for professional development and advancement.
- Employee discounts on jewelry pieces.
