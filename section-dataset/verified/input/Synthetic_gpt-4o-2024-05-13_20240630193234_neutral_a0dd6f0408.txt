Company: Howard Group

Role: Preplacement Nurse Practitioner

Company Background:
Howard Group is a leading healthcare provider renowned for its commitment to patient care and innovative health solutions. We thrive on the diversity of our employees and believe that varied perspectives drive our success.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Preplacement Nurse Practitioner to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will provide comprehensive health assessments, develop care plans, and collaborate with healthcare teams to ensure optimal patient outcomes. We foster an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct preplacement health assessments and screenings.
- Develop and implement individualized care plans.
- Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to provide patient-centered care.
- Maintain accurate and timely medical records.
- Educate patients on health maintenance and disease prevention.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Master’s degree in Nursing or equivalent.
- Valid Nurse Practitioner license.
- Minimum of 3 years of clinical experience.
- Strong communication and collaborative skills.
- Experience in managing budgets is required.

- Professional development opportunities.
- A supportive and inclusive workplace culture.
