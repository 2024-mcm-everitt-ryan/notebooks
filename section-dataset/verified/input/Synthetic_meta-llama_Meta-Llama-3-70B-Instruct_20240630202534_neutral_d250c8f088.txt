Job Title: Architectural Designer
Company: Monroe-Li

Monroe-Li is a forward-thinking architecture firm committed to creating innovative and sustainable designs. We pride ourselves on our diverse and inclusive workforce, and we're looking for a talented Architectural Designer to join our team.

We're seeking a detail-oriented and organised Architectural Designer to work on a range of exciting projects. As an Architectural Designer, you will be responsible for designing and developing building designs, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and communicating with clients.

 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Architecture or related field
 2+ years of experience in architectural design
 Proficiency in Autodesk Revit, SketchUp, and Adobe Creative Suite
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills
