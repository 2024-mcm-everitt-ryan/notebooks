Company: Wilkinson, Bryant and Gray

Role: Program Assistant

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Program Assistant to support our team in delivering exceptional results. As a Program Assistant, you will provide administrative support, assist in project coordination, and maintain accurate records.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to the program team, including preparing materials, taking minutes, and maintaining records
 Assist in coordinating projects, including scheduling meetings, booking travel arrangements, and preparing project materials
 Develop and maintain accurate and up-to-date records, reports, and databases
 Collaborate with team members to achieve program goals and objectives
 Perform other administrative tasks as required

Qualifications and Experience:
 1-2 years of experience in an administrative role
 Strong organizational and time management skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work accurately and attention to detail
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and Google Suite

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 40,000 to USD 55,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. Our employees enjoy a flexible work schedule, opportunities for professional development, and a supportive team environment.
