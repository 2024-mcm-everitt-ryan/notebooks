Company: James-Miller

Paramedic

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and dedicated Paramedic to join our team. As a Paramedic, you will be responsible for providing high-quality patient care and transportation in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Responsibilities:

 Respond to emergency calls and provide medical care to patients in need
 Assess patients' conditions and develop appropriate treatment plans
 Collaborate with other emergency medical services (EMS) professionals to ensure seamless care
 Maintain accurate and detailed records of patient interactions and treatments
 Stay up-to-date with the latest medical procedures and protocols

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience as a Paramedic
 Current certification in Paramedicine
 High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree in a related field preferred

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our team members also enjoy comprehensive health insurance, paid time off, and opportunities for professional development and growth.
