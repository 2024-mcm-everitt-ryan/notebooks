Company: Hunter-Massey

Job Title: Strategic Partnership Representative

About Us:
Hunter-Massey is a leading consulting firm dedicated to fostering meaningful connections between businesses and communities. We believe that diverse perspectives and experiences are essential to driving innovation and growth.

Job Description:
We are seeking a Strategic Partnership Representative to join our team. As a Strategic Partnership Representative, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining relationships with key stakeholders, identifying new business opportunities, and driving revenue growth.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and execute strategic partnerships with key stakeholders to drive business growth
- Identify and pursue new business opportunities to expand our client base
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement partnership strategies
- Analyze market trends and competitor activity to inform partnership decisions
- Communicate effectively with internal and external stakeholders to drive results

Qualifications:
- 3+ years of experience in business development, sales, or a related field
- Proven track record of developing and maintaining strategic partnerships
- Excellent communication, negotiation, and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities
- Fluency in English; proficiency in additional languages is a plus

Benefits:
- Salary range: $70,000 - $90,000 per year, plus performance-based bonuses
- Comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Recognition and rewards for outstanding performance
