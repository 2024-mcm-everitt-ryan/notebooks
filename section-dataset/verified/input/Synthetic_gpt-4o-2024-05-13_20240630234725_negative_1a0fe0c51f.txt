Company Background:
Johnson, Thompson and Barajas is a leading civil engineering firm specializing in innovative infrastructure solutions. Our projects span public and private sectors, focusing on sustainable and efficient design.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Title:
Civil Design Lead

Job Description:
We are seeking a Civil Design Lead to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will spearhead design projects, ensuring they meet our high standards of excellence and innovation.

Responsibilities:
- He will lead a team of engineers and technicians.
- Oversee project timelines, budgets, and deliverables.
- Collaborate with clients and stakeholders to ensure project alignment with goals.
- Conduct site visits and inspections.
- Mentor junior staff and provide technical guidance.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering or a related field.
- Minimum of 8 years of experience in civil design and project management.
- Strong leadership skills and the ability to manage a team.
- Proficiency in civil design software and tools.
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving capabilities.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from €80,000 to €120,000, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive healthcare and dental insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday allowance.
- Professional development opportunities and continuous training.
- Employee wellness programs and work-life balance initiatives.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and a cover letter detailing their experience and why they are a fit for this role to hr@jtb-civil.com.
