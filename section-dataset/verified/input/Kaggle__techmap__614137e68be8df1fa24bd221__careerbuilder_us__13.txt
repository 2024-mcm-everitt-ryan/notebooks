Summary

We are seeking a construction Superintendent with ground-up schools and public works project experience at a general contractor. The Superintendent's role is to develop and maintain a collaborative partnership with all project stakeholders to ensure the project meets expectations in a safe, timely, and cost-effective manner.

Client Details

Our client is a leading general contractor and construction management firm serving Southern California school districts and public works clients for over 100 years.

Description

- Take ownership of the project with regard to phasing, logistics, schedule, subcontractor coordination, planning, and project execution.
- Conduct project safety meetings, subcontractor weekly foreman's meetings, schedule update review meetings, 3-week look-ahead meetings, and participate in OAC meetings.
- Enforce safety policies and procedures throughout the course of the project.
- Assist in the development of the baseline schedule, and maintain the project schedule on a monthly basis and ensure the project maintains the scheduled completion date.
- Develop and maintain relationships with the owner, subcontractors, and architect.
- Coordinate field construction work through organization and proper planning, and collaborative relationship with subcontractor representatives on site.
- Set the standard for excellent quality control.
- Knowledge of building codes.
- Review project drawings, specifications, and submittals.
- Review and vet CORs from subcontractors.
- Sign T & M timesheets only when they are justified (do not sign TS verifying time only).
- Attend or hold regular subcontractor and management meetings.
- Perform start-up testing and prepare for owner turnover.
- Document final close-out and guarantee owner acceptance.
- Coordinate the punchlist process with owner and architect.

Profile

- Associate's degree in a related field and 5 years of Project Superintendent-related experience.
- Commercial and/or public works construction experience required.
- Excellent communication skills.
- Possessing a sense of urgency.
- Good understanding of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing building systems.
- Knowledge of legal issues and safety standards is essential.
- Client and vendor relationship management.
- Capacity to motivate, lead, and boost morale of the teams.
- Effective time management and logical decision-making ability.
- Physical and mental fitness is a given.
- Strong focus on quality.
- Working knowledge and competence of Microsoft Office, including MS Teams, Bluebeam.
- Maintain company confidentiality.
