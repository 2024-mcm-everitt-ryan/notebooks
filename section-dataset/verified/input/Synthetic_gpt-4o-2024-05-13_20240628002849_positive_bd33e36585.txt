Company Background:
Mcdaniel, Garcia, and Williams is a leading construction firm known for its commitment to excellence and innovation in building and construction projects. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and dynamic working environment.

Job Type:
Full-Time Cement Mason

Job Description:
We are seeking skilled and dedicated Cement Masons to join our experienced team. The ideal candidate will have a solid understanding of concrete work and a passion for high-quality craftsmanship. 

Responsibilities:
- Prepare and set concrete forms.
- Mix concrete and pour it into forms.
- Smooth and finish surfaces of poured concrete, such as floors, walks, sidewalks, roads, or curbs.
- Monitor the effects of weather on the curing process.
- Apply hardening and sealing compounds to cure surfaces.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a cement mason or similar role.
- Ability to read blueprints, drawings, and sketches.
- Familiarity with construction tools and equipment.
- Strong physical stamina and the ability to lift heavy materials.
- High school diploma or equivalent.
