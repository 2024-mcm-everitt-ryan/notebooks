Job Title: Probation Assistant
Company: Collins, Sanchez and Fowler

We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Probation Assistant to join our team. As a Probation Assistant, you will play a critical role in supporting our probation officers to ensure the successful rehabilitation of offenders.

Job Description:
The Probation Assistant will provide administrative support to our probation team, including data entry, file management, and communication with clients and stakeholders. The ideal candidate will be detail-oriented, possess excellent communication skills, and be able to work effectively in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to probation officers, including data entry, file management, and communication with clients and stakeholders
 Assist in the development and implementation of rehabilitation programs
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records and reports
 Develop and maintain effective relationships with clients, stakeholders, and community partners

Qualifications and Experience:

 Associate's degree in Criminal Justice, Social Work, or a related field
 1-2 years of experience in a similar role, preferably in a probation or corrections setting
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
 Strong organizational and time management skills

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
