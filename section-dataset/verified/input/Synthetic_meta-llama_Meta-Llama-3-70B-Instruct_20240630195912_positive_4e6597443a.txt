Company: Frazier LLC
Job Title: Environmental Health & Safety Regional Manager

About Us:
Frazier LLC is a leading environmental consulting firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions for a sustainable future. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Environmental Health & Safety Regional Manager to join our team. As a Regional Manager, you will be responsible for developing and implementing health and safety programs, conducting site visits, and providing training to employees. Your expertise will help us maintain a safe and healthy work environment for our employees and clients.

We are particularly interested in candidates who share our passion for diversity and inclusion. As part of our commitment to increasing female representation in leadership roles, we encourage women to apply for this position.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement health and safety programs to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
 Conduct site visits to identify potential hazards and implement corrective actions
 Provide training to employees on health and safety procedures
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement safety protocols
 Analyze data to identify trends and areas for improvement

Qualifications:

 Bachelor's degree in Environmental Health, Occupational Health, or a related field
 5+ years of experience in environmental health and safety management
 Proven track record of developing and implementing successful health and safety programs
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and leadership skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
