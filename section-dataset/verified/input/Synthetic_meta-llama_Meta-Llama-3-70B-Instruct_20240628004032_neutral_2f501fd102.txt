Mendez-Hansen is seeking a compassionate and skilled Occupational Therapist Assistant to join our team. As an Occupational Therapist Assistant, you will work closely with our occupational therapists to develop and implement individualized treatment plans that cater to the unique needs of our patients.

Our ideal candidate is a team player who is passionate about creating a welcoming environment for everyone. You will be responsible for assisting with patient assessments, implementing treatment plans, and maintaining accurate records.

To be successful in this role, you will need:

 An associate's degree in occupational therapy assistant studies
 Current certification as a certified occupational therapy assistant (COTA)
 At least 2 years of experience in a healthcare setting
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

 A competitive salary ranging from $55,000 to $65,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills
 Comprehensive health insurance, including medical, dental, and vision coverage
 Generous paid time off, including vacation days, sick leave, and holidays
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 A supportive and inclusive work environment that celebrates diversity and promotes work-life balance
