Company: Mcdonald, Moran and Colon

Job Title: Claim Specialist

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Claim Specialist to join our team. As a Claim Specialist, you will be responsible for investigating and resolving customer claims in a timely and efficient manner. The ideal candidate will have excellent analytical skills, strong communication abilities, and the capacity to work well under pressure.

Responsibilities:

 Investigate and resolve customer claims in a fair and timely manner
 Gather and analyze evidence to support claim resolutions
 Communicate effectively with customers, agents, and internal stakeholders
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date records of claims and resolutions
 Identify and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and customer satisfaction

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a related field or equivalent experience
 2+ years of experience in claims handling or a related field
 Strong analytical, communication, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities
 Proficient in Microsoft Office and claims management software

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
 401(k) matching program
 Paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
