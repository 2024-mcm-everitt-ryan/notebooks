Full-time

Job Description:
Allen-Bennett is seeking a Mechanical Engineer who is passionate about developing advanced mechanical systems. The ideal candidate will contribute to the design, development, and testing of innovative mechanical solutions in a collaborative environment.

Responsibilities:
- Design and develop mechanical components and systems.
- Coordinate with cross-functional teams to ensure project success.
- Conduct tests and evaluations to ensure the reliability and performance of mechanical systems.
- Prepare detailed documentation and reports on project progress.
- Adhere to company standards and industry regulations.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from an accredited university.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in mechanical engineering or a related field.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Ability to manage multiple projects effectively.
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
- Professional growth and consistent work history valued.

- Competitive salary: We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £40,000 to £60,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health insurance and retirement plans.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Flexible working hours and remote work options.
- Supportive and inclusive work culture.
