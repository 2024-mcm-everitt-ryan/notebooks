Company: Williams-Stephens
Role: Licensing Clerk

At Williams-Stephens, we're committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace where everyone can thrive. As part of our efforts to maintain a gender-balanced team, we're actively seeking talented women to join our team as a Licensing Clerk.

Job Description:
We're looking for a highly organized and detail-oriented individual to join our licensing team. As a Licensing Clerk, you'll be responsible for managing licensing agreements, tracking renewals, and ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and maintain accurate records of licensing agreements and renewals
 Coordinate with cross-functional teams to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
 Develop and implement process improvements to increase efficiency
 Provide exceptional customer service to internal and external stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in a related field (licensing, compliance, or administration)
 Strong organizational and communication skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize tasks effectively
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and ability to learn new software quickly

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 15 days of paid vacation and 10 paid holidays per year
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
