Company Background:
Elliott Group is a leader in emergency services, providing reliable and high-quality support in fire and medical emergencies. We pride ourselves on our swift response times and excellent service record.

Job Title:
Fire Medical Officer

Job Description:
Elliott Group is seeking a dedicated and highly skilled Fire Medical Officer to join our team. The Fire Medical Officer will be responsible for responding to fire and medical emergencies, providing on-site medical care, and ensuring the safety of all personnel.

Responsibilities:
- Respond to fire and medical emergencies promptly.
- Provide immediate medical care to injured individuals.
- Operate and maintain firefighting and medical equipment.
- Collaborate with fire and emergency medical teams to ensure coordinated efforts.
- Perform routine equipment checks and maintenance.
- Maintain detailed records of incidents and medical care provided.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- A minimum of 5 years of experience in emergency medical services or firefighting.
- Must be able to carry heavy equipment across various locations.
- Requires excellent motor skills and the ability to reach overhead frequently.
- Must have the ability to meet tight deadlines and manage time effectively.
- Certification as an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) or Paramedic required.
- Advanced knowledge of firefighting techniques and medical procedures.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $50,000 to $70,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.

Application Process:
Interested applicants should submit their resume and cover letter detailing their qualifications and experience. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis until the position is filled.

Elliott Group is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage applications from all qualified individuals.
