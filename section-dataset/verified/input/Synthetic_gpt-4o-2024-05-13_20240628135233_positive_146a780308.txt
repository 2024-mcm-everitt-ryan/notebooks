Company: Moss, Nash and Cunningham  
Role: Lobby Attendant  

Company Background:  
Moss, Nash and Cunningham is a leading firm in the hospitality industry, dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and creating welcoming environments for all guests. We are committed to diversity and inclusion in our workforce, ensuring that all employees feel valued and supported.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:  
We are seeking a dedicated and professional Lobby Attendant to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for maintaining the cleanliness and orderliness of our lobby area, greeting and assisting guests, and ensuring a pleasant experience for all visitors.

Responsibilities:  
- Maintain cleanliness and order in the lobby area.  
- Greet and assist guests with a friendly and helpful demeanor.  
- Provide information and directions to guests.  
- Ensure that all amenities in the lobby are well-stocked and functioning correctly.  
- Report any maintenance issues to appropriate personnel.  
- Assist with luggage and other guest needs as required.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- High school diploma or equivalent.  
- Strong communication and customer service skills.  
- Ability to stand for long periods.  
- Previous experience in a hospitality or customer service role is preferred.  
- We welcome applications from all, especially those with disabilities, to enhance our workforce.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive salary ranging from €25,000 to €30,000 annually, based on experience and skills.  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Paid time off and holidays.  
- Professional development opportunities.  
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.

We invite candidates with disabilities to apply and help us enhance our diverse team.

Application Process:  
Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their resume and cover letter to our HR department at careers@mossnashcunningham.com.
