Company: Strickland-Hernandez

Job Title: Associate & Venture Fellow

- 1-2 years of experience in venture capital, investment banking, or a related field
- Highly proficient in Estonian and French
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills
