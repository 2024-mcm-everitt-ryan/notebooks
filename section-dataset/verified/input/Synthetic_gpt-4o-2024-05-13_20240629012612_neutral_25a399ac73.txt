Company Background:
Aguirre, Vega and Jones is a leading facility management company specializing in providing top-notch maintenance services to various industries. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion, ensuring a supportive workplace for all employees.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and skilled Maintenance Facilitator to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for ensuring our clients' facilities are maintained to the highest standards. This role involves regular inspections, performing repairs, and coordinating with other team members to deliver exceptional results.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct regular inspections of facilities to identify maintenance needs.
- Perform routine maintenance tasks, including plumbing, electrical, and HVAC repairs.
- Coordinate with vendors and contractors for specialized repairs.
- Ensure compliance with safety regulations and company policies.
- Maintain detailed records of maintenance activities and repairs.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Prior experience in a maintenance role preferred but not required.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Excellent communication and organizational skills.

- Competitive salary ranging from €30,000 to €40,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and sick leave.
- Opportunities for professional development and training.
- Parking available for employees with mobility needs.
- We value diverse viewpoints and affiliations.
