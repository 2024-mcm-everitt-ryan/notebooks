Company: Schroeder, Mason and Herrera

Role: Structural Iron and Steel Worker

Company Background:
Schroeder, Mason and Herrera is a well-established construction firm with a history of delivering quality infrastructure projects. Our commitment to innovation and safety has made us a leader in the industry.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented Structural Iron and Steel Worker to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for fabricating, assembling, and installing steel frameworks for various construction projects. This role requires a keen eye for detail, strong technical skills, and a commitment to safety.

Responsibilities:
- Fabricate and install structural iron and steel components for buildings, bridges, and other infrastructure projects.
- Read and interpret blueprints, specifications, and construction plans.
- Operate hand tools, welding equipment, and machinery safely and efficiently.
- Inspect and maintain equipment to ensure it is in good working condition.
- Collaborate with team members to ensure project timelines and goals are met.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in structural iron and steel work.
- Proficient in reading blueprints and technical drawings.
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
- Physical stamina and manual dexterity.
- Commitment to workplace safety practices.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- Inclusive and diverse work environment.

Application Process:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and a cover letter detailing their relevant experience and qualifications. We look forward to welcoming a passionate and skilled professional to our team.
