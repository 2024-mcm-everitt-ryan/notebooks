Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated and disciplined Sergeant to join our team. This role requires leadership, vigilance, and an unwavering commitment to the safety of our clients and team members. The ideal candidate will uphold the highest standards of conduct and professional ethics.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and manage a team of security personnel.
- Develop and implement security protocols.
- Conduct regular training sessions to ensure team readiness.
- Monitor and report on security activities.
- Coordinate with other law enforcement agencies as needed.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in a security or law enforcement role.
- Proven leadership skills and ability to manage a team.
- Strong understanding of security protocols and procedures.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- High moral and ethical standards.

- Competitive salary ranging from $50,000 to $70,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Health and dental insurance.
- Retirement plans.
