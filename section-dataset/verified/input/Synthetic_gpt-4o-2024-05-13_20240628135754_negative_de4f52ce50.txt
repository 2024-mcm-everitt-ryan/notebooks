Company: Barber-Cook

Role: Tour Guide

Company Background:
Barber-Cook is a leading global travel company known for providing exceptional travel experiences to adventurous clients. We specialize in offering guided tours across various exotic locations, emphasizing rich cultural heritage and thrilling adventures.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Barber-Cook is seeking an enthusiastic and dynamic Tour Guide to join our team. The successful candidate will lead groups through various tour locations, providing insightful information and ensuring an engaging experience for all participants. This role is perfect for someone who loves travel and has a deep knowledge of diverse cultures and attractions.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct guided tours in various locations, ensuring the safety and satisfaction of all participants.
- Prepare and present information about tour destinations in an engaging manner.
- Handle tour logistics, including transportation and scheduling.
- Respond to customer inquiries and provide exceptional customer service.
- Maintain high energy and enthusiasm throughout tours.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Must be able to reach overhead frequently.
- Requires the ability to stand for long periods during presentations.
- Must be able to work in noisy environments.
- Excellent communication skills.
- Previous experience in tour guiding or a similar role is preferred.
- Ability to handle unexpected situations and solve problems efficiently.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from 30,000 EUR to 45,000 EUR, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.
- Paid travel expenses and accommodation during tours.
- Employee discounts on all Barber-Cook travel packages.

Join Barber-Cook and be part of an exciting team that brings adventure and knowledge to travelers worldwide!
