Job Title: Team Lead - Red Team
Company: Richardson Inc

About Us:
Richardson Inc is a leading cybersecurity firm dedicated to protecting our clients' digital assets. Our team of experts works collaboratively to stay ahead of emerging threats and provide innovative solutions.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Team Lead to join our Red Team, responsible for simulating real-world attacks to test our clients' defenses. As a Team Lead, you will oversee a team of penetration testers, guiding them to identify vulnerabilities and develop remediation strategies.

Responsibilities:
- Lead a team of penetration testers to conduct comprehensive security assessments
- Develop and implement testing methodologies to identify system vulnerabilities
- Collaborate with clients to understand their security concerns and develop tailored solutions
- Foster a culture of innovation, encouraging team members to share their ideas and expertise
- Stay up-to-date with emerging threats and technologies, applying this knowledge to improve our testing methodologies

Qualifications:
- 5+ years of experience in penetration testing or a related field
- Strong understanding of network protocols, operating systems, and cloud architectures
- Proven leadership experience, with the ability to motivate and guide a team
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Relevant certifications, such as OSCP or CEH

Benefits:
- Salary range: $100,000 - $130,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative work environment that values diversity and innovation
- Flexible work arrangements, including remote work options
- Access to cutting-edge technologies and training
