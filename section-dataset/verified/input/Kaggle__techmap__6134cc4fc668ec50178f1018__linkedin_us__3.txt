Position Summary

We have an exciting opportunity to join our team as a Payroll Systems Analyst. In this role, the successful candidate will be responsible for configuration of MyTime pay policies with analysis of documented Pay Policies including implementing and testing of system changes. This role will work closely with the MyTime Systems Manager to support new and existing processes and systems initiatives. The ideal candidate is able to operate in a fast-paced changing environment. He/she should have the ability to foster relationships by establishing and working collaboratively within the department. They will focus on building the MyTime system by participation on MyTime related projects with clear deliverables and due dates in alignment with signed-off functional specifications in adherence with Institutional Pay Policies.
