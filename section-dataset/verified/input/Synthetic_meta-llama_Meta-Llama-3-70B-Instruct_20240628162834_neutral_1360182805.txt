 Master's degree in Organizational Development, Human Resources, or related field
 At least 5 years of experience in organizational development and training
 Proven track record of designing and delivering effective training programs
 Excellent communication, facilitation, and project management skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities
