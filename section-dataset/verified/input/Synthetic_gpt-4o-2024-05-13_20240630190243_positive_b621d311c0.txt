Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Perez-Rodriguez is seeking a dedicated and passionate Program Specialist to join our dynamic team. This role is ideal for women looking to advance their careers and make a significant impact within the company. The Program Specialist will be responsible for overseeing and implementing various projects, ensuring they align with our organisational goals and values.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and manage program strategies to achieve company objectives.
- Coordinate cross-functional teams to ensure project completion.
- Monitor and report on program progress, making adjustments as needed.
- Collaborate with stakeholders to identify and address program needs.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Project Management, or a related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in program management or a similar role.
- Strong organisational and leadership skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Female candidates will be given priority in the selection process.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health insurance plans.
- Flexible working hours and remote work options.
- Professional development and career advancement opportunities.
