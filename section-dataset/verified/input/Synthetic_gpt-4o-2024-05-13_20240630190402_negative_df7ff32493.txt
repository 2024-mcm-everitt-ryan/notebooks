Company: Smith, Hughes and Jackson

Role: Senior Forester for Restitution

About Us:
Smith, Hughes and Jackson is a leading environmental consultancy firm dedicated to sustainable forestry practices and ecological restoration. Our mission is to foster a balanced relationship between humanity and nature through innovative and ethical solutions.

Job Description:
We are seeking a Senior Forester for Restitution to lead our team in restoring and managing forest ecosystems. She will be responsible for developing and implementing forest management plans, conducting ecological assessments, and collaborating with stakeholders to ensure the success of our projects. Her ability to teach and nurture new hires is crucial for the role.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and execute forest management and restoration plans.
- Conduct ecological assessments and prepare detailed reports.
- Collaborate with local communities and stakeholders.
- Mentor and support new hires, bringing warmth and positivity to the team.
- Ensure compliance with environmental regulations and standards.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Forestry, Environmental Science, or a related field.
- Minimum of 7 years of experience in forestry management and ecological restoration.
- Strong leadership and team management skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
- Her experience in nurturing and mentoring new hires is essential.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $100,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Generous vacation and paid time off.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Flexible work schedule to promote work-life balance.
