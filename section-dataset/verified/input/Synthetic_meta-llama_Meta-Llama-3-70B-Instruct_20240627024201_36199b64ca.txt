Company: Norton, Maynard and Williamson
Role: Events & Communications Manager

About Us:
Norton, Maynard and Williamson is a leading marketing agency dedicated to delivering innovative solutions to our clients. Our diverse team of professionals is committed to excellence, creativity, and collaboration.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Events & Communications Manager to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for planning, executing, and evaluating corporate events, conferences, and product launches. Additionally, they will develop and implement effective communication strategies to engage our target audience.

Responsibilities:

- Develop and manage event budgets, timelines, and logistics
- Coordinate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless event execution
- Design and implement communication plans, including social media, email, and press releases
- Build and maintain relationships with key stakeholders, including vendors, clients, and partners
- Analyze event metrics and provide recommendations for future improvements

Qualifications:

- Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or a related field
- Minimum 5 years of experience in event planning and communications
- Proven track record of successful event execution and communication campaigns
- Excellent project management, communication, and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
