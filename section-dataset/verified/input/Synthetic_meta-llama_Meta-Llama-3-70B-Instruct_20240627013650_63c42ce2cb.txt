Company: Curtis-Gordon
Role: Patient Communications Representative

About Us:
Curtis-Gordon is a leading healthcare organization committed to delivering exceptional patient care and promoting diversity in the workplace. Our mission is to foster an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives and experiences thrive.

Job Description:
We are seeking a Patient Communications Representative to join our team. As a key member of our patient services department, you will be responsible for communicating with patients, addressing their concerns, and providing exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:
- Respond to patient inquiries via phone, email, and mail
- Resolve patient concerns in a timely and professional manner
- Collaborate with healthcare professionals to ensure seamless communication
- Maintain accurate records of patient interactions
- Participate in quality improvement initiatives to enhance patient satisfaction

Qualifications:
- High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree preferred
- 1-2 years of experience in customer service or healthcare
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

What We Offer:
- Salary range: $40,000 - $50,000 per year
- Comprehensive benefits package including health, dental, and vision insurance, company matched pension, and paid time off
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Recognition and rewards for outstanding performance

We prioritize applications from professionals of diverse racial backgrounds, including but not limited to those of Asian heritage, Hispanic and Latino professionals, individuals of West African descent, professionals of Igbo descent, and candidates educated in Southeast Asia. We believe that a diverse workforce is essential to delivering exceptional patient care and achieving our mission.
