Company: Ford, Bennett and Rangel
Job Title: Recruiting Specialist
Job Type: Full-time

About Us:
Ford, Bennett and Rangel is a leading recruitment firm dedicated to providing top-notch talent acquisition services to clients across various industries. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to create a workplace culture that is welcoming and respectful to all.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Recruiting Specialist to join our team. As a Recruiting Specialist, you will be responsible for sourcing, selecting, and hiring top talent for our clients. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills, be proficient in applicant tracking systems, and have a strong understanding of the recruitment process.

Responsibilities:
- Source and attract top talent through various channels, including social media and job boards
- Conduct phone and in-person interviews to assess candidate fit
- Manage candidate pipelines and ensure timely communication
- Collaborate with hiring managers to understand client needs and preferences
- Develop and implement effective recruitment strategies to meet client goals

Qualifications:
- 2+ years of experience in recruitment or a related field
- Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business, or a related field
- Proficiency in applicant tracking systems and recruitment software
- Excellent communication, interpersonal, and organizational skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
- Competent in Mandarin, Spanish, or other languages is an asset
- Dedicated to fostering an inclusive culture and promoting diversity and equity in the workplace

Benefits:
- Salary range: $50,000 - $70,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Recognition and rewards for outstanding performance
