Company: Wallace-Jones

Role: Animal Caretaker

Company Background: Wallace-Jones is a leading animal welfare organization dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation, and rehoming of animals in need. We strive to create a safe and loving environment for all animals, supported by a team of compassionate and dedicated professionals.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:  
We are seeking a dedicated and compassionate Animal Caretaker to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for animal welfare and the ability to provide exceptional care to a variety of animals, ensuring their health, safety, and well-being.

Responsibilities:
- Provide daily care, feeding, and exercise for animals.
- Monitor animal health and behavior, reporting any concerns to the veterinary team.
- Clean and maintain animal enclosures and facilities.
- Assist with medical treatments and rehabilitation as needed.
- Participate in community outreach and educational programs.
- Maintain accurate records of animal care and treatment.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Experience in animal care or a related field preferred.
- Strong communication and teamwork skills.
- Ability to perform physically demanding tasks.
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- We invite individuals with disabilities to apply and contribute to our diverse workforce.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary: We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 35,000 to USD 50,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and training.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.

Wallace-Jones is an equal opportunity employer. We value diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
