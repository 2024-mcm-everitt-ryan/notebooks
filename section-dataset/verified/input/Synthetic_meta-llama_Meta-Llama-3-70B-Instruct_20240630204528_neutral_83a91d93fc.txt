Company: Young-Davila

Role: Jeweler

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Jeweler to join our team at Young-Davila. As a Jeweler, you will be responsible for creating and repairing jewelry pieces, working with customers to design custom pieces, and maintaining a high level of quality and attention to detail in your work.

 Work with customers to understand their design preferences and create custom pieces
 Repair and restore jewelry pieces, including cleaning and polishing
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace, adhering to safety protocols
 Collaborate with other team members to meet sales and customer service goals

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience as a Jeweler or in a related field
 Strong knowledge of jewelry design and repair techniques
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
