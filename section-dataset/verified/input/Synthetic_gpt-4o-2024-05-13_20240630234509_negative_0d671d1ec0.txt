Company Background:
Hall-Fowler is a leading logistics and fleet management company operating globally. We specialize in efficient and reliable transportation solutions for a wide array of industries.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Hall-Fowler is seeking a Fleet Assistant to join our dynamic team. This individual will play a crucial role in supporting the fleet management operations, ensuring the smooth functioning of our transportation services.

Responsibilities:
- Assist in the scheduling and coordination of logistics.
- Maintain records of vehicle maintenance and inspections.
- Monitor fuel usage and expenses.
- Ensure compliance with transportation regulations.
- Provide support in the tracking of fleet performance metrics.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- A strong background in manpower planning is essential.
- Proven experience in logistics or fleet management.
- Excellent organizational and time management skills.
- Detail-oriented individuals will thrive in this role.
- Strong physique and ability to handle manual tasks.
- His experience in customer service is preferred.

Perks and Benefits:

- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid vacation and sick leave.
- Opportunities for career growth and development.
- Access to company vehicles for personal use when necessary.
