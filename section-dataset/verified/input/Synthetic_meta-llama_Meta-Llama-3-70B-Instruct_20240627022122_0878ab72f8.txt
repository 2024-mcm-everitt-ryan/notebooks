Company: Powers-Martinez

Job Title: Business Unit Director

About Us:
Powers-Martinez is a leading retail corporation dedicated to providing exceptional customer experiences. We value diversity, equity, and inclusion in our workplace culture.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Business Unit Director to lead our sales team in driving business growth and profitability. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of success in sales management, excellent communication skills, and the ability to build strong relationships with cross-functional teams.

Responsibilities:

- Develop and execute sales strategies to achieve business objectives
- Lead and motivate a team of sales representatives to meet targets
- Analyze market trends and competitor activity to identify opportunities
- Collaborate with marketing and operations teams to drive business growth
- Foster a culture of innovation, accountability, and continuous improvement

Qualifications:

- 5+ years of experience in sales management
- Strong leadership and communication skills
- Proven ability to drive sales growth and profitability
- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration or related field

Benefits:
We offer a salary range of $90,000 to $120,000 per year, performance-based bonuses, comprehensive health insurance, and opportunities for professional growth and development.
