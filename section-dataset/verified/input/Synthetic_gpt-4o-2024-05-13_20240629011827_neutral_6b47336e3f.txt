Company: Moore, Glass and Williams

Role: Medical Assistant

Company Background:
Moore, Glass and Williams is a reputable medical practice dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services. We believe in fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment that supports the professional growth of our employees.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a compassionate and detail-oriented Medical Assistant to join our team. The ideal candidate will support our healthcare providers in delivering high-quality patient care.

Responsibilities:
- Prepare patients for examinations and treatments
- Assist healthcare providers with procedures and medical paperwork
- Maintain patient records and ensure accurate data entry
- Administer medications and perform basic lab tests
- Manage inventory of medical supplies and equipment
- Provide patient education and support

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent required
- Medical Assistant certification preferred but not mandatory
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to balance work and personal life effectively
- Previous experience in a medical setting is advantageous
- Must be able to maintain a professional appearance in accordance with company policy
- Background checks will be conducted for all applicants

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
- Retirement savings plan with company match
- Paid time off and holiday pay
- Professional development opportunities

To Apply:
Interested candidates are encouraged to apply with their resume and cover letter. Moore, Glass and Williams is an equal opportunity employer and welcomes applicants from all backgrounds.
