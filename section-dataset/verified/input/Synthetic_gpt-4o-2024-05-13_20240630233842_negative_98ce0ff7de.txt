Company Background:
Adkins, Mcgee and Harris is a leading consultancy firm specializing in public policy, training, and organizational development. We collaborate with a wide range of clients, from government agencies to private corporations, to develop effective policies and training programs that drive meaningful change.

Job Type:
Public Policy and Training Coordinator

Job Description:
We are seeking a dynamic Public Policy and Training Coordinator to join our team. This role requires a results-driven individual who can lead and inspire our training initiatives while managing public policy projects. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in policy analysis and training program development.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and manage public policy projects, including research, analysis, and report writing.
- Develop and deliver training programs that enhance organizational effectiveness.
- Coordinate with various stakeholders to ensure the successful implementation of policies and training initiatives.
- Monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of training programs and policy implementations.
- Provide expert advice and recommendations to senior management.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Public Policy, Political Science, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in public policy analysis and training program development.
- Strong analytical and research skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Ability to lead and inspire a team.
- Experience in coordinating with government agencies and private sector clients.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $90,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Flexible work environment.
