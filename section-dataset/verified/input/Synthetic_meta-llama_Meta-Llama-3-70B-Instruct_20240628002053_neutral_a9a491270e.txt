Job Title: Bill and Account Collector

Company Background:
Beard PLC is a leading financial services company dedicated to providing innovative solutions to individuals and businesses. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace that values the contributions of all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Bill and Account Collector to join our team. As a Bill and Account Collector, you will be responsible for collecting payments from customers, resolving billing discrepancies, and maintaining accurate records. If you have excellent communication skills, are detail-oriented, and have a passion for providing exceptional customer service, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Collect payments from customers via phone, email, or mail
 Resolve billing discrepancies and provide solutions to customers
 Maintain accurate records of customer interactions and payments
 Meet and exceed monthly collection targets
 Collaborate with internal teams to resolve customer issues

Qualifications and Experience:
We are open to candidates with necessary skills and experience in billing, collections, or customer service. A high school diploma or equivalent is required.

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary, ranging from $40,000 to $60,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
