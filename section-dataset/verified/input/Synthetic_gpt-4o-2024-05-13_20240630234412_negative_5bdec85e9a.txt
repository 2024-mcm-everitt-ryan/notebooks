Company Background:
Potts, Miller and Stone is a renowned firm specializing in logistics and postal services with a strong presence in multiple regions. Our commitment to efficiency and innovation has made us a trusted name in the industry.

Job Title: Mail Superintendent

Job Description:
We are seeking a dynamic and skilled individual to join our team as a Mail Superintendent. The role is pivotal in ensuring smooth mail operations and requires a candidate with strong leadership skills.

Responsibilities:
- Oversee the daily operations of mailroom staff.
- Ensure timely processing and distribution of mail.
- Maintain equipment and order necessary supplies.
- Implement and monitor security procedures for mail handling.
- Coordinate with other departments to streamline mail processes.
- Train and mentor mailroom personnel.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Previous experience in a supervisory role within a mailroom or similar environment.
- Strong communication and organizational skills.
- Ability to lift and move heavy mail bags and packages. Reasonable accommodations will be made for those who require them.
- Familiarity with postal regulations and procedures.
- Preferred: Experience as a crewman or in a similar physically demanding role.
- Proud of our heritage and achievements.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from 30,000 to 45,000 GBP annually, based on experience and skills.
- Health insurance and retirement benefits.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Paid time off and flexible working hours.

Join our team and be a part of a company that values dedication and excellence in mail operations.
