Job Title: Letterer and Sign Painter
Company: Mclaughlin and Sons

About Us:
Mclaughlin and Sons is a boutique sign-making company dedicated to creating visually stunning signs for local businesses. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and commitment to quality.

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented Letterer and Sign Painter to join our team. As a key member of our design team, she will be responsible for creating beautiful, hand-painted signs that capture the essence of our clients' brands. The ideal candidate will have a keen eye for detail, excellent color-matching skills, and the ability to work efficiently in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Design and paint signs for clients, ensuring accuracy and attention to detail
 Collaborate with designers to ensure seamless execution of design concepts
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace, adhering to safety protocols
 Meet tight deadlines while maintaining high-quality standards

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in lettering and sign painting
 Portfolio showcasing attention to detail and color-matching skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Excellent communication skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on experience and skills. Our team enjoys a relaxed, creative atmosphere, and we prioritize work-life balance. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
