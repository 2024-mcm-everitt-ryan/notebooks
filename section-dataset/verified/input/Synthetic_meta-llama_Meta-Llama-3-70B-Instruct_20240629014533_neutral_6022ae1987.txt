Company: Morton, Smith and Norton

Job Title: Cement Mason

Job Description:
Morton, Smith and Norton is seeking a skilled and experienced Cement Mason to join our team. As a Cement Mason, you will be responsible for laying and finishing cement, concrete, and other materials to construct and repair structures. You will work closely with our construction team to ensure projects are completed efficiently and to a high standard.

Responsibilities:

 Lay and finish cement, concrete, and other materials to construct and repair structures
 Read and interpret blueprints, drawings, and specifications
 Operate hand and power tools, including trowels, edgers, and mixers
 Work safely and efficiently to meet project deadlines
 Collaborate with other construction team members to achieve project goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience as a Cement Mason
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced environment
 High school diploma or equivalent required; vocational training or apprenticeship in Cement Masonry preferred
 Applicants from all educational backgrounds are welcome
 Looking for candidates with a friendly and engaging personality

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental insurance
 Opportunities for professional development and advancement
 A dynamic and supportive work environment
