Job Title: Fire Inspector
Company: Crawford Group

Job Description:
The Crawford Group is seeking a highly skilled Fire Inspector to join our team. As a Fire Inspector, you will be responsible for conducting fire safety inspections, investigating fires, and providing fire prevention education to the community.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct fire safety inspections of commercial and residential properties to identify potential hazards
 Investigate fires to determine cause and origin
 Provide fire prevention education to the community through public outreach programs
 Develop and implement fire safety plans and procedures
 Collaborate with other emergency responders to ensure effective response to emergencies

Qualifications and Experience:

 Must possess a strong technical background in fire science or a related field
 Must have excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Must be able to work independently and as part of a team
 Must have a strong attention to detail and ability to analyze complex information
 2+ years of experience in fire inspection or a related field

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan. We also provide opportunities for professional development and growth within the company.
