The Maintenance teammate is responsible for:
- Keeping a clean and safe storage facility to ensure condition meets company guidelines (i.e. landscaping, parking and paving, fence, walls, doors, painting)
- Performing minor and routine maintenance activities throughout the facility
- Providing upkeep of the appearance of the building and property by sweeping hallways, stairs and storage spaces, including restroom areas
- Cleaning vacated units on a timely basis and preparing units for future rentals
- Reporting maintenance requirements, potential equipment failures and unsafe conditions to management
- Other duties as assigned

You’d be great in this role if you can:

- Communicate and interact professionally with teammates and customers
- Make basic repairs
- Use handheld tools and equipment
