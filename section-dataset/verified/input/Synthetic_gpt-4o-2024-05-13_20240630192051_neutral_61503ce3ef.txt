Company: Avila Inc

Role: Naval Architect

About Avila Inc:
Avila Inc is a pioneering maritime engineering firm dedicated to designing the naval vessels of the future. We prioritize innovation, sustainability, and excellence in all our projects. We strive to create a workplace where everyone feels valued and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives and skills.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking a qualified Naval Architect to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have strong technical skills and a passion for designing vessels that meet rigorous safety and performance standards. As a Naval Architect at Avila Inc, you will collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop innovative maritime solutions.

Responsibilities:
- Design and develop naval vessels, ensuring they meet safety, performance, and regulatory standards.
- Collaborate with engineers, project managers, and clients to understand requirements and deliver optimal design solutions.
- Conduct simulations and tests to validate vessel performance.
- Prepare detailed technical drawings and specifications.
- Stay updated with the latest industry trends and technological advancements.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Naval Architecture, Marine Engineering, or a related field.
- 3+ years of experience in naval architecture or maritime design.
- Proficiency in CAD software and other relevant design tools.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and flexible working hours.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- Collaborative and inclusive work environment.
