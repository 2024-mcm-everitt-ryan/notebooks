Company: Kelly-Sanchez

Job Title: Radio and Telecommunications Equipment Installer

Job Type: Full-time

About Us:
Kelly-Sanchez is a leading provider of telecommunications solutions, dedicated to delivering innovative and reliable services to our customers.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Radio and Telecommunications Equipment Installer to join our team. As a Radio and Telecommunications Equipment Installer, you will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing radio and telecommunications equipment. You will work closely with our team to ensure that our equipment is functioning at optimal levels, providing excellent service to our customers.

Responsibilities:

 Install, maintain, and repair radio and telecommunications equipment
 Conduct site surveys to determine equipment installation requirements
 Collaborate with team members to ensure efficient equipment operation
 Troubleshoot and resolve equipment faults
 Provide exceptional customer service

Qualifications and Experience:

 Relevant qualifications in electronics, telecommunications, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in installing and maintaining radio and telecommunications equipment
 Strong understanding of telecommunications principles and practices
 Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
