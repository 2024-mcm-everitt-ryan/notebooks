Company Background:
Pearson-Fleming is a leading provider of e-learning solutions, renowned for our innovative approach and commitment to education. We pride ourselves on delivering top-notch content that empowers learners globally.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are seeking an assertive e-learning content developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be a forceful communicator and possess a firm and commanding presence. If you are driven by success and motivated to achieve results, Pearson-Fleming is the place for you.

Responsibilities:
- Develop engaging and interactive e-learning content.
- Collaborate with subject matter experts to ensure content accuracy.
- Implement feedback to refine and improve learning materials.
- Provide regular updates on progress and meet tight deadlines.
- Uphold the high standards of Pearson-Fleming’s educational approach.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Education, Instructional Design, or a related field.
- Proven experience in content development, preferably in e-learning.
- Strong writing and editing skills with attention to detail.
- Ability to work independently and assertively.
- Familiarity with e-learning authoring tools and learning management systems.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from £40,000 to £55,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance.
- Opportunities for professional development.
- Flexible working hours.
- Collaborative and energetic work environment.
