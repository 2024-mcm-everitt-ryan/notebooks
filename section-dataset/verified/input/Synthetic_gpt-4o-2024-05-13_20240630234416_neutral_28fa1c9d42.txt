Company Background:
Randall, Waters and Vazquez is a globally renowned dance company known for pushing the boundaries of contemporary dance. We are dedicated to fostering creativity and excellence in the arts, providing a platform for both emerging and established dancers to showcase their talents.

Job Type:
Full-Time Dancer

Job Description:
We are seeking dedicated and skilled dancers to join our dynamic team. This role requires individuals who are passionate about dance and willing to contribute to our diverse and inclusive environment. Candidates should be proficient in various dance styles and willing to participate in rigorous training and performances.

Responsibilities:
- Participate in daily rehearsals and training sessions.
- Perform in scheduled performances and tours.
- Collaborate with choreographers and other dancers to create innovative routines.
- Maintain physical fitness and adhere to a healthy lifestyle to meet the demands of the role.
- Engage with the community through workshops and outreach programs.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience in professional dance, with proficiency in multiple styles.
- Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
- Strong physical endurance and the ability to perform complex routines.
- Open to feedback and continuous improvement.
- A passion for the arts and commitment to the company's mission.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from €30,000 to €45,000, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits.
- Opportunities for professional development and further training.
- Travel opportunities for tours and performances.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.

Application Process:
Interested candidates should submit a resume, a cover letter detailing their dance experience, and a video of their recent performance.
