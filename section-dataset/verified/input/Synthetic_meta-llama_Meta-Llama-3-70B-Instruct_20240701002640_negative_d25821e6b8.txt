Company: Torres, Smith and Jenkins

Job Type: Bellhop

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled and physically fit bellhop to join our hospitality team. As a bellhop, you will be responsible for assisting guests with their luggage, providing exceptional customer service, and maintaining a high level of energy and enthusiasm throughout your shift.

Responsibilities:

 Greet guests upon arrival and assist with luggage
 Escort guests to their rooms and provide room orientation
 Maintain a clean and organized lobby area
 Provide exceptional customer service and respond to guest requests in a timely and professional manner

Qualifications and Experience:
 High school diploma or equivalent required
 Previous experience in a customer-facing role preferred
 Ability to lift 50 pounds (with reasonable accommodations) and man the reception area for long periods of time
 Strong communication and problem-solving skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $30,000 to $40,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
