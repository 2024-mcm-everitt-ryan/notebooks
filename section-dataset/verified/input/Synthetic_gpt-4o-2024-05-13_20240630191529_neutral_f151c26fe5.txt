Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking an administrative associate to support our executive director. The ideal candidate will be organized, detail-oriented, and possess excellent written and verbal communication skills. This role is crucial for ensuring the executive director's office operates smoothly and efficiently.

Responsibilities:
- Manage and prioritize the executive director’s schedule and appointments.
- Coordinate meetings and prepare agendas, minutes, and follow-up actions.
- Handle confidential information with the utmost discretion.
- Assist in the preparation of reports, presentations, and correspondence.
- Act as a liaison between the executive director and internal/external stakeholders.
- Oversee special projects and perform other administrative duties as assigned.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in an administrative role, preferably in a fast-paced environment.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) and other relevant software.
- Strong organizational skills with the ability to multitask and prioritize effectively.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills required.
- Proven ability to handle confidential information with integrity.

- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from GBP 35,000 to GBP 45,000, based on your experience and skills.
