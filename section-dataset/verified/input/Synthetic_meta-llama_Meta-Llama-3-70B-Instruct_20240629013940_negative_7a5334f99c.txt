 Bachelor's degree in Economics or related field
 At least 3 years of experience in economic analysis
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Proficiency in econometric software and programming languages (e.g., R, Python)

Note: We are looking for candidates with a strong academic background and a proven track record of success in economic analysis. If you have a passion for numbers and a drive to succeed, we encourage you to apply.
