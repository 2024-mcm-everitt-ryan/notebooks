Company: Booth, Contreras and Black

Job Title: City Research Scientist

Job Description:
Booth, Contreras and Black is seeking a highly motivated and dedicated City Research Scientist to join our team. As a City Research Scientist, you will be responsible for conducting research and analyzing data to inform urban planning decisions. You will work closely with our team of experts to identify trends, develop predictive models, and provide recommendations to stakeholders.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct research and analyze data to inform urban planning decisions
 Develop and maintain databases and data visualization tools
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify trends and develop predictive models
 Provide recommendations to stakeholders on urban planning decisions
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Master's degree in Urban Planning, Geography, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in research and analysis
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills
 Proficiency in data visualization tools and programming languages such as Python and R

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
