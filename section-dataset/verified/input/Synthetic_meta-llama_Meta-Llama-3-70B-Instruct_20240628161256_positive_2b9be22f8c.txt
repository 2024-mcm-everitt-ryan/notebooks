Company: Ruiz-Gilmore
Role: Usher

About Us:
Ruiz-Gilmore is a leading entertainment company dedicated to providing exceptional customer experiences. We are committed to creating an inclusive workplace that celebrates diversity and promotes equal opportunities for all.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and enthusiastic Usher to join our team. As an Usher, you will be responsible for ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience for our patrons. Your duties will include greeting and directing customers, assisting with seating, and providing exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

 Greet and direct patrons to their seats
 Assist with crowd control and ensure a safe environment
 Provide exceptional customer service and respond to patron inquiries
 Collaborate with other staff members to ensure a smooth event operation

Qualifications and Experience:

 Previous experience in customer service or a related field
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Availability to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. In addition, you will be eligible for a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.

Our Commitment to Diversity:
We are actively seeking to hire individuals with disabilities to enhance our diversity. Our commitment to diversity includes welcoming applications from people with disabilities. We believe that a diverse workforce is essential to achieving our goals and providing exceptional customer experiences.
