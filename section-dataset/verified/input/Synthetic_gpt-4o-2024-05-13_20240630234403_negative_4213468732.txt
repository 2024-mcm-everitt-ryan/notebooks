Company: Oneal Inc

Role: Watershed Maintainer

Company Background:
Oneal Inc is dedicated to maintaining and preserving critical watershed areas. Our goal is to ensure sustainable water resources for communities and ecosystems. We pride ourselves on our hands-on approach and commitment to environmental stewardship.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Oneal Inc is seeking a dedicated Watershed Maintainer to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for maintaining and improving watershed areas to ensure their health and sustainability. We are looking for someone with a strong work ethic, physical resilience, and a competitive spirit to excel in this role.

Responsibilities:
- Perform regular maintenance and monitoring of watershed areas.
- Use heavy machinery and tools to manage vegetation and soil erosion.
- Conduct detailed inspections to identify and address potential issues.
- Work independently and take decisive action to solve problems as they arise.
- Collaborate with local authorities and stakeholders to ensure compliance with regulations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience in environmental management or a related field.
- Physical strength and stamina to perform labor-intensive tasks.
- Ability to operate heavy machinery and tools.
- Strong problem-solving skills and a competitive drive.
- Must be decisive and capable of working independently.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from £30,000 to £40,000 based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid leave and holiday benefits.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
