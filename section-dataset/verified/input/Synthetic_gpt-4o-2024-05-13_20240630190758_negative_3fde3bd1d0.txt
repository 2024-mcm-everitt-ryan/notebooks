Company Background:  
Johnson-Kent is a leading law firm specializing in labor relations and employment law. Our dedicated team provides unparalleled legal services to a diverse range of clients, ensuring fair and just workplace standards.

Job Type:  
Full-Time Labor Relations Attorney

Job Description:  
Johnson-Kent seeks a labor relations attorney who is passionate about supporting and advocating for employees. The ideal candidate will have a strong commitment to connecting with clients on a personal level and ensuring their well-being. She will be responsible for providing legal counsel, representing clients in negotiations and disputes, and promoting fair labor practices.

Responsibilities:  
- Represent clients in labor disputes and negotiations.
- Provide legal advice on employment laws and regulations.
- Draft and review legal documents related to labor relations.
- Work closely with clients to understand their needs and offer compassionate support.
- Maintain strong relationships with clients, unions, and other stakeholders.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Juris Doctor (JD) from an accredited law school.
- Admission to the state bar.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in labor relations law.
- Strong interpersonal skills to connect with clients and understand their needs.
- Excellent communication and negotiation skills.
