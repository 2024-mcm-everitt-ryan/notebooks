Company: Mason-Reed
Role: Supervising Public Health Advisor

Job Description:
Mason-Reed is seeking a highly skilled Supervising Public Health Advisor to lead our team in developing and implementing effective public health strategies. As a key member of our organization, you will be responsible for providing expert guidance and oversight to our public health programs, ensuring they are evidence-based, culturally sensitive, and tailored to meet the unique needs of our diverse communities.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement comprehensive public health programs that address the needs of our diverse communities
 Provide technical assistance and guidance to public health staff, ensuring they have the necessary skills and resources to excel in their roles
 Collaborate with internal and external stakeholders, including community organizations, healthcare providers, and government agencies, to advance public health goals
 Analyze data and trends to identify areas for improvement and inform program development
 Foster a culture of inclusion, respect, and empathy within our organization and in our community partnerships

Qualifications and Experience:

 Master's degree in Public Health, Epidemiology, or a related field
 At least 5 years of experience in public health program management, with a focus on health equity and social determinants of health
 Proven track record of developing and implementing successful public health programs, with a strong emphasis on community engagement and culturally sensitive practice
 Excellent communication, leadership, and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $85,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a generous retirement plan. Our organization values work-life balance and provides a flexible work schedule, including telecommuting options.
