Company Background:
Bonilla, Wolf and Medina is a leading firm specializing in insulation services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. With over 20 years of experience, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality insulation solutions. Our team values safety, efficiency, and excellence in all our undertakings.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled insulation worker to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for installing and repairing insulation in various types of buildings. This role requires precision, attention to detail, and the ability to work effectively within a team.

Responsibilities:
- Install insulation materials in walls, floors, ceilings, and crawlspaces.
- Measure and cut insulation to fit specific spaces.
- Seal gaps and edges to ensure maximum insulation efficiency.
- Follow safety protocols and guidelines to ensure a safe working environment.
- Collaborate with other team members to complete projects on time.
- Maintain tools and equipment in good working condition.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Previous experience in insulation installation is preferred but not mandatory.
- Ability to read and interpret construction blueprints.
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
- Good physical condition, able to lift heavy materials and work in confined spaces.
- Basic math skills for measuring and cutting materials.
- Effective communication skills and the ability to work well in a team.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from 30,000 USD to 45,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for career advancement and professional development.
