Company Background:
Smith, Hernandez and Martinez is a leading construction and engineering firm known for its commitment to innovative solutions and quality craftsmanship. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and supportive work environment for all employees.

Job Title:
Pipefitter

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled pipefitter to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for assembling, installing, and maintaining piping systems in various settings. This role calls for detailed craftsmanship, problem-solving skills, and a dedication to safety standards.

Responsibilities:
- Read and interpret blueprints and schematics
- Measure, cut, thread, and bend pipes to required angles
- Assemble and secure pipes, tubes, fittings, and related equipment
- Conduct tests to ensure system functionality and integrity
- Collaborate with other trades and contractors on-site
- Adhere to all safety protocols and regulations

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience as a pipefitter or in a similar role
- Strong knowledge of pipe fitting procedures and techniques
- Ability to read technical plans and blueprints
- Excellent problem-solving and troubleshooting skills
- Physical stamina and dexterity
- Commitment to safety and high-quality standards

- Paid parental leave for all employees
- Retirement savings plan with company match
- Ongoing professional development and training opportunities
- Inclusive and supportive work environment
