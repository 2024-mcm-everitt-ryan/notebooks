Company: Rodriguez-Gibson

Job Title: Teller

About Us:
Rodriguez-Gibson is a leading financial institution dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and innovative banking solutions. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace where everyone can thrive.

Job Description:
As a Teller, you will be responsible for handling customer transactions, responding to customer inquiries, and providing excellent customer service. You will work closely with our team to achieve sales and service goals, identify opportunities to deepen customer relationships, and contribute to the overall success of our branch.

Responsibilities:

 Process customer transactions accurately and efficiently
 Respond to customer inquiries and resolve issues promptly
 Identify opportunities to cross-sell and upsell products and services
 Collaborate with team members to achieve sales and service goals
 Maintain a high level of customer satisfaction and loyalty

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required
 1-2 years of experience in a banking or customer service environment
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
 Basic math skills and accuracy

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $50,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off. We also offer spousal support programs to help you and your husband or wife thrive. Our workplace is designed to be mother/baby friendly, ensuring a supportive environment for new mothers.
