Walker PLC is seeking a Student Services Assistant to join our team. As a key member of our student support services, you will be responsible for providing administrative assistance to our student body.

- Manage student inquiries and provide timely responses to ensure prompt resolution of issues
- Coordinate student events and activities, promoting a sense of community and inclusivity
- Assist with data entry, record-keeping, and reporting to maintain accurate student records
- Develop and maintain effective working relationships with students, faculty, and staff

To be successful in this role, you will need:

- 1-2 years of experience in administration or customer service
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities
- Preference for candidates who have a native-level command of Italian

We offer a salary range of $40,000 to $45,000 per year and a benefits package that includes health insurance, 15 days of paid time off, and opportunities for professional development.
