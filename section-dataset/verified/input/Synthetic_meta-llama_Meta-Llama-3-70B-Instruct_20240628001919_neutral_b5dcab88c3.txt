Company: Eaton-Fernandez

Job Title: Radiation Emergency Response Specialist

Job Description:
Eaton-Fernandez is seeking a highly skilled Radiation Emergency Response Specialist to join our team. As a Radiation Emergency Response Specialist, you will be responsible for responding to radiation emergencies, assessing radiation levels, and implementing corrective actions to ensure public safety.

Responsibilities:

 Respond to radiation emergencies and assess radiation levels
 Develop and implement corrective actions to ensure public safety
 Collaborate with internal teams and external stakeholders to resolve radiation emergencies
 Provide training and guidance to team members on radiation safety protocols
 Maintain accurate records of radiation emergencies and responses

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Nuclear Science, Health Physics, or related field
 3+ years of experience in radiation emergency response or a related field
 Expertise in radiation safety protocols and regulations
 Proficiency in radiation detection and measurement equipment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Eaton-Fernandez offers a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
