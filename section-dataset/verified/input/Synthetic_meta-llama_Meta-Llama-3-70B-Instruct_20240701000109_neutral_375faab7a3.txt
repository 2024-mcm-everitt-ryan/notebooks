Data and Technology Analyst at Hall, Johnson and Hudson

Company Background:
Hall, Johnson and Hudson is a leading data analysis firm dedicated to helping businesses make informed decisions. We're a team of passionate individuals who thrive on collaboration and innovation.

Job Description:
We're seeking a highly skilled Data and Technology Analyst to join our team. As a Data and Technology Analyst, you will be responsible for collecting, analyzing, and interpreting complex data sets to drive business insights. You will work closely with our stakeholders to identify areas of improvement and develop data-driven solutions to optimize business performance.

Responsibilities:

 Collect, analyze, and interpret large data sets to identify trends and patterns
 Develop and maintain databases, data systems, and tools to support business decisions
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business needs and develop data-driven solutions
 Communicate complex data insights to non-technical stakeholders
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in data analysis or a related field
 Strong technical skills in programming languages such as Python, R, or SQL
 Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from USD 60,000 to USD 80,000 per year, based on your experience and skills. In addition, you'll receive a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement plan matching, and 15 days of paid vacation.
