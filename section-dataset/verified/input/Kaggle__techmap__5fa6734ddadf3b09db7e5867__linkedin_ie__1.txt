We're looking for a candidate to fill this position in an exciting company.

- To be responsible for operating and sustaining the Nursing Home for residents.
- To provide, through high standards of professional practice, a caring environment conducive to the physical, emotional, social, and spiritual needs of each resident in the Nursing Home in a way that ensures their privacy and dignity and enhances their quality of life.
- To review as necessary a care plan for each resident based upon an assessment carried out by a qualified nurse employed by the nursing home. The care plan is formulated and reviewed in conjunction with the patient, the family, or outside agency as appropriate.
- Be responsible and accountable for the standard and quality of the nursing care provided in the nursing home.

Requirements:

- Registered General Nurse and/or Registered Mental Nurse.
- Minimum 3 out of the last 5 years managerial experience within an elderly care setting.
- A Health Care Management Qualification or a minimum of at least 2 years management experience with well-developed administrative skills.
- Experience of staff training and development needs.
- An appreciation of budgeting and best value principles is desirable.
