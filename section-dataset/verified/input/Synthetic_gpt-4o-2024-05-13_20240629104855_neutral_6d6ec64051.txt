Company: Webb Inc

Role: Real Estate Agent

Company Background: Webb Inc is a leading real estate firm that prides itself on delivering exceptional service to its clients. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace where all employees can thrive.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description and Responsibilities:
We are seeking a motivated and professional Real Estate Agent to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will be responsible for:
- Assisting clients in buying, selling, and renting properties
- Conducting property tours and open houses
- Negotiating contracts and closing deals
- Providing market analysis and property evaluations
- Maintaining up-to-date knowledge of real estate laws and regulations

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- A valid real estate license
- Excellent communication and negotiation skills
- Strong customer service orientation
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Prior experience in real estate is a plus, but not mandatory

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary: £30,000 - £45,000 per annum, depending on experience
- Performance-based bonuses
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance
- Paid time off and holidays
- Professional development opportunities

Webb Inc is an equal opportunity employer, and we encourage candidates from all backgrounds to apply.
