Company: Morales and Sons

Job Title: Penalty Processing Unit Cashier

Job Description:
We are seeking a detail-oriented and organized individual to join our Penalty Processing Unit as a Cashier. As a key member of our team, you will be responsible for accurately processing penalties, handling customer payments, and maintaining a high level of customer satisfaction.

Responsibilities:

 Process penalties and customer payments in a timely and efficient manner
 Maintain accurate records of transactions and customer interactions
 Provide exceptional customer service and respond to customer inquiries
 Collaborate with internal teams to resolve payment discrepancies

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required; some college or degree in a related field preferred
 1-2 years of experience in a cashier or customer service role
 Basic math skills and attention to detail
 Excellent communication and customer service skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
