Company Background:
Lee and Sons is a reputable logistics company known for its dedication to excellence and community values. We are committed to providing top-notch services and fostering a collaborative work environment.

Job Type: 
Field Operations Training Coordinator

Job Description:
Lee and Sons is seeking a Field Operations Training Coordinator to join our team. The successful candidate will develop and implement training programs for field operations staff, ensuring that all team members are equipped with the necessary skills and knowledge.

Responsibilities:
- Develop comprehensive training materials and programs for field operations staff.
- Conduct training sessions and workshops.
- Monitor and assess the effectiveness of training programs.
- Work closely with other department heads to ensure alignment of training with company goals.
- Maintain detailed records of training activities and outcomes.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Education, or related field.
- Proven experience in training and development, preferably within a logistics or field operations environment.
- Strong communication and organizational skills.
- Ability to work independently and collaboratively.
- He/she should value traditional community principles and bring a sense of unity to the team.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary, with a range from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000 based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health benefits.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
- Our social events are open to all guys and gals in the office.
