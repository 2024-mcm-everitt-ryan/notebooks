Job Type: 
Full-time

Job Description: 
We are seeking a dedicated Climber & Pruner to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for horticulture and a commitment to safe work practices. They will be responsible for climbing trees and pruning branches, ensuring the health and aesthetics of the trees.

Responsibilities:
- Climb trees using appropriate safety gear and techniques.
- Prune branches to promote healthy growth and improve tree aesthetics.
- Assess and identify tree health issues and recommend solutions.
- Maintain equipment and tools in good working order.
- Work independently and as part of a team to complete projects on time.
- Communicate effectively with clients and team members.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Previous experience in tree climbing and pruning is preferred.
- Certification in arboriculture or related field is a plus.
- Strong understanding of tree health and care techniques.
- Ability to work independently and handle physical demands.
- Excellent communication skills and ability to manage stakeholder relationships.
- Commitment to safety and adherence to company policies.
