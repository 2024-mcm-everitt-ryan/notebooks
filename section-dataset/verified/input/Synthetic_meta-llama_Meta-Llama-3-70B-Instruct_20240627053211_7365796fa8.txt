Huynh-Harrison is seeking a highly skilled Senior Staff ASIC Engineer to join our diverse team of innovation-driven professionals. As a leader in the semiconductor industry, we are committed to fostering an inclusive work environment that celebrates the unique perspectives and experiences of our employees.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
As a Senior Staff ASIC Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and verifying complex Application-Specific Integrated Circuits (ASICs) for various applications. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure timely delivery of high-quality ASIC products, while maintaining excellent relationships with internal and external stakeholders.

Responsibilities:

- Design and develop ASICs from concept to production, ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulations
- Lead and mentor junior engineers, providing guidance on ASIC design and development best practices
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and resolve technical issues, ensuring project timelines are met
- Develop and maintain technical documentation, including design specifications and test plans
- Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies, applying this knowledge to improve ASIC design and development processes

Qualifications:

- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or a related field
- At least 8 years of experience in ASIC design and development, with a focus on digital ASIC design
- Proficiency in Verilog, SystemVerilog, and ASIC design tools such as Synopsys, Cadence, or Mentor Graphics
- Excellent leadership and communication skills, with the ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
- Strong problem-solving skills, with the ability to debug complex technical issues

- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development in a dynamic, innovative environment
- Collaborative, diverse, and inclusive work environment that values individual contributions
