Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Tax Preparer to join our team. As a Tax Preparer, you will be responsible for preparing and reviewing tax returns, identifying and resolving errors, and providing exceptional customer service to our clients.

 Identify and resolve errors and discrepancies in tax returns
 Provide exceptional customer service to clients
 Stay up-to-date with changes in tax laws and regulations
 Collaborate with other team members to achieve department goals

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in tax preparation
 Bachelor's degree in Accounting or related field
 Strong knowledge of tax laws and regulations
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
