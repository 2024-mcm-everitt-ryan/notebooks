Company: Mccullough-Mitchell

Role: Trial Preparation Assistant

Job Description:

We are seeking a detail-oriented and organized Trial Preparation Assistant to join our team. As a Trial Preparation Assistant, you will play a critical role in preparing trial materials, maintaining case files, and providing administrative support to our legal team.

Responsibilities:

 Assist in preparing trial materials, including exhibits, witness lists, and trial binders
 Maintain accurate and up-to-date case files, both physical and digital
 Provide administrative support to our legal team, including scheduling appointments and making travel arrangements
 Coordinate with vendors and external parties to obtain necessary documents and materials
 Perform other administrative tasks as needed

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in a legal administrative role
 High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree preferred
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Word and Excel
 Strong organizational and time management skills
 Ability to work well under pressure and meet deadlines

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance
 Paid time off and holidays
