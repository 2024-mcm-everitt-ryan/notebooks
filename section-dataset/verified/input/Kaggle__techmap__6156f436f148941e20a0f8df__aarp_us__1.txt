As a part of our continued growth, LKQ is seeking a Drain Technician to join our team! You will be responsible for removing all fluids, lubricants, and components as required in a safe and efficient manner. To excel in this position, you must be able to multitask, communicate well with others, and be very organized in tasks. The ideal candidate needs to be very goal-oriented and willing to work at a very fast pace while maintaining the quality and accuracy set forth in the job expectations. If you like growth and working with happy, enthusiastic individuals, you'll enjoy a career with us!

Essential Job Duties:
- Remove all fluids, lubricants, and components as required in a safe and efficient manner, as well as prep car for setting on yard.
- Set out cars as assigned and record information on cars set accurately and in a timely manner.
- Fill out appropriate paperwork as required.
- Work closely with lift operator to maximize processing.
- Store all containers of parts in a secure area each night.
- Label and stretch wrap the container when full.
- Ensure the container is securely stored at shift end.
- Perform other duties as assigned.

Requirements:
- High School graduate or GED equivalent desired.
- Prior knowledge of automobiles or mechanics preferred.
- Willingness to be trained and learn company's products and procedures.
- Basic math and reading skills to perform job tasks and complete necessary paperwork.
- Ability to follow oral and/or written instruction.
- Must possess a safety-conscious mindset as you will be working with many different substances.

Work Environment:
- Regular exposure to moving mechanical parts.
- Sitting for relatively long periods of time while driving.
- Tolerate high and low temperature levels, and occasional inclement driving conditions.
- Occasional exposure to extreme cold and extreme heat.
- Noise level in some work environments is moderately loud.
- Frequent bending, climbing, moving, and lifting up to 50 pounds.
