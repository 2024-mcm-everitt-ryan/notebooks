Company: Carlson, Johnson and Harris

Job Title: Speaker / Conference Panelist

About Us:
Carlson, Johnson and Harris is a leading event management company dedicated to hosting informative and engaging conferences, seminars, and workshops. We strive to create a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and idea exchange among professionals from diverse backgrounds.

Job Description:
We are seeking a dynamic and experienced Speaker / Conference Panelist to join our team. As a Speaker / Conference Panelist, you will be responsible for delivering keynote speeches, moderating panel discussions, and participating in thought-provoking conversations at our events. Your expertise and insights will play a vital role in shaping the narrative of our conferences and leaving a lasting impact on our audience.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and deliver engaging speeches, presentations, and panel discussions on assigned topics
- Collaborate with our content team to craft compelling narratives and storylines for our events
- Engage with attendees, respond to questions, and facilitate meaningful conversations during Q&A sessions
- Participate in pre-event preparation, including research, rehearsal, and logistical planning
- Network with fellow speakers, sponsors, and attendees to foster connections and opportunities

Qualifications:
- 5+ years of experience in public speaking, event moderation, or a related field
- Proven track record of delivering high-quality presentations and engaging audiences
- Excellent communication, interpersonal, and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Strong research and analytical skills

Benefits:
- Speaker fees ranging from $1,000 to $5,000 per event, depending on experience and event size
- Travel reimbursement up to $1,500 per event
- Opportunities to expand your professional network and build relationships with industry leaders
- Access to cutting-edge research and insights in your area of expertise
- A dynamic and supportive work environment that values diversity and inclusion

We encourage applicants of all backgrounds to apply, and we are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive environment for our speakers and attendees.
