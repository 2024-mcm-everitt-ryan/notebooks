Company: Aguilar, Sims and James

Job Title: Administrative Associate to the Executive Director

About Us:
Aguilar, Sims and James is a leading consulting firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions to our clients. We value diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to create a work environment that is welcoming and empowering for all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking an Administrative Associate to support our Executive Director. The successful candidate will be a highly organized and detail-oriented individual with excellent communication and problem-solving skills. The role involves working closely with clients, creating and implementing strategies, and thinking critically and strategically to drive business results.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to the Executive Director, including managing calendars, coordinating travel arrangements, and preparing correspondence and presentations
 Develop and implement administrative processes and procedures to improve efficiency and productivity
 Coordinate with other departments to ensure seamless communication and collaboration
 Analyze data and provide insights to inform business decisions
 Develop and maintain relationships with clients and stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Logistics, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in an administrative role
 Proven ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Excellent communication, organizational, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to think critically and strategically

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
