Company Background:
Anderson Ltd is a dynamic and inclusive hospitality company dedicated to providing exceptional customer experiences. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion, and strive to create a workplace culture that values and celebrates individual differences.

Job Type:
Bartender Helper

Job Description:
We are seeking a talented and enthusiastic Bartender Helper to join our team! As a key member of our beverage service team, you will assist our bartenders in preparing and serving drinks, maintaining a clean and organized workspace, and providing exceptional customer service. If you're a team player with a passion for mixology and a commitment to excellence, we want to hear from you!

Responsibilities:

 Assist bartenders in preparing and serving drinks
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace
 Provide exceptional customer service
 Restock supplies and ingredients as needed
 Collaborate with other team members to ensure seamless service

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in a hospitality or customer service role
 Ability to lift up to 25 pounds and stand for long periods
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills
 Familiarity with point-of-sale systems and basic math skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $25,000 to $35,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and opportunities for professional development.

At Anderson Ltd, we're dedicated to creating a workplace culture that values and celebrates individual differences. We're particularly interested in hearing from women who are passionate about hospitality and customer service, as we strive to achieve gender parity in our leadership roles.
