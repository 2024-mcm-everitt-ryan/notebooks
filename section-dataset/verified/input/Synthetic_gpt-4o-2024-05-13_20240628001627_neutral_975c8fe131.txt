Company Background:
Smith, Sanchez and Nelson is a community-focused organization dedicated to ensuring the safety and well-being of residents. With a commitment to diversity and inclusion, we strive to create a welcoming environment for all.

Job Title:
Crossing Guard

Job Description:
We are seeking a responsible and attentive Crossing Guard to join our team. The primary role of the Crossing Guard is to ensure the safety of children and pedestrians at designated crossing points. This position is vital in providing a safe passage for school children and other pedestrians.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor traffic flow at assigned crossing points.
- Assist children and pedestrians in safely crossing the street.
- Communicate effectively with pedestrians and drivers to maintain order.
- Report any incidents or hazardous conditions to the appropriate authorities.
- Follow all safety protocols and procedures.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Strong communication skills.
- Ability to remain alert and attentive for extended periods.
- Previous experience in a similar role is a plus but not required.
- Must pass a background check.

- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $25,000 to $30,000 per year, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive training provided.
- Flexible working hours.
