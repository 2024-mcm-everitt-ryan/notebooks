Company: Herrera Group
Role: Senior IT Project Manager

Job Description:
The Herrera Group is seeking an experienced Senior IT Project Manager to lead our team in delivering high-quality IT projects on time and within budget. As a Senior IT Project Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing project planning, execution, and monitoring, ensuring that all projects are aligned with our organization's strategic objectives.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and manage project plans, resource allocation, and budgets
 Lead and mentor a team of project managers and coordinators
 Ensure timely and within-budget project delivery
 Collaborate with stakeholders to identify and mitigate risks
 Facilitate communication and ensure stakeholder expectations are met
 Support a culture of open dialogue and respect

Qualifications and Experience:
 5+ years of experience in IT project management
 Proven track record of delivering complex IT projects on time and within budget
 Excellent leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills
 Strong understanding of project management methodologies (Agile, Waterfall)
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities
