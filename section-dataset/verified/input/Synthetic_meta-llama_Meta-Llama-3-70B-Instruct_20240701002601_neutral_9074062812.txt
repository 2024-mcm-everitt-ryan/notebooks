Job Title: Senior Windows Administrator
Company: Austin, Warner and Wilson

About Us:
Austin, Warner and Wilson is a leading IT consulting firm that provides innovative solutions to businesses of all sizes. Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering exceptional service and support to our clients.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Windows Administrator to join our team. As a Senior Windows Administrator, you will be responsible for managing and maintaining our Windows-based infrastructure, ensuring high availability, and resolving complex technical issues. You will work closely with our team of IT professionals to design, implement, and support our clients' IT systems.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and maintain Windows servers, including patches, updates, and troubleshooting
 Design and implement Windows-based infrastructure solutions for our clients
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to resolve technical issues and improve system performance
 Develop and maintain technical documentation and procedures
 Provide training and support to junior team members

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
 5+ years of experience in Windows administration
 In-depth knowledge of Windows Server operating systems, including Active Directory, DNS, and DHCP
 Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $90,000 to $120,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, we offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
