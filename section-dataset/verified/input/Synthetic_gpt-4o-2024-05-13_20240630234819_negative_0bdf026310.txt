Company: Ball Group

Role: Information Security Audit and Compliance Manager

Company Background: Ball Group specializes in delivering cutting-edge security solutions to diverse industries. We pride ourselves on our dynamic and competitive work environment, always pushing the limits to stay ahead in the market.

Job Description: Ball Group is seeking an Information Security Audit and Compliance Manager to lead our security audit and compliance initiatives. Responsibilities:

- Develop and maintain security policies and procedures.
- Coordinate with various departments to ensure compliance with security standards.
- Prepare detailed audit reports and provide recommendations for improvement.
- Stay updated with the latest security trends and regulations.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Information Security, Computer Science, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in information security or a related field.
- Proven track record of managing security audits and compliance.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Certifications such as CISSP, CISM, or CISA are preferred.
