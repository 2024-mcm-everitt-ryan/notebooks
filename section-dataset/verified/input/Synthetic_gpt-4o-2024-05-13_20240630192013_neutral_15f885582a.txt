Company: Green-Cardenas  
Role: Safety Accident Investigator  

Company Background:  
Green-Cardenas is a leader in environmental and safety consulting, with a commitment to fostering diversity, equity, and inclusion in the workplace. We believe that diverse perspectives drive innovation and growth.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:  
Green-Cardenas is seeking a Safety Accident Investigator to join our team. The candidate will be responsible for investigating workplace accidents, identifying root causes, and developing recommendations to prevent future incidents. The role involves working closely with clients, employees, and regulatory bodies to ensure compliance with safety standards and regulations. The position requires excellent negotiation skills and a results-oriented approach.

Responsibilities:  
- Conduct thorough investigations of workplace accidents and near-misses.
- Analyze data to identify trends and root causes.
- Develop and implement corrective actions and preventive measures.
- Collaborate with clients and regulatory agencies to ensure compliance.
- Prepare detailed reports and present findings to stakeholders.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s degree in Occupational Safety, Environmental Science, Industrial Engineering, or a related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in safety accident investigation or a related role.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Proficiency in project planning and data analysis tools.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
