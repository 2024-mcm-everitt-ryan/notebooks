Company: Young-House
Role: Probation Officer

Company Background:
Young-House is a leader in community rehabilitation and support services, dedicated to helping individuals reintegrate into society. We pride ourselves on our inclusive and supportive work environment.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are looking for a dedicated and empathetic Probation Officer to join our team. This role involves supervising offenders, helping them understand and meet the requirements of their probation, and providing resources for rehabilitation.

Responsibilities:
- Supervise a caseload of probationers.
- Conduct regular meetings with probationers to monitor progress.
- Prepare detailed reports on probationers’ status.
- Collaborate with community resources and support agencies.
- Provide guidance and support to help probationers comply with court orders.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in criminal justice, social work, or a related field.
- Previous experience in probation, social work, or criminal justice preferred.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- We prioritize female candidates for this leadership role.
- Empathy and a strong commitment to helping others.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday pay.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- Professional development opportunities.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 50,000 to USD 70,000, based on your experience and skills.

How to Apply:
If you are passionate about making a positive difference in the lives of others and meet the qualifications listed, we encourage you to apply. We prioritize the recruitment of women in our talent acquisition strategy.
