Company: Porter, Walker and Bowman

Job Title: PeopleSoft Developer

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced PeopleSoft Developer to join our team. As a PeopleSoft Developer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining our PeopleSoft applications. If you have a passion for innovative technology and a keen eye for detail, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Design, develop, and test PeopleSoft applications
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize project requirements
 Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues
 Maintain good financial practices and ensure compliance with organizational policies
 Communicate technical information to non-technical stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:
 3+ years of experience in PeopleSoft development
 Strong understanding of PeopleSoft architecture and modules
 Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
 We value a positive attitude and the ability to contribute to a supportive work culture

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
