Company Background:
Allen PLC is a leading company in the construction and renovation industry, renowned for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. We pride ourselves on creating inclusive and supportive work environments where all team members can thrive.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
Allen PLC is seeking a skilled and motivated Paperhanger to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will possess exceptional attention to detail and the ability to work efficiently both independently and as part of a team. This role involves preparing and hanging wallpaper in various residential and commercial settings.

Responsibilities:
- Measure and cut wallpaper to fit specific room dimensions.
- Apply adhesives and position wallpaper with precision.
- Ensure surfaces are clean and prepped for wallpaper application.
- Collaborate with team members to complete large projects.
- Maintain equipment and workspace in an orderly condition.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as a Paperhanger or similar role.
- Strong attention to detail and manual dexterity.
- Ability to read and interpret blueprints and plans.
- Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
- A valid driver's license and reliable transportation.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 per year, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.
