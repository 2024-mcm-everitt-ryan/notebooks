Company: Taylor PLC

Job Type: Environmental Program Manager

Job Description:
Taylor PLC is seeking an experienced Environmental Program Manager to lead our efforts in developing and implementing sustainable environmental practices. As a key member of our team, you will be responsible for designing and managing environmental programs, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement environmental programs to reduce our carbon footprint
 Collaborate with teams to integrate environmental considerations into business operations
 Ensure compliance with environmental regulations and standards
 Analyze and report on environmental performance metrics

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Engineering, or a related field
 5+ years of experience in environmental program management
 Proven track record of developing and implementing successful environmental programs
 Excellent communication and project management skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and provide opportunities for professional growth and development.

We are focused on building an inclusive workforce and welcome applications from people with disabilities. We believe that a diverse and inclusive team is essential to driving innovation and achieving our sustainability goals.
