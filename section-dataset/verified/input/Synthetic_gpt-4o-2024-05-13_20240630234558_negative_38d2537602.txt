Company: Elliott, Perez and Lopez  
Role: Chauffeur  

Company Background:  
Elliott, Perez and Lopez is a premier transportation service providing bespoke chauffeur experiences for our elite clientele. We pride ourselves on punctuality, professionalism, and discretion.

Job Description:  
We are looking for an experienced chauffeur who can meet the high standards of our distinguished clients. The ideal candidate will possess exceptional driving skills, a strong understanding of city routes, and a keen attention to detail. He will not only drive but also ensure the comfort and safety of passengers at all times.

Responsibilities:  
- Safely transporting clients to and from specified locations in a timely manner.
- Maintaining the cleanliness and presentation of the vehicle.
- Assisting clients with luggage and other needs.
- Monitoring traffic and weather conditions to adjust routes accordingly.
- Communicating effectively with clients and dispatch as needed.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Minimum of 5 years of professional driving experience, preferably as a chauffeur.
- Valid driver’s license with a clean driving record.
- Excellent knowledge of city routes and local geography.
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
- He should be able to handle stressful situations calmly and effectively.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holiday packages.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £30,000 to £40,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Uniform provided and vehicle maintenance covered by the company.

How to Apply:  
Interested candidates should submit their resume and a cover letter detailing their experience and suitability for the role.
