Company Background:

Job Type:
Full-Time Economist

Job Description:
Smith-Jennings is seeking a qualified Economist to join our dynamic and diverse team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in economic theory and practice, with a proven track record of applying their skills to real-world problems. This role involves rigorous analytical work, managing stakeholder relationships, and contributing to policy recommendations that drive societal and economic progress.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct in-depth economic research and analysis.
- Develop and present economic models and forecasts.
- Manage relationships with stakeholders, including clients, government agencies, and non-profits.
- Provide strategic policy recommendations based on data-driven insights.
- Collaborate with team members to ensure comprehensive analysis and reporting.

Qualifications and Experience:
- A Master's degree or PhD in Economics or a related field.
- At least 3-5 years of relevant experience in economic analysis, policy research, or consulting.
- Strong quantitative and analytical skills.
- Excellent communication and presentation abilities.
- Experience in conflict resolution and managing stakeholder relationships.
- Commitment to fostering diversity and respecting individuals from all walks of life.

- Professional development opportunities.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.
