WHO ARE WE LOOKING FOR?

We are looking for bright talents with disruptive ideas who will question and challenge the status quo and find better solutions to business problems. This is how we maintain our leadership in the market, by attracting people with fresh ideas.

UFLP IS:

- 2.5 years of experiences and rotations within not only the function you choose, but also cross-functional projects.
- Deep dive into strategic tasks, where you will be able to boost your business acumen.
- A Mentor who will guide you through skills you get on the program, to build a better journey through unique experiences.

TO APPLY YOU NEED TO:

- Be a graduate of a bachelor's or master's degree (not earlier than the year 2017).
- Speak English at the upper-intermediate level.
- Be flexible for business trips during your rotations.
- Have the ability to work full-time.
- Have a Ukrainian residency or a permit to work in Ukraine.
- Be interested in either Customer Development, Marketing, or Supply Chain!
