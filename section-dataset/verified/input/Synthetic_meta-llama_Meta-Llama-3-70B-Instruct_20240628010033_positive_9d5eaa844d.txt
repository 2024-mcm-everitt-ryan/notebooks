Company: Carter-Miller

Job Title: Senior Cook

About Us:
Carter-Miller is a renowned culinary institution dedicated to providing exceptional dining experiences. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion, and we're excited to expand our team with talented individuals who share our values.

Job Description:
We're seeking a skilled Senior Cook to join our kitchen team. As a Senior Cook, you'll be responsible for preparing and cooking delicious meals, leading culinary teams, and maintaining a clean and organized kitchen environment. If you're passionate about food, teamwork, and creating unforgettable dining experiences, we'd love to hear from you.

Responsibilities:

 Prepare and cook high-quality dishes in a fast-paced kitchen environment
 Lead and mentor junior kitchen staff, providing guidance and support
 Maintain a clean and organized kitchen, adhering to health and safety standards
 Collaborate with the culinary team to develop new recipes and menus
 Ensure timely food preparation and presentation

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience as a cook or senior cook in a high-volume kitchen
 Strong culinary skills, with the ability to work efficiently in a fast-paced environment
 Excellent leadership and communication skills
 Food safety certification an asset

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you'll enjoy a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a generous paid time-off policy.

Why Work with Us:
