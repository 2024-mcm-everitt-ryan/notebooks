Company: Robinson PLC
Role: Administrator on Duty

About Us:
Robinson PLC is a leading provider of administrative services, committed to delivering exceptional support to our clients. We foster an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive, and value diverse perspectives and experiences.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Administrator on Duty to join our team. As an Administrator on Duty, you will be responsible for providing administrative support to our management team, ensuring the smooth operation of our office, and maintaining a high level of customer satisfaction.

Responsibilities:

 Provide administrative support to management team, including preparing reports, schedules, and other documents as needed
 Manage and maintain accurate and up-to-date records and databases
 Coordinate travel arrangements, meetings, and events
 Develop and implement administrative processes and procedures to improve efficiency and productivity
 Ensure a high level of customer satisfaction through prompt and professional communication

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in an administrative role
 High school diploma or equivalent required; associate's or bachelor's degree preferred
 Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite and Google Workspace
 Excellent organizational, communication, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize tasks effectively

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
