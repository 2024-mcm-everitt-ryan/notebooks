Job Title: Environmental Health & Safety Auditor
Company: Lane Ltd

About Us:
Lane Ltd is a leading organization dedicated to promoting sustainable practices and ensuring a safe working environment. We strive to create a workplace where everyone feels valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Environmental Health & Safety Auditor to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for conducting audits, identifying areas for improvement, and developing strategies to mitigate risks. This is an excellent opportunity to make a meaningful impact on our organization's commitment to sustainability and safety.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct regular audits to ensure compliance with environmental, health, and safety regulations
 Identify areas for improvement and develop strategies to mitigate risks
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to implement safety protocols and procedures
 Analyze data and provide recommendations to improve our environmental, health, and safety performance

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Environmental Health, Occupational Health, or a related field
 3+ years of experience in environmental health and safety auditing
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
