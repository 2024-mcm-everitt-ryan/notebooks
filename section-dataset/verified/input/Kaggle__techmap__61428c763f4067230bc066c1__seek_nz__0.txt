Are you looking for a job that allows you to give back to the community and the environment? This position could be the role for you!

You'll be the key contact for dropping off recycling and green waste to the Awapuni Resource Recovery Centre. In this role, you'll be helping many members of the community, so you need to be great at communicating with a wide range of people. Your enthusiasm to participate fully in training provided by the Council will help you to grow and develop in your role. You'll also need to be self-motivated and honest, as you'll have sole charge of the station at times. Organisation and attention to detail are crucial, as duties include recording weights and taking bookings. You will be a team player, working constructively with others and be flexible to work outside of normal hours when required.

A current full driver's licence, computer skills, and EFTPOS knowledge are desirable. This role is 13.5 hours per week:
- Saturdays, 7.15 am - 4.45 pm
- Sundays, 11.45 am - 4.15 pm

If this sounds like you, then apply today! Applications close: 11.00 pm, Thursday 30 September 2021.

We are an Equal Employment Opportunity Employer and welcome applications from all those suitably qualified for this position.
