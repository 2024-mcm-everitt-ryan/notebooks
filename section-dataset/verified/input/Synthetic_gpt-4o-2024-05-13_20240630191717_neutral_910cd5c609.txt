Company Background:
Torres Ltd is a respected name in the baking industry, known for our commitment to quality and innovation. We believe that an inclusive culture drives creativity and excellence, benefiting both our employees and customers.

Job Type:
Full-time Baker

Job Description:
We are looking for a skilled and dedicated Baker to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be passionate about baking and eager to contribute to a collaborative environment where every voice is valued.

Responsibilities:
- Prepare and bake a variety of bread, pastries, and cakes.
- Maintain a clean and organized workspace.
- Collaborate with team members to develop new recipes and products.
- Mentor and train junior team members.
- Ensure compliance with health and safety regulations.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience as a baker.
- Strong understanding of baking techniques and ingredients.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Excellent time management skills.
- Commitment to maintaining high standards of hygiene and quality.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $30,000 to $45,000 annually, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance.
- Paid vacation and sick leave.
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.

Torres Ltd is committed to a diverse and inclusive hiring process. We encourage candidates from all backgrounds to apply.
