Job Title: Police Surgeon at Lopez, Baker and Perez

Company Background:
Lopez, Baker and Perez is a leading law enforcement agency dedicated to serving and protecting our communities. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment that values the contributions of all individuals.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Police Surgeon to join our team. As a Police Surgeon, you will be responsible for providing medical services to our law enforcement personnel, conducting fitness-for-duty evaluations, and advising on medical standards and protocols.

Responsibilities:

 Provide medical consultations and treatment to police officers and personnel
 Conduct fitness-for-duty evaluations and make recommendations on medical standards and protocols
 Develop and implement medical policies and procedures
 Collaborate with law enforcement agencies and medical professionals to ensure best practices
 Maintain accurate and confidential medical records

Qualifications and Experience:
 Medical degree from an accredited institution
 Licensure to practice medicine in the relevant jurisdiction
 At least 5 years of experience as a police surgeon or in a related field
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $150,000 to $200,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a generous retirement plan. Our benefits are designed to support all families, and we strive to create an inclusive environment for LGBTA individuals.
