Company Background:
Price Ltd is a leading global services provider specializing in language solutions. Our mission is to bridge communication gaps and promote understanding through high-quality interpreting services. We are dedicated to fostering a diverse and inclusive work environment.

Job Title:
Interpreter

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled interpreter to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will possess excellent language skills and the ability to interpret spoken communication accurately. As an interpreter, you will be responsible for facilitating communication between parties who speak different languages.

Responsibilities:
- Accurately interpret spoken communication between parties.
- Maintain a high level of professionalism and confidentiality.
- Work with various clients across different industries.
- Prepare and maintain records of interpreting assignments.
- Continuously improve language skills and industry knowledge.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Fluency in at least two languages, including English.
- Proven experience as an interpreter or similar role.
- Excellent listening, retention, and note-taking skills.
- Ability to remain impartial and maintain confidentiality.
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
