Company: Long-Mills

Job Title: Animal Trainer

About Us:
Long-Mills is a leading animal training and welfare organization dedicated to providing expert care and training to animals. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment that values the contributions of all employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Animal Trainer to join our team. As an Animal Trainer at Long-Mills, you will be responsible for developing and implementing customized training programs for animals, collaborating with our veterinary team to ensure the health and well-being of our animals, and maintaining accurate records of animal behavior and progress.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement comprehensive training programs for animals
 Collaborate with veterinarians to ensure animal health and well-being
 Maintain accurate records of animal behavior and progress
 Provide exceptional animal care and handling
 Stay up-to-date with industry best practices and advancements in animal training

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Animal Science, Biology, or a related field
 Minimum 3 years of experience in animal training or a related field
 Proven experience in developing and implementing effective training programs
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $55,000 to $70,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Additionally, you will receive a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
