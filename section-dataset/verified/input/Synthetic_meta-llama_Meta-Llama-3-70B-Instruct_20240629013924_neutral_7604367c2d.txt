Company: Mathis, Mason and Reed
Role: Sewer Pipe Cleaner

Job Description:
Mathis, Mason and Reed is seeking a reliable and skilled Sewer Pipe Cleaner to join our team. As a Sewer Pipe Cleaner, you will be responsible for ensuring the efficient flow of wastewater through our sewer systems. This is a great opportunity to work with a dynamic team and contribute to the maintenance of our city's infrastructure.

Responsibilities:

 Clean and maintain sewer pipes to prevent blockages and ensure smooth wastewater flow
 Operate specialized equipment, such as sewer cleaning trucks and drain snakes
 Identify and report any issues or concerns with the sewer system
 Collaborate with other team members to complete tasks efficiently and effectively
 Demonstrate attention to detail and a commitment to quality work

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in sewer pipe cleaning or a related field
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work in confined spaces
 Valid driver's license and a clean driving record
 Demonstrate financial responsibility and ability to manage time effectively
 Relevant certifications or training an asset

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $65,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage
 Opportunities for professional development and advancement
 A supportive and inclusive work environment
 Relevant health accommodations will be provided
