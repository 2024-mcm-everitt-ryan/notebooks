Company Background:
Lee Group is a leading institution dedicated to providing exceptional library services to the community. Our mission is to foster a love of reading and learning through accessible and inclusive resources.

Role:
Library Technician

Job Description:
We are seeking a detail-oriented and organized Library Technician to join our dedicated team. The ideal candidate will assist in the maintenance and organization of library materials, support library patrons, and ensure smooth library operations. 

Responsibilities:
- Cataloging and classifying library materials.
- Assisting patrons with locating and using library resources.
- Maintaining accurate records and inventory of library collections.
- Supporting library programs and events.
- Providing excellent customer service to library users.
- Ensuring the library remains a welcoming and inclusive environment for all.

Qualifications and Experience:
- A degree or diploma in Library Science or related field.
- Strong organizational and multitasking skills.
- Proficiency in using library management systems.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Commitment to fostering an inclusive and diverse community.

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment.
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 40,000 to USD 50,000, based on your experience and skills.
