Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Quality Assurance Manager to join our team. As a Quality Assurance Manager, you will be responsible for developing and implementing quality control processes, ensuring compliance with industry standards, and leading a team of quality assurance specialists.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement quality control processes to ensure compliance with industry standards
 Lead a team of quality assurance specialists to achieve quality goals
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and resolve quality issues
 Analyze data to identify trends and opportunities for improvement
 Develop and maintain quality metrics and reports

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in a related field
 Minimum 5 years of experience in quality assurance or a related field
 Proven track record of developing and implementing quality control processes
 Excellent leadership and communication skills
