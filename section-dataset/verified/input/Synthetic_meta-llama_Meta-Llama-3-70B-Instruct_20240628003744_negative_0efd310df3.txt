Job Title: CRM Developer
Company: Gray, Jennings and Prince

About Us:
Gray, Jennings and Prince is a leading consulting firm that helps businesses build strong relationships with their customers. We are committed to delivering exceptional results and providing a supportive work environment for our employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled CRM Developer to join our team. As a CRM Developer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing CRM solutions that meet our clients' needs. You will work closely with our sales team to understand client requirements and develop solutions that drive business growth.

Responsibilities:

 Design, develop, and implement CRM solutions using Microsoft Dynamics 365
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize project requirements
 Develop and maintain technical documentation of CRM solutions
 Troubleshoot and resolve CRM-related issues
 Stay up-to-date with the latest CRM trends and technologies

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
 At least 3 years of experience in CRM development
 Proficiency in Microsoft Dynamics 365 and C#
 Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills
