Company Background:

Burton, Cochran and Mendez is a leading private investigation firm known for delivering thorough and unbiased investigative services. We are dedicated to upholding justice and offering top-tier services to all our clients.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
We are currently seeking a civilian investigator to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a background in investigative work, strong analytical skills, and the ability to handle sensitive information with discretion. This role requires a keen eye for detail, the ability to follow leads meticulously, and the persistence to see investigations through to completion.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct comprehensive investigations into various cases, including fraud, missing persons, and background checks.
- Gather, analyze, and interpret evidence and data from multiple sources.
- Prepare detailed reports and present findings to clients and stakeholders.
- Work collaboratively with law enforcement and other agencies when necessary.
- Maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with the utmost care.
- Stay updated with the latest investigative techniques and legal requirements.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- A degree in Criminal Justice, Criminology, or a related field is preferred.
- Proven experience in investigative work, preferably in a civilian or private sector role.
- Strong analytical and critical thinking skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Proficiency with investigative tools and technologies.
