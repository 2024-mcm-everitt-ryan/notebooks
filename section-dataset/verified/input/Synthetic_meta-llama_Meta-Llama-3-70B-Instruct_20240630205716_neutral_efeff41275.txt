Company: Stuart-Sullivan
Role: Environmental Specialist

About Us:
Stuart-Sullivan is a leading environmental consulting firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions for a sustainable future. We value diverse perspectives and experiences, and our team is committed to creating a inclusive and equitable work environment.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated Environmental Specialist to join our team. As an Environmental Specialist, you will be responsible for conducting environmental site assessments, developing remediation plans, and collaborating with clients to ensure compliance with environmental regulations.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct site visits to assess environmental conditions and identify potential hazards
 Develop and implement remediation plans to mitigate environmental impacts
 Collaborate with clients to ensure compliance with environmental regulations
 Analyze data and prepare technical reports for clients and stakeholders
 Stay up-to-date with changing environmental regulations and industry best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Engineering, or a related field
 At least 2 years of experience in environmental consulting or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes health, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. We also provide opportunities for professional development and growth within the company.
