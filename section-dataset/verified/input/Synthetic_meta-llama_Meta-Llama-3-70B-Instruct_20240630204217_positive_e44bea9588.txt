Data Management Assistant at Charles, Simon and Moore

Company Background:
Charles, Simon and Moore is a leading data analytics firm dedicated to empowering businesses with actionable insights. We believe that a diverse and inclusive workforce is essential to driving innovation and growth. Our commitment to gender diversity has earned us recognition as a top employer for women in the industry.

Job Type:
Full-time Data Management Assistant

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Data Management Assistant to join our team. As a Data Management Assistant, you will play a critical role in managing and analyzing data to inform business decisions. If you are a detail-oriented and organized individual with a passion for data, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Manage and maintain large datasets to ensure data quality and integrity
 Develop and implement data management processes to improve efficiency
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to analyze data and inform business decisions
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in data management

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
 2+ years of experience in data management or a related field
 Proficiency in data management tools and technologies, including Excel, SQL, and data visualization software
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive compensation package, including a salary ranging from $65,000 to $85,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes health insurance, retirement savings, and paid time off. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices and provide opportunities for professional growth and development.
