Company: Peters, Turner, and Young
  Role: Climber & Pruner

  Background
    Peters, Turner, and Young is a renowned landscaping and arboriculture company that has been serving the community for over 20 years. We specialize in tree care, pruning, and maintenance, providing top-notch services to both residential and commercial clients.
  Job Type: Full-time

  Description
    We are seeking a dedicated and passionate Climber & Pruner to join our team. The ideal candidate will have experience in tree climbing, pruning, and maintenance. This role demands physical strength, agility, and a strong work ethic.

  Responsibilities
    Perform tree climbing and pruning tasks to maintain the health and aesthetics of trees.
    Ensure all work is conducted safely and according to industry standards.
    Communicate effectively with clients to understand their needs and provide exceptional service.
    Maintain and care for climbing and pruning equipment.
 
  Qualifications
    Minimum of 3 years of experience in tree climbing and pruning.
    Physical strength and endurance to handle the demands of the job (with reasonable accommodations).
    Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
    He must possess strong organizational skills and a proven track record of success in similar roles.

  Benefits
    Competitive salary ranging from $40,000 to $55,000, based on experience and skills.
    Health insurance and retirement benefits.
    Opportunities for professional development and certification.
    Supportive and dynamic team environment.
