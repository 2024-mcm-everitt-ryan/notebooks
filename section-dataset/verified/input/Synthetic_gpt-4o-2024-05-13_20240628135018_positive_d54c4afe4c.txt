Company Background:  
Knapp-Maynard is a leading consultancy firm specializing in project management solutions. Our mission is to deliver exceptional service and innovative project planning to a diverse range of clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to diversity and inclusion within our workforce.

Job Type:  
Full-time

Job Description:  
We are seeking a dedicated Project Planner to join our dynamic team. In this role, you will be responsible for managing project timelines, coordinating resources, and ensuring key milestones are met. This position offers a great opportunity to work on high-profile projects and make a significant impact.

Responsibilities:  
- Develop and maintain project schedules and plans.  
- Coordinate with cross-functional teams to ensure project deliverables are met.  
- Monitor project progress and adjust plans as necessary.  
- Communicate project status to stakeholders.  
- Mitigate risks and resolve project issues.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor’s degree in Project Management, Business, or a related field.  
- At least 3 years of experience in project planning or management.  
- Proficiency with project management software (e.g., MS Project, Asana).  
- Strong communication and organizational skills.  
- Ability to work collaboratively and independently.
