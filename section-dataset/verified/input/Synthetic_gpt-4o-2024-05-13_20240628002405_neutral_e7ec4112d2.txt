Job Description:  
Bowen Group is seeking a diligent and detail-oriented Special Examiner to join our compliance team. The Special Examiner will be responsible for conducting thorough investigations, ensuring that all operations comply with regulatory standards, and providing detailed reports on findings.

Responsibilities:  
- Conduct comprehensive examinations of financial documents and transactions.  
- Identify and report any discrepancies or violations of regulations.  
- Collaborate with other team members to ensure accurate and timely reporting.  
- Provide recommendations for improving compliance practices.  
- Stay updated on relevant laws and regulations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or related field.  
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in a similar role.  
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.  
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.  
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office and financial analysis software.
