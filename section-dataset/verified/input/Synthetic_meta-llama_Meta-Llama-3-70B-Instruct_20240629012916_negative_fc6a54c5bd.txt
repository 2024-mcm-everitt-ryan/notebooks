Company: Fleming, Newman and Peterson

Job Title: Transit and Railroad Police

Job Description:
We're seeking a top-notch transit and railroad police officer to join our team. As a key member of our law enforcement unit, you'll be responsible for ensuring the safety and security of our passengers and employees. If you're a self-starter with a strong work ethic, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities:

 Patrol railroad property and transit facilities to prevent crimes and protect people and property
 Respond to calls for service, including accidents, disturbances, and other emergencies
 Enforce laws and regulations related to railroad and transit operations
 Collaborate with local law enforcement agencies to share intelligence and best practices
 Develop and maintain positive relationships with the community

Qualifications and Experience:
 High school diploma or equivalent required; college degree in criminal justice or a related field preferred
 2+ years of experience in law enforcement, preferably in a transit or railroad setting
 Ability to pass a thorough background check and obtain necessary certifications
 Valid driver's license with a clean driving record

Perks and Benefits:
 We offer a competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills
 Comprehensive health insurance with dental and vision coverage
 Generous paid time off, including vacation, sick leave, and holidays
 Opportunities for professional development and advancement
