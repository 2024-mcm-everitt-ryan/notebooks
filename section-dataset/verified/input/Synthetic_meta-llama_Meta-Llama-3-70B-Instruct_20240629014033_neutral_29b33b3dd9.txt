Company: Simpson and Sons
Role: Assistant Commissioner of Housing Policy

Job Description:
Simpson and Sons is seeking an experienced and dedicated Assistant Commissioner of Housing Policy to join our team. As a key member of our leadership team, you will contribute to the development and implementation of housing policies that promote affordable, accessible, and sustainable housing for all members of our community.

Responsibilities:

 Collaborate with stakeholders to develop and implement housing policies that align with our organization's mission and values
 Conduct research and analysis to identify trends, opportunities, and challenges in the housing sector
 Develop and maintain relationships with key stakeholders, including government agencies, community organizations, and industry partners
 Provide expert advice and guidance to internal and external stakeholders on housing policy matters
 Develop and manage budgets, reports, and other documentation related to housing policy initiatives

Qualifications and Experience:

 A degree in a relevant field, such as urban planning, public policy, or a related field
 At least 5 years of experience in housing policy, research, or a related field
 Strong analytical, communication, and project management skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
 Commitment to promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion in all aspects of work

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 80,000 to USD 110,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement savings plan, and paid time off.
