Company Background:
Colon-Gray is a reputable company in the construction industry, dedicated to providing top-notch services to our clients. We value teamwork, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Job Type: Paperhanger

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Paperhanger to join our team. As a Paperhanger, you will be responsible for hanging and finishing wallpaper, borders, and other decorative wall coverings. You will work closely with our clients to understand their design preferences and provide expert advice on paper selection and installation.

Responsibilities:

    Measure and cut wallpaper to fit specific areas
    Apply adhesive and hang wallpaper, ensuring a smooth and even finish
    Finish and trim edges to perfection
    Collaborate with clients to select paper designs and patterns
    Maintain a clean and organized workspace

Qualifications and Experience:

    2+ years of experience as a Paperhanger or in a related field
    Strong knowledge of wallpaper installation techniques and materials
    Excellent communication and customer service skills
    Ability to work independently and as part of a team

We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as paid time off and holidays.
