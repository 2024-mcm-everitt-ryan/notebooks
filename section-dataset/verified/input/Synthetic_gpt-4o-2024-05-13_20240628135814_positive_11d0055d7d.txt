Company Background:
Jones-Wagner is a leading global manufacturer in the casting industry, dedicated to innovation, quality, and sustainability. We pride ourselves on our commitment to fostering an inclusive workplace that reflects the diversity of our community.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and diligent Caster to join our dynamic team. This role involves the operation of casting equipment, monitoring the production process, and ensuring the quality of our final products.

Responsibilities:
- Operate and manage casting equipment according to safety standards.
- Monitor the production process and make adjustments as necessary to ensure product quality.
- Perform routine maintenance and troubleshooting of casting machinery.
- Maintain detailed records of production and quality control measures.
- Collaborate with team members to optimize production efficiency.

Qualifications and Experience:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Previous experience in a manufacturing or casting environment is preferred.
- Ability to read and interpret technical documents and blueprints.
- Strong attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
- Physical stamina and ability to lift up to 50 pounds.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $40,000 to $60,000 per annum, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Retirement plan with company match.
- Professional development and training opportunities.
- Inclusive work environment committed to diversity.
