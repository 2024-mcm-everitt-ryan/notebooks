Company: Lucas, Brooks and Fernandez

Job Title: Office Machine Repairer

Job Summary:
We are seeking a skilled Office Machine Repairer to join our team at Lucas, Brooks and Fernandez. As an Office Machine Repairer, you will be responsible for repairing and maintaining office equipment, including printers, copiers, and fax machines. If you have excellent problem-solving skills and a passion for fixing things, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities:

 Repair and maintain office equipment, including printers, copiers, and fax machines
 Troubleshoot issues with office equipment and diagnose problems
 Perform routine maintenance tasks to prevent equipment failures
 Collaborate with our customer service team to resolve issues and provide excellent customer service
 Keep accurate records of repairs and maintenance

Requirements:

 2+ years of experience in office machine repair
 Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced environment
 Excellent communication and customer service skills

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. Our facilities include Nursing Rooms for convenience, and we are proud to be an equal opportunities employer.
