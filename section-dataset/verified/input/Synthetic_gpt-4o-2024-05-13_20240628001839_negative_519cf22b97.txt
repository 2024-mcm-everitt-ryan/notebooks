Company: Lambert-Jones  
Role: Massage Therapist  

Company Background:  
Lambert-Jones is a boutique wellness center dedicated to providing high-quality massage therapy and holistic health services. Our team is committed to delivering personalized care and maintaining a serene environment for our clients.

Job Type: Full-Time  

Job Description and Responsibilities:  
We are seeking an experienced Massage Therapist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in various massage techniques and the ability to create a calming, therapeutic experience for clients. Responsibilities include:
- Conducting client consultations to understand their needs
- Designing and delivering tailored massage sessions
- Maintaining a clean and organized workspace
- Upholding the highest standards of professionalism and ethics

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Certification from an accredited massage therapy program
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in a professional setting
- Strong understanding of anatomy and physiology
- Ability to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends
- Excellent communication skills

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive salary ranging from $50,000 to $70,000 per year, based on experience and skills
- Health insurance and wellness benefits
- Maternity leave support is available to our female employees
- Paid vacation and sick leave
- Benefits are extended to employees' husbands or wives
- Continuing education opportunities

Application Process:  
Interested candidates should submit their resume and a cover letter detailing their experience and suitability for the role. We look forward to welcoming a dedicated professional to our team.
