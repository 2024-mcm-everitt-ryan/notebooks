Job Title: Appliance Repairer
Company: Rodriguez-Wells

About Us:
Rodriguez-Wells is a leading provider of home appliance repair services, dedicated to delivering exceptional customer experiences. We are committed to fostering an inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities for all.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Appliance Repairer to join our team. As an Appliance Repairer, you will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing a wide range of household appliances, providing excellent customer service, and ensuring timely completion of tasks.

Responsibilities:

 Diagnose and repair appliances, including refrigerators, ovens, dishwashers, and washing machines
 Communicate effectively with customers to understand their needs and provide solutions
 Manage work hours effectively to meet deadlines and prioritize tasks
 Collaborate with the team to share knowledge and best practices
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace, adhering to safety protocols

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in appliance repair or a related field
 Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds (with reasonable accommodations) and work in a fast-paced environment
 Excellent communication and customer service skills
 Valid driver's license and reliable transportation (for on-site repairs)

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $45,000 to $60,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive training program to enhance your skills and knowledge
 Ongoing opportunities for professional growth and development
 A dynamic and supportive work environment with a team of experienced professionals
 Flexible scheduling and a healthy work-life balance
