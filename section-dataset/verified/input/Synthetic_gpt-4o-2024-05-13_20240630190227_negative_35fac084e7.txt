Company Background:
Anderson, Lewis and Forbes is a renowned health consultancy firm dedicated to providing top-notch healthcare solutions. We pride ourselves on our empathetic approach and our commitment to the well-being of our community.

Job Type:
Full-Time Health Adviser

Job Description:
Anderson, Lewis and Forbes is seeking a Health Adviser to join our team. This role is essential in providing personalized health advice and support to our valued clients. She will be responsible for developing individualized health plans, providing emotional support, and ensuring the overall well-being of our clients.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement personalized health plans for clients.
- Provide emotional support and comfort to clients.
- Maintain detailed records of client progress and health status.
- Collaborate with other healthcare professionals to ensure comprehensive care.
- Engage with the community to promote health awareness and education.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Health Sciences, Nursing, or a related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in a health advisory role.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Her ability to soothe and comfort clients is essential.
- Experience working with diverse populations is preferred.

- Comprehensive health insurance.

- Supportive and inclusive work environment.
