Company: Harris, Sherman and Cohen

Job Title: Media Worker

About Us:
Harris, Sherman and Cohen is a leading media company that values diversity and inclusion. We are committed to creating a workplace where everyone feels valued, respected, and empowered to bring their authentic selves to work.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and creative Media Worker to join our team. As a Media Worker, you will be responsible for developing and implementing media campaigns that drive engagement and conversion. You will work closely with our creative team to concept and produce innovative media content that resonates with our target audience.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and execute media plans across multiple channels, including social media, email, and influencer partnerships
 Collaborate with the creative team to concept and produce innovative media content
 Analyze campaign performance and provide recommendations for optimization
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging platforms

Qualifications and Experience:

 2+ years of experience in media planning and buying
 Strong understanding of media metrics and analytics
 Excellent communication and project management skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, flexible working hours, and professional development opportunities. We provide training on accessibility and inclusion, and we ensure all candidates can participate fully in the hiring process.
