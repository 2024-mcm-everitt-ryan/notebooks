Company: Bryant, Roth and Nelson

Job Title: Mailroom Control Clerk

About Us:
Bryant, Roth and Nelson is a leading financial services firm dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and innovative solutions.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Mailroom Control Clerk to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for receiving, sorting, and distributing mail and packages to various departments within our organization.

Responsibilities:

 Receive and sort mail and packages from various carriers
 Distribute mail and packages to designated departments and personnel
 Maintain accurate records of mail and package delivery
 Perform other administrative tasks as required

Qualifications and Experience:

 High school diploma or equivalent required; college degree preferred
 1-2 years of experience in a mailroom or administrative role
 Strong organizational and communication skills
 Ability to lift up to 25 pounds and stand for long periods

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program.

Work Environment:
Our office environment is fast-paced and dynamic, with a strong focus on teamwork and collaboration. We are looking for individuals who are flexible, adaptable, and able to thrive in a rapidly changing environment.

Note: Please be advised that we require a clean criminal record and adhere to a strict dress code policy, which includes being clean-shaven daily.
