Company: Barnes, Payne and Sanders

Job Type: Nuclear Technicians

Job Description:
Barnes, Payne and Sanders is seeking experienced and skilled Nuclear Technicians to join our team. As a Nuclear Technician, you will be responsible for operating and maintaining nuclear equipment, ensuring compliance with safety protocols, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to achieve project goals.

Responsibilities:

 Operate and maintain nuclear equipment and facilities
 Conduct routine tests and inspections to ensure equipment functionality and safety
 Collaborate with engineers and scientists to develop and implement new nuclear technologies
 Ensure compliance with safety protocols and industry regulations
 Provide technical support and training to junior team members

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Nuclear Engineering, Physics, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in nuclear technology or a related field
 Strong understanding of nuclear safety protocols and industry regulations
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 65,000 to USD 90,000, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
