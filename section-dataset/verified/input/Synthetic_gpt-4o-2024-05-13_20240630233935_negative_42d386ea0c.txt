Job Description:
Kent LLC is seeking a skilled tool grinder to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for grinding and sharpening various tools to ensure optimal performance and precision. This role demands meticulous attention to detail and a strong commitment to quality.

Responsibilities:
- Grind and sharpen tools to precise specifications.
- Inspect tools and equipment for defects and ensure proper functionality.
- Maintain grinding equipment and perform routine maintenance.
- Collaborate with the engineering team to improve tool designs.
- Uphold safety standards and ensure a clean work environment.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Proven experience as a tool grinder or in a similar role.
- Strong mechanical aptitude and an eye for detail.
- Ability to read and interpret technical drawings.
- Excellent hand-eye coordination and manual dexterity.
- Previous experience in a male-dominated industry is a plus.
- Driven by success and motivated to achieve results.
