Company: Wall, Benitez and Freeman

Job Title: Criminalist

Job Type: Full-time

About Us:
Wall, Benitez and Freeman is a leading forensic science firm dedicated to providing expert analysis and consultation services to law enforcement agencies and private entities. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, integrity, and innovation.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Criminalist to join our team. As a Criminalist, you will be responsible for analyzing evidence, conducting research, and providing expert testimony in court. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in forensic science, excellent analytical skills, and the ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze evidence using various scientific techniques and instruments
 Conduct research and stay up-to-date on the latest developments in forensic science
 Provide expert testimony in court and prepare written reports
 Collaborate with law enforcement agencies and other stakeholders
 Maintain accurate and detailed records of evidence and analysis

Qualifications and Experience:

 Master's degree in Forensic Science, Criminal Justice, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in forensic analysis or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills
 Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment

Note: This role requires the ability to lift up to 25 pounds, frequent walking, and standing for extended periods. Applicants must be able to perform tasks that require manual dexterity and hand-eye coordination.
