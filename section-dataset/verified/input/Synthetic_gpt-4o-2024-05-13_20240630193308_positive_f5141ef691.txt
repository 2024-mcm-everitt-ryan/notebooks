Company Background:

Gonzalez Inc is dedicated to providing top-notch personal care and service to our clients. With a strong commitment to creating a diverse and inclusive workforce, we strive to ensure that every member of our team feels valued and respected.

Job Type:
Personal Care and Service Worker

Job Description:

As a Personal Care and Service Worker at Gonzalez Inc, you will be integral in providing exceptional care and support to our clients. Your role will involve assisting with daily activities, ensuring their comfort and well-being, and contributing to a positive and nurturing environment.

Responsibilities:
- Assist clients with personal care tasks such as bathing, dressing, and grooming
- Provide companionship and emotional support
- Monitor and document the client's health and progress
- Perform light housekeeping duties and assist with meal preparation
- Coordinate with healthcare professionals as needed

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent
- Previous experience in personal care or a similar field preferred
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills
- Empathy, patience, and a caring nature
- Ability to work flexible hours, including weekends and holidays

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary range of $30,000 to $40,000 annually, based on experience and skills
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
- Paid time off and flexible scheduling
- Ongoing training and professional development opportunities
- Supportive and inclusive work environment

Note:
Gonzalez Inc. is committed to fair and equitable pay practices. 

Application Process:

To apply, please submit your resume and a cover letter detailing your experience and why you are passionate about personal care and service.

Equal Opportunity Employer:
Gonzalez Inc. is dedicated to increasing the number of women in our workforce. We encourage women to apply and share their unique perspectives and skills.
