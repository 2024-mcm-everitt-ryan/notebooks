Fabrication Foreman – Oldham Area - £33,000 to £37,000

About the Company
Due to company growth, the current Fabrication Manager is moving to a more technical-based role across the business. Therefore, the opportunity has arisen for a talented foreman to join the team. The current Manager will remain involved at a higher level of oversight alongside the Workshop Manager and Production Director. In this role, you’ll be working closely with the Managers, Production Engineers, Design Engineer, and Sales teams, as well as being responsible for the running of the fabrication department.
