Animal Breeder at Craig Group:

Company Background:
Craig Group is a renowned organization dedicated to the highest standards of animal care and breeding. We pride ourselves on our commitment to ethical practices and excellence in animal husbandry.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description:
We are looking for a passionate and knowledgeable Animal Breeder to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for planning and overseeing the breeding of animals, ensuring high standards of animal care, and maintaining accurate records. This role offers the opportunity to work in a supportive and inclusive environment dedicated to the welfare of animals.

Responsibilities:

  - Plan and manage breeding programs.
  - Ensure the health and well-being of animals.
  - Maintain detailed records of breeding and animal health.
  - Collaborate with veterinarians and other animal care staff.
  - Adhere to ethical breeding practices and guidelines.


Qualifications and Experience:

  - Bachelor’s degree in Animal Science or related field.
  - Minimum of 2 years of experience in animal breeding.
  - Strong knowledge of animal genetics and breeding techniques.
  - Excellent communication and organizational skills.
  - Passion for animal welfare and ethical breeding practices.


Perks and Benefits:

  - Comprehensive health insurance.
  - Paid time off and sick leave.
  - Professional development opportunities.
  - Supportive work environment valuing diversity and inclusion.
  - We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £35,000 to £50,000, based on your experience and skills.
