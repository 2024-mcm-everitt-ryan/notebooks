Responsibilities

As a Warehouse Associate, you will:
- Work in a fast-paced order fulfillment environment.
- Safely operate a stand-up forklift (order selector/cherry picker) to pull and prepare outbound customer orders.
- Utilize RF devices to complete shipping/receiving transactions in our operating system.
- Accurately and timely receive, verify, stage, and stock all incoming material.
- Clean the workspace as you go and participate with the team in keeping our facility clean, safe, and accident-free.

Qualifications
- Positive attitude, dependability, and strong work ethic.
- Self-starter with the ability to learn our systems quickly.
- Prior warehouse experience in shipping, receiving, delivery, or inventory is required.
- Previous stand-up forklift (order selector/cherry picker) operating at heights is strongly recommended.
- Previous forklift experience is strongly recommended.
- Must be able to lift items that weigh up to 50 lbs regularly.
- A safe individual with knowledge of safety regulations and procedures.
