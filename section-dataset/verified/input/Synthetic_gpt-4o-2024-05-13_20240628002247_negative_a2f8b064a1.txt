Job Description and Responsibilities:
We are seeking a skilled Aircraft Mechanic to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for inspecting, repairing, and maintaining aircraft to ensure they meet safety regulations and operational standards. Key responsibilities include:

- Performing regular maintenance checks on aircraft.
- Diagnosing and repairing mechanical and electrical issues.
- Maintaining detailed records of maintenance work.
- Collaborating with other technicians and engineers to improve processes.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in aircraft maintenance.
- FAA Airframe and Powerplant (A&P) certification.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
- Excellent communication skills.
