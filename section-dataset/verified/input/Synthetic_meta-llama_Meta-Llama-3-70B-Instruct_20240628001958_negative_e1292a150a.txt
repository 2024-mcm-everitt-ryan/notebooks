Company: Stark and Sons
Role: Safe Event Coordinator

About Us:
Stark and Sons is a leading event management company that specializes in hosting safe and memorable events. We're a team of innovative thinkers who are passionate about creating unique experiences for our clients.

Job Description:
We're seeking a talented Safe Event Coordinator to join our team. As a Safe Event Coordinator, you'll be responsible for ensuring the safety and success of our events. This is an exciting opportunity for a young, up-and-coming professional to gain hands-on experience in the events industry.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate with vendors, suppliers, and internal teams to ensure seamless event execution
 Conduct risk assessments and develop safety protocols for events
 Collaborate with the events team to design and implement event layouts and logistics
 Ensure compliance with safety regulations and industry standards
 Identify and mitigate potential safety risks

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Event Management, Hospitality, or a related field
 1-2 years of experience in event planning or a related field
 Strong knowledge of safety protocols and regulations
 Excellent communication and organizational skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
