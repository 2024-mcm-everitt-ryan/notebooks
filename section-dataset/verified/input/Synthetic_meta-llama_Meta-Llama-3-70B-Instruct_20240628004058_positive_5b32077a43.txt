Company: Montoya, Alvarado and Santana

Job Title: Medicolegal Analyst

About Us:
Montoya, Alvarado and Santana is a leading consulting firm specializing in medicolegal services. We pride ourselves on our diverse and inclusive team, and we're committed to creating a workplace where everyone can thrive.

Job Description:
We're seeking a skilled Medicolegal Analyst to join our team. As a Medicolegal Analyst, you will be responsible for reviewing and analyzing medical records, identifying key findings, and providing expert opinions to support our clients' cases. If you're passionate about the intersection of medicine and law, and you're committed to delivering high-quality results, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities:

 Review and analyze medical records to identify key findings and trends
 Provide expert opinions to support clients' cases
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop comprehensive case strategies
 Stay up-to-date with the latest medical and legal developments in the field

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a life science, nursing, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in a medicolegal or healthcare setting
 Strong analytical and communication skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and a generous paid time-off policy.

We celebrate the contributions of LGBTQ+ professionals and encourage them to apply. We aim to create a diverse team and are actively seeking professionals from the LGBTQ+ community to bring their unique perspectives and experiences to our organisation.
