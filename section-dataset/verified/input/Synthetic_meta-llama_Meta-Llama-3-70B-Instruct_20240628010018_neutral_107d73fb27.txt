Company: White-Davis

Role: Groundskeeping Worker

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Join our team at White-Davis as a Groundskeeping Worker and contribute to maintaining the beauty of our outdoor spaces. We are seeking a dedicated individual who can work efficiently to ensure our grounds are well-manicured and safe for our employees and visitors.

Responsibilities:

 Maintain lawns, gardens, and outdoor spaces by mowing, pruning, and watering
 Remove trash and debris from outdoor areas
 Operate groundskeeping equipment and tools safely and efficiently
 Collaborate with other team members to achieve groundskeeping goals
 Perform other duties as assigned by the supervisor

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in groundskeeping or a related field
 Ability to lift 50 pounds and work in a fast-paced outdoor environment
 Valid driver's license (not required but an asset)

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $35,000 to $45,000, based on your experience and skills (USD).
 Comprehensive health insurance
 Paid time off for all employees, including parental leave
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 Our company culture is built on respect and inclusivity, and we are dedicated to building a team that reflects the diversity of our customers
