Company: Gordon, Hill and Atkins

Role: Staff Auditor

Company Background:
Gordon, Hill and Atkins is a well-established public accounting firm dedicated to providing exceptional audit, tax, and consulting services to a diverse range of clients. We pride ourselves on fostering a collaborative and inclusive environment where all team members can thrive.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a detail-oriented Staff Auditor to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will perform financial audits, ensure compliance with regulations, and provide valuable insights to our clients.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct comprehensive audits of financial statements.
- Review and evaluate internal controls.
- Prepare detailed audit reports with findings and recommendations.
- Collaborate with team members to ensure audit projects are completed efficiently.
- Stay current with industry trends and regulatory changes.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field.
- Certified Public Accountant (CPA) designation preferred.
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in auditing or related field.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite is essential.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Ability to work collaboratively with diverse team members.
- Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
- Solid understanding of industry trends and regulations.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $50,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Generous paid time off and holiday schedule.
- Professional development opportunities and support for continuing education.
- Retirement savings plan with company match.
- A supportive and inclusive work environment that values diversity.
