Company: Thomas-Powell
Role: Cargo and Freight Agent

About Us:
Thomas-Powell is a leading logistics company that connects businesses and individuals worldwide. We are committed to delivering exceptional service, building strong relationships, and promoting a culture of respect and inclusion.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated and organized Cargo and Freight Agent to join our team. As a Cargo and Freight Agent, you will be responsible for coordinating the transportation of goods, managing shipments, and ensuring timely delivery. You will work closely with our customers, carriers, and internal teams to provide exceptional service and resolve any issues that may arise.

Responsibilities:

 Coordinate the transportation of goods, including tracking and tracing shipments
 Manage and prioritize multiple shipments simultaneously
 Communicate with customers, carriers, and internal teams to provide updates and resolve issues
 Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and company policies
 Collaborate with other teams to improve processes and procedures

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience in logistics, transportation, or a related field
 Strong communication and organizational skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize tasks effectively
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and logistics software

Perks and Benefits:
 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 45,000 to USD 60,000, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 A dynamic and inclusive work environment
