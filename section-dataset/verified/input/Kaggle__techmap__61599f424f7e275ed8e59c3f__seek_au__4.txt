About the business

SEQ Ice Cream is a FMCG Distribution Company that distributes Streets & Ben & Jerry's Ice cream to the Route Trade throughout South East QLD. We are a highly motivated and passionate business delivering excellence in customer service. The team adopts a winning culture, whereby both individual and team performance is encouraged and recognised.

About the role

We are looking for a dynamic & energetic person who will enjoy servicing new & existing customers across South East QLD including the Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast & Brisbane. You will be required to deliver everyone's favourite ice creams on set runs in our modern fleet of trucks. If you enjoy developing customer relationships, merchandise stock to look its best, and promoting brands loved by so many, then this is for you. Applications close Sunday 17th October 2021.

Benefits and perks

- Work in a dynamic and energetic team with market-leading brands like Ben & Jerry's, Paddle Pop, Magnum, and more.
- Enjoy the outdoors and the interaction with an exciting variety of customers.
- The role is Monday-Friday with early starts that free up your afternoons.
- Work in some of Southeast QLD's best locations from the Gold Coast to the Sunny Coast.
- Opportunities arise to progress within a growing business.

Skills and experience

The successful candidate MUST hold a current MR truck driver's license OR greater with a minimum of 2 years driving experience. Solid experience in customer service is essential along with the ability to operate a handheld device and problem-solve on the go.
