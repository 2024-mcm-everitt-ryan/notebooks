Job Title: Food Scientist
Company: Green PLC

We are seeking a highly motivated and innovative Food Scientist to join our team. As a key player in our research and development department, you will be responsible for developing new food products and improving existing ones.

Job Description:
We are looking for individuals who are new to the field and eager to bring fresh insights to the table. If you have recent credentials and are passionate about creating innovative food products, we want to hear from you. This role is perfect for those who have recently entered the industry and are looking to make a significant impact.

Responsibilities:

 Develop new food products and improve existing ones
 Conduct sensory evaluations and analyze data to inform product development
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to launch new products
 Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in food science

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Food Science or related field
 0-2 years of experience in food product development
 Recent knowledge of food trends and technologies

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
