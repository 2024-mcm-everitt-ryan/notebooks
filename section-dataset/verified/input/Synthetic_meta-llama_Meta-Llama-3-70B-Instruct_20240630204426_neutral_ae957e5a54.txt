Job Title: Juvenile Justice Trainer
Company: Ryan-Flores

About Us:
Ryan-Flores is a leading organization dedicated to providing high-quality training and support services to juvenile justice professionals. We are committed to a diverse and inclusive hiring process and strive to create a work environment that values and respects individual differences.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Juvenile Justice Trainer to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for designing and delivering training programs to juvenile justice professionals, ensuring that they have the necessary skills and knowledge to provide effective services to young people.

Responsibilities:

 Design and deliver training programs on juvenile justice topics, including trauma-informed care, cultural competence, and evidence-based practices
 Collaborate with subject matter experts to develop training content and materials
 Facilitate training sessions, workshops, and conferences for juvenile justice professionals
 Evaluate the effectiveness of training programs and make recommendations for improvement
 Develop and maintain relationships with key stakeholders, including juvenile justice agencies and community organizations

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g., social work, education, criminal justice)
 Minimum 3 years of experience in juvenile justice, training, or a related field
 Proven experience in designing and delivering training programs
 Strong facilitation and presentation skills
 Ability to work effectively with diverse groups of people
 Knowledge of juvenile justice issues and trends

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $75,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan.
