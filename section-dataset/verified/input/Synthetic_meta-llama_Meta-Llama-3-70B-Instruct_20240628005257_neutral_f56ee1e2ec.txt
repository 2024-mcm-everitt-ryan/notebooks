Company: Galvan Inc

Job Title: Nurse Home Visitor

About Us:
Galvan Inc is a leading healthcare provider dedicated to delivering high-quality patient care in the comfort of their own homes. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment that values the unique contributions of our employees.

Job Description:
We are seeking a compassionate and experienced Nurse Home Visitor to join our team. As a Nurse Home Visitor, you will provide medical care and support to patients in their homes, fostering a positive and supportive relationship with them and their families.

Responsibilities:

 Conduct patient assessments and develop personalized care plans
 Administer medications and treatments as prescribed by physicians
 Educate patients and their families on disease management and self-care techniques
 Collaborate with other healthcare professionals to ensure comprehensive care

Qualifications and Experience:

 Registered Nurse (RN) license
 2+ years of experience in home healthcare or a related field
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including:
 Competitive salary (USD 65,000 - 80,000 per year, based on experience and skills)
 Generous paid time off and holidays
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 A supportive and inclusive work environment

We believe that diversity makes us stronger and more innovative. We encourage applications from individuals of all gender identities and orientations. Our team values contributions from LGBTQ+ members, and we support and celebrate all forms of family structures.
