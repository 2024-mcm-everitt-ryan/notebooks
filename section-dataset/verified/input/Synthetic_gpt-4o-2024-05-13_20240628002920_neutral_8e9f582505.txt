Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a detail-oriented Office Machine Aide to join our administrative team. The ideal candidate will assist with the setup, operation, and maintenance of office machines, ensuring they function efficiently to support the smooth operation of the office.

Responsibilities:
- Setup and configure office machines such as copiers, printers, and fax machines.
- Perform routine maintenance and troubleshooting to resolve technical issues.
- Keep track of supply levels and reorder as necessary.
- Provide support to employees in using office machinery.
- Maintain records of repairs and service schedules.
- Coordinate with external service providers for major repairs or upgrades.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Previous experience in a similar role or with office machinery is preferred.
- Basic technical skills and the ability to troubleshoot equipment issues.
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication skills and the ability to work well in a team.
- Proficiency in basic computer applications.
