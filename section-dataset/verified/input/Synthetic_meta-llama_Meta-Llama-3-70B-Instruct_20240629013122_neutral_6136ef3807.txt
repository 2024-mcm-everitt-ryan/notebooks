Company: Torres-Villegas
Role: Payment Analyst

About Us:
Torres-Villegas is a leading financial services company dedicated to providing innovative payment solutions to businesses worldwide. We strive to create an inclusive work environment that values diversity, equity, and respect for all individuals.

Job Description:
We are seeking a detail-oriented and analytical Payment Analyst to join our team. As a Payment Analyst, you will be responsible for ensuring the accuracy and timeliness of payment processing, identifying and resolving payment discrepancies, and maintaining a high level of customer satisfaction.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze and reconcile payment transactions to ensure accuracy and compliance with company policies
 Identify and resolve payment discrepancies in a timely and professional manner
 Collaborate with internal teams to improve payment processing efficiency and reduce errors
 Develop and maintain strong relationships with customers to ensure high levels of customer satisfaction
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices to continuously improve payment processing procedures

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in payment analysis or a related field
 Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
 Strong communication and customer service skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and generous paid time off.
