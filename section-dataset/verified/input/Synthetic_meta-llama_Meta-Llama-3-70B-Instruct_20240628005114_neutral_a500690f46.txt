Company: Martin, White and Casey

Job Title: Elevator Installer

Job Description:
Martin, White and Casey is seeking a skilled and experienced Elevator Installer to join our team. As an Elevator Installer, you will be responsible for installing, testing, and repairing elevators, escalators, and moving walkways. You will work closely with our team to ensure that all installations are done safely and efficiently.

Responsibilities:

 Install, test, and repair elevators, escalators, and moving walkways
 Read and interpret blueprints and technical diagrams
 Work closely with the installation team to ensure efficient and safe installation
 Perform routine maintenance and repairs on existing equipment

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience as an Elevator Installer
 Strong mechanical aptitude and problem-solving skills
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds and work at heights
 High school diploma or equivalent required

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 55,000 to USD 75,000, based on your experience and skills. We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program. Our facilities include Nursing Rooms for convenience.
