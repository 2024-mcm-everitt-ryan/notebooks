Systems Analyst at Huffman-Robinson

About Us:
Huffman-Robinson is a leading technology firm that specializes in innovative solutions for businesses. Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering exceptional results and pushing the boundaries of what is possible.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly competitive and driven Systems Analyst to join our team. As a key player in our IT department, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining our systems infrastructure. If you're a self-starter who thrives in a fast-paced environment and is always looking to improve processes, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities:

 Design and implement system architecture to meet business needs
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and prioritize system improvements
 Develop and maintain technical documentation
 Troubleshoot system issues and provide timely resolutions

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience in systems analysis or a related field
 Strong understanding of system architecture and infrastructure
 Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program.
