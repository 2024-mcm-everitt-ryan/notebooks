Company: Dunn, Dickson and George  
Role: Field Technician

Company Background:
Dunn, Dickson and George is a leading provider of technology solutions, known for its commitment to innovation and excellence. Our team is dedicated to delivering superior service to our clients across various industries.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Field Technician to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will excel in fast-paced environments and be passionate about providing top-notch technical support to our clients.

Responsibilities:
- Install, maintain, and repair various technological equipment.
- Conduct regular site visits to ensure optimal functionality of client systems.
- Provide technical support and troubleshooting both on-site and remotely.
- Maintain detailed records of service calls and report issues to the management team.
- Collaborate with other team members to enhance service efficiency.

Qualifications and Experience:
- A minimum of 2 years of experience in a similar role.
- Strong technical skills and knowledge of various technological systems.
- Excellent problem-solving abilities and attention to detail.
- Strong communication skills and the ability to work independently.
- Valid driver's license and willingness to travel.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from $45,000 to $60,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Parental leave designed to support our diverse workforce.
- Nursing rooms available for employees who need them.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- Supportive work environment that celebrates all forms of family structures.

Application Process:
Interested candidates are encouraged to apply with their resume and a cover letter detailing their relevant experience and why they would be a great fit for Dunn, Dickson and George.
