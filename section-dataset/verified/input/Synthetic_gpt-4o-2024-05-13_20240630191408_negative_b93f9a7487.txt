Company: Reed, Cervantes and Martinez

Role: Vessel Construction Manager

Company Background:
Reed, Cervantes and Martinez is a leading player in the marine construction industry, known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and safety. We have been delivering top-notch vessel construction services for over three decades.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
Reed, Cervantes and Martinez seeks a Vessel Construction Manager who will be responsible for overseeing the planning, coordination, and completion of vessel construction projects. She will ensure that all projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of quality.

Responsibilities:
- Managing day-to-day operations of vessel construction projects.
- Overseeing the work of subcontractors and ensuring adherence to safety regulations.
- Coordinating with clients to understand their needs and expectations.
- Preparing project schedules and budgets.
- Ensuring that all team members are motivated and work collaboratively.
- Bringing warmth and positivity to the team environment.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Marine Engineering or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years experience in vessel construction management.
- Proven ability to handle stress and remain detail-oriented.
- Strong understanding of marine construction methodologies and safety standards.
- Excellent communication and leadership skills.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from €70,000 to €90,000 annually, based on experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health insurance plans.
- Paid time off and flexible working hours.
- Professional development opportunities.
