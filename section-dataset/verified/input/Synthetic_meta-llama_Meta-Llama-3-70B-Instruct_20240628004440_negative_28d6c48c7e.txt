Company Background: 
Mclaughlin, Bell and Ellis is a leading provider of administrative services, dedicated to delivering exceptional support to our clients. We pride ourselves on our traditional values and commitment to excellence.

Job Type: 
Principal Administrative Associate

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented individual to join our team as a Principal Administrative Associate. As a key member of our administrative team, you will provide critical support to our senior management, ensuring the smooth operation of our day-to-day activities.

Responsibilities:

 Manage complex calendars, coordinate travel arrangements, and prepare comprehensive itineraries
 Develop and maintain effective filing systems, both physical and digital
 Prepare and distribute confidential documents, reports, and presentations
 Provide exceptional customer service to internal and external stakeholders
 Develop and maintain positive working relationships with senior management and colleagues

Qualifications and Experience:

 5+ years of experience in an administrative role, preferably in a traditional or conservative industry
 High school diploma or equivalent required; bachelor's degree preferred
 Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite and Google Workspace
 Excellent organizational, communication, and problem-solving skills
 Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion

Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision coverage, as well as a retirement matching program. We also offer a range of family-friendly benefits, including generous parental leave and flexible work arrangements.
