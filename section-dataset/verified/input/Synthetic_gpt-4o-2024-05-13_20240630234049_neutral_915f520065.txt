Company: Harper LLC  
Role: Recruiting & Onboarding Coordinator  

Company Background:  
Harper LLC is a dynamic company specializing in workforce solutions. We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive culture that values diversity and innovation.  

Job Type:  
Full-Time  

Job Description:  
Harper LLC is seeking a dedicated Recruiting & Onboarding Coordinator to join our team. The ideal candidate will excel in managing the full cycle of recruiting, from talent acquisition to onboarding, ensuring a seamless experience for new hires.  

Responsibilities:  
- Manage the end-to-end recruitment process, from job posting to offer acceptance.  
- Develop and implement effective onboarding programs.  
- Coordinate with hiring managers to identify staffing needs.  
- Conduct candidate interviews, both in person and virtually.  
- Maintain accurate records of candidates and new hires.  
- Ensure compliance with company policies and legal regulations.  
- Assist in developing strategies to improve recruitment processes.  

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field.  
- At least 2 years of experience in recruiting or human resources.  
- Strong organizational and communication skills.  
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.  
- Proficiency in HR software and Microsoft Office Suite.  
- High attention to detail and ability to multitask.

Perks and Benefits:  
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.  
- Paid time off and company holidays.  
- Professional development opportunities.  
- Flexible work hours.  
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from €40,000 to €55,000, based on your experience and skills.
