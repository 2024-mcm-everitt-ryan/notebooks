Company: Smith, Wong and Taylor

Job Title: Pipefitter

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Pipefitter to join our team. As a Pipefitter, you will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing pipes, fixtures, and appliances. The ideal candidate will have experience working with various types of pipes and fittings, as well as a strong understanding of safety protocols.

Responsibilities:

 Install, maintain, and repair pipes, fixtures, and appliances
 Collaborate with team members to achieve project goals
 Develop and implement efficient pipefitting methods
 Ensure compliance with safety protocols and industry standards
 Mentor and train junior team members

Qualifications and Experience:

 3+ years of experience as a Pipefitter
 Strong understanding of safety protocols and industry standards
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team
 Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
 Candidates must be able to lift up to 50 pounds and work in confined spaces

Perks and Benefits:

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health and dental benefits
 Opportunities for professional development and growth
 Collaborative and inclusive work environment
