Company Background
King-Thomas is a leading construction firm known for delivering high-quality projects globally. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace, focusing on safety and quality in everything we do.

Job Type
Full-Time

Job Description
We are seeking a dedicated Construction Safety and Quality Inspector to join our team. This position is part of our effort to promote women in the industry. The ideal candidate will ensure that all construction projects adhere to the highest safety and quality standards.

Responsibilities
- Conduct regular safety and quality inspections on construction sites.
- Report and document safety violations and quality defects.
- Collaborate with project managers to implement corrective actions.
- Provide training and guidance on safety protocols and quality standards.
- Ensure compliance with all relevant regulations and company policies.

Qualifications and Experience Needed
- Bachelor's degree in a related field or equivalent work experience.
- At least 3 years of experience in construction safety and quality inspection.
- Strong knowledge of safety regulations and quality standards.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Certifications in safety and quality management are a plus.

Perks and Benefits
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Professional development opportunities.
- Flexible working hours and a supportive work environment.

How to Apply
Interested candidates, especially women looking to make an impact in the construction industry, are encouraged to apply by submitting their resume and cover letter to careers@king-thomas.com.

King-Thomas is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
