Company Background:

Bradford-Scott is a leading provider of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) services. We specialize in providing high-quality maintenance and repair services to residential and commercial clients. Our team is committed to delivering exceptional customer service and ensuring the comfort and satisfaction of our clients.

Job Type:

Full-Time Thermostat Repairer

Job Description:

Bradford-Scott is seeking a skilled thermostat repairer to join our team. The ideal candidate will possess a strong technical background and experience in diagnosing and repairing thermostat issues. They will be responsible for ensuring that all repairs are completed efficiently and to the highest standards.

Responsibilities:

- Diagnose and repair thermostat issues in residential and commercial properties.
- Perform routine maintenance on HVAC systems.
- Communicate effectively with clients to explain issues and solutions.
- Ensure all repairs meet safety and quality standards.
- Maintain accurate records of repairs and services performed.
- Stay updated on the latest industry trends and technologies.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:

- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Technical certification in HVAC or related field preferred.
- Minimum 2 years of experience in thermostat repair or HVAC services.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and customer service skills.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Valid driver's license and reliable transportation.

- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Ongoing training and professional development opportunities.
- Company-provided tools and equipment.
- Opportunities for career advancement within the company.
