Company: Reid, Brown and Price

Job Title: Community Marketing Manager

About Us:
Reid, Brown and Price is a marketing firm dedicated to building strong relationships with our clients and community. We believe that diversity and inclusivity are essential to our success.

Job Description:
We are seeking a Community Marketing Manager to lead our community outreach efforts. The ideal candidate will have experience in developing and implementing marketing campaigns that engage diverse audiences.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and execute community marketing strategies to reach underserved populations
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to create culturally sensitive marketing materials
- Build relationships with community leaders and organizations to promote our brand
- Analyze campaign results and provide recommendations for improvement

Qualifications:
- 3+ years of experience in marketing or a related field
- Proven track record of developing successful community marketing campaigns
- Excellent communication and project management skills
- Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
- Fluency in multiple languages is a plus

Benefits:
- Salary range: $60,000 - $75,000 per year
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Company matched pension
- Paid time off and holidays
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
