Job Title: Watershed Maintainer
Company: Kirk-Aguilar

About Us:
Kirk-Aguilar is a leading environmental conservation organization dedicated to preserving and protecting our planet's natural resources. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace that values the contributions of all team members.

Job Description:
We are seeking a knowledgeable and skilled Watershed Maintainer to join our team. As a Watershed Maintainer, you will be responsible for maintaining and preserving watersheds, monitoring water quality, and implementing conservation practices. If you have a strong professional background and expertise in watershed management, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Maintain and preserve watersheds, including monitoring water quality and implementing conservation practices
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop and implement watershed management plans
 Conduct site visits to monitor and assess watershed conditions
 Develop and maintain relationships with local stakeholders, including community groups and government agencies
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in watershed management

Qualifications and Experience:

 Must have a degree in Environmental Science, Biology, or a related field
 3+ years of experience in watershed management or a related field
 Strong knowledge of watershed management principles and practices
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $55,000 to $70,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
