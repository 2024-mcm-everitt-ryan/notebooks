Company: Scott-Golden
Role: Criminalist

Job Description:
As a criminalist at Scott-Golden, you will play a critical role in analyzing evidence and providing expert testimony to support law enforcement agencies in their investigations. Your attention to detail, analytical skills, and ability to communicate complex information effectively will be essential in this role.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze physical evidence, including DNA, fingerprints, and other trace materials
 Develop and maintain databases of evidence and case files
 Provide expert testimony in court proceedings
 Collaborate with law enforcement agencies to understand their evidence needs and provide guidance on best practices
 Stay up-to-date with the latest forensic science techniques and technologies

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Forensic Science, Biology, Chemistry, or a related field
 At least 2 years of experience in a forensic laboratory or related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement plan. Our team-oriented work environment encourages collaboration, innovation, and professional growth.
