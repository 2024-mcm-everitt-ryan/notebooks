Company: Brown-Baker

Job Title: Network Services Manager

Job Description:
Brown-Baker is seeking a highly skilled Network Services Manager to join our IT team. As a Network Services Manager, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining our network infrastructure. You will also oversee the management of network services, ensuring high availability, scalability, and security.

Responsibilities:

 Design and implement network architecture and infrastructure
 Manage and maintain network services, including routers, switches, and firewalls
 Ensure network security and compliance with industry standards
 Troubleshoot network issues and perform root cause analysis
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver business objectives

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
 5+ years of experience in network administration and management
 CCNA or CCNP certification is an asset
 Strong understanding of network protocols, architecture, and security
 Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 80,000 to USD 110,000, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
