Company: Sanders-Smith

Role: Office Administrator

Company Background:
Sanders-Smith is a dynamic and inclusive organization dedicated to fostering an environment where all employees can thrive. We prioritize diversity and encourage a workplace culture that values the unique contributions of each team member.

Job Type: Full-Time

Job Description:
Sanders-Smith is seeking an organized and efficient Office Administrator to ensure the smooth running of our office operations. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication and organizational skills, and a proactive approach to problem-solving.

Responsibilities:
- Manage daily office operations and provide administrative support to various departments.
- Coordinate office activities and schedule meetings and appointments.
- Maintain office supplies inventory and place orders when necessary.
- Assist in preparing reports, presentations, and correspondence.
- Handle incoming and outgoing mail and emails promptly.
- Ensure a welcoming environment for visitors and employees.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Proven experience as an Office Administrator, Administrative Assistant, or relevant role.
- Proficiency in MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and office management software.
- Strong organizational and time management skills.
- Excellent written and verbal communication abilities.
- Ability to multitask and prioritize tasks effectively.
- High school diploma; a degree in office administration or a related field is a plus.
