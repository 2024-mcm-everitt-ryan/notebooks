Company: Duncan-Garcia

Job Title: Sanitation Engineer

Company Background:
Duncan-Garcia is a leading environmental services company dedicated to providing innovative solutions for a sustainable future. We are committed to creating a workplace culture that values diversity, equity, and inclusion.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Sanitation Engineer to join our team. As a Sanitation Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining waste management systems that meet the highest environmental standards.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement effective sanitation systems to minimize waste and maximize recycling
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure compliance with environmental regulations
 Conduct site visits to assess waste management facilities and provide recommendations for improvement
 Analyze data to identify trends and areas for improvement in sanitation operations

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Environmental Engineering, Civil Engineering, or a related field
 5+ years of experience in sanitation engineering or a related field
 Proven track record of designing and implementing effective sanitation systems
 Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
 Must be able to work independently with minimal supervision and navigate uneven terrain

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $80,000 to $110,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
