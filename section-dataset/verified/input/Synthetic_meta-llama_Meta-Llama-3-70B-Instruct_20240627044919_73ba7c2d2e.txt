Company: Melton-Sharp

Job Title: Senior Program Project Manager

About Us:
Melton-Sharp is a leading consulting firm that specializes in delivering strategic project management solutions to clients across various industries. We are committed to fostering a collaborative work environment that values diversity, innovation, and excellence.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Program Project Manager to lead our project management team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing multiple projects, developing project plans, and ensuring timely delivery within budget. The ideal candidate will possess excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement project management plans, resource allocation, and schedules
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and mitigate project risks
- Manage project budgets, track expenses, and ensure cost-effectiveness
- Communicate project progress to stakeholders through regular reports and meetings
- Lead and mentor project team members to achieve project goals

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Project Management, or a related field
- Minimum 5 years of experience in program/project management
- Proven track record of successfully delivering projects on time and within budget
- Excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills
- Certification in project management (PMP, Prince2, or Agile) is an asset

What We Offer:
- Salary range: $90,000 - $120,000 per year
- Comprehensive benefits package including health, dental, and vision insurance
- Opportunities for professional growth and development
- Collaborative and dynamic work environment
- Recognition and rewards for outstanding performance

Melton-Sharp is an equal opportunities employer committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace.
