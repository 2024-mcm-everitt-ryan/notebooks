Full-time

Job Description:
Lee PLC is seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented File Clerk to join our team. This role is crucial in maintaining the organization and accessibility of company records. The ideal candidate will be efficient, reliable, and capable of handling confidential information with discretion.

Responsibilities:
- Organize and maintain physical and electronic filing systems.
- Retrieve files as requested by employees and management.
- Ensure files are accurately labeled and stored.
- Scan and digitally archive documents.
- Assist in the preparation of reports and documents.
- Perform data entry and ensure data accuracy.
- Follow company policies regarding file management and confidentiality.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent required.
- Previous experience in a similar role is preferred but not mandatory.
- Must demonstrate competency in handling and organizing files.
- Capable candidates encouraged to apply.
- Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
- Proficiency in using basic office software (e.g., MS Office).
- Open to all qualified applicants.
