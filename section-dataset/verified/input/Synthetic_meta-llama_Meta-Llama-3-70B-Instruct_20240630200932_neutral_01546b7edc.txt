Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Mainframe Programmer Analyst to join our team.  Design, develop, and maintain mainframe applications using COBOL, JCL, and DB2

 Develop and implement testing strategies to ensure high-quality deliverables
 Manage and mentor junior team members to ensure knowledge sharing and skill development
 Participate in code reviews to ensure adherence to coding standards and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field
 At least 5 years of experience in mainframe programming, with a focus on COBOL, JCL, and DB2
 Experience in quality assurance is advantageous
 Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
 Excellent communication and teamwork skills
