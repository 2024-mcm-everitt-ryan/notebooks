Company Background:
Rivas Group, a leader in providing top-notch security solutions, is seeking an Information Security Officer to join our dynamic team. Our mission is to safeguard the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of our clients' information.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
As an Information Security Officer, you will be responsible for implementing and managing security measures to protect our information assets. You will develop security policies, conduct risk assessments, and ensure compliance with regulatory standards.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement security policies and procedures.
- Conduct regular security audits and risk assessments.
- Monitor and respond to security incidents.
- Ensure compliance with relevant regulations and standards.
- Provide security training and awareness programs for employees.

Qualifications and Experience:
- Bachelor’s degree in Information Security or related field.
- Minimum of 5 years’ experience in information security.
- Must have a clean criminal record.
- Prior employment in hands-on roles is preferred.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary: We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 70,000 to USD 100,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Company matched pension.
- Paid time off and holidays.
