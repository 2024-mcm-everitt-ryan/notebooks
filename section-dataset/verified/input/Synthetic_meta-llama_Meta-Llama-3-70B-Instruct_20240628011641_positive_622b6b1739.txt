Company: Macias, Cummings and Weaver

Job Title: Animal Breeder

About Us:
Macias, Cummings and Weaver is a leading animal breeding organisation dedicated to improving the health and well-being of animals. We are committed to creating an inclusive and diverse workforce that celebrates and supports queer identities.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Animal Breeder to join our team. As an Animal Breeder, you will be responsible for breeding animals for research, conservation, and education purposes. You will work closely with our team of veterinarians, geneticists, and researchers to develop and implement breeding programs that meet our organisational goals.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement breeding programs for various animal species
 Collaborate with veterinarians, geneticists, and researchers to ensure the health and well-being of animals
 Monitor and maintain accurate records of animal breeding, health, and behaviour
 Develop and maintain relationships with external partners and stakeholders

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Animal Science, Biology, or a related field
 At least 3 years of experience in animal breeding or a related field
 Strong understanding of animal behaviour, genetics, and breeding principles
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Perks and Benefits:
We offer a competitive salary ranging from $60,000 to $80,000 USD per year, based on your experience and skills. We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. Our benefits package includes comprehensive health insurance, retirement savings plan, and paid time off.

We prioritise LGBTQ+ candidates to apply and bring their authentic selves to our organisation. We celebrate LGBTQ+ identities and strive to have them prominently reflected in our workforce.
