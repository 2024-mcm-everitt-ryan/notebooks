Company: Jones-Little  
Role: Head Cook  

Company Background:  
Jones-Little is a renowned culinary establishment known for its commitment to culinary excellence and innovation. We are dedicated to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace where all employees feel valued and respected.

Job Description:  
We are seeking an experienced and creative Head Cook to lead our kitchen team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for culinary arts and a strong background in managing kitchen operations.

Responsibilities:  
- Oversee daily kitchen operations and ensure the highest quality of food preparation.  
- Develop and update menus to reflect seasonal ingredients and culinary trends.  
- Train and supervise kitchen staff, ensuring adherence to health and safety standards.  
- Manage inventory, ordering supplies as needed, and controlling costs.  
- Collaborate with management to create new dining experiences for our customers.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Proven experience as a Head Cook or in a similar role in a high-end restaurant.  
- Strong leadership skills with the ability to manage and motivate a diverse team.  
- Excellent knowledge of culinary techniques and trends.  
- Exceptional organizational and multitasking abilities.  
- Culinary degree or equivalent certification is preferred.

Perks and Benefits:  
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from £40,000 to £55,000, based on your experience and skills.  
- Comprehensive health and dental insurance.  
- Opportunities for professional development and career growth.  
- Parental time off for all new parents.  
- Employee discounts on dining and events.

Jones-Little is an equal opportunities employer, dedicated to diversity and inclusion. We believe in equal opportunities for employees of all backgrounds and are dedicated to supporting the LGBTQ community and all its members.
