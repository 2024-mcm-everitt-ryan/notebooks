Company Background:
Lawrence-Navarro is a leader in providing compassionate and professional animal care services. We are dedicated to the well-being of all animals and strive to create a supportive environment for both our staff and the animals in our care.

Job Type:
Full-Time Animal Caretaker

Job Description:
We are seeking a dedicated Animal Caretaker to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for animals and a commitment to their well-being. This role involves daily care, feeding, and monitoring of animals, ensuring their environment is clean and safe, and assisting with medical treatments as needed.

Responsibilities:
- Provide daily care and feeding for animals
- Clean and maintain animal habitats
- Monitor animal health and behavior
- Assist with medical treatments and check-ups
- Engage in enrichment activities for animals
- Communicate with team members about animal needs and conditions

Qualifications and Experience:
- Previous experience in animal care is required
- Strong attention to detail and compassion for animals
- Ability to work in a physically demanding environment
- Applicants must include a cover letter detailing his/her previous experience
- Preference for candidates with conventional backgrounds

Perks and Benefits:
- Comprehensive health plans that include coverage for employees and their husbands or wives
- Access to our employee wellness program
- Opportunities for professional development and training
- Moms and Dads can join our parent employee network
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 30,000 to 45,000, based on your experience and skills.

At Lawrence-Navarro, we believe in fostering a supportive and inclusive workplace. Join us in our mission to provide the highest standard of care for animals.
