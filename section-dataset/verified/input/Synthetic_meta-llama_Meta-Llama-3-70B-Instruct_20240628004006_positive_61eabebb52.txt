Job Title: Cartographer
Company: Fox, Deleon and Swanson

At Fox, Deleon and Swanson, we are dedicated to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace where everyone can thrive. We believe that our differences are what make us strong, and we are committed to building a workforce that celebrates and supports queer identities.

Job Description:
As a Cartographer, you will be responsible for creating accurate and visually appealing maps for our clients. You will work closely with our team of researchers and designers to develop innovative mapping solutions that meet our clients' needs.

Responsibilities:

 Create detailed and accurate maps using GIS software
 Collaborate with researchers and designers to develop mapping solutions
 Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in cartography
 Communicate effectively with clients to understand their needs

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Cartography, Geography, or related field
 3+ years of experience in cartography or a related field
 Proficiency in GIS software and mapping technologies
 Strong communication and collaboration skills

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $85,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. You will also be eligible for our comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement savings, and paid time off.
