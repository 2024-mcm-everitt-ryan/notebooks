Company: Gardner, Caldwell and Scott

Job Title: Survey Researcher

About Us:
Gardner, Caldwell and Scott is a leading market research firm dedicated to providing insightful data to our clients. We pride ourselves on our inclusive and diverse work environment, where everyone has a voice and an opportunity to grow.

Job Description:
We are seeking a skilled Survey Researcher to join our team. As a Survey Researcher, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and analyzing surveys to gather data for our clients. You will work closely with our team to identify research objectives, develop survey instruments, and analyze data to extract meaningful insights.

Responsibilities:

 Design and implement surveys using various methodologies
 Analyze data and extract meaningful insights
 Develop reports and presentations to communicate findings to clients
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify research objectives
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in survey research or a related field
 Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 Proficiency in statistical software and programming languages (e.g. R, Python)
