Requirements:

3. No medical condition that will adversely affect the person in the role

Key Skills:

2. Good numerical skills and basic computer literacy
3. Good general communication and collaboration skills
4. Works in a disciplined manner
5. Flexibility in shift pattern

This is a temporary ongoing role, working Sunday to Friday dependent on shift.
