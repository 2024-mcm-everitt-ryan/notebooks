Job Title: Juvenile Implementation Manager
Company: Kane, Dominguez and Galloway

About Us:
Kane, Dominguez and Galloway is a leading organization dedicated to providing innovative solutions for juvenile development. Our team of experts is committed to creating a positive impact in the lives of young individuals.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Juvenile Implementation Manager to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing the implementation of our programs and services, ensuring their effectiveness and efficiency. This role requires strong leadership and project management skills, as well as the ability to work collaboratively with our team and stakeholders.

Responsibilities:

 Develop and implement program strategies to achieve desired outcomes
 Manage and coordinate program activities, including budgeting and resource allocation
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure program goals are met
 Analyze program data to identify areas for improvement and implement changes as needed
 Participate in community outreach and engagement activities to promote our programs

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g. social work, education, psychology)
 5+ years of experience in program management, preferably in a juvenile development setting
 Proven track record of successful program implementation and management
 Excellent communication, leadership, and project management skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with multiple priorities and deadlines
 Must be able to lift up to 25 pounds and participate in off-site activities, including community service projects

What We Offer:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $65,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. We also offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
