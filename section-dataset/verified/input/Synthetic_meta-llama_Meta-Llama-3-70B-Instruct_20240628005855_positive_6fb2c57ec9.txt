Company Background:
Aguilar-Howard is a leading manufacturer of precision components, dedicated to delivering high-quality products and fostering an inclusive work environment.

Job Type: Etchers

Job Description:
We are seeking skilled etchers to join our team, responsible for engraving designs onto metal surfaces with precision and accuracy. If you have a keen eye for detail and enjoy working with intricate designs, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities:

 Engrave designs onto metal surfaces using various etching techniques
 Collaborate with designers to ensure accurate reproduction of designs
 Maintain a clean and organized workspace, adhering to safety protocols
 Meet production deadlines while ensuring quality standards

Qualifications and Experience:
 2+ years of experience in etching or a related field
 Strong attention to detail and ability to work precisely
 Excellent communication skills and ability to collaborate with designers

Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
