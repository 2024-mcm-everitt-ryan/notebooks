Company Background:
Brady, Faulkner and Johnson is a traditional values-driven company, committed to excellence in providing comprehensive consulting services in the healthcare sector. We pride ourselves on maintaining a family-oriented and cohesive work environment.

Job Type:
Full-time

Job Description:
We are seeking a Senior Human Resources Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for managing HR activities, developing policies, and ensuring a positive work environment that aligns with our core values.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement HR policies and procedures.
- Manage recruitment and selection processes.
- Oversee employee relations and handle grievances.
- Ensure compliance with labor laws and regulations.
- Conduct performance reviews and identify training needs.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor's degree in Human Resources or related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in a HR role.
- Strong proficiency in HR software and MS Office Suite.
- Excellent interpersonal and communication skills.
- Proven ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines.

Perks and Benefits:
- Competitive salary ranging from 50,000 to 70,000 USD annually, based on experience and skills.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance.
- Maternity leave support available to our female employees.
- Nursing mothers can use our mother stations.
- Positions open for men and women who are passionate about innovation.
- We support employees undergoing a sex change.
