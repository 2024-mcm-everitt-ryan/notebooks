Company: Martinez, Ross and Martinez

Job Type: Nuclear Technicians

Job Description:
Martinez, Ross and Martinez is seeking highly skilled Nuclear Technicians to join our team. As a Nuclear Technician, you will be responsible for operating and maintaining nuclear equipment, conducting experiments, and analyzing data. Our ideal candidate is a team player with excellent problem-solving skills and a strong attention to detail.

Responsibilities:

 Operate and maintain nuclear equipment and instruments
 Conduct experiments and analyze data to support nuclear research and development
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to achieve project goals
 Ensure compliance with safety protocols and regulations

Qualifications and Experience:

 Bachelor's degree in Nuclear Engineering, Physics, or a related field
 2+ years of experience in a nuclear laboratory or research setting
 Strong understanding of nuclear principles and regulations
 Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills

We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, as well as a retirement matching program.
