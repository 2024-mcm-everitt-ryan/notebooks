Company Background:
Jones-Johnson is a leading financial services company dedicated to providing innovative solutions for our clients.

Job Type:
Bill and Account Collector

Job Description:
We are seeking a results-driven Bill and Account Collector to join our team. As a Bill and Account Collector, you will be responsible for collecting payments from clients, resolving billing discrepancies, and maintaining accurate records.

Responsibilities:

 Collect payments from clients via phone, email, or mail
 Resolve billing discrepancies and address client concerns
 Maintain accurate records of client interactions and payments
 Meet or exceed monthly collection targets

Qualifications and Experience:

 1-2 years of experience in a customer-facing role, preferably in a collections environment
 High school diploma or equivalent required; some college coursework in business or a related field preferred
 Strong communication and negotiation skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines
 Proficiency in Microsoft Office and accounting software

Perks and Benefits:
We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $40,000 to $55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills. Our comprehensive benefits package includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, retirement plan matching, and paid time off.
