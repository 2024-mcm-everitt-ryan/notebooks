Company: Moyer-Lawrence  
Role: Case Management Team Leader  

Company Background:  
Moyer-Lawrence is a leading firm in providing comprehensive case management services. Our mission is to deliver exceptional support to our clients through personalized and effective strategies.

Job Description:  
We are seeking a dedicated and experienced Case Management Team Leader to join our diverse team. The ideal candidate will lead a team of case managers, ensuring high standards of service while fostering a supportive and inclusive workplace. 

Responsibilities:  
- Lead and mentor a team of case managers.
- Develop and implement case management strategies.
- Coordinate with other departments to streamline processes.
- Monitor and evaluate team performance.
- Ensure compliance with all regulatory requirements.
- Foster a collaborative and inclusive team environment.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor's degree in Social Work, Psychology, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in case management.
- Proven leadership skills and experience managing a team.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
- Proficiency in project planning and management tools.
- Commitment to diversity and inclusivity in the workplace.
