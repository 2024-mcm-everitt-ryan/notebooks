We are seeking a meticulous Logistician to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for coordinating and optimizing our supply chain operations. This role requires a deep understanding of logistics and the ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously.

- Collaborate with various departments to ensure seamless operations.  
- Analyze and report on logistics performance metrics.

- A Bachelor's degree in Logistics, Supply Chain Management, or a related field.  
- Proven experience in logistics or supply chain management.  
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.  
- Excellent communication and organizational abilities.  
- Ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously.
