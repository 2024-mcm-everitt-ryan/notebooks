Company: Johnson Ltd  
Role: Assistant Commissioner of Environmental Health Unit  

Company Background:  
Johnson Ltd is a leading organization in environmental consultancy, providing top-tier services and solutions to promote sustainable practices. We are driven by a commitment to environmental stewardship and innovation.  

Job Type: Full-time  

Job Description:  
Johnson Ltd is seeking a highly motivated individual to join our team as the Assistant Commissioner of the Environmental Health Unit. The successful candidate will lead initiatives aimed at promoting and ensuring environmental health standards across various projects.  

Responsibilities:  
- Oversee the implementation of environmental health policies and procedures.  
- Engage with stakeholders to advocate for sustainable practices.  
- Manage and guide a team of environmental health professionals.  
- Conduct thorough inspections and assessments to ensure compliance with environmental regulations.  
- Develop and present detailed reports on environmental health findings.  

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science or a related field. Credentials from recognized and respected schools are preferred.  
- Minimum of 5 years of consistent work history in environmental health or a related discipline.  
- Demonstrated leadership experience, with a preference for individuals with a background in political activism.  
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.  
- Applicants should align with environmentalist values.  

Perks and Benefits:  
- Competitive salary, ranging from $70,000 to $90,000 annually, based on experience and skills.  
- Comprehensive health insurance package.  
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.  
- Flexible work hours and remote work options.  

Application Process:  
Interested candidates should submit their resume, a cover letter, and any relevant certifications. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis, and only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
