Company Background:
Salazar, Lewis and McDaniel is dedicated to nurturing young minds with a strong focus on inclusivity and diversity. Our preschool aims to provide a supportive and engaging environment for children to learn and grow.

Job Type:
Full-Time

Job Description and Responsibilities:
As a Preschool Teacher at Salazar, Lewis and McDaniel, you will be responsible for creating a nurturing and stimulating educational environment for young children. Your duties will include developing lesson plans, encouraging social and emotional development, and fostering a love of learning. You will also be expected to engage with parents and caregivers to support children’s overall growth.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- Bachelor’s Degree in Early Childhood Education or related field
- Teaching certification preferred
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in preschool or early childhood education
- Strong communication skills and ability to work collaboratively with a diverse team
- Passion for inclusive education and celebrating diversity

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from 40,000 USD to 55,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
- Professional development opportunities
- Paid time off and holidays
- Retirement savings plan with employer match
