Company: Bray, Marquez and Reed

Role: Executive Agency Counsel

Bray, Marquez and Reed is a leading law firm seeking an exceptional Executive Agency Counsel to join our team. As a valued member of our legal department, you will provide expert counsel to our executives and play a critical role in shaping our company's future.

The Executive Agency Counsel will provide strategic legal guidance to our executives, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards. Responsibilities:

 Provide expert legal counsel to executives on regulatory matters, contracts, and business transactions
 Develop and implement policies, procedures, and best practices that align with industry standards and regulatory requirements
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and mitigate legal risks
 Stay up-to-date with changing regulations and industry trends, advising the executive team on implications and opportunities
 Manage and coordinate with external counsel on complex legal matters

Qualifications and Experience:

 Juris Doctor (JD) degree from an accredited law school
 8+ years of experience as in-house counsel or at a reputable law firm
 Proven track record of providing strategic legal guidance to executives
 Excellent communication, collaboration, and project management skills
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities
