Job Title: Photogrammetrist
Company: Martinez, Black and Torres

Company Background: 
Martinez, Black and Torres is a geospatial technology firm dedicated to providing innovative solutions for mapping and spatial data analysis. We strive to create an environment where everyone can succeed, and our team is committed to delivering high-quality results while fostering a culture of respect and inclusivity.

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly skilled Photogrammetrist to join our team. As a Photogrammetrist, you will be responsible for analyzing and interpreting aerial photographs and other remotely sensed data to extract geographic information. Your expertise will help us provide accurate and reliable data to our clients.

Responsibilities:

 Analyze and interpret aerial photographs and other remotely sensed data to extract geographic information
 Create 2D and 3D models from photogrammetric data
 Collaborate with project managers to ensure project deliverables meet client requirements
 Develop and maintain quality control procedures to ensure accuracy and consistency of data
 Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in photogrammetry and remote sensing

Qualifications and Experience:
 Bachelor's degree in Geomatics, Geography, Computer Science, or a related field
 At least 2 years of experience in photogrammetry and remote sensing
 Proficiency in data analysis and project planning
 Experience in customer relations is preferred
 Strong communication and problem-solving skills

 We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from $60,000 to $80,000 USD, based on your experience and skills.
 Comprehensive health insurance and retirement plan
 Opportunities for professional growth and development
 Collaborative and dynamic work environment
 Flexible work arrangements, including remote work options
