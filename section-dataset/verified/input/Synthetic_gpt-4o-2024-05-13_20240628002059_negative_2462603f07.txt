Company: Moss-Rice  
Role: Environmental Health & Safety Incident Investigator  

Company Background:  
Moss-Rice is a leading industry player in promoting environmental health and safety standards. We are committed to safeguarding the well-being of our employees and the communities we serve. Our efforts span multiple sectors including manufacturing, construction, and utilities.

Job Description:  
We are seeking an Environmental Health & Safety Incident Investigator to join our team. This role involves conducting in-depth investigations of workplace incidents, ensuring compliance with safety regulations, and implementing preventive measures.

Responsibilities:  
- Conduct thorough investigations of workplace incidents.
- Develop and implement corrective actions to prevent future incidents.
- Ensure compliance with local, state, and federal safety regulations.
- Prepare detailed reports on findings and recommendations.
- Collaborate with department heads to enhance safety protocols.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:  
- Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science, Occupational Health, or related field.
- 3+ years of experience in environmental health and safety, preferably in an industrial setting.
- Certification in OSHA, NEBOSH, or equivalent is a plus.
- Strong analytical skills with a keen attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
