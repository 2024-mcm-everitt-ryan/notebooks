Company Background: Singh Group is a leader in infrastructure and facility management, committed to delivering top-notch maintenance services. We pride ourselves on cultivating an inclusive and diverse workplace.

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description: We are seeking a dedicated Maintenance Worker to join our team. This position is part of our initiative to hire more women, aimed at enhancing female representation in our workforce. The successful candidate will be responsible for performing routine maintenance tasks, ensuring our facilities are in excellent condition, and adhering to safety protocols.

Responsibilities:
- Perform regular inspections and maintenance of facilities.
- Repair and replace defective equipment and systems.
- Maintain records of maintenance and repair work.
- Respond promptly to maintenance requests and emergencies.
- Ensure compliance with health and safety regulations.

Qualifications and Experience Needed:
- High school diploma or equivalent.
- Prior experience in maintenance work is preferred.
- Basic knowledge of plumbing, electrical, and carpentry.
- Ability to work independently and manage time efficiently.
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Perks and Benefits:
- We are committed to fair and equitable pay practices. The salary for this position ranges from USD 30,000 to USD 45,000, based on your experience and skills.
- Health insurance and retirement plans.
- Paid time off and holidays.
- Ongoing training and professional development opportunities.
- Supportive and inclusive work environment.

Application Process: Female candidates will receive special attention during the hiring process. All interested candidates are encouraged to apply by submitting their resume and cover letter through our online portal.
